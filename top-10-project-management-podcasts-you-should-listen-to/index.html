Top 10 Project Management Podcasts You Should Listen To


In the fast-paced world of project management, keeping your skills sharp and staying updated with industry trends is crucial. Podcasts have emerged as one of the most effective ways to absorb knowledge while on the go, making them an ideal resource for project managers. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, these podcasts will offer insights, tips, and the latest news in project management. Here are the top ten project management podcasts you should consider adding to your playlist.

  1. Project Management Podcast by Cornelius Fichtner Cornelius Fichtner's Project Management Podcast has been a staple in the industry for over a decade. He interviews thought leaders and experts in project management, providing listeners with insights into various methodologies such as Agile, Scrum, and Lean. Each episode is packed with practical advice that you can implement in your daily work. If you're looking to deepen your understanding of project management principles, this podcast is a must-listen.
  2. PM Happy Thoughts PM Happy Thoughts is hosted by Daniela P. and offers a refreshing perspective on project management. The show's aim is to make project management enjoyable and innovative. Daniela discusses various topics, from team dynamics to leadership skills, all while emphasizing the importance of positivity. The light-hearted format makes it easy to absorb serious concepts without feeling overwhelmed.
  3. The Digital Project Manager Podcast For those interested in the intersection of project management and digital technology, The Digital Project Manager podcast is invaluable. Host Derek Gaunt explores the implications of digital trends on project management and the skill sets needed for success in a highly technological landscape. This podcast not only addresses traditional project management practices but also highlights the importance of adaptation in a digital world.
  4. The Project Management Show Hosted by Michael O. and Emily G., The Project Management Show covers a variety of topics relevant to project managers across industries. Each episode features expert guests who share their experiences and tips on overcoming common challenges in project management. With a focus on practical strategies and tools, this podcast helps listeners navigate the complexities of managing projects efficiently.
  5. Project Management for the Masses This podcast, hosted by Antonio D. and Richard M., offers a straightforward approach to project management, making it accessible for everyone. They focus on best practices, tools, and techniques used in both traditional and Agile project management. Antonio and Richard's relaxed banter adds to the charm of the podcast, ensuring that you not only learn but also enjoy the process.
  6. Manage This Are you looking for real-world advice from knowledgeable project managers? Manage This, hosted by the PM Podcasters, features engaging discussions about the day-to-day realities faced by project managers. The hosts share their experiences, interviewing guests who have successfully navigated various project management challenges. Listening to their stories offers practical wisdom and helps you feel less alone in your project management journey.
  7. The Project Management Podcast by Sarah C. Sarah C. brings her years of experience to the podcasting arena with her Project Management Podcast. Through insightful interviews and in-depth discussions, she delves into project management's vital aspects, best practices, and emerging trends. What sets her apart is her commitment to inclusivity; the podcast often highlights the unique experiences of women in project management, making it a diverse and enriching resource.
  8. The PMO Podcast As a PMO (Project Management Office) expert, host Dave L. dives deep into the nitty-gritty of what makes a successful PMO. The PMO Podcast covers topics like governance, strategy, and effective communication within project management. Dave's clear and concise style makes complex topics more digestible, providing listeners with actionable insights to improve their PMO practices.
  9. The Agile Coach If you’re venturing into the world of Agile, The Agile Coach is essential listening. Hosted by the experienced Agilists, this podcast focuses on the nuances of Agile project management. It explores frameworks like Scrum, Kanban, and Lean with an emphasis on real-world application. The hosts make it a point to share practical tips and common pitfalls, helping you avoid mistakes as you embrace Agile methodologies.
  10. Women in Project Management Podcast As the name suggests, this podcast focuses on women in project management, offering a platform to share their stories and insights. Hosted by industry leaders, it covers a range of topics, from navigating challenges in the workplace to celebrating achievements. The Women in Project Management Podcast is not only empowering but also educational, providing listeners with diverse perspectives and strategies.

Why Podcasts Matter for Project Managers Podcasts have turned into a powerful tool for continuous learning and professional development. The flexibility they offer allows you to learn while commuting, working out, or even doing household chores. Here’s why you should integrate podcasts into your learning routine:

Accessibility: You can listen to them anytime, anywhere, making it easy to fit learning into your busy schedule. Expert Insights: Many podcasts feature industry experts who share their experiences, providing a wealth of knowledge you might not find in traditional books or courses. Engaging Content: Unlike reading, podcasts often include interviews and discussions that make learning more engaging and interactive. Diverse Perspectives: You'll hear from a variety of voices and opinions, which can broaden your understanding of project management challenges and solutions.

Final Thoughts Diving into project management podcasts can significantly enhance your skills, keep you informed about trending topics, and inspire your own project management journey. From expert interviews to practical tips, the insights shared in these podcasts can help you navigate the complexities of managing projects effectively. Start with a few episodes from the podcasts mentioned, and you’ll discover a wealth of knowledge that could transform your approach to project management. Happy listening!

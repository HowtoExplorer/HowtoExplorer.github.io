Romancing the Planet: The Most Romantic Cities & Islands to Visit


Love is in the air! There’s nothing quite as magical as traveling to new and captivating destinations with your significant other, or even planning that perfect honeymoon. As you embark on your romantic escapades, it’s essential to know which locations will set the right ambiance for amour. Here’s our list of the world’s most enchanting and romantic cities and islands to visit, perfect for twosomes seeking unforgettable experiences.

1. Venice, Italy

Ah, Venezia! The city of love that floats serenely along the Adriatic Sea. Walking hand-in-hand through the narrow, winding cobblestone streets, getting lost in each other’s company as you discover hidden canals, magnificent bridges, and centuries-old Vinci-designed masterpieces is an experience of a lifetime. Don’t forget to share a gondola ride, indulge in mouthwatering Italian cuisine, and sip on some Prosecco as you watch the sunset from the Rialto Bridge.

2. Santorini, Greece

With its sun-kissed beaches, picture-perfect sunsets, white-washed houses nestled into cliffs, and dazzling blue-domed churches, Santorini island can transport you into a fairytale. Wander along the caldera, visit the ancient ruins of Akrotiri, and unwind at one of the charming seaside tavernas. Share the beauty of this unique paradise with your adored one at Oia or Emporio villages, the crown jewels of Santorini, and treat yourselves to the iconic Greek delicacies like moussaka and tzatziki.

3. Paris, France

It’s hard to make a list of romantic destinations without mentioning the jewel of Europe, the city of love - Paris. The Eiffel Tower, Seine River, Louvre Museum, and such, Paris keeps on giving. Whether you’re strolling along the Champs-Élysées, watching the riverboats float by on the Seine or engaging in a moment of bliss at the top of the Montmartre hill, you and your beloved are in for a delightful time. Don’t forget to indulge in scrumptious macarons, croissants or succulent escargots!

4. Bali, Indonesia

This exotic Indonesian island with its lush landscapes, black sand beaches, and cultural virtues, Bali is an oasis for couples seeking tranquility and adventure. Discover rice terraces, ancient temples, and jungle waterfalls while never escaping the views and aromas of Bali’s beautiful sunrises and sunsets. Enjoy tailored spa treatments for two, and let the island’s soul nurse your love.

5. Quebec City, Canada

Founded in 1608, Quebec City is arguably one of the most romantic cities in North America. Its European charm lies in the fact that time seems to slow down as you saunter through its narrow streets and meander along the historic Quartier Petit Champlain. The sharp-eyed amoureux will delight in the Old Québec architecture, the local sugar shacks, and the scrumptious poutine.

6. Bora Bora, French Polynesia

Bora Bora, with its turquoise lagoons, luxurious overwater bungalows, and deadlier-than-fiction beach bars is the epitome of a romantic getaway. The lush rainforests and rugged mountains that surround this South Pacific paradise set the perfect stage for your love and passion. Make a splash with a snorkeling trip or indulge in a couples’ massage at one of the resorts - your love will surely bloom under the Polynesian sun.

7. Edinburgh, Scotland

If you and your love are fans of history, architecture, and crumbling fairy tales, Edinburgh awaits. Wander hand-in-hand through the Royal Mile or atop the Edinburgh Castle Esplanade, clutching each other in the chilly Scottish wind. The city is a must-visit, not just for the acclaimed Fringe Festival, but for its dramatic hilltop setting, the shadowy depths of the National Museum, and the magical atmosphere that envelops the historic heart of the city.

8. Dubrovnik, Croatia

Often described as the “Pearl of the Adriatic,” Dubrovnik boasts astounding beauty - both architecturally and naturally. Walk along the ancient city walls, take a beautiful sunset cruise, visit the historic Lovrijenac Fort, or simply meander around the sunset-drenched terraces of the Old Town. And don’t forget to enjoy the authentic Croatian cuisine at the many exquisite restaurants that line the cobblestoned streets.

9. Maui, Hawaii

Come and conquer the enchanting island of Maui, where a perfect balance between relaxing luxuriousness and spirited adventure awaits. Gaze in awe at the breathtaking Haleakala sunrise, saunter along the secluded black sand beaches, surf the colossal waves at Hookipa Beach Park, or soar above the panoramic views in a helicopter tour. Your nights are filled with balmy sunsets, luaus, and oceanfront dining.

10. Cusco, Peru

A timeless encounter with the ancient cultures of the Incas awaits one and all in the mystical city of Cusco. Lose yourselves to the intoxicating plumes of pungent Peruvian cuisine at one of the city’s street stalls or the hallowed halls of Koricancha. Then pack your bags, hike the infamous Inca Trail, or simply while away the hours soaking up the breathtaking Andean mountain landscape.

As you embark on your romantic journey, embrace the shared experiences, create memories to last a lifetime, and realize that it is not merely a place that makes a destination romantic, but the bond that you nurture with your partner that breathes life into these unique landscapes.

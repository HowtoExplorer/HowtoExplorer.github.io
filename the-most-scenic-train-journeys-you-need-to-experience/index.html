The Most Scenic Train Journeys You Need to Experience


Imagine winding through breathtaking landscapes, passing through quaint villages, and witnessing the ever-changing beauty of nature's palette. If you're a train enthusiast like many others, you're in for a treat. Scenic train journeys offer a unique way to experience the world's most stunning landscapes without compromising comfort and convenience. In this blog post, we'll explore the most breathtaking train journeys that will leave you in awe.

  1. The Glacier Express - Switzerland The Glacier Express is a 8-hour, 291-kilometer journey that winds its way through the picturesque Swiss Alps, a true marvel of engineering and natural beauty. Passengers can enjoy views of towering glaciers, serene lakes, and picturesque hamlets, including the iconic Jungfraujoch, also known as the "Top of Europe." With its comfortable coaches and fine dining options, this journey is a culinary and visual feast. As the train chugs along the tracks, you'll traverse through no less than 291 bridges and viaducts, offering breathtaking views of the Rhine Gorge and the Albula Alps. You might even spot a stunning glimpse of the Oberalp Pass, a breathtaking section through the Rhine Valley and Bernina Landscapes. Be sure to book a seat with a window to maximize the views, as this is a perspective you'll want to savor.
  2. The Blue Train - South Africa Luxury, opulence, and unparalleled scenery come together in this South African icon, affectionately known as The Blue Train. Over 27 hours, you'll journey from Pretoria to Cape Town, traversing along rolling hills, crystalline rivers, and rugged vistas of the kingdom of Swaziland. Expect white-sand beaches, star-studded skies, and an inviting Cape Malay-inspired cuisine to tantalize your taste buds. Experience the nation's iconic landscape, surrounded by unparalleled views of both the mountain and the desert. Witness mountain passes as massive as the Otterberg Pass mountain. Find yourself passing through the sun-drenched savanna with its unique fauna, stopping for game viewing at some choice locations.
  3. The Rocky Mountaineer - Canada This sophisticated journey traverses through vast wilderness in Western Canada. Delight in charming little railway towns, the stunning snow-capped Rock Mountain tops around you. Savor your experiences of pure Canadian nature, including imposing valleys, ravines, and snow-tipped mountains that will take your breath away. The train brings breathtaking scenery to the door of each passenger, featuring golden sunsets and majestic mountain vistas. Feel at one with this tranquil natural setting and indulge in first-class dining. With the finest comforts and panoramic windows, enjoy one breathtaking landmark after another. Watch Canada's most stunning beauty unfold naturally.
  4. The Bernina Express - Switzerland and Italy Located in the heart of a majestic mountainous range in the canton Graubünden, take the breathtaking Bernina Express from Chur in Switzerland. This 14-hour captivating train route offers an entrancing ride where passengers transcend several mountain ranges, both known Swiss and Italian ones with enthralling landscapes of European mountaineering. While the Bernina Express whips you through its beautiful panoramic scenes inside massive mountains. It forms part of a UNESCO World Heritage site. Experience of towering glaciers and frozen snows blanketing glacial lakes. Treat yourself to stunning scenery teeming with lovely larch, which shines green between its dark boughs, bringing forth majestic colors during autumn. Book your seat wisely because this window to nature's breathtaking views must be savored.
  5. The Belmond Hiram Bingham - Peru If scenic railway travel can merge with top culture and stunning history, the Belmond Hiram Bingham railway from Cusco matches that claim perfectly. With your exclusive trip behind Peru's second railway from the ever-inclined mountain grade and into a historic Andean landscape, a once-weekly express service into world-renowned Machu Picchu awaits you. On this grand eventful rail service experience the breathtaking country views, mountain trails intertwined with dramatic mountain cascades, a memorable plunge along granite valleys, along which rise mighty Andean mountains on the rail to South America's greatest Inca royal heritage sight.
  6. The Flåm Railway - Norway Journey over to one of the Westfjords of Norway, where you can start a ride to your personal happy place at Flåm Railway. Travel from Myrdal to Flåm in the heart of Fjord, a haven. On this unforgettable train tour, begin your sightseeing trip first at Aurland Station with panoramic views over the snow-capped Tvindefossen cascade. With magnificent sights to find your very own views the views develop powerfully along the winding rail route until Myrdal Valley is found near the Fjords at Flåm, rich in natural beauty. It will transport you from serene lakes, waterfalls, and fjords across and through national parks that capture your imagination.
  7. The Coast to Coast - New Zealand Experience the diverse aspects of what beauty may encompass as New Zealand offers one of its finest train journeys in which numerous geographic views change, blending both extremes of New Zealand. This two-day trip includes a trip along and across dramatic seaside zones - rugged cliffs with gulls screaming and glaciers entwined to make an unforgettable holiday. Take the coast-to-coast train adventure, which goes to coast-to-coast amidst New Zealand's most spectacular landscapes, some unmissable sights of great excitement are given to passengers by stunning features such as Auckland. Whether you choose to see your views from both the bay-side edges or, from the side window to view wide valleys with glaciers from top, the diversity in an appealingly eclectic but majestic scene you can ever call in your life sets out these views every time offering sensational experiences through each journey.
  8. The West Highland Line - Scotland This historic route has captured several local artists, world travelers, and, more importantly, breathtaking railroad-goers! The ever-present towering vistas leave impressions in tourists which go beyond the limits of imagination while this Scottish travel trek shares what Scottish scenery can offer of great charm. Located at the west of Scotland, passengers enjoy seeing their charming surroundings that go from sea and large areas such as lochs as high snow-capped peaks until high heather, soft rocks in the bracken which bring enchanting images of Scotland. This exceptional West Highland train journey operates from Glasgow to Mallaig or Fort William, leaving you at a memorable footpath for the iconic Ben Nevis. Its route leads through the Aonach Mòr mountains, Glencoe, one of the most beautiful passes in the U.K., along and after an intense loop of waterfalls in the impressive glacial rocks you can head towards the wild landscapes like rugged mountains, wind-swept shores, and turquoise lochs. The world's scenic train journeys paint the canvas of grandeur and awe-inspiring wonder that one experiences while watching the changing scenery at the observation windows of your carriage. From mighty mountains and glassy waterfalls to gentle sea shores and idyllic hamlets, a journey in a scenic train conveys profound feelings of excitement and elation to the traveler.

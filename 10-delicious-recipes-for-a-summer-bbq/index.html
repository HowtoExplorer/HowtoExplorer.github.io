10 Delicious Recipes for a Summer BBQ


Summer BBQs are the epitome of outdoor dining, bringing together friends, family, and great food. Whether you’re hosting a small get-together or a large gathering, the right recipes can elevate your BBQ to an unforgettable experience. From smoky grilled meats to refreshing salads and sides, the options are endless. Here are 10 delicious recipes that are perfect for any summer BBQ.

1. Classic BBQ Ribs

Nothing says BBQ like a plate of smoky, tender ribs. The key to great ribs is all in the marinade and slow cooking process. For a tangy yet sweet flavor, create a simple BBQ sauce with brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, and your favorite spices. Let the ribs marinate overnight for the best flavor, then slow-cook them on the grill over indirect heat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Rub the ribs with brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the ribs on the grill over indirect heat, cover, and cook for 2-3 hours, flipping halfway through.
  4. In the last 30 minutes, brush with BBQ sauce and continue cooking, allowing the sauce to caramelize.
  5. Once the ribs are tender and juicy, remove from the grill and let them rest before cutting into portions.

2. Grilled Chicken Skewers

Grilled chicken skewers are easy to prepare and packed with flavor. You can customize these skewers with your favorite marinades, from teriyaki to lemon herb or even spicy Cajun seasoning. The key is to marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes to infuse it with all those delicious flavors before grilling.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water for 30 minutes)

Directions:

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized cubes.
  2. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss the chicken cubes in the marinade and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Thread the chicken onto the skewers.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and cook the skewers for 5-7 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through.

3. Grilled Veggie Medley

Grilled vegetables are the perfect side dish for a BBQ, offering a healthy and flavorful addition to your meal. You can use a variety of vegetables such as bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms, and onions. The best part? Grilled veggies require minimal preparation and cook quickly.

Ingredients:

  • 2 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced into rings
  • 1 cup mushrooms, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Toss the vegetables with olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the vegetables on the grill and cook for 3-5 minutes per side, until charred and tender.
  4. Serve immediately with a drizzle of balsamic vinegar for an extra touch of flavor.

4. Corn on the Cob with Herb Butter

Corn on the cob is a summer BBQ classic that pairs beautifully with grilled meats. Smothering the corn with herbed butter adds an extra layer of flavor that will have your guests coming back for more. You can also grill the corn in its husks for a smoky touch.

Ingredients:

  • 6 ears of corn, husked
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the corn directly on the grill for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until slightly charred and tender.
  3. While the corn is grilling, combine butter, parsley, chives, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Once the corn is done, brush it generously with the herbed butter and serve hot.

5. Crispy BBQ Chicken Wings

No BBQ is complete without chicken wings. These crispy BBQ chicken wings are coated in a smoky, sweet sauce that caramelizes perfectly on the grill. They’re ideal for finger food and will disappear in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 12 chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Coat the chicken wings with olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the wings for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway through, until crispy and cooked through.
  4. Brush the wings with BBQ sauce in the last 5 minutes of grilling, allowing the sauce to caramelize.

6. Grilled Shrimp Tacos

For something a little different, try grilled shrimp tacos. The shrimp is marinated in a zesty lime and garlic marinade and grilled to perfection. Serve it in soft tortillas with fresh salsa, avocado, and a drizzle of creamy chipotle sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Fresh salsa, avocado, and chipotle sauce for serving

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lime juice, garlic, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss the shrimp in the marinade and let them sit for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  4. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until they turn pink and opaque.
  5. Warm the tortillas on the grill for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Assemble the tacos by placing the shrimp on the tortillas, then topping with fresh salsa, avocado, and a drizzle of chipotle sauce.

7. Caprese Salad Skewers

A fresh and light salad is always a welcome addition to a BBQ. Caprese salad skewers are easy to assemble, and their fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and basil make for a vibrant and flavorful bite. Drizzle with balsamic glaze for a sweet and tangy finish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Directions:

  1. Alternate threading cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and basil leaves onto wooden skewers.
  2. Arrange the skewers on a platter and drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

8. Grilled Pineapple with Cinnamon and Honey

For a sweet finish to your BBQ, grilled pineapple with cinnamon and honey is a simple yet delicious option. The natural sugars in the pineapple caramelize as it grills, creating a delightful treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pineapple, peeled and sliced into rings
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the pineapple slices with honey and sprinkle with cinnamon.
  3. Grill the pineapple for 3-4 minutes per side until caramelized.
  4. Serve warm as a sweet side or dessert.

9. BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

These mini pulled pork sliders are the perfect way to serve BBQ in bite-sized portions. Slow-cooked pulled pork is topped with coleslaw and tangy BBQ sauce, all nestled inside soft slider buns.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork shoulder
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 12 slider buns
  • Coleslaw for topping

Directions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Rub the pork shoulder with smoked paprika, garlic powder, and brown sugar.
  3. Cook the pork shoulder on the grill over indirect heat for 4-5 hours, basting with apple cider vinegar every hour.
  4. Once the pork is tender and pulls apart easily, shred it with forks.
  5. Serve the pulled pork on slider buns, topped with BBQ sauce and coleslaw.

10. Lemonade Iced Tea

No BBQ is complete without a refreshing drink to wash it all down. This lemonade iced tea combines the tartness of lemonade with the smoothness of iced tea for a sweet and tangy beverage that’s perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups brewed iced tea
  • 2 cups freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Directions:

  1. Brew the iced tea and let it cool.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the iced tea, lemon juice, and honey or sugar.
  3. Stir well until the sweetener is dissolved.
  4. Serve over ice cubes with lemon slices for garnish.

Conclusion

A summer BBQ is a perfect opportunity to indulge in delicious food and enjoy good company. From grilled meats to fresh salads and refreshing drinks, these 10 recipes will help you create a memorable BBQ feast that everyone will love. So fire up the grill, gather your ingredients, and get ready for a summer celebration full of flavor!

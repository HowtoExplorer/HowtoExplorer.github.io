DIY Jewelry Displays: Showcase Your Accessories with Style


Are you tired of rummaging through a tangled mess of necklaces and bracelets every time you need to find the perfect accessory? Do you find yourself constantly misplacing your favorite pair of earrings? If so, it’s time to take matters into your own hands and create your own DIY jewelry displays. Not only will these displays keep your jewelry organized, but they will also add a touch of style to your bedroom or dressing area. In this blog post, we will explore some creative and easy-to-make jewelry display ideas that will help you showcase your accessories with style.

1. Branch Jewelry Display

One of the simplest and most rustic ways to display your jewelry is by using a branch. Find a sturdy branch with interesting shapes and textures, and clean it thoroughly. Once dry, you can either mount it on the wall or place it in a decorative vase. Hang your necklaces, bracelets, and even rings on the branches of the display. Not only will this create a unique and eye-catching display, but it will also prevent your jewelry from getting tangled.

2. Frame Jewelry Organizer

Transform an old picture frame into a stylish jewelry organizer. Remove the glass and backing from the frame, and paint it in the color of your choice. You can also add some decorative touches like ribbons or hooks to hang your jewelry. Once dry, attach a piece of mesh or chicken wire to the back of the frame using a staple gun. This will serve as the perfect surface to hang your earrings and pins. Hang the frame on the wall, and voila! You have a chic and functional jewelry display.

3. Vintage Tray Jewelry Display

If you’re a fan of vintage aesthetics, a vintage tray jewelry display is the perfect choice for you. Look for a beautiful vintage tray at a thrift store or flea market. Clean it thoroughly and line it with a soft fabric or velvet to protect your jewelry. Arrange your earrings, rings, and necklaces on the tray, and place it on your vanity or dresser. This elegant display will not only keep your jewelry organized but also add a touch of old-world charm to your space.

4. Pegboard Jewelry Wall

For those who have a large collection of jewelry, a pegboard jewelry wall is a practical and visually appealing solution. Purchase a pegboard from a hardware store and paint it in a color that complements your room’s decor. Attach hooks, pegs, and small shelves to the board to hang your necklaces, bracelets, and earrings. You can also use small baskets or cups to store your rings and pins. Mount the pegboard on the wall, and you’ll have a customizable and efficient jewelry display.

5. Driftwood Jewelry Stand

If you’re a beach lover, why not bring a piece of the seaside into your home with a driftwood jewelry stand? Find a piece of driftwood that has interesting shapes and textures. Clean it thoroughly and let it dry. Once dry, you can attach small hooks or nails to hang your necklaces, bracelets, and earrings. Place the stand on your vanity or dresser, and let the natural beauty of the driftwood enhance your jewelry collection.

Conclusion

Creating your own DIY jewelry displays not only allows you to showcase your accessories with style but also adds a personal touch to your space. Whether you prefer a rustic branch display, a vintage tray, or a pegboard wall, there are endless possibilities to organize and display your jewelry in a way that reflects your unique style. So, gather your materials, unleash your creativity, and start crafting your own jewelry displays today. Your jewelry will thank you, and you’ll never have to search for the perfect accessory again.

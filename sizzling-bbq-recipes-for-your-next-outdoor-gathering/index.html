Sizzling BBQ Recipes for Your Next Outdoor Gathering


Summer is the perfect time for outdoor gatherings, and there’s no better way to celebrate than with mouthwatering BBQ. Whether you’re hosting a family get-together, a friendly neighborhood party, or a competitive cookout, these sizzling recipes will delight your guests and leave them yearning for more. So grab your apron and let’s get grilling!

BBQ Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs or breasts
  • BBQ seasoning (store-bought or homemade)
  • Your favorite BBQ sauce

Instructions

  1. Begin by seasoning the chicken with your preferred BBQ seasoning, making sure to cover all surfaces.
  2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Place the seasoned chicken on the grill, and cook for about 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  4. Once the chicken is done, brush generously with your chosen BBQ sauce, and let it grill for another 2-3 minutes to allow the sauce to caramelize.
  5. Serve hot, and enjoy the compliments!

Smoked Brisket

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs beef brisket
  • BBQ seasoning (store-bought or homemade)
  • Your favorite BBQ sauce

Instructions

  1. Begin by applying a generous amount of BBQ seasoning to the beef brisket, ensuring even coverage.
  2. Preheat your smoker or grill to 250°F (121°C) using a combination of hardwood and charcoal.
  3. Place the seasoned brisket on the smoker or grill, fat side up.
  4. Maintain the temperature at 250°F (121°C) throughout the process, adding more charcoal as needed.
  5. Smoke the brisket for about 1 hour per pound, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 190-200°F (88-93°C).
  6. Once the brisket is done, remove it from the smoker or grill and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Slice against the grain and serve with your favorite BBQ sauce, or enjoy it plain for purists.

Sweet Potato Grilled BBQ Wedges

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Your favorite BBQ seasoning

Instructions

  1. Begin by washing and peeling the sweet potatoes.
  2. Cut them into wedges, making sure to remove the leaves and sides.
  3. In a bowl, mix olive oil, salt, and pepper to create a simple marinade.
  4. Coat the potato wedges in the marinade and let them rest for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Once the grill is hot, place the sweet potato wedges on the grill.
  7. Cook for about 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked and slightly caramelized.
  8. While still hot, sprinkle BBQ seasoning over the wedges for added flavor.
  9. Serve with ketchup, ranch, or your preferred dipping sauce.

Grilled Pineapple Upside-Down Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 box cake mix
  • 1 can (8 oz) crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • Eggs (as specified on the cake mix box)
  • Water (as specified on the cake mix box)
  • Brown sugar
  • Caramel sauce
  • Whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Begin by preheating your grill to medium heat.
  2. In a skillet, cook the crushed pineapple in a bit of butter until they begin to soften.
  3. Once the pineapple is cooked, remove it from the skillet and spoon it into a baking dish.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the cake mix, oil, eggs, and water as instructed on the box.
  5. Pour the cake batter over the pineapple in the baking dish.
  6. Sprinkle brown sugar evenly over the cake batter.
  7. Place the baking dish on the grill, and close the lid.
  8. Cook for about 30-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the cake comes out clean.
  9. Remove the dish from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes.
  10. Once cool, flip the dish over onto a serving plate, and let the cake slide out.
  11. Drizzle with caramel sauce and top with a generous dollop of whipped cream.
  12. Slice and serve, offering each guest a slice of BBQ heaven.

With these delicious BBQ recipes in your arsenal, you’ll be the talk of the town at every outdoor gathering. From tantalizing chicken to savory brisket and sweet potato wedges, your guests will be asking for seconds. And don’t forget the star of the show, the delightful pineapple upside-down cake, which is sure to become a crowd favorite. So get grilling, and make some unforgettable memories this summer!

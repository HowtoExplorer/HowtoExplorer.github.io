Embark on a journey of discovery and wonder with the most breathtaking road trip routes that will leave you awestruck. Northern Ireland's Causeway Coastal Route

Winding its way along the Antrim coast of Northern Ireland, the Causeway Coastal Route is an experience not to be missed. Breathe in the salty air, feel the roar of the Atlantic beneath your wheels, and take in the staggering vistas of rugged cliffs, sparkling waterfalls, and the iconic Giant's Causeway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site of interlocking basalt columns. As you chug along, keep your eyes peeled for the eerie Goat Fell, the imposing Mussenden Temple, and the picturesque town of Ballintoy, a haven for foodies and admirers of folk music.

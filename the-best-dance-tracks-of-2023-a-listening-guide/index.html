The Best Dance Tracks of 2023: A Listening Guide


Dance music has the power to transport us to a different world, to make us feel alive, and to bring people together. As we reflect on the past year, it's clear that dance music has evolved significantly, with a wide range of styles and sub-genres emerging. From the euphoric melodies of EDM to the hip-swiveling beats of Afrobeats, there's been no shortage of infectious and memorable dance tracks in 2023. The Rise of Future Rave and Hardstyle One of the most notable trends in dance music this year has been the resurgence of Future Rave and Hardstyle. These styles have been characterized by their fast-paced beats, heavy kick-drums, and mind-blowing synthesizers. Artists like Blasterjaxx, Brennan Heart, and Brennan & Daverse have been pushing the boundaries of these genres, incorporating new elements and creating a fresh wave of bass-heavy soundscapes. The Iconic Three-Way Probs of RM Summers, Elevated Mono But it's not just Future Rave and Hardstyle that have been making waves in 2023. Other sub-genres like House, Techno, and Drum and Bass have also experienced a resurgence in popularity. RM Summers' "Elevated Mono" has been a standout track in the Drum and Bass scene, with its aggressive bassline and next-level production-quality earning high praise from fans and critics alike. Meanwhile, Elevated Mono's "Running Man" remix has made waves in the Techno world, showcasing the artist's versatility and creative approach to production. The Mainstream Breakthrough of Peggy Folvork 2023 has also seen a number of dance artists breaking into the mainstream. One of the most notable examples is Peggy Folvork, whose 2023 track "Sugar Kids" has taken the dance scene by storm. With its enticing vocals and harmonic melodies, "Sugar Kids" is an addictive anthem that captures the hearts of listeners.

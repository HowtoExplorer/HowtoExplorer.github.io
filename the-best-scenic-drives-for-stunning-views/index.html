The Best Scenic Drives for Stunning Views


Are you ready for an adventure? Buckle up and get ready to embark on a journey through some of the most breathtaking landscapes nature has to offer. We’ve curated a list of the best scenic drives that will leave you in awe of the stunning views that await you. So, let’s hit the road and explore these picturesque routes!

Pacific Coast Highway, California

Starting off our list is the iconic Pacific Coast Highway in California. This epic drive along the rugged coastline offers jaw-dropping views of the Pacific Ocean, dramatic cliffs, and golden sandy beaches. As you wind your way through Big Sur, make sure to stop at Bixby Creek Bridge for a perfect photo op. The Pacific Coast Highway is a must-do for any road trip enthusiast seeking unforgettable scenery.

Icefields Parkway, Canada

Prepare to be mesmerized by the natural wonders of the Canadian Rockies as you navigate the Icefields Parkway in Alberta, Canada. This 144-mile stretch of road takes you through the heart of Banff and Jasper National Parks, showcasing pristine turquoise lakes, towering glaciers, and majestic mountain peaks. Keep an eye out for wildlife sightings, including bears, elk, and mountain goats. The Icefields Parkway is a visual feast that will leave you breathless.

Great Ocean Road, Australia

Down under, the Great Ocean Road in Victoria, Australia, offers a coastal drive like no other. This 150-mile stretch of road hugs the southern coastline, revealing stunning rock formations, including the famous Twelve Apostles. Be prepared to witness the power of the Southern Ocean as waves crash against towering cliffs. Don’t forget to stop at the Loch Ard Gorge and London Arch for more awe-inspiring vistas.

Ring of Kerry, Ireland

For a taste of the Emerald Isle’s beauty, take a drive along the Ring of Kerry in southwestern Ireland. This 111-mile loop showcases Ireland’s rugged coastline, picturesque villages, and rolling green hills. Keep your camera handy as you pass by Killarney National Park, where you’ll find enchanting lakes, ancient forests, and the picturesque Torc Waterfall. The Ring of Kerry is a scenic drive that will transport you to a fairytale land.

Trollstigen, Norway

If you’re looking for a drive with a touch of adventure, head to Norway and conquer the hairpin bends of Trollstigen. This dramatic mountain road offers breathtaking views of steep cliffs, cascading waterfalls, and deep fjords. Be prepared for narrow passages and a sense of thrill as you navigate the winding road. Trollstigen is not for the faint of heart but promises an unforgettable journey through Norway’s stunning landscapes.

Conclusion

Whether you’re a seasoned road trip enthusiast or a first-time traveler, these scenic drives will leave you in awe of the beauty that surrounds us. From the rugged coastline of California to the majestic Canadian Rockies, each drive offers its own unique charm and mesmerizing views. So, pack your bags, hit the road, and let these stunning vistas take your breath away. Happy driving!

Note: Remember to follow local traffic laws and regulations, and always prioritize safety while enjoying these scenic drives.

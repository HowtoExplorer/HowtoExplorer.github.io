Crafting with Shells: Beach-Inspired Decor and Jewelry


Are you longing for the calming and soothing vibes of the beach? Do you want to bring a piece of the ocean into your home? Crafting with shells is the perfect way to create beach-inspired decor and unique jewelry pieces that will transport you to the sandy shores every time you look at them. In this blog post, we will explore different ways to incorporate shells into your crafting projects, from stunning wall hangings to elegant necklaces. So, grab your crafting supplies and let’s dive in!

Shell Wall Hangings

One of the most popular ways to use shells in crafting is by creating beautiful wall hangings. Start by gathering an assortment of shells in various sizes, shapes, and colors. Next, find a piece of driftwood or a wooden hoop that will serve as the base for your wall hanging.

Begin by arranging the shells on the base in a way that pleases your eye. You can create a symmetrical design or go for a more organic and random placement. Once you’re satisfied with the arrangement, use a hot glue gun to secure the shells to the base. Be careful not to burn yourself!

For an added touch, you can incorporate other beach-inspired elements like pieces of sea glass, starfish, or even small pieces of driftwood. Hang your creation on a wall in your home, and every time you look at it, you’ll be transported to the beach, no matter how far away you are.

Shell Jewelry

Shells also make for stunning and unique jewelry pieces. To create shell necklaces or bracelets, you will need a few basic jewelry-making supplies like jewelry wire, clasps, and jump rings. Start by selecting shells with natural holes or drill small holes in them carefully.

Next, cut a piece of jewelry wire to your desired length for the necklace or bracelet. String the shells onto the wire, alternating them with colorful beads or other small charms if you desire. Once you have the desired number of shells and beads on the wire, attach the clasp and jump rings to complete your jewelry piece.

If you want to take your shell jewelry to the next level, consider using shells as the main pendant for a necklace or as focal points in statement earrings. The possibilities are endless, and you can let your creativity run wild.

Shell Decorative Accents

In addition to wall hangings and jewelry, shells can be used to create various decorative accents. Consider filling a glass vase with an assortment of shells and placing it on a shelf or coffee table for a touch of coastal charm. You can also glue shells onto picture frames, mirrors, or even create a unique shell-covered candle holder.

For a more functional craft, try making shell-embellished coasters or a shell-adorned jewelry dish. These small accents can add a touch of the beach to any room in your home and make for great conversation starters when guests come over.

Conclusion

Crafting with shells is a wonderful way to bring the beauty and tranquility of the beach into your home. From shell wall hangings to unique jewelry pieces, the possibilities are endless. So, gather your shells, unleash your creativity, and start crafting! Let the ocean’s treasures inspire you to create stunning decor and jewelry pieces that will transport you to the beach whenever you look at them.

Remember, crafting is not just about the end result, but also about the joy and relaxation it brings. So, take your time, enjoy the process, and let your imagination guide you. Happy crafting!

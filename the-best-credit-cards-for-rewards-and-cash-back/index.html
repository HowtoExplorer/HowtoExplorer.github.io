The Best Credit Cards for Rewards and Cash Back


In today’s world, credit cards have become more than just a payment tool. Many credit cards offer a wide variety of perks, including rewards and cash back. Whether you’re a frequent traveler, a grocery shopper, or someone looking to make the most of your everyday spending, there’s a credit card out there that can provide valuable benefits.

Navigating the sea of credit card options can be overwhelming, especially with the numerous programs available, each with its unique set of advantages. But understanding how to leverage credit card rewards can transform your spending habits, allowing you to earn more with every purchase. In this guide, we will break down some of the best credit cards for rewards and cash back, exploring their key features, how to maximize your earnings, and which card may be the right fit for you.

What Makes a Credit Card Good for Rewards and Cash Back?

Before diving into the specifics of various cards, it’s important to understand what factors make a credit card ideal for earning rewards or cash back:

  • Rewards Structure: Different cards offer rewards on different types of purchases. Some cards offer higher returns on certain categories, like dining or travel, while others provide flat-rate rewards on all purchases.
  • Sign-Up Bonuses: Many credit cards offer attractive sign-up bonuses to incentivize new customers. These bonuses can be in the form of extra points, miles, or cash back, making them a great way to earn a large amount of rewards early on.
  • Annual Fees: Some of the best rewards cards come with annual fees, which can sometimes be worth paying if the rewards outweigh the cost. However, many no-annual-fee cards also offer substantial benefits, so it’s important to compare them.
  • Redemption Flexibility: The ability to redeem rewards easily is essential. Whether you’re using points for travel, applying cash back directly to your account, or receiving gift cards, flexibility is key.
  • Additional Perks: Some rewards cards offer extra perks, such as extended warranties, travel insurance, and purchase protections, which add value to the card beyond just the rewards.

Now that we have an understanding of what makes a great rewards card, let’s take a look at some of the top options available on the market.

1. The Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card

The Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card is often regarded as one of the best rewards cards on the market for travelers. This card offers a great combination of earning potential, redemption flexibility, and valuable travel benefits.

Key Features:

  • Sign-Up Bonus: Earn 60,000 bonus points after you spend $4,000 on purchases in the first 3 months from account opening. These points are worth $750 toward travel through Chase Ultimate Rewards®.
  • Rewards: Earn 2X points on travel and dining at restaurants & 1X point on all other purchases.
  • Annual Fee: $95.
  • Points Redemption: Points can be redeemed through Chase Ultimate Rewards®, which provides a variety of options, including travel, cash back, and gift cards. Points are worth 25% more when redeemed for travel through the portal.
  • Travel Protections: The card includes valuable travel insurance benefits like trip cancellation/interruption insurance, car rental insurance, and more.

Why It’s Great:

The Chase Sapphire Preferred® Card is ideal for those who travel often or dine out frequently. The sign-up bonus is highly valuable, and the 2X points on travel and dining make it a great card for earning while enjoying everyday activities. Additionally, the card’s ability to enhance the value of your points when used for travel through the Chase Ultimate Rewards® portal is a big draw.

2. The Blue Cash Preferred® Card from American Express

For those who primarily focus on cash back, the Blue Cash Preferred® Card from American Express is one of the best options available. This card offers excellent rewards on everyday spending categories like grocery stores and streaming services.

Key Features:

  • Sign-Up Bonus: Earn a $250 statement credit after you spend $3,000 in purchases in the first 3 months.
  • Rewards: Earn 6% cash back on the first $6,000 spent at U.S. supermarkets each year (then 1%), 6% on select U.S. streaming subscriptions, 3% on transit and U.S. gas stations, and 1% on other purchases.
  • Annual Fee: $95.
  • Cash Back: Cash back is received in the form of a statement credit, which helps reduce your balance directly.
  • Intro APR: 0% intro APR on purchases for the first 12 months, then a variable APR.

Why It’s Great:

If you spend a lot at supermarkets, this card will deliver great rewards, offering a fantastic 6% cash back. The sign-up bonus is a solid incentive for new cardholders, and the additional rewards on streaming services and transit make it an excellent choice for modern consumers. Plus, the 0% intro APR on purchases makes it easier to finance larger purchases in the first year.

3. The Citi Double Cash Card

If you prefer a straightforward, no-fuss cash-back rewards card, the Citi Double Cash Card is an excellent option. With no annual fee and a simple rewards structure, this card is ideal for those who want to earn cash back without having to keep track of rotating categories.

Key Features:

  • Sign-Up Bonus: No sign-up bonus.
  • Rewards: Earn 2% on every purchase: 1% when you buy, and 1% when you pay.
  • Annual Fee: $0.
  • Cash Back: Cash back is earned as statement credits or checks, with no limit on how much you can earn.
  • Redemption Flexibility: Cash back can be redeemed at any time, with no minimum redemption amount required.

Why It’s Great:

The Citi Double Cash Card offers the simplicity of a flat-rate 2% cash back on every purchase. This makes it a great option for people who want to maximize their rewards without the need to track bonus categories. Additionally, there is no annual fee, which is a big plus for those who want a high-earning rewards card with minimal costs.

4. The Capital One Quicksilver Cash Rewards Credit Card

The Capital One Quicksilver Cash Rewards Credit Card is another excellent choice for earning cash back, especially for those who want a flat-rate rewards structure without any annual fee. This card offers consistent earning opportunities, whether you’re spending on groceries, gas, or entertainment.

Key Features:

  • Sign-Up Bonus: Earn a one-time $200 cash bonus after you spend $500 on purchases within the first 3 months from account opening.
  • Rewards: Earn 1.5% on every purchase, every day.
  • Annual Fee: $0.
  • Cash Back: Cash back can be redeemed for statement credits, gift cards, or checks.
  • Additional Benefits: Access to extended warranty protection and 24-hour travel assistance services.

Why It’s Great:

The Capital One Quicksilver Cash Rewards Credit Card is perfect for those who prefer a simple cash-back rewards program. The 1.5% cash back on every purchase is higher than many other flat-rate cards, and with no annual fee, it’s a cost-effective choice for most people. Plus, the $200 sign-up bonus is an easy-to-earn incentive.

5. The Wells Fargo Active Cash® Card

The Wells Fargo Active Cash® Card stands out for offering a high rewards rate on all purchases. This card is perfect for those who want to earn cash back without having to worry about rotating categories or specific spending thresholds.

Key Features:

  • Sign-Up Bonus: Earn a $200 cash rewards bonus after spending $500 in the first 3 months.
  • Rewards: Earn unlimited 2% cash rewards on all purchases.
  • Annual Fee: $0.
  • Cash Back: Redeem your cash rewards as a statement credit or use them for other rewards like gift cards.
  • Intro APR: 0% intro APR for 15 months on purchases and qualifying balance transfers, then a variable APR.

Why It’s Great:

The Wells Fargo Active Cash® Card offers a simple yet highly rewarding cash-back structure. With 2% back on every purchase, it’s ideal for those who want a high, flat-rate return without needing to track spending categories. The $200 sign-up bonus provides a strong start, and the 0% intro APR is helpful for financing large purchases.

Conclusion

When it comes to selecting the best credit card for rewards and cash back, there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you’re looking for a card with travel perks, flat-rate cash back, or high returns on everyday spending, there’s a card that can meet your needs. By carefully considering factors like annual fees, reward categories, and redemption options, you can find a card that offers significant value for your spending habits.

Ultimately, the best card for you will depend on how you spend and what type of rewards you’re seeking. Whether you opt for a card that provides cash back on every purchase, a card with bonus rewards for specific categories, or a travel rewards card that offers flexibility, you can maximize your earnings by using the right card for your lifestyle. Take the time to compare different options and choose the one that aligns with your financial goals to get the most out of your spending.

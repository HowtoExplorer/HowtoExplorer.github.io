The Most Unique Accommodations You Can Book


Are you looking for a way to spice up your next vacation or weekend getaway? Look no further than these truly one-of-a-kind accommodations that will leave you and your loved ones in awe. From undersea hotels to treehouse retreats, we've scoured the globe to bring you the most unique and extraordinary places to stay.

  1. Underwater Hotels: A Submarine Stay Imagine waking up to the sound of waves gently lapping against the hull and drifting off to sleep amidst the vibrant coral and marine life. The underwater hotels of Dubai and the Maldives offer guests the chance to experience a truly immersive underwater experience. Each room is designed to provide unobstructed views of the ocean floor, complete with large glass windows and even an underwater bedroom for the ultimate thrill-seeker. The hotels' state-of-the-art exterior designs are shaped like the undulations of the seafloor, creating a seamless fusion of man-made architecture and natural beauty. Enjoy fine dining under the sea, or simply relax in your en-suite with a glass of champagne and watch the marine life glide effortlessly by.
  2. Glamping Treehouses For the outdoor enthusiasts, a stay in one of these magnificent treehouses is the ultimate way to connect with nature while still enjoying the comforts of home. Elevated deep into the forest canopy, these structures blend seamlessly into their surroundings, becoming one with the surrounding foliage. Imagine gazing out through the latticework of branches and leaves as the sun rises over the treetops, casting dappled shadows across the forest floor. These enchanting treehouses are equipped with plush furnishings, luxurious beddings, and even fully-stocked kitchens to ensure that guests can indulge in their favorite meals while surrounded by the tranquility of the forest.
  3. Glass Igloos: Unwind Under the Starry Skies Experience the serene beauty of the Arctic landscape from the comfort of a glass-roofed igloo. These stunning abodes offer unobstructed views of the night sky, allowing you to unwind under the starry expanse of the polar night. Snuggle up in the plush bed and watch the aurora borealis dance across the curtains of light above. Each igloo is equipped with lavish amenities, including fine linens, gourmet tea, and a personalized concierge service to cater to your every whim. Wake up to the melodic song of the northern birds and step outside to experience the breathtaking vistas of the Arctic wilderness.
  4. Overwater Bungalows: Paradise in the Pacific Combine the romance of the Pacific with the luxury of a high-end resort at the overwater bungalows of Bora Bora. Each bures (that's Tahitian for "bungalow") is perched delicately above the crystal-clear waters of the South Pacific. Step out onto the private deck, submerge yourself in the ocean, and float effortlessly among a parade of tropical fish. These magnificent structures rise out of the lagoon like giant crabs, their roofs a clever construction of layered palm thatch. Inside, the richly adorned furnishings and oversize windows ensure that you never lose sight of the sun-kissed island paradise. Unwind to the sound of the waves and listen to the balmy whisper of the tropical breeze.

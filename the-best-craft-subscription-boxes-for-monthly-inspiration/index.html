The Best Craft Subscription Boxes for Monthly Inspiration


Crafting is not just a hobby; it’s a passion that fuels creativity and provides a therapeutic escape from the daily grind. With busy schedules and the overwhelming number of crafting options available, finding the right inspiration can sometimes become a challenge. This is where craft subscription boxes come into play, delivering curated materials, tools, and project ideas directly to your doorstep each month. Whether you are a seasoned crafter or a beginner looking to explore new techniques, these subscription boxes offer something for everyone.

In this post, we will explore some of the best craft subscription boxes available today, highlighting what makes each one unique and how they can ignite your creative spirit month after month.

1. Craft Box Club

Overview

Craft Box Club is a delightful subscription service that caters to both children and adults, making it perfect for families looking to engage in creative activities together. Each box includes all the materials needed to complete a specific project, along with easy-to-follow instructions.

What’s Inside?

Every month, subscribers receive a themed box that could include anything from painting supplies to jewelry-making kits. The projects are designed to be fun and engaging, ensuring that crafters of all skill levels can participate.

Why Choose Craft Box Club?

  • Family-Friendly: Perfect for bonding time with kids.
  • Variety of Projects: Each box introduces a new crafting technique.
  • Eco-Friendly Materials: They prioritize sustainability in their product choices.

2. ArtSnacks

Overview

For those who have a love for art, ArtSnacks is a must-try subscription box. This service is dedicated to artists and creatives who want to explore new art supplies and mediums every month.

What’s Inside?

Each box includes premium art supplies such as paints, markers, brushes, and more, often accompanied by a unique challenge or project that encourages subscribers to push their artistic boundaries.

Why Choose ArtSnacks?

  • Premium Quality: Only high-end supplies are included.
  • Creative Challenges: Each month presents an opportunity to experiment with new techniques.
  • Community Engagement: Subscribers can share their creations online and connect with other artists.

3. KiwiCo

Overview

KiwiCo primarily targets younger audiences but has options for all ages, focusing on STEM-based projects that also involve creativity. Their boxes are designed to be educational and fun, making them perfect for inquisitive minds.

What’s Inside?

Each crate includes hands-on projects that cover various themes such as engineering, art, and science. From building robots to crafting intricate models, every box aims to educate while inspiring creativity.

Why Choose KiwiCo?

  • Educational Value: Projects are designed to teach while they entertain.
  • Variety of Crates: Options available for different age groups and interests.
  • Engaging and Interactive: Projects encourage critical thinking and problem-solving.

4. The Crafter’s Box

Overview

The Crafter’s Box is perfect for serious crafters looking to hone their skills. This subscription service focuses on a specific craft each month, featuring expert instructors who guide subscribers through the process.

What’s Inside?

Each box contains high-quality tools and materials tailored to the selected craft, along with instructional videos and resources from skilled artisans.

Why Choose The Crafter’s Box?

  • Skill Development: Ideal for those wanting to improve their crafting skills.
  • Expert Guidance: Learn from professionals in a variety of crafting fields.
  • Community Support: Join a vibrant community of fellow crafters.

5. Doodle Crate

Overview

Doodle Crate, a subsidiary of KiwiCo, is aimed at older kids and adults who love to create and express themselves artistically. This subscription box focuses on art and design projects that inspire creativity.

What’s Inside?

Every Doodle Crate includes materials for a specific project such as textile design, painting, or crafting. Step-by-step instructions help guide you through the process, ensuring a successful outcome.

Why Choose Doodle Crate?

  • Creativity Unleashed: Projects encourage personal expression.
  • High-Quality Materials: Only the best supplies are included.
  • Skill Variety: Learn different art techniques each month.

6. Pipsticks

Overview

For sticker lovers, Pipsticks offers a subscription service that brings joy and creativity through an abundance of stickers. This box is ideal for anyone wanting to add some flair to their crafts or personal items.

What’s Inside?

Subscribers receive a collection of unique stickers, along with additional crafting materials and project ideas to help incorporate them into various creative projects.

Why Choose Pipsticks?

  • Fun and Whimsical: Perfect for all ages, especially children and teens.
  • Creative Uses: Stickers can be used for scrapbooking, journaling, and more.
  • Exclusive Designs: Many stickers are unique to Pipsticks.

7. BoxyCharm

Overview

BoxyCharm is typically associated with beauty products, but their monthly subscription box has recently incorporated crafting materials that appeal to the creative side of beauty enthusiasts.

What’s Inside?

Each box includes beauty items along with crafting supplies such as DIY beauty products, skincare crafting kits, and accessories.

Why Choose BoxyCharm?

  • Dual Purpose: Combines beauty and crafting for a unique experience.
  • High-Value Products: Receive quality items at a fraction of the cost.
  • Surprise Factor: Each month offers a delightful mix of products.

8. Sewing Box

Overview

For sewing enthusiasts, The Sewing Box provides a monthly subscription that includes all the essentials needed to create a new sewing project.

What’s Inside?

Each box includes fabric, patterns, and notions tailored to a specific project, making it easy for subscribers to dive right in.

Why Choose The Sewing Box?

  • Focused on Sewing: Perfect for those who want to enhance their sewing skills.
  • Variety of Projects: Each month features a new design or technique.
  • Beginner to Advanced: Projects cater to all skill levels.

9. MakersKit

Overview

MakersKit offers a subscription box filled with DIY kits that focus on home decor and lifestyle projects. This box is perfect for those looking to beautify their living space with handcrafted items.

What’s Inside?

Subscribers receive all the materials needed to complete a project such as wall art, planters, or seasonal decorations, along with detailed instructions.

Why Choose MakersKit?

  • Home Decor Focus: Enhance your space with handmade items.
  • Variety of Styles: Projects cater to different aesthetics and trends.
  • Easy to Follow: Instructions are clear and concise, making projects accessible.

10. Cratejoy

Overview

While Cratejoy is a marketplace for various subscription boxes, it features numerous craft options that cater to different interests and skill levels.

What’s Inside?

Subscribers can choose from a range of craft boxes, including those focused on knitting, painting, scrapbooking, and more. Each box varies depending on the selected subscription.

Why Choose Cratejoy?

  • Wide Selection: Find the perfect box tailored to your crafting interests.
  • Customizable Options: Choose subscriptions that best fit your needs.
  • Community Reviews: Get insights from other crafters before making a choice.

Conclusion

Craft subscription boxes are an excellent way to spark creativity and provide a consistent source of inspiration. With options catering to various crafts, skill levels, and interests, there’s something for everyone. Whether you want to explore new techniques, bond with family, or simply indulge in your passion, these monthly boxes deliver the tools and materials needed to unleash your inner artist.

By choosing a craft subscription box, you not only receive curated supplies but also become part of a vibrant community of fellow crafters. So, go ahead and explore these options to find the perfect match for your crafting journey. Embrace the joy of creating and let your imagination run wild each month!

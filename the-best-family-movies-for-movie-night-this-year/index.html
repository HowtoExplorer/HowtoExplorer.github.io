The Best Family Movies for Movie Night This Year


When it comes to family movie nights, finding the perfect film that everyone can enjoy can be quite the challenge. With so many options available, it’s essential to curate a selection that caters to all ages, ensuring laughter, excitement, and even a few heartfelt moments. This year has brought forth an array of fantastic family films that are sure to captivate and entertain. Below, we dive into an engaging list of the best family movies that should be on your watchlist for an unforgettable movie night.

1. The Super Mario Bros. Movie

Based on the beloved video game franchise, The Super Mario Bros. Movie brings the iconic characters of Mario and Luigi to the big screen in a vibrant animated adventure. Join the brothers as they navigate the Mushroom Kingdom to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of Bowser. With stunning visuals, catchy songs, and a nostalgic nod to fans of the franchise, this film is perfect for families seeking a fun and energetic movie night.

Key Themes:

  • Friendship
  • Bravery
  • Adventure

Why Watch:

The film’s humor appeals to both children and adults, making it an excellent choice for family bonding.

2. Elemental

Elemental transports viewers to a world where the elements—fire, water, land, and air—are personified. The story follows Ember and Wade, two unlikely friends from different elemental backgrounds, who discover that their differences might just be what they need to overcome obstacles. This Pixar creation is not only visually stunning but also offers valuable life lessons about acceptance and understanding.

Key Themes:

  • Diversity
  • Acceptance
  • Friendship

Why Watch:

The animation is top-notch, and the storyline encourages families to discuss the importance of embracing differences.

3. Encanto

Encanto takes us to Colombia, where the Madrigal family lives in a magical house. Each member possesses unique gifts, except for Mirabel, who struggles to find her place. This Disney film beautifully captures themes of family dynamics, self-worth, and the significance of community. With catchy songs and heartfelt moments, it’s a film that resonates with audiences of all ages.

Key Themes:

  • Family
  • Self-discovery
  • Community

Why Watch:

The music is unforgettable, and the vibrant animation will keep kids and adults alike engaged throughout the film.

4. Paw Patrol: The Mighty Movie

For fans of the hit TV series, the Paw Patrol gang is back with their first big-screen adventure. In Paw Patrol: The Mighty Movie, the pups gain superpowers and must work together to save Adventure City. This film is packed with action, teamwork, and valuable lessons about courage and friendship.

Key Themes:

  • Teamwork
  • Courage
  • Adventure

Why Watch:

Ideal for younger children, the film’s positive messages and beloved characters make it a delightful watch for families.

5. Strange World

In Strange World, we follow the Clade family as they embark on a groundbreaking expedition into an unexplored territory. The film focuses on adventure, discovery, and the importance of family unity in the face of challenges. With its imaginative setting and engaging plot, it’s sure to spark the curiosity of both kids and adults.

Key Themes:

  • Exploration
  • Family bonds
  • Adventure

Why Watch:

The stunning visuals and humorous moments make this a fantastic choice for families looking for an adventurous movie night.

6. Turning Red

Turning Red tells the story of Mei, a 13-year-old girl who transforms into a giant red panda whenever she gets too excited. This coming-of-age tale addresses themes of adolescence, cultural identity, and the pressure of expectations from family. The film blends humor with heartfelt moments, making it relatable for both kids and their parents.

Key Themes:

  • Growing up
  • Cultural identity
  • Family expectations

Why Watch:

The unique premise and vibrant animation create an engaging viewing experience, making it perfect for family discussions about growing up.

7. The Secret Garden

A fresh adaptation of the classic novel, The Secret Garden brings the enchanting story to life. Follow Mary Lennox, a lonely girl who discovers a hidden garden that changes her life and the lives of those around her. This film beautifully illustrates themes of healing, friendship, and the transformative power of nature.

Key Themes:

  • Friendship
  • Healing
  • Nature

Why Watch:

The film’s lush visuals and emotional depth make it a poignant choice for families looking to experience a timeless story together.

8. Lightyear

Lightyear tells the origin story of Buzz Lightyear, the legendary Space Ranger from the Toy Story franchise. This action-packed film explores Buzz’s journey as he attempts to return home after being marooned on a hostile planet. With its thrilling adventure and heartfelt moments, it’s a must-see for both fans of the original films and new audiences.

Key Themes:

  • Heroism
  • Friendship
  • Adventure

Why Watch:

The film’s blend of action, humor, and nostalgia makes it a fantastic choice for families seeking an exciting movie night.

9. Hotel Transylvania: Transformania

In the final installment of the Hotel Transylvania series, the monsters embark on a hilarious adventure as they face a new threat that transforms them into humans. The film’s humor and heartwarming moments showcase the importance of family, no matter what form it takes.

Key Themes:

  • Family
  • Acceptance
  • Humor

Why Watch:

The goofy antics of the beloved characters will keep the whole family laughing, making for a delightful movie night.

10. The Mitchells vs. The Machines

The Mitchells vs. The Machines follows a quirky family as they embark on a road trip, only to find themselves in the midst of a robot apocalypse. This animated film cleverly combines humor with themes of technology, family dynamics, and connection in the modern world.

Key Themes:

  • Technology
  • Family connection
  • Humor

Why Watch:

The film’s unique animation style and relatable family moments make it an entertaining choice for all ages.

Conclusion

This year offers a diverse selection of family movies that are perfect for movie nights. Whether you’re in the mood for adventure, humor, or heartfelt storytelling, these films cater to various tastes and ages. Gather your loved ones, grab some popcorn, and enjoy these cinematic gems that promise to create lasting memories together. Each of these films not only entertains but also provides valuable lessons and moments that spark important conversations among family members.

So, as you plan your next family movie night, consider including some of these fantastic films on your watchlist. They’re bound to bring laughter, joy, and a sense of togetherness that makes family movie nights so special.

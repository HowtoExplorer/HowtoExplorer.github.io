10 Delicious Ways to Cook with Chickpeas


Chickpeas, also known as garbanzo beans, are a versatile and nutritious legume that has been a staple in diets around the world for centuries. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, they serve as an excellent base for a plethora of dishes. Whether you’re looking to add more plant-based meals to your diet or simply want to experiment with new recipes, here are ten delicious ways to cook with chickpeas that you’ll love.

1. Classic Hummus

No list of chickpea recipes would be complete without hummus. This creamy dip is not only easy to make but also highly customizable. To prepare classic hummus, blend together one can of drained chickpeas, two tablespoons of tahini, the juice of one lemon, two cloves of garlic, and a drizzle of olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. For a twist, try adding roasted red peppers, jalapeños, or fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro.

Serving Suggestion:

Serve your hummus with fresh vegetables, pita bread, or as a spread on sandwiches and wraps.

2. Chickpea Salad

A refreshing chickpea salad is perfect for warm weather or as a nutritious side dish. Combine drained canned chickpeas with diced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and bell pepper. Dress with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for a zesty flavor. To amp up the nutrition, toss in some spinach or arugula and top with feta cheese or avocado.

Variations:

Experiment with different dressings or add roasted chickpeas for an extra crunch.

3. Chickpea Curry

Chickpeas are a fantastic addition to curries, adding both texture and protein. Start by sautéing onions, garlic, and ginger in a pot. Add spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric, followed by canned tomatoes and coconut milk. Stir in your chickpeas and let them simmer until heated through. Finish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime for a burst of flavor.

Serving Suggestion:

Serve your chickpea curry over rice or with naan bread for a complete meal.

4. Roasted Chickpeas

Looking for a healthy snack? Roasted chickpeas are the answer! Simply rinse and drain your chickpeas, toss them with olive oil and your favorite spices—like paprika, garlic powder, or cayenne—and roast them in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-30 minutes until crispy. These make for a satisfying, crunchy snack that’s also high in protein.

Flavor Combinations:

Try different spice blends like curry powder, Italian seasoning, or even cinnamon and sugar for a sweet version.

5. Chickpea Stew

Warm and hearty, chickpea stew is perfect for chilly evenings. Start by sautéing onions, carrots, and celery in a pot. Add garlic, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and your chickpeas. Season with herbs such as thyme and rosemary, and let it simmer until the vegetables are tender. Serve with crusty bread for a comforting meal.

Tips:

Add kale or spinach towards the end of cooking for extra nutrients.

6. Chickpea Tacos

Chickpeas can bring a delightful twist to tacos. Mash or lightly crush cooked chickpeas and mix with taco seasoning. Sauté until heated through, then fill corn or flour tortillas with the mixture. Top with avocado, salsa, and shredded cabbage for a fresh, tasty meal.

Serving Suggestion:

Accompany with a side of cilantro-lime rice for a complete taco night.

7. Chickpea Burgers

For a plant-based burger, chickpeas are a fantastic option. Mash a can of chickpeas and mix with breadcrumbs, minced onion, garlic, and your choice of herbs and spices. Form into patties and pan-fry or bake until golden. Serve on a bun with your favorite toppings such as lettuce, tomato, and a tangy sauce.

Customization:

Add grated carrots, zucchini, or even cheese for more flavor and texture.

8. Chickpea Pasta

Chickpea pasta is a nutritious alternative to traditional pasta, and it’s incredibly easy to prepare. Simply cook chickpea pasta according to package instructions and toss it with sautéed vegetables, olive oil, and your choice of sauce. Top with grated cheese or nutritional yeast for added flavor.

Flavor Ideas:

Try a simple garlic and olive oil sauce or a rich marinara for a comforting dish.

9. Chickpea Stir-Fry

For a quick and healthy meal, try a chickpea stir-fry. Sauté your choice of vegetables such as bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas in a wok. Add cooked chickpeas and a splash of soy sauce or teriyaki sauce for a burst of flavor. Serve over rice or quinoa for a filling meal.

Variations:

Switch up the sauce with a spicy sriracha or a peanut sauce for an Asian-inspired dish.

10. Chickpea Pancakes

Chickpea flour, also known as besan, can be used to create savory pancakes. Combine chickpea flour with water, spices, and chopped vegetables like spinach or onions to form a batter. Cook on a skillet until golden brown. These pancakes can be enjoyed for breakfast or as a light lunch, served with yogurt or chutney.

Creative Ideas:

Add herbs like cilantro or dill to enhance the flavor profile.

Conclusion

Chickpeas are not only nutritious but also incredibly versatile, making them a fantastic ingredient to incorporate into a variety of dishes. From snacks to main courses, these ten delicious ways to cook with chickpeas will inspire you to get creative in the kitchen. Whether you’re a seasoned cook or just starting out, these recipes are sure to please your palate and keep you coming back for more. So grab a can of chickpeas and start experimenting with these flavorful dishes today!

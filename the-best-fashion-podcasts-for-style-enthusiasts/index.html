The Best Fashion Podcasts for Style Enthusiasts


Are you a fashion enthusiast looking for some inspiration and insight into the world of style? Look no further! In this blog post, we have curated a list of the best fashion podcasts that will keep you entertained and informed about the latest trends, industry news, and expert advice. So grab your headphones and get ready to dive into the world of fashion through the power of audio.

1. The Style Success Podcast

Hosted by Mallory Sills, a certified professional image consultant and personal stylist, The Style Success Podcast is a must-listen for anyone looking to elevate their personal style. Mallory shares practical tips and tricks to help you build a wardrobe that reflects your unique personality and boosts your confidence. From tackling fashion dilemmas to exploring current trends, this podcast covers it all.

2. The Business of Fashion Podcast

If you’re interested in the business side of the fashion industry, The Business of Fashion Podcast is perfect for you. Hosted by Imran Amed, the founder and CEO of Business of Fashion, this podcast features interviews with industry leaders, designers, and entrepreneurs. Gain valuable insights into the ever-evolving fashion landscape and stay up-to-date with the latest business strategies and trends.

3. The Wardrobe Crisis Podcast

Hosted by Clare Press, The Wardrobe Crisis Podcast explores the intersection of fashion, sustainability, and ethics. Clare delves into the environmental and social impact of the fashion industry, as well as the importance of conscious consumerism. This thought-provoking podcast invites you to think more deeply about your fashion choices and how they can contribute to a more sustainable future.

4. American Fashion Podcast

If you want an insider’s perspective on the American fashion industry, look no further than the American Fashion Podcast. Hosted by Charles Beckwith and Cathy Schepis, this podcast features interviews with designers, journalists, and industry professionals. Gain a deeper understanding of the American fashion scene and stay informed about the latest trends, events, and developments.

5. The Fashion Law Podcast

For those interested in the legal aspects of the fashion industry, The Fashion Law Podcast is a fascinating listen. Hosted by Julie Zerbo, the founder of The Fashion Law, this podcast explores the legal issues surrounding fashion, including intellectual property, counterfeiting, and labor rights. Stay informed about the legal challenges faced by designers and brands, and gain a unique perspective on the industry.

6. Fashion No Filter

Hosted by Monica Ainley and Camille Charrière, Fashion No Filter offers an unfiltered look into the fashion world. This podcast dives deep into various topics, from fashion week experiences to the impact of social media on the industry. With a mix of interviews, discussions, and personal anecdotes, this podcast offers a fresh and honest take on the fashion industry.

7. Dressed: The History of Fashion

If you’re a fashion history buff, Dressed: The History of Fashion is a must-listen. Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, this podcast explores the fascinating stories behind our favorite garments and trends. From the evolution of the little black dress to the symbolism of fashion during wartime, this podcast will transport you through time and deepen your appreciation for fashion’s rich history.

8. Pop Fashion

Hosted by Lisa Rowan and Kaarin Vembar, Pop Fashion is a fun and informative podcast that covers a wide range of fashion-related topics. From industry news and trends to pop culture references, this podcast offers a lighthearted take on the world of fashion. Tune in for insightful discussions and the hosts’ unique perspectives on all things style.

9. The Glossy Podcast

For those interested in the intersection of fashion, beauty, and technology, The Glossy Podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Hilary Milnes, this podcast features interviews with leaders in the fashion and beauty industries. Gain insights into the latest innovations, trends, and strategies shaping these dynamic industries.

10. Fashion Hags

Bringing a fresh and humorous approach to fashion, Fashion Hags is a podcast hosted by Abby Shumka, Evan Ducharme, and Katie Garnham. This trio of fashion enthusiasts discusses everything from fashion news to personal style journeys. With a fun and light-hearted vibe, this podcast is perfect for those looking to enjoy a casual and entertaining conversation about all things fashion.

Conclusion

Whether you’re a fashion industry professional or simply a style enthusiast, these fashion podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge, inspiration, and entertainment. From practical style advice to thought-provoking discussions, each podcast brings a unique perspective to the world of fashion. So plug in your headphones, subscribe to your favorites, and let these podcasts be your go-to source for all things fashion. Happy listening!

Note: This blog post is not sponsored, and the order of the podcasts does not indicate preference or ranking. The list is curated based on the content and relevance to fashion enthusiasts.

The Best Fashion Podcasts for Style Enthusiasts


Fashion has always been a powerful way to express personal identity, creativity, and cultural significance. Whether you’re looking to stay updated on the latest trends, understand the history behind iconic fashion movements, or discover how the industry operates behind the scenes, fashion podcasts have become an invaluable resource. They provide a unique way for style enthusiasts to stay engaged with the ever-evolving world of fashion while on the go.

If you’re passionate about fashion, tuning into the best fashion podcasts can be an exciting and informative way to deepen your knowledge and elevate your style. In this post, we’ll dive into some of the top podcasts that every fashion lover should be listening to.

1. The Business of Fashion Podcast

The Business of Fashion Podcast is a go-to resource for anyone interested in the intricate workings of the fashion industry. Hosted by BoF’s founder Imran Amed, the show takes a deep dive into topics that go beyond the runway and into the business behind fashion. From discussions on the future of retail to interviews with influential figures in the fashion world, this podcast offers insights that will keep you informed about all aspects of the industry.

Whether you’re a budding designer, an entrepreneur, or just someone who wants to understand the business behind the trends, The Business of Fashion Podcast is an essential listen. The topics covered are always timely and relevant, making it a must-listen for anyone eager to understand the future of fashion.

2. Dressed: The History of Fashion

Fashion is more than just a modern-day phenomenon; it’s a reflection of history, culture, and social movements. Dressed: The History of Fashion takes listeners on an educational journey through time, exploring the origins of iconic fashion pieces and the evolution of style through the ages.

Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, Dressed covers a wide range of topics, from the history of haute couture to the stories behind everyday items like the T-shirt and jeans. For anyone looking to gain a deeper understanding of fashion’s cultural impact, Dressed offers a fascinating and fun perspective on the world of fashion.

3. Fashion No Filter

For a more informal and lighthearted approach to fashion podcasts, Fashion No Filter is an excellent choice. Hosted by fashion journalists Chiara Ferragni and Francesca Sarti, the show focuses on the latest trends, celebrity style, and insider gossip within the fashion industry. The hosts have an authentic and unfiltered way of discussing fashion, making it feel as if you’re having a conversation with close friends who just happen to be in the know.

Fashion No Filter also delves into the personal journeys of successful figures in the fashion world, making it both an entertaining and inspirational listen. Whether you want to stay updated on the latest fashion news or hear about the lives of your favorite designers and influencers, this podcast offers plenty of insights and fun moments.

4. The Vogue Podcast

Vogue has long been synonymous with cutting-edge fashion, and their podcast brings the iconic magazine’s style to life in a new way. The Vogue Podcast showcases interviews with the biggest names in fashion, from designers and models to artists and photographers. Hosted by the magazine’s editors, the podcast provides listeners with firsthand accounts of fashion shows, photoshoots, and other exclusive events in the fashion world.

This podcast is perfect for anyone who wants to get an inside look at the people and processes that define the fashion industry. Whether you’re interested in learning more about emerging designers, the creative process behind editorials, or the cultural influences that shape fashion trends, The Vogue Podcast covers it all.

5. The Fashion Revolution Podcast

The Fashion Revolution Podcast is an essential listen for anyone who cares about the ethical and sustainable side of fashion. Fashion Revolution is a global movement that advocates for better practices in the fashion industry, focusing on issues like fair wages, transparency, and environmental sustainability. The podcast brings attention to the intersection of fashion, politics, and social justice, making it a powerful platform for thought-provoking discussions.

Hosted by the founders of Fashion Revolution, this podcast features interviews with activists, designers, and industry experts who are leading the charge for positive change in the fashion world. It’s a must-listen for anyone interested in learning more about how the fashion industry can evolve to be more responsible and ethical.

6. Pop Fashion

For those looking for a podcast that blends humor, fashion, and pop culture, Pop Fashion is the ideal choice. Hosted by fashion experts Kaarin Vembar and Lisa Corcoran, this podcast takes a playful approach to the latest happenings in the fashion world. From discussing the best and worst red carpet looks to analyzing trends and fashion news, Pop Fashion offers an entertaining take on the fashion industry.

The hosts also dive into topics related to fashion business and marketing, providing practical insights for anyone interested in the behind-the-scenes operations that drive the fashion world. If you enjoy casual yet informative conversations about style, Pop Fashion is a great addition to your playlist.

7. High Low Fashion

For a podcast that combines high-end fashion with accessible street style, High Low Fashion is the perfect choice. Hosted by fashion editor Emma Hope Allwood, this podcast explores how fashion exists in two worlds: high fashion and streetwear. From designer collaborations to emerging streetwear trends, High Low Fashion delves into how these two spheres influence each other and the broader fashion industry.

The podcast takes a nuanced approach to understanding how trends evolve across different levels of fashion, making it an interesting listen for anyone who’s curious about the intersection between high fashion and everyday style. Whether you’re a luxury fashion enthusiast or a streetwear lover, High Low Fashion offers a fresh perspective on both.

8. Wardrobe Crisis

Fashion has an enormous environmental footprint, and Wardrobe Crisis explores how we can work toward a more sustainable fashion industry. Hosted by Clare Press, Vogue’s sustainability editor, this podcast is dedicated to discussing the environmental impact of fashion and how we can reduce waste, improve sourcing practices, and embrace ethical fashion choices.

The podcast features interviews with activists, sustainable designers, and industry leaders who are driving change. It’s a great resource for anyone who wants to become more conscious about their fashion choices and support a sustainable future for the industry. Wardrobe Crisis challenges listeners to think critically about the fashion they buy and wear and encourages positive change through conscious consumption.

9. The Fashion Hags

If you love combining fashion with humor, The Fashion Hags is a must-listen. Hosted by three fashion-loving friends, the podcast offers a lighthearted and candid approach to the world of style. The hosts discuss everything from the latest trends to personal style advice, all while keeping things fun and relatable. The Fashion Hags also cover topics like fashion history, celebrity style, and the occasional fashion disaster.

With a relaxed and conversational tone, this podcast feels like hanging out with friends who just happen to be incredibly stylish. If you’re looking for a fashion podcast that’s entertaining, educational, and easy to listen to, The Fashion Hags is an excellent choice.

10. Style Like U

Style Like U offers an empowering take on fashion, focusing on personal style, self-expression, and body positivity. This podcast is part of the larger Style Like U platform, which celebrates diversity in fashion by sharing the stories of people from all walks of life and their unique approaches to style. The podcast features interviews with individuals who challenge conventional beauty standards and embrace fashion as a form of self-expression.

If you’re interested in hearing from real people who wear what makes them feel good, Style Like U is a great listen. It’s perfect for those who believe that fashion should be an inclusive and empowering experience for everyone, no matter their size, gender, or background.

Conclusion

The world of fashion is diverse, dynamic, and constantly evolving. Whether you’re interested in the history of fashion, the latest trends, or how the industry is changing, podcasts provide an excellent way to stay informed, entertained, and inspired. From in-depth discussions on the business side of fashion to conversations about sustainability and personal style, there’s a podcast for every kind of fashion enthusiast.

Listening to these podcasts will not only help you stay up-to-date with the latest trends and industry news but also deepen your appreciation for the artistry and creativity that define the fashion world. So, grab your headphones, hit play, and immerse yourself in the fascinating world of fashion. Happy listening!

10 Must-Read Books of 2022


2022 has already proven to be an exciting year for book lovers, with a plethora of new releases across various genres. Whether you enjoy diving into gripping thrillers, exploring fantastical worlds, or delving into thought-provoking non-fiction, there is something for everyone in this year’s literary landscape. Here are 10 must-read books of 2022 that are capturing readers’ attention and earning critical acclaim:

1. The Atlas Six by Olivie Blake

The Atlas Six is a mesmerizing blend of fantasy and mystery that follows six ambitious magicians vying for a coveted spot in a secret society. With intricate world-building and complex characters, this novel promises to enchant readers looking for a fresh take on magic academia.

2. Matrix by Lauren Groff

Lauren Groff’s Matrix is a historical fiction masterpiece that transports readers to medieval England, where the protagonist, Marie de France, navigates power, religion, and her own desires. Rich in detail and beautifully written, this novel offers a compelling exploration of feminine strength and resilience.

3. Project Hail Mary by Andy Weir

For science fiction enthusiasts, Project Hail Mary is a thrilling ride through space and time. Andy Weir, the author of The Martian, delivers another gripping tale of survival and discovery that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very last page.

4. The Sentence by Louise Erdrich

Louise Erdrich’s The Sentence is a poignant reflection on love, loss, and the power of storytelling. Through intertwining narratives, Erdrich weaves a captivating tale that explores the ways in which language shapes our lives and relationships.

5. The Maid by Nita Prose

Nita Prose’s debut novel, The Maid, is a delightful mix of mystery and humor that follows the eccentric maid, Molly Gray, as she unravels a murder mystery in a prestigious hotel. With its quirky protagonist and clever plot twists, this book is a must-read for fans of cozy mysteries.

6. The Prophets by Robert Jones Jr.

In The Prophets, Robert Jones Jr. delivers a powerful and lyrical exploration of love and resilience in the face of oppression. Set on a plantation in the antebellum South, this novel challenges readers to confront the complexities of race, sexuality, and freedom.

7. The Push by Ashley Audrain

Ashley Audrain’s The Push is a chilling psychological thriller that delves into the dark and often taboo aspects of motherhood. Through its gripping narrative and unreliable narrator, this book will keep you guessing until its shocking conclusion.

8. The Recovery Agent by Janet Evanovich

Janet Evanovich’s The Recovery Agent is a fast-paced and witty adventure featuring a strong female protagonist, Gabriela Rose, as she navigates the world of high-stakes recovery missions. Filled with humor and heart-pounding action, this novel is perfect for readers craving a dose of escapism.

9. Black Buck by Mateo Askaripour

Black Buck is a satirical novel that offers a sharp critique of corporate culture and race relations in modern America. Mateo Askaripour’s debut is both thought-provoking and darkly humorous, making it a compelling read for those interested in social commentary.

10. The Final Revival of Opal & Nev by Dawnie Walton

Dawnie Walton’s The Final Revival of Opal & Nev is a riveting exploration of music, race, and identity that follows the rise and fall of a fictional rock duo in the 1970s. Blending fiction with oral history, this novel is a unique and immersive reading experience that will stay with you long after you turn the final page.

These 10 must-read books of 2022 offer a diverse range of genres, themes, and writing styles, ensuring that there is something for every reader to enjoy. Whether you’re a fan of gripping thrillers, literary fiction, or speculative worlds, these novels promise to captivate, provoke thought, and transport you to new and exciting literary landscapes. Happy reading!

Remember, a good book can be a perfect escape from the ordinary, taking you on a journey without ever leaving your favorite reading spot!

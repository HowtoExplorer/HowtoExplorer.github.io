The Best Dance-themed TV Shows to Binge-Watch


Are you ready to get your groove on and indulge in some fantastic dance-themed TV shows? From sizzling Latin rhythms to swaying ballroom dances, these electrifying series will transport you to a world of music, movement, and unforgettable characters. Grab your favorite snacks, get cozy, and let's dive into the best dance-themed TV shows to binge-watch. Trapeze Artists and Tappers: Modern Shows to Watch While classic dance dramas like Dirty Dancing and Flashdance continue to charm audiences, there are many modern dance-themed TV shows that have taken the world by storm. These series blend captivating storylines with vibrant dance performances, showcasing various styles and genres. You'll be mesmerized by intricate choreography, emotional arcs, and stunning visuals that will leave you moved to tears and inspired to dance.

So You Think You Can Dance (Seasons 1-17) This award-winning reality competition show has been a staple on Fox since 2005, showcasing talented performers and judges who critically evaluate their performances. The series highlights various dance styles, from contemporary and hip-hop to ballet, tap, and ballroom. If you're a dance enthusiast or just looking for a fun, feel-good show, So You Think You Can Dance is an excellent choice.
  2. Dance 100 (Season 1) Debuting in 2020 on Fox, Dance 100 is a unique dance competition where talented dancers vye for a $100,000 prize, a spot in the production of a major film or stage musical, and mentorship from renowned choreographers and producers.
  3. Bring It! (Seasons 1-8) Airs on Lifetime since 2014, Bring It! features talented squads competing in hip-hop routines and fierce battles. The series shines a light on talented young dancers from across the country, showcasing their intricate choreography, stamina, and energy.

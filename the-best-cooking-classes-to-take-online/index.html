The Best Cooking Classes to Take Online


In the fast-paced world we live in, cooking at home has become more than just a necessity; it has transformed into a passion for many. Whether you’re a novice looking to whip up your first meal or an experienced chef aiming to refine your skills, online cooking classes offer an excellent opportunity to learn from the comfort of your kitchen. With a plethora of options available, we’ve curated a list of some of the best online cooking classes that cater to a variety of tastes, skill levels, and culinary interests.

Why Take Online Cooking Classes?

Taking cooking classes online provides several advantages:

  1. Flexibility: You can learn at your own pace and on your own schedule.
  2. Diverse Offerings: From baking to international cuisines, there’s a class for every interest.
  3. Expert Instructors: Many classes are taught by professional chefs who bring a wealth of knowledge to the table.
  4. Community: Online platforms often have vibrant communities where you can share experiences and recipes.

Let’s explore some of the best online cooking classes that can help you elevate your culinary skills.

1. MasterClass

Overview

MasterClass is renowned for its high-quality production and the caliber of its instructors. From Gordon Ramsay to Alice Waters, the platform features some of the most celebrated chefs in the culinary world.

Course Highlights

  • Celebrity Chefs: Learn directly from world-famous chefs.
  • Diverse Courses: Courses range from Italian cooking to modern American cuisine.
  • Engaging Format: Each class includes video lessons, downloadable resources, and interactive assignments.

Ideal For

Food enthusiasts eager to learn from the best and elevate their culinary craft.

2. Udemy

Overview

Udemy is a massive online learning platform that offers thousands of cooking classes. With both free and paid options, it caters to various budgets and interests.

Course Highlights

  • Wide Variety: From vegan cooking to baking artisan bread, there’s something for everyone.
  • Lifetime Access: Once you purchase a course, you have lifetime access to the content.
  • User Ratings: Classes come with reviews, helping you choose the right one.

Ideal For

Those looking for specific skills or cooking styles without committing to a full program.

3. Skillshare

Overview

Skillshare is another popular online learning platform that focuses on community-driven learning. It hosts a variety of cooking classes ranging from beginner to advanced levels.

Course Highlights

  • Project-Based Learning: Many classes encourage you to create projects, fostering hands-on experience.
  • Subscription Model: A monthly subscription grants access to all classes.
  • Active Community: Engage with fellow learners through discussions and project feedback.

Ideal For

Creative cooks who enjoy sharing their culinary journey and learning collaboratively.

4. The Kitchn Cooking School

Overview

The Kitchn, a well-known food and lifestyle website, offers a comprehensive online cooking school that covers the essentials of cooking.

Course Highlights

  • Structured Curriculum: The course is divided into lessons, making it easy to follow.
  • Skill Building: Focuses on foundational skills, perfect for beginners.
  • Free Resources: Many lessons and recipes are available for free.

Ideal For

Beginners who want to build a solid foundation in cooking techniques.

5. America’s Test Kitchen Online Cooking School

Overview

America’s Test Kitchen is synonymous with high-quality, tested recipes. Their online cooking school provides a rigorous approach to culinary education.

Course Highlights

  • Scientific Approach: Classes are rooted in the science of cooking.
  • Extensive Library: Access to hundreds of recipes and cooking techniques.
  • Expert Instructors: Learn from seasoned chefs who share their tested methods.

Ideal For

Serious home cooks looking to deepen their understanding of culinary science.

6. ChefSteps

Overview

ChefSteps is a platform designed for both professional chefs and enthusiastic home cooks. It emphasizes modern cooking techniques and innovative approaches.

Course Highlights

  • Modern Techniques: Focus on sous vide, fermentation, and other contemporary methods.
  • Interactive Videos: Engaging video content that brings recipes to life.
  • Community Engagement: Connect with other learners and share your culinary creations.

Ideal For

Cooks looking to experiment with modern cooking techniques and tools.

7. Online Cooking Classes by Sur La Table

Overview

Sur La Table, a well-known kitchenware retailer, offers a variety of online cooking classes that focus on different cuisines and skills.

Course Highlights

  • Live Classes: Participate in live, interactive cooking classes.
  • Variety of Cuisines: Explore Italian, French, Asian, and more.
  • Ingredient Kits: Option to purchase ingredient kits for certain classes.

Ideal For

Those who enjoy a hands-on, interactive learning experience.

8. Food52

Overview

Food52 is a vibrant community for food lovers, and their online cooking classes are designed to bring people together through food.

Course Highlights

  • Community-Centric: Classes often include group discussions and shared experiences.
  • Focus on Techniques: Learn specific techniques rather than just recipes.
  • Diverse Topics: Classes cover a wide range of topics, from seasonal cooking to meal prep.

Ideal For

Social cooks who appreciate community interaction and shared culinary experiences.

9. Craftsy

Overview

Craftsy is known for its creative classes, and their cooking section offers a variety of options for aspiring chefs.

Course Highlights

  • Varied Focus: From baking to cooking techniques, there’s something for everyone.
  • On-Demand Classes: Watch classes at your own convenience.
  • Expert Instructors: Learn from industry professionals and experienced home cooks.

Ideal For

Cooks looking for a creative outlet and diverse culinary skills.

10. Plated

Overview

Plated is a meal kit delivery service that also offers cooking classes. Their classes focus on preparing the meals they deliver.

Course Highlights

  • Hands-On Learning: Classes are designed as interactive cooking experiences.
  • Meal Kits Available: Conveniently receive all ingredients needed for classes.
  • Diverse Recipes: Explore a wide range of culinary traditions and techniques.

Ideal For

Those who prefer a guided cooking experience with all ingredients provided.

Conclusion

With the rise of online cooking classes, there has never been a better time to explore your culinary passions. Whether you’re looking to master the art of French pastry, delve into the world of vegan cooking, or simply learn how to cook a perfect steak, there’s a class out there for you. The key is to find a course that resonates with your interests and skill level, allowing you to unlock your full potential in the kitchen.

As you embark on your culinary journey, remember to enjoy the process and experiment with flavors, techniques, and cuisines. Happy cooking!

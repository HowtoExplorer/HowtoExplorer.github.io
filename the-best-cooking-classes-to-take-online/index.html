The Best Cooking Classes to Take Online


In recent years, cooking classes have become a popular way for food enthusiasts to learn new skills from the comfort of their own homes. Whether you’re a novice looking to understand the basics or a seasoned cook wanting to refine your techniques, online cooking courses offer a wide range of options for every skill level. From learning to bake delicate pastries to mastering the art of international cuisine, the internet is brimming with top-tier cooking classes. In this guide, we’ll explore some of the best online cooking classes available today, helping you find the perfect course to elevate your culinary skills.

Why Take Online Cooking Classes?

Online cooking classes have surged in popularity due to their convenience, flexibility, and the ability to learn at your own pace. Traditional in-person cooking courses may not be an option for everyone, especially those with busy schedules or limited access to culinary schools. Online classes allow you to explore new techniques, try different cuisines, and practice at your own convenience without the pressure of a traditional classroom setting.

Another key advantage is the broad range of subjects covered. Whether you want to learn the basics of meal prep or dive into advanced topics like fermentation or sous vide cooking, there are online cooking classes available to suit all interests. Plus, many of these classes are taught by world-renowned chefs, providing you with expert-level knowledge that might otherwise be hard to access.

Top Online Cooking Classes to Try

1. MasterClass

MasterClass offers a variety of high-quality online cooking courses taught by some of the world’s most renowned chefs. The platform’s culinary offerings include lessons from names like Gordon Ramsay, Thomas Keller, Alice Waters, and Wolfgang Puck.

Why Choose MasterClass?

  • Expert Instruction: Learn from Michelin-starred chefs who share their expert techniques and recipes.
  • Comprehensive Courses: Each course is structured in a way that covers both basic and advanced cooking skills, making it perfect for learners at any stage.
  • Flexibility: With a MasterClass subscription, you get unlimited access to all courses, allowing you to explore a wide range of culinary topics.
  • Production Quality: The videos are beautifully shot, offering a cinematic experience that makes learning enjoyable.

Courses to Check Out:

  • Gordon Ramsay’s “Cooking Techniques”
  • Alice Waters’ “The Art of Simple Food”
  • Thomas Keller’s “Essentials of French Cooking”

2. America’s Test Kitchen Online Cooking School

If you’re looking for a more structured and hands-on approach, America’s Test Kitchen (ATK) Online Cooking School provides an array of interactive lessons that teach both basic and advanced cooking techniques. Their classes are designed to ensure you master every aspect of cooking, from knife skills to plating your food beautifully.

Why Choose America’s Test Kitchen?

  • Science-based Approach: ATK is known for its detailed, science-backed approach to cooking. This helps students understand why certain techniques work and how ingredients interact.
  • Step-by-step Guidance: The platform offers clear, easy-to-follow instructions, ideal for beginners.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: You can take courses on specific topics like cooking for beginners, mastering baking, or learning about the science of cooking.

Courses to Check Out:

  • “How to Cook Everything”
  • “Baking Essentials”
  • “Knife Skills Mastery”

3. Sur La Table Online Cooking Classes

Sur La Table is another great option for those looking to dive into online cooking classes. They offer a range of live and on-demand courses, covering everything from knife skills to learning how to cook specific cuisines, like French, Italian, or Asian-inspired dishes.

Why Choose Sur La Table?

  • Live and On-Demand Classes: Sur La Table offers both live cooking sessions and the ability to purchase on-demand videos, allowing for maximum flexibility.
  • Interactive Learning: The live classes allow you to interact with instructors in real-time, asking questions and receiving feedback during the class.
  • Variety of Courses: Their classes range from beginner to expert-level, so there’s something for everyone.

Courses to Check Out:

  • “Knife Skills: The Essential Techniques”
  • “Cooking French Bistro Classics”
  • “Sushi Making 101”

4. The Chef & The Dish

The Chef & The Dish offers a unique approach to online cooking classes. Instead of pre-recorded content, you book private one-on-one cooking sessions with professional chefs from around the world. Whether you’re looking to learn how to make a traditional Italian dish or explore the flavors of Asian cuisine, The Chef & The Dish connects you with culinary experts for an immersive experience.

Why Choose The Chef & The Dish?

  • Personalized Experience: You get to choose the dish and cuisine, and the chef tailors the class to your preferences.
  • Live, Interactive Classes: The cooking sessions are conducted via video chat, so you can ask questions and get immediate feedback.
  • Cultural Immersion: The chefs are often native to the region they specialize in, providing authentic insight into the cuisine you’re learning to cook.

Courses to Check Out:

  • “Classic Italian Pasta”
  • “Sushi with a Tokyo Chef”
  • “Greek Meze with a Local Chef”

5. Udemy

Udemy is a popular online learning platform that offers a variety of cooking courses. The courses on Udemy are taught by experienced chefs and food enthusiasts, covering everything from basic kitchen skills to advanced culinary techniques.

Why Choose Udemy?

  • Affordable: Many courses on Udemy are budget-friendly, often with a one-time purchase price and lifetime access to course materials.
  • Wide Range of Topics: You can find a wide range of cooking styles, including vegan cooking, cake decorating, or even molecular gastronomy.
  • Self-paced Learning: Udemy courses are pre-recorded, allowing you to learn at your own pace.

Courses to Check Out:

  • “The Complete Knife Skills Masterclass”
  • “Vegetarian Cooking: Plant-Based Recipes”
  • “Baking for Beginners”

6. Cook Space

Cook Space is an online cooking platform that offers group and private cooking experiences. Their classes are designed to be fun, interactive, and full of flavor. They focus on hands-on learning and cater to various skill levels. From learning how to make simple weeknight dinners to diving deep into advanced pastry techniques, Cook Space has something for everyone.

Why Choose Cook Space?

  • Group and Private Classes: You can opt for a group cooking experience, making it a fun and social activity, or choose a private session for a more personalized experience.
  • Wide Range of Courses: Cook Space offers classes focused on everything from baking to plant-based cooking to international cuisine.
  • Fun and Engaging: The classes are designed to be interactive and enjoyable, with an emphasis on creating great food in a relaxed setting.

Courses to Check Out:

  • “Fresh Pasta Making”
  • “Intro to Baking: Pastry 101”
  • “Global Flavors: Cooking from Different Cultures”

7. Skillshare

Skillshare offers various cooking classes that focus on practical cooking techniques and creative recipes. The classes on Skillshare are taught by industry professionals, including chefs and food bloggers. Whether you’re looking to learn how to create a beautiful charcuterie board or want to master the art of French pastry, Skillshare provides an extensive selection of cooking courses.

Why Choose Skillshare?

  • Community-based Learning: Skillshare has a supportive community of learners and instructors who can provide feedback on your cooking progress.
  • Diverse Cooking Styles: The platform offers courses on everything from everyday cooking to food styling and food photography.
  • Subscription-based Access: A Skillshare membership gives you unlimited access to all courses, so you can explore multiple topics without paying for individual courses.

Courses to Check Out:

  • “Cooking for Beginners: How to Make Simple Meals”
  • “Food Styling and Photography”
  • “Baking Fundamentals”

8. The Great Courses Plus

The Great Courses Plus offers expert-level cooking classes that focus on both the art and science of food. The courses are designed to provide an in-depth exploration of culinary topics, allowing learners to understand the principles behind the recipes.

Why Choose The Great Courses Plus?

  • Comprehensive Learning: The platform provides courses that dive deep into cooking techniques, food science, and global cuisine.
  • Master Class Instructors: You’ll learn from culinary experts who have years of experience in the industry.
  • Flexible Learning: You can learn at your own pace, watching the lectures whenever you have time.

Courses to Check Out:

  • “The Everyday Gourmet: The Joy of Mediterranean Cooking”
  • “Cooking Techniques: A Beginner’s Guide”
  • “Chocolate Making and Pastry Arts”

Tips for Choosing the Right Online Cooking Course

With so many online cooking classes available, choosing the right one can feel overwhelming. Here are some tips to help you pick the perfect course:

  1. Consider Your Skill Level: Are you a beginner, or are you looking for more advanced techniques? Make sure the course aligns with your current skills or challenges you in areas where you’d like to improve.

  2. Look for Interactive Features: If you learn best by engaging with instructors or fellow students, look for courses that offer live sessions, Q&A opportunities, or feedback on your cooking.

  3. Check Reviews and Ratings: Before committing to a course, take the time to read reviews or watch sample videos to ensure the teaching style and content meet your expectations.

  4. Set Your Learning Goals: Do you want to focus on a particular cuisine, improve specific skills, or become a well-rounded home cook? Clarify your learning goals before choosing a course.

  5. Watch for Discounts and Promotions: Many platforms offer seasonal promotions or discounts for first-time users. Take advantage of these offers to save money while learning.

Conclusion

Online cooking classes are an excellent way to expand your culinary knowledge and skills from the comfort of your home. Whether you want to master baking, learn how to cook international dishes, or perfect your knife skills, there’s a course out there for you. From platforms like MasterClass and Udemy to more personalized experiences offered by The Chef & The Dish, the options are vast and varied.

Choose a course that aligns with your cooking goals, budget, and learning style, and embark on a delicious journey to enhance your culinary expertise. Happy cooking!

The Best Baking Shows to Watch for Inspiration


Whether you're a seasoned baker or a novice looking to hone your skills, watching baking shows can be a great way to learn new techniques, stay inspired, and gain confidence in the kitchen. With the rise of food television and online platforms, there's never been a better time to indulge in the world of baking.

  1. The Great British Baking Show One of the most popular and beloved baking shows out there, "The Great British Baking Show" is a must-watch for any fan of pastry and precision. Hosted by an affable Paul Hollywood and fiery Prue Leith, contestants compete in outlandish challenges, proving their mettle over the course of 10 weeks. As the Britain's best amateur bakers face off in epic battles, you'll find yourself swept up in a world of artisanal bread, intricately decorated cakes, and exquisite pastries.
  2. Nailed It! Who doesn't love an underdog story? This baking competition on Netflix pits enthusiastic but hapless contestants against each other, challenging them to recreate showstopping desserts in less than an hour. Led by effervescent hosts Nicole Byer and Jacques Torres, "Nailed It!" brings joy, humor, and impressive creativity to the world of baking.
  3. The Best Thing I Ever Ate: Baking Edition A spinoff of the popular "The Best Thing I Ever Ate" series, this baking-focused special showcases top chefs and food personalities traveling the globe in search of the most divine baked goods. From elegant croissants to velvety-rich cheesecakes, "Baking Edition" is your ticket to explore the world's most delicious pastries and sweet treats.
  4. Cake Boss In the competitive world of the Cake Boss, no challenge is too daunting for Buddy Valastro, master baker and owner of New Jersey's iconic Carlo's Bake Shop. "Cake Boss" spills the cameras into the intense daily operations of a thriving bakery, as Buddy, his team, and a host of visiting bakers create awe-inspiring confections that leave audiences speechless.
  5. Gordon Ramsay's Ultimate Cookery Course The wrath of Hell's Kitchen's most infamous chef might be intimidating, but when it comes to baking, Gordon Ramsay is all smiles. This instructional series breaks down basic baking principles, offering encouragement and technical know-how for aspiring bakers. Follow along as Gordon shares expert tips and recipes to transform your dessert game.
  6. Cupcake Wars Another popular Cake TV series, "Cupcake Wars" has all the hallmarks of classic competition: creativity, fierce contestants, and, of course, the much-loved format. Watching cupcake experts battle to outdo one another is like catching lightning in a bottle – it's short-lived but leaves a lasting impression.

Sizzling Summer Salad Recipes for Al Fresco Dining


Summer is finally here, and with it comes the perfect excuse to fire up the grill, gather some friends and family, and enjoy some delicious al fresco dining. While grilled meats and seafood are always a hit, a refreshing summer salad can be the perfect accompaniment to your outdoor feast. In this post, we’ll explore some sizzling summer salad recipes that are sure to impress your guests and satisfy your taste buds.

Grilled Peach and Arugula Salad

The sweetness of grilled peaches pairs perfectly with the peppery flavor of arugula in this summer salad recipe. To make it, start by grilling sliced peaches over medium heat for about 2-3 minutes per side, or until they’re caramelized and slightly charred. Meanwhile, toss together 4 cups of arugula, 1/2 cup of crumbled goat cheese, and 1/4 cup of chopped pecans. Drizzle with a balsamic vinaigrette and top with the grilled peaches.

Smoked Salmon and Avocado Salad

Smoked salmon adds a rich and savory flavor to this summer salad recipe, which is perfectly balanced by the creaminess of avocado. To make it, start by combining 1/2 cup of smoked salmon, 1 ripe avocado diced, 1/2 cup of chopped red onion, and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh dill. Drizzle with a lemon-tahini dressing and serve on a bed of mixed greens.

Heirloom Tomato and Burrata Salad

Heirloom tomatoes are the star of this summer salad recipe, which is elevated by the creamy texture of burrata cheese. To make it, start by combining 2 large heirloom tomatoes, diced, 1 ball of burrata cheese, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil, and 1/4 cup of chopped red onion. Drizzle with a basil-infused olive oil and serve on a bed of mixed greens.

Grilled Chicken and Quinoa Salad

This summer salad recipe is a great option for a light and refreshing meal. To make it, start by grilling chicken breast over medium heat for about 5-6 minutes per side, or until it’s cooked through. Meanwhile, cook 1 cup of quinoa according to package instructions. Combine the cooked quinoa, grilled chicken, 1 cup of diced cucumber, 1/2 cup of diced red bell pepper, and 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley. Drizzle with a lemon-tahini dressing and serve on a bed of mixed greens.

Watermelon and Feta Salad

This refreshing summer salad recipe is perfect for hot summer days. To make it, start by combining 2 cups of diced watermelon, 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh mint, and 1/4 cup of chopped red onion. Drizzle with a balsamic vinaigrette and serve on a bed of mixed greens.

Black Bean and Corn Salad

This summer salad recipe is a great option for a vegetarian meal. To make it, start by combining 1 cup of cooked black beans, 1 cup of cooked corn kernels, 1/2 cup of diced red bell pepper, 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro, and 2 tablespoons of lime juice. Drizzle with a chili-lime dressing and serve on a bed of mixed greens.

These sizzling summer salad recipes are sure to be a hit at your next al fresco dining gathering. Whether you’re looking for something light and refreshing or something more substantial, there’s a salad recipe on this list that’s sure to satisfy your taste buds. So fire up the grill, gather your friends and family, and get ready to enjoy the perfect summer salad.

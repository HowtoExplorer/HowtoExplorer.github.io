Delicious Recipes for Vegan Brownies


Are you a chocolate lover but follow a vegan lifestyle? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share some mouthwatering recipes for vegan brownies that will satisfy your sweet tooth without compromising your dietary choices. Get ready to indulge in a world of rich, fudgy, and absolutely delicious vegan brownies!

Classic Fudgy Vegan Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a square baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the applesauce, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until well combined and the batter is smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the edges are set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  6. Allow the brownies to cool completely in the baking dish before cutting into squares.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Decadent Peanut Butter Swirl Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a square baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the applesauce, melted coconut oil, and sugar. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.
  5. In a small microwave-safe bowl, heat the peanut butter for about 20 seconds or until it becomes slightly runny.
  6. Drizzle the peanut butter over the brownie batter and use a knife to create a swirl pattern.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the brownies are set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  8. Allow the brownies to cool completely before cutting into squares.
  9. Serve and enjoy the irresistible combination of chocolate and peanut butter!

Sinfully Rich Dark Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free dark chocolate chunks

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a square baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the applesauce, melted coconut oil, almond milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until well combined.
  4. Fold in the dark chocolate chunks, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the brownies are set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  7. Allow the brownies to cool completely before cutting into squares.
  8. Experience the ultimate chocolate bliss with these sinfully rich dark chocolate brownies!

Conclusion

There you have it – three delectable vegan brownie recipes that will leave you wanting more. Whether you prefer classic fudgy brownies, peanut butter swirl brownies, or sinfully rich dark chocolate brownies, these recipes are sure to satisfy your sweet cravings. Enjoy the indulgence guilt-free, knowing that these treats are entirely plant-based. So grab your apron, gather your ingredients, and get ready to bake your way to vegan brownie heaven!

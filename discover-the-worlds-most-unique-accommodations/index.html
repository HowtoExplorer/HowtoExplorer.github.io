Discover the World's Most Unique Accommodations


raveling is more than just visiting exotic locales; it’s about immersing yourself in different cultures, trying new experiences, and creating memories that last a lifetime. When it comes to where you lay your head at night, the traditional hotel or motel might not be the best option. Sometimes, the most unforgettable travels come from the most unique accommodations, offering you the perfect blend of luxury and adventure. Here are some of the most unique accommodations from all over the world, in no particular order, where your next adventure might just begin!

1. Crystal Wigwam, Sweden

Nestled close to the Norwegian border, in the vicinity of Abisko, Sweden, lies the magical ‪Crystal Wigwam. This glass igloo allows you to experience the stunning Northern Lights in complete comfort and luxury. The design integrates several floor-to-ceiling panoramic windows, allowing you to gaze upon the starlit skies and dancing auroras, matching Mother Nature’s beauty with the warmth of contemporary living.

2. The Manta Resort, Pemba Island, Tanzania

The Manta Resort, located on the remote island of Pemba in Tanzania, offers breathtaking accommodation with a twist. This floating underwater room provides an unparalleled ocean experience, allowing guests to sleep just below the water’s surface. Freestanding on a house reef with clear waters and vibrant marine life, the room is designed to optimize your experience with built-in portholes for mapping the night sky.

3. The Treehotel, Harads, Sweden

For those who love a mix of luxury and environment, The Treehotel in Harads, Sweden, is an ideal destination. This establishment offers a host of beautiful hanging cabins, designed by various architects and resembling different bird’s nests or smaller saunas. The unique blend of astonishing structure and serene nature allows guests to fully enjoy the Swedish forests while keeping up with modern amenities.

4. Costa Rica Treehouses, Costa Rica

Whether you’re a solo adventurer, couples, or families, Costa Rica’s Treehouses offer an elevated accommodation choice that connects you with nature in the most unique ways possible. Each treehouse is uniquely designed, nestled in lush, dense forests with local wildlife thriving around and above you. It’s the perfect mix of rustic elegance, affordability, and of course, adventure!

5. The 1926 Ice Retreat, Sweden

In the heart of the Swedish Lapland, you can find The 1926 Ice Retreat, an award-winning collection of luxury ice rooms that provide a once-in-a-lifetime experience in subzero conditions. Surrounded by arctic landscapes and midnight sun, this frosty accommodation promises a truly frosty adventure for the seasoned traveler who’s up for a challenge, or anyone seeking a cold-weather experience like no other.

6. Arenal Volcano Natural Lodge, Costa Rica

An eco-adventure lodge, Arenal Volcano Natural Lodge, located near the Arenal Volcano National Park, offers guests exclusive sustainable lodging made from locally sourced materials. Not only do these unique villas offer breathtaking views of the volcano, but they also blend in with the natural beauty of the area, making it a haven for bird watchers and nature lovers alike.

7. The Vanishing Point, Australia

Set against the desert plains, the Vanishing Point, located in South Australia, offers a unique glamping experience in a luxurious off-grid retreat. Made from shipping containers, the dwellings are cool and sustainable during the day, transforming into cozy and warm havens as the night comes in. Marvel at the constellations, thanks to the clear skies and the unusual placement of the ports.

8. The Waterberg Wilderness Camp, Namibia

Namibia’s Waterberg Wilderness Camp makes luxury camping a reality by creating secluded, traditional African bush camps. Each camp is secured by a private game reserve and offers visitors the golden opportunity to experience local wildlife in a comfortably unique setting complete with an open-air dining area.

9. Capsule Hotel La Soirée, France

Located in Paris, Capsule Hotel La Soirée gives a nod to Japan’s famous cube hotel rooms, with a European twist. The compact hotel is designed for the budget traveler and offers a playful twist on traditional lodgings by housing guests in simple yet clean and comfortable capsules. Although micro, the common areas are vibrant and bustling, allowing for a unique social experience during your stay.

10. The Wharekeke Tree Houses, New Zealand

The Wharekeke Tree Houses, nestled in a lush forest in Hahei, New Zealand, offers a unique, romantic getaway. Each tree house features panoramic ocean views, with direct access to secluded beach areas. Guests can enjoy the tranquility and stunning views while still taking advantage of their luxurious amenities.

These unique accommodations provide an unforgettable way to explore the world’s most awe-inspiring destinations while ensuring your stay is one-of-a-kind. So pack your bags, rise to new heights, and discover the world beneath the waves—your extraordinary adventure awaits!

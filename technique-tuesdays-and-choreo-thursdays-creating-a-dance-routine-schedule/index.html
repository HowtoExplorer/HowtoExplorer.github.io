Technique Tuesdays and Choreo Thursdays: Creating a Dance Routine Schedule


Ever felt the urge to let go and get lost in the world of dance, only to be bogged down by the same old routines and schedules? If your passion for dance and a desire to progress are ever at odds, it’s time to reinvent the way you practice and perform. Today, let’s delve into a fresh approach to your dance journey, one that incorporates Technique Tuesdays and Choreo Thursdays into your weekly routine.

Unleash the Dance Beast: Introducing Technique Tuesdays

Honing your skills is a critical part of any dancer’s journey. Technique Tuesdays could be the answer to your quest for improvement. Not only will it carve out a dedicated time for focused practice, but it’ll also minimize the stress of fitting training into a chaotic week.

What to do on Technique Tuesdays:

  1. Choose your Focus: Pick a specific skill or style, from turns and leaps to jazz or ballet – the choice is yours. Dedicate your whole day to mastering that one section of your dance.
  2. Structured Learning: Start the day with a guided technique class, either online or in-person. This will lay a strong foundation for the day.
  3. Practice, Repeat, Reflect: After the class, spend the rest of your day practicing that chosen section of dance. Try different variations, learn new tricks, and be mindful of your technique and execution. Finally, end the day by reflecting on what you’ve learned.

Elevate Your Moves: Embracing Choreography on Choreo Thursdays

While Technique Tuesdays help solidify your technical abilities, Choreo Thursdays are your chance to shine with originality. Choreographing your own routines is not only a way to express your creativity but can also revolutionize your dancing skills.

What to do on Choreo Thursdays:

  1. Details, Details, Details: The day begins with brainstorming. Think about the music, the energy, and the style you want to portray. Break the choreography down into smaller sections.
  2. Build your Routines: Start executing the sections you’ve planned with the rhythm and emotion of the music in mind. Take pauses to reassess your movements and make necessary changes.
  3. Polish and Performance: Once your routine is set, dedicate time to refining your performance, which could include stage presence, facial expressions, and transitions.

Creating the Schedule

To implement this revitalizing dance regimen, consider scheduling Technique Tuesdays and Choreo Thursdays at the beginning of each week. Decide on the specific skills you want to improve or routines you want to create well in advance. Ensuring consistency and dedication to this schedule allows you time to observe progress and make necessary adjustments to your choreography and technique.

Remember, the key to transforming your dance journey is more than just mechanical perfection—it’s about nurturing a love for the art, expressing yourself creatively, and pushing your boundaries consistently. So, give Technique Tuesdays and Choreo Thursdays a chance. They may just be the fresh start you were seeking! Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned dancer, these days are all about rediscovery, practice, and progress. The dance floor is calling; are you ready to answer?

10 Real Estate Investment YouTube Channels You Don't Want to Miss


Real estate investing has always been a popular means of generating wealth, but with the rise of YouTube, it’s now easier than ever to learn about the opportunities and challenges in this domain. No longer are investors locked into traditional learning channels; now, they can learn from the experts and enthusiasts right from their favorite screens. Among the countless channels available, here are ten that deserve a spot on your must-watch list:

1. Freeland Investment Group

  • Freeland Investment Group

Don’t let the name fool you - Freeland Investment Group isn’t just about Dave and Todd constantly proving their friendship, but also on their consistent sharing of investment strategies, property analysis reviews, market insights, as well as deep-dives into specific house types, whether it’s multi-family homes, fixer-uppers, or raw land. They even host regular live streams for audience Q&A sessions - the ultimate real estate education experience.

2. BiggerPockets Real Estate

  • BiggerPockets Real Estate

If you’re new to investing, BiggerPockets Real Estate is a YouTube channel that will create a strong foundation for you. Their step-by-step guides, interviews with successful investors, and thorough discussions on topics like finance, property management, negotiating techniques, leverages, and real estate law all make them the perfect mentors for beginners and experts alike.

3. The Real Estate Investory

  • The Real Estate Investory

Hosted by the dynamic personality, Robert Kiyosaki’s partner & personally mentored by Mr. Kiyosaki, Miguel Perez, The Real Estate Investory brings in-depth interviews and discussions with millionaire investors, mentors, and guests sharing about success strategies, overcoming failures, and investment insights. This channel also feeds your mind with ideas and inspiration to take your real estate investments to the next level.

4. FlipNerd

  • FlipNerd

With a focus on real estate investing education, marketing, and networking, FlipNerd does a fantastic job of providing expert advice, interviews with real estate investors, and visitor testimonies. It doesn’t end there; the channel also features free mastermind coaching sessions, expert roundtable discussions, as well as real-life case studies and reviews on financing, due diligence, and negotiation strategies.

5. Craig Curell

  • Craig Curell

Craig Curell is a young real estate investor who quickly gained popularity for his eye-opening, transformational videos centered on how to succeed in real estate investing. Learn from his journey and insights on subjects ranging from rehabbing properties to holding strategy, creative rei, buy-and-hold, variable cash flow, and much more as he shares his experiences.

6. Teanious Turner aka LEA

  • Teanious Turner aka LEA

An investor since his teenage years, Teanious Turner, or LEA, shares his extraordinary insight into various types of real estate investments and real estate wealth-building strategies. From multifamily properties to mobile homes, Teanious covers it all. See how he navigates finance, marketing, and negotiation as he holds nothing back, making it the perfect platform for ambitious newbie investors.

7. Wil Caple III

  • Wil Caple III

For a dose of creative, unconventional real estate investments, Wil Caple III can’t be surpassed. This do-it-yourself, low-budget, and wide-ranging approach to house flipping and rental property investing spans everything from small urban lot homes to shipping-container buildings. Wil brings a different vibe to the real estate investment world and is perfect for those who prefer breaking the mold.

8. Nathan Prinz

  • Nathan Prinz

With an emphasis on maximizing earnings from rental properties, Nathan Prinz’s YouTube channel focuses on building successful portfolios, tax strategies, and maximizing profits. It’s an immensely useful platform for those with an interest in rental properties.

9. Carlosmacxtreme Real Estate Investing

  • Carlosmacxtreme Real Estate Investing

Carlos shares incredible motivation and success tips on real estate investing based on his experiences. Carlosmacxtreme offers viewers a blend of his journey and mentorship in growing his real estate empire, from beauty salons to student housing and residential investment properties. His channel is for those looking for raw honesty and knowledge from an experienced investor.

10. Think Realty

  • Think Realty

Think Realty is all about the up-to-the-minute insights in the world of real estate investing. They talk about flipping houses, rentals, analysis, and more - all, expertly designed to help you navigate the ever-changing real estate market. If you’re looking to adjust your investing strategy, you’ll find real value by following this channel.

Making the most of YouTube for real estate investing is a game-changer for those willing to implement the strategies and lessons shared by these remarkable channels. Who knows; you might just be the next investor sensation, thanks to the inspiration and expertise found in these video formats. Happy investing!

The Best High-Interest Savings Accounts of 2023


When it comes to saving money, finding the right savings account is key to ensuring your funds grow as efficiently as possible. In 2023, there are plenty of options available to savers, from traditional savings accounts to online high-interest savings accounts that can offer significantly better returns. Whether you’re just starting your savings journey or you’re looking for a way to grow your emergency fund, a high-interest savings account can help you earn more from the money you already have.

In this guide, we’ll explore the top high-interest savings accounts available in 2023. These accounts offer competitive rates, low fees, and flexible terms, making them a great choice for anyone looking to make the most out of their savings.

Why High-Interest Savings Accounts Matter

High-interest savings accounts are a great way to grow your money over time. Unlike traditional savings accounts, which typically offer low-interest rates, high-interest accounts allow you to earn more on your balance. This can add up quickly, especially if you have a significant amount of money sitting in your savings account.

One of the main advantages of high-interest savings accounts is that they provide a safe and low-risk way to save money. Your funds are typically insured by government programs (like FDIC insurance in the United States), which means your money is protected up to a certain amount.

Additionally, many high-interest savings accounts come with flexible terms and easy access to your funds. You can usually withdraw money whenever you need it, without penalties. This makes them an excellent option for short-term savings goals or for building an emergency fund.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a High-Interest Savings Account

Before you open a high-interest savings account, it’s important to consider several factors that can affect your overall savings experience. Here are some key aspects to keep in mind:

1. Interest Rate

The primary draw of a high-interest savings account is the rate of return. Make sure to compare the rates offered by different institutions to find the best deal. However, remember that interest rates can fluctuate, so be sure to choose an account that suits your long-term savings goals.

2. Fees

Some high-interest savings accounts come with fees that could eat into your earnings. Look for accounts with low or no fees. Common fees to watch out for include monthly maintenance fees, minimum balance fees, or transaction fees for withdrawing money too often.

3. Minimum Deposit

Many high-interest savings accounts require a minimum deposit to open. Be sure to check this requirement before committing to an account. Some accounts may also require a minimum balance to earn the advertised interest rate.

4. Accessibility

While high-interest savings accounts offer great returns, you want to ensure that you can access your funds when you need them. Look for accounts that offer convenient online or mobile banking features. Also, make sure that withdrawals are easy and don’t come with penalties.

5. FDIC or NCUA Insurance

Ensure that the account you choose is insured by a government agency like the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) or the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA). This protects your money up to a certain limit in case the financial institution fails.

6. Customer Service

Good customer service can make a big difference when managing your savings account. Whether you have questions about your account, need assistance with a transaction, or are troubleshooting an issue, having responsive and helpful customer support is essential.

Top High-Interest Savings Accounts in 2023

Now that we’ve covered the key factors to consider, let’s take a look at some of the best high-interest savings accounts available in 2023. These accounts offer competitive interest rates, low fees, and a variety of features to help you grow your savings efficiently.

1. Marcus by Goldman Sachs High-Yield Online Savings Account

One of the top choices for savers looking for a reliable high-interest savings account is the Marcus by Goldman Sachs High-Yield Online Savings Account. This account offers a competitive interest rate and no monthly fees, making it an attractive option for those who want to grow their savings without worrying about extra costs.

  • Interest Rate: 3.50% APY (annual percentage yield)
  • Fees: No monthly fees
  • Minimum Deposit: $1
  • Accessibility: Online and mobile banking available
  • FDIC Insurance: Yes, up to $250,000

Marcus by Goldman Sachs is known for its simplicity and ease of use. You can open an account online in just a few minutes, and there are no minimum balance requirements to earn the advertised interest rate. With no fees and easy access to your funds, it’s a solid option for those who want a hassle-free way to save.

2. Discover Online Savings Account

Discover is another well-known financial institution offering a high-interest savings account with competitive rates. The Discover Online Savings Account has been a popular choice among savers for its strong customer service, easy online access, and lack of monthly fees.

  • Interest Rate: 3.50% APY
  • Fees: No monthly fees
  • Minimum Deposit: $0
  • Accessibility: Online and mobile banking available
  • FDIC Insurance: Yes, up to $250,000

Discover’s account offers flexibility in terms of minimum deposit, which makes it easy to start saving with any amount. Plus, their mobile app is user-friendly, and you can easily transfer money between accounts or set up automatic transfers to help you save consistently. Discover also has a strong reputation for customer service, which is a major bonus.

3. Synchrony High-Yield Savings Account

Synchrony’s High-Yield Savings Account is another great option for those looking to earn a competitive return on their savings. With a strong interest rate and no monthly fees, this account offers solid value for savers.

  • Interest Rate: 3.55% APY
  • Fees: No monthly fees
  • Minimum Deposit: $0
  • Accessibility: Online banking, mobile app, and ATM access
  • FDIC Insurance: Yes, up to $250,000

What sets Synchrony apart is its ATM access, which allows you to withdraw money from your account using over 1 million ATMs nationwide. This feature gives you greater flexibility when accessing your savings, and the online and mobile banking options are very convenient for managing your funds.

4. American Express National Bank High Yield Savings Account

American Express, known for its credit cards, also offers a competitive high-yield savings account. The American Express National Bank High Yield Savings Account provides a strong interest rate with no fees, making it a solid choice for savers looking to maximize their returns.

  • Interest Rate: 3.50% APY
  • Fees: No monthly fees
  • Minimum Deposit: $1
  • Accessibility: Online and mobile banking available
  • FDIC Insurance: Yes, up to $250,000

American Express’s online savings account offers a straightforward approach to growing your money. It has no hidden fees, and the low minimum deposit makes it accessible to anyone. However, unlike some other accounts, it does not offer ATM access, which may be a drawback for some users.

5. CIT Bank Savings Builder Account

CIT Bank offers a unique high-interest savings option known as the Savings Builder Account. This account offers an excellent interest rate, but to earn the top rate, you’ll need to meet certain criteria, such as making regular monthly deposits or maintaining a certain balance.

  • Interest Rate: 3.75% APY (for those who meet criteria)
  • Fees: No monthly fees
  • Minimum Deposit: $100
  • Accessibility: Online banking and mobile app
  • FDIC Insurance: Yes, up to $250,000

What makes the CIT Bank Savings Builder Account stand out is its higher interest rate for customers who can commit to monthly deposits or maintain a minimum balance. This is an ideal choice for savers who want to maximize their returns and can commit to growing their savings consistently.

6. Capital One 360 Performance Savings Account

The Capital One 360 Performance Savings Account is another great choice for savers who want to earn a competitive interest rate without worrying about monthly fees. It’s easy to open and offers a great mobile app to manage your account on the go.

  • Interest Rate: 3.40% APY
  • Fees: No monthly fees
  • Minimum Deposit: $0
  • Accessibility: Online banking, mobile app
  • FDIC Insurance: Yes, up to $250,000

Capital One 360 has earned a reputation for excellent customer service and ease of use. This account offers a solid interest rate and the convenience of mobile banking, making it a great option for people who want to manage their savings from anywhere.

Conclusion

As we’ve seen, there are many excellent high-interest savings accounts available in 2023. Whether you’re looking for a no-fee option, easy access to your funds, or a higher interest rate for consistent deposits, there’s an account to suit your needs. Be sure to compare interest rates, fees, and account features to find the best fit for your financial goals.

By choosing one of the top high-interest savings accounts listed above, you can make the most of your savings and watch your money grow over time. Start saving today and set yourself up for a more financially secure future!

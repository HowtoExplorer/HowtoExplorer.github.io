The Best Project Management Blogs to Follow in 2023


In today's fast-paced business landscape, effective project management is a vital skill that organizations need to thrive. With the rapid evolution of methodologies like Agile, Scrum, and Lean, project managers must stay updated with the latest trends, tools, and best practices. One of the best ways to achieve this is through insightful blogs dedicated to project management. In this post, we will explore some of the best project management blogs you should follow in 2023 for up-to-date information, expert guidance, and the latest industry trends.

  1. ProjectManagement.com Overview:ProjectManagement.com is a comprehensive resource for project managers at all levels. It offers articles, templates, tools, and discussion forums, making it a hub for project management enthusiasts. Why Follow:This blog covers a wide range of topics from the latest project management methodologies to career development advice. Regular contributors include industry professionals who bring practical insights from their own experiences. The site also hosts webinars and online seminars that can help you expand your knowledge and network.
  2. PMI - Project Management Institute Blog Overview:As the world's leading association for project management professionals, the Project Management Institute (PMI) has a wealth of resources. Their blog features articles from seasoned project managers, thought leaders, and academic researchers. Why Follow:The PMI blog provides information on best practices, certifications, and industry news. Regularly published thought pieces tackle emerging trends and challenges in the field, making it a valuable resource for ensuring you stay ahead of the curve.
  3. Wrike Blog Overview:Wrike is a well-known project management software platform, and their blog is a reflection of that. It not only focuses on how to best utilize their software but also offers insights into broader project management topics. Why Follow:The Wrike Blog features a plethora of articles on productivity, collaboration, and time management. It includes actionable tips, case studies, and guides that help project managers improve their skills and effectiveness in managing projects and teams.
  4. TeamGantt Blog Overview:TeamGantt specializes in Gantt chart software and project management tools. Its blog is filled with valuable information on project planning and execution strategies. Why Follow:What sets this blog apart is its focus on practical project management tools and methodologies. Expect to find posts covering everything from how to create effective Gantt charts to tips on leading successful teams. The writing is engaging and easy to understand, making it ideal for both novice and experienced project managers.
  5. LiquidPlanner Blog Overview:LiquidPlanner is known for its predictive project management capabilities, and their blog offers a strategic approach to understanding project management methodologies. Why Follow:The LiquidPlanner Blog dives into progressive topics such as resource management, forecasting, and team dynamics. Their content is rich with insights and real-world applications, making it perfect for project managers looking to deepen their expertise.
  6. A Girl’s Guide to Project Management Overview:This blog, created by Elizabeth Harrin, is designed to inspire and empower project managers, especially women in the field. Why Follow:Elizabeth’s perspective is unique and adds diversity to project management discussions. The blog covers everything from practical how-to guides to reflections on leadership and career growth. The community aspect of the blog allows for an exchange of ideas and support amongst project management professionals.
  7. The Digital Project Manager Overview:The Digital Project Manager focuses on the unique challenges faced by digital project managers. This blog provides a wealth of resources for anyone working in the digital space. Why Follow:By following The Digital Project Manager, you’ll learn about the latest tools and technologies to streamline your workflow. The blog also features expert interviews, resources, and tips on managing digital teams effectively.
  8. The Project Management Coach Overview:Hosted by project management consultant and educator, The Project Management Coach blog is dedicated to practical advice and insights geared toward aspiring and seasoned project managers alike. Why Follow:This blog offers practical insights on communication, leadership, and strategic thinking within the realm of project management. Whether it’s through in-depth articles, podcasts, or video content, you’ll find valuable resources to help you grow your project management skills.
  9. MindTools Blog Overview:MindTools provides training and resources for various professional skills, including project management. Their blog focuses on tools, techniques, and strategies relevant to project execution. Why Follow:The content is diverse, covering topics like stakeholder management, risk assessment, and team engagement. MindTools is particularly beneficial for project managers looking to develop a comprehensive skill set that extends beyond traditional project management.
  10. PM Times Overview:PM Times is an online magazine featuring articles dedicated to project management professionals. It serves as a platform for experts to share their knowledge with the project management community. Why Follow:The quality of contributions is what makes PM Times stand out. The blog covers a vast array of topics, ensuring there’s something valuable for everyone. Whether you’re seeking opinions on current trends or deep dives into specific methodologies, PM Times delivers.
  11. Capterra Project Management Blog Overview:Capterra is a software discovery and reviews platform, and they offer a blog focused on project management tools and software. Why Follow:Their content is tailored for those looking to evaluate project management software, making it easier to make informed purchasing decisions. You’ll find detailed comparisons, reviews, and feature guides that can help you choose the best tools for your projects.
  12. ProjectManager.com Blog Overview:ProjectManager.com is another software platform, and their blog is rich with information that goes beyond their toolset. Why Follow:This blog offers expert advice on various project management topics, practical how-tos, and insights into trends. The information is digestible and an excellent resource for project managers who want to improve their capabilities across different areas.
  13. The PMO Perspectives Overview:This blog is written specifically for project management office (PMO) professionals. The PMO Perspectives provides insights and advice tailored for the strategic and operational aspects of running a PMO. Why Follow:With a focus on executing strategic initiatives and improving project delivery, this blog offers in-depth articles that explore common PMO challenges and solutions. It's a treasure trove of insights for those looking to enhance PMO operations. Conclusion Staying updated with the latest project management trends, tools, and best practices is crucial for your career development and the success of your projects. In 2023, the blogs we’ve explored in this post provide a diverse array of resources tailored to various needs within the project management field. Whether you're a novice or an experienced project manager, these blogs offer valuable insights, skill enhancement, and community support. Follow them regularly to enrich your knowledge and stay at the forefront of project management in an ever-evolving landscape. Engage with the content, apply what you learn, and remember, continuous learning is key to mastering project management! Make sure to explore these blogs, subscribe for updates, engage with articles, and become a part of the vibrant online project management community.

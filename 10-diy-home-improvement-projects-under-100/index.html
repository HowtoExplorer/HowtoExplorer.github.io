10 DIY Home Improvement Projects Under $100


Looking to spruce up your living space without breaking the bank? DIY home improvement projects are a fantastic way to add a personal touch to your home while staying within budget. Whether you’re a seasoned DIY enthusiast or just starting out, these 10 projects under $100 are sure to inspire you to get creative and transform your space.

1. Accent Wall with Washi Tape

Cost: $10-20
Create a stunning accent wall using colorful washi tape. This versatile and removable tape comes in various patterns and colors, allowing you to customize your wall without the commitment of paint. Get creative with geometric shapes or patterns for a unique look.

2. Floating Shelves

Cost: $20-30
Add both style and functionality to any room with floating shelves. These easy-to-install shelves can hold books, plants, or decorative items, instantly elevating the aesthetic of your space. Customize the size and layout to suit your needs.

3. Gallery Wall

Cost: $30-40
Turn a blank wall into a personalized gallery with a collection of framed photos, artwork, and prints. Mix and match frames for a dynamic look, or keep it cohesive with a monochromatic theme. This project allows you to showcase your memories and style.

4. Upcycled Furniture

Cost: Varies
Give old furniture a new lease on life with a fresh coat of paint or new hardware. Visit thrift stores or online marketplaces for budget-friendly pieces that you can transform to suit your aesthetic. Upcycling not only adds character to your space but also reduces waste.

5. Plant Wall

Cost: $20-30
Bring the outdoors in by creating a plant wall with hanging planters or wall-mounted pots. Opt for air-purifying plants like pothos or spider plants to enhance your indoor air quality while adding a touch of greenery to your home.

6. Statement Light Fixture

Cost: $40-50
Upgrade your lighting with a statement light fixture that serves as both a functional piece and a focal point. Look for affordable options like pendant lights or chandeliers to instantly elevate the ambiance of your room.

7. Peel-and-Stick Backsplash

Cost: $30-40
Transform your kitchen or bathroom with peel-and-stick backsplash tiles. These easy-to-install tiles come in a variety of designs, from subway tiles to mosaic patterns, allowing you to achieve a customized look without the hassle of traditional tiling.

8. Customized Doormat

Cost: $10-20
Make a welcoming statement at your front door with a customized doormat. Use stencils and outdoor paint to add a personal touch or opt for a quirky design that reflects your personality. This simple project instantly boosts your curb appeal.

9. Curtain Refresh

Cost: $20-30
Give your windows a makeover by updating your curtains. Choose new curtain panels in a different color or pattern to change the look of your room. You can also add decorative trim or tassels for a custom touch.

10. DIY Wall Art

Cost: $10-20
Unleash your creativity with DIY wall art projects. Use canvas, paint, or even recycled materials to create one-of-a-kind artwork for your walls. Abstract paintings, geometric designs, or inspirational quotes are just a few ideas to explore.

With a little creativity and a modest budget, you can transform your living space with these 10 DIY home improvement projects under $100. Whether you tackle one project at a time or combine several for a complete makeover, the key is to have fun and let your personality shine through in your home decor. Happy crafting!

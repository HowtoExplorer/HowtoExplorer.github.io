Global Local: Unravel Real, Authentic Immersive Experiences Around the World


Imagine you have the chance to step into different worlds – away from the bustling cities and back into the ancient times of folklore, spend an afternoon indulging in a traditional feast, or get lost in the mesmerizing beauty of the night sky. This is what global local is all about: providing a taste of various cultures by focusing on authentic, immersive experiences around the world. So pack your bags, and let’s begin our incredible journey!

Tokelau - Wake Up to the Sounds of Waves

As one of the most remote locations on Earth, Tokelau offers an incredible opportunity for travelers willing to venture off the beaten path. An island nation consisting of three small atolls in the South Pacific, Tokelau invites you to forget about modern life and rediscover simplicity. Immerse yourself by staying with a local family, participating in daily activities such as fishing, weaving, or traditional canoe building, and learn their indigenous ways. The most magical part? You’ll witness mesmerizing sunrises every morning, accompanied by the rhythmic sounds of waves lapping against the shore.

Bhutan - Embrace the Art of Happiness

The Land of the Thunder Dragon, Bhutan, is a slice of paradise nestled between India and China. Known for its unique focus on happiness and well-being, Bhutan is a country where you can truly enhance your mind, body, and soul by participating in several authentic experiences. Find tranquility during a hike through the serene forests, where you can meet Tashigang’s local monks and learn the art of silent meditation. Another spectacular opportunity is exploring the vibrant, colorful dzongs (fortress-monasteries) or enjoying traditional Bhutanese archery matches, which are considered the national sport.

Sardinia - Dive into a Sea of Memories

Sardinia—a jewel in the Mediterranean Sea—is more than pristine beaches and turquoise waters. This Italian island reveals its unique culture and heritage through an authentic, immersive experience every traveler should try. Join the locals on a traditional pitta bread making workshop where you can learn the ancient technique of cooking this island favorite bread in the ashes of a wood fire. Next, your Sardinian adventure shouldn’t be complete without a stay in one of the traditional stone houses called “cends,” nestled in serene countryside landscapes, where you can truly disconnect from the world and be embraced by Sardinian warmth.

Kalahari, South Africa - Astronomy and San Culture

Forget about the crowded, city skies; the heart of the Kalahari Desert allows you to embark on a truly celestial journey. Engage in South Africa’s oldest form of storytelling when you spend an incredible night under the star-filled sky with the San people, descendants of the first inhabitants of the African continent. Your guide will be the renowned San elder, Snangelo “Mars” Kruiper, who, alongside his knowledgeable team, promises an unforgettable stargazing experience. Besides being a celestial wonderland, the Kalahari is also a UNESCO-protected Biosphere Reserve, providing peaceful space to connect with diverse wildlife and landscapes.

Patagonia - Embrace the Wild Spirit

The magnificent landscapes of Patagonia, a region shared by Argentina and Chile, never cease to amaze. Capturing the essence of Patagonia inevitably includes embracing its wild nature. Become one with the wild spirit during an unforgettable horseback riding tour deep into the Wilsons Range, led by Araucanía indigenous people who have honed their equestrian skills throughout generations. Living closely to the land, they offer an unmatched sense of connection to the natural world. After your ride, grasp the opportunity to indulge in their traditional emap cooking, a sensory treat that will tantalize your taste buds.

Every adventure the world has to offer is a chance to learn, grow, and become immersed in different ways of life. The more we connect with each culture, the wealthier we become both emotionally and mentally. So grab your passports, pack those bags, and let these incredible global local experiences be the backdrop for your next unforgettable holiday. Time to live, breathe, and savor the world.

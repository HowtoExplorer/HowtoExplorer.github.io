Top 10 Must-See Broadway Shows of the Year


Broadway, the heart of live theater, offers a diverse range of shows that cater to all tastes and preferences. From dazzling musicals to thought-provoking dramas, there’s something for everyone. To help you navigate the sea of options, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 must-see Broadway shows of the year. Get ready to be captivated, inspired, and entertained!

1. Hamilton

No list would be complete without mentioning the cultural phenomenon that is Hamilton. Lin-Manuel Miranda’s groundbreaking musical tells the story of Founding Father Alexander Hamilton through a blend of hip-hop, rap, and traditional show tunes. The energy, creativity, and historical significance of this show make it an absolute must-see.

2. Dear Evan Hansen

Dear Evan Hansen is a powerful and emotionally charged musical that explores themes of isolation, mental health, and the search for acceptance. With its stunning performances and beautiful score, this show will leave you reflecting on the importance of human connection.

3. The Book of Mormon

Prepare to laugh uncontrollably with The Book of Mormon. This irreverent and hilarious musical follows two Mormon missionaries as they navigate their way through a remote Ugandan village. With its clever satire and catchy songs, this show is a guaranteed night of laughter and entertainment.

4. Hadestown

Immerse yourself in the mythical world of Hadestown, where ancient Greek mythology meets modern folk music. This Tony Award-winning musical takes you on a journey to the underworld and back, exploring themes of love, hope, and the power of music.

5. Wicked

Discover the untold story of the Wicked Witch of the West in this beloved Broadway classic. Wicked transports you to the land of Oz, where you’ll witness the unlikely friendship between Elphaba and Glinda and see how their paths diverge. With its mesmerizing music and stunning visuals, Wicked will leave you spellbound.

6. The Lion King

Experience the magic of Disney’s The Lion King on Broadway. This visually stunning production brings the beloved animated film to life with breathtaking costumes, captivating puppetry, and familiar songs. Join Simba on his journey to reclaim his rightful place as king of the Pride Lands.

7. Come From Away

In a world filled with uncertainty, Come From Away offers a heartwarming story of kindness and compassion. Based on true events, this uplifting musical tells the tale of a small town in Newfoundland that welcomed thousands of stranded passengers after the 9/11 attacks. Prepare to be moved by the power of human connection.

8. Moulin Rouge! The Musical

Step into the glitz and glamour of 19th-century Paris with Moulin Rouge! The Musical. This extravagant production features a dazzling array of costumes, high-energy dance numbers, and a soundtrack filled with popular hits. Get ready to be swept away by the romance and spectacle of this modern-day Moulin Rouge.

9. The Phantom of the Opera

Enter the haunting world of The Phantom of the Opera, the longest-running Broadway show in history. With its iconic score, stunning set designs, and tragic love story, this timeless classic continues to captivate audiences night after night. Experience the magic and mystery of the Phantom for yourself.

10. To Kill a Mockingbird

Based on Harper Lee’s Pulitzer Prize-winning novel, To Kill a Mockingbird tackles issues of racial injustice and morality. This thought-provoking play, adapted for the stage by Aaron Sorkin, shines a light on the enduring power of compassion and the fight for equality.

These top 10 must-see Broadway shows of the year represent the best of what the theater world has to offer. Each show offers a unique experience, whether it’s through its powerful storytelling, breathtaking performances, or unforgettable music. So, grab your tickets and get ready to be transported to a world of imagination and wonder. Broadway awaits!

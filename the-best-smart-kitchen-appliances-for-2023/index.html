The Best Smart Kitchen Appliances for 2023


In 2023, kitchens have evolved into spaces of innovation, blending convenience, efficiency, and smart technology. The growing popularity of smart appliances is transforming how we cook, clean, and even store food. From refrigerators that help you plan meals to ovens that can be controlled from anywhere, smart kitchen appliances are making everyday tasks more convenient and streamlined.

As we continue to integrate technology into every facet of our lives, kitchens are no exception. These smart devices offer a mix of practicality, energy efficiency, and enhanced usability. But with so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. Whether you’re a professional chef or a home cook, here’s a guide to the best smart kitchen appliances of 2023.

1. Smart Refrigerators: The Hub of Your Kitchen

Smart refrigerators have become a game-changer for modern kitchens. Equipped with Wi-Fi connectivity and various advanced features, these fridges do much more than just preserve your food. In 2023, the best models include built-in cameras, touch screens, and voice assistants, offering a wealth of options that simplify food management.

Key Features to Look For:

  • Built-in Cameras: Some refrigerators come with internal cameras that let you check the contents remotely via an app. This feature is particularly helpful when you’re out grocery shopping and unsure if you have enough milk or eggs at home.
  • Smart Temperature Control: Many smart refrigerators now offer precise temperature controls that can be adjusted remotely. This feature helps to keep your food fresher for longer.
  • Voice Assistant Integration: Integration with voice assistants like Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant lets you control the fridge, check inventory, and even order groceries with just your voice.
  • Touchscreen Display: Some smart fridges have a touchscreen display that allows you to manage your shopping lists, write notes, and even watch videos while preparing meals.

Best Smart Refrigerator Models in 2023:

  • Samsung Family Hub Refrigerator
  • LG InstaView Refrigerator
  • Whirlpool Smart French Door Refrigerator

These models are top-rated for their intuitive design, user-friendly apps, and seamless connectivity with other smart devices in your home.

2. Smart Ovens: A Culinary Revolution

Smart ovens are bringing precision and efficiency to home cooking. They offer features that allow you to preheat, bake, broil, and even air-fry your meals with the touch of a button. These ovens often include advanced technologies like convection cooking, automatic timers, and remote access through smartphone apps.

Key Features to Look For:

  • Remote Control: With smart ovens, you can preheat your oven or adjust cooking times remotely through an app. This is perfect for busy individuals who want to get dinner started before they even step foot in the kitchen.
  • Cooking Presets: Many smart ovens come with built-in presets for common dishes, making it easier to cook your favorite recipes with precise temperature and cooking time.
  • Voice Control Integration: Smart ovens are now often compatible with voice assistants, allowing you to control the oven hands-free while your hands are busy with other kitchen tasks.
  • Air Frying Functionality: Many modern smart ovens come with air frying capabilities, offering a healthier way to cook crispy and delicious dishes without using much oil.

Best Smart Oven Models in 2023:

  • June Oven (Pro)
  • Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer
  • GE Profile Smart Oven

These ovens are highly praised for their versatility, accuracy, and ability to streamline the cooking process.

3. Smart Coffee Makers: Wake Up in Style

For many, the day begins with a cup of coffee, and smart coffee makers are enhancing this experience. These appliances allow you to brew coffee from your smartphone or schedule brew times, so you can wake up to a freshly brewed cup every morning. Some models even offer personalized coffee settings for the perfect brew.

Key Features to Look For:

  • App Integration: Smart coffee makers connect to your smartphone, allowing you to start brewing remotely, set up automatic brewing schedules, and even monitor the coffee-making process from anywhere.
  • Customizable Settings: Many models allow you to adjust the strength, temperature, and volume of your coffee, ensuring a perfect brew every time.
  • Voice Control: Integration with voice assistants means you can start brewing your coffee just by saying a simple command.
  • Brew Strength Selection: Whether you prefer a mild cup or a strong espresso, smart coffee makers let you customize the brew strength for your perfect cup of coffee.

Best Smart Coffee Makers in 2023:

  • Nespresso Expert Coffee Machine
  • Keurig K-Elite Coffee Maker
  • Smarter Coffee 2nd Generation

These coffee makers stand out for their ability to brew a variety of coffee styles with the convenience of remote control.

4. Smart Dishwashers: Clean with Ease

Dishwashing is often one of the most tedious tasks in the kitchen, but with a smart dishwasher, it doesn’t have to be. These dishwashers offer more than just efficient cleaning—they feature apps, voice control, and even personalized wash settings, making cleanup simpler and faster.

Key Features to Look For:

  • Remote Monitoring: Smart dishwashers allow you to check the status of your wash cycle from anywhere via an app. You can start, pause, or monitor your cycle remotely, making it convenient if you’re running errands or working in another room.
  • Customized Wash Cycles: Many models come with adjustable wash settings for different types of dishes, including delicate glassware, pots and pans, and heavily soiled items.
  • Voice Control: Integration with voice assistants allows you to operate the dishwasher hands-free, making multitasking much easier while you focus on other cooking tasks.
  • Energy Efficiency: Smart dishwashers often come with energy-efficient settings, which reduce water and power consumption without compromising cleaning quality.

Best Smart Dishwasher Models in 2023:

  • Bosch 800 Series
  • Samsung Smart Dishwasher
  • Whirlpool Smart Dishwasher

These dishwashers are known for their efficient cleaning, user-friendly apps, and energy-saving features.

5. Smart Blenders: Blend Your Way to Healthier Meals

Whether you’re making smoothies, soups, or sauces, a smart blender can make the job easier and more efficient. These high-tech blenders often feature pre-programmed settings, remote control, and even personalized blending options, giving you complete control over your recipes.

Key Features to Look For:

  • Preset Programs: Many smart blenders come with preset programs for smoothies, soups, and other recipes, automatically adjusting the speed and blending time to deliver the best results.
  • Remote Control: With app control, you can start and monitor the blending process from your smartphone, so you don’t have to hover over the appliance.
  • Voice Assistant Compatibility: Some smart blenders are compatible with voice assistants, allowing for hands-free operation while you prep your ingredients.
  • Advanced Blade Technology: Top-of-the-line models feature blades designed for efficient blending, even with tough ingredients like ice, frozen fruit, and leafy greens.

Best Smart Blenders in 2023:

  • Vitamix Ascent Series
  • Ninja Foodi Power Blender
  • Blendtec Designer 725 Blender

These blenders stand out for their performance, versatility, and the convenience of smart features that simplify meal prep.

6. Smart Scales: Precision and Portion Control

A smart kitchen scale is a must-have for anyone looking to improve their cooking precision or manage their health and portion sizes. With a smart scale, you can measure ingredients more accurately and even track nutrition information. Many smart scales sync with health apps to help you monitor your dietary intake.

Key Features to Look For:

  • Nutritional Information: Some smart scales provide detailed nutritional information for the food you’re weighing, helping you make healthier meal choices.
  • App Connectivity: Many scales come with apps that track your food intake and offer insights into your eating habits, helping you make more informed decisions.
  • Multi-Unit Measurements: Top-tier smart scales offer the ability to measure in multiple units, such as grams, ounces, pounds, and milliliters, for greater flexibility.
  • Portion Control: These scales can help you manage portions more easily, which is beneficial for people focusing on dietary goals or cooking precise recipes.

Best Smart Kitchen Scales in 2023:

  • Etekcity Smart Food Scale
  • Greater Goods Digital Food Scale
  • Ozeri Pronto Digital Kitchen Scale

These models are widely recognized for their accuracy, easy-to-read displays, and app synchronization features.

7. Smart Cooktops and Induction Stoves: Cook with Precision

Smart cooktops and induction stoves are a revolution in cooking technology, offering energy efficiency, precision control, and safety. Induction stoves, in particular, are known for their rapid heat-up time and energy efficiency, making them ideal for modern kitchens.

Key Features to Look For:

  • Touch Controls: Smart cooktops often feature touch-sensitive controls that allow for easy adjustments to cooking temperatures and timers.
  • Remote Control: Many models allow you to control the heat level and cooking settings remotely, either through an app or voice commands, offering more convenience when multitasking.
  • Energy Efficiency: Induction stoves and smart cooktops are highly energy-efficient, as they heat cookware directly instead of the surrounding air.
  • Safety Features: Many smart cooktops come with safety features, such as automatic shut-off functions if the pot is removed or the surface gets too hot.

Best Smart Cooktops in 2023:

  • Samsung Smart Induction Cooktop
  • Bosch 800 Series Induction Cooktop
  • GE Profile Smart Cooktop

These smart cooktops provide excellent heat control, energy efficiency, and modern technology for every cooking need.

As we move further into 2023, the integration of smart appliances into our kitchens is not just a trend but a way to enhance our cooking experiences. Whether you’re looking to save time, cook more efficiently, or just enjoy the convenience of managing your kitchen tasks remotely, these smart devices are setting new standards in culinary technology. The best smart kitchen appliances of 2023 offer a seamless blend of innovation, convenience, and user-friendly design, making them essential additions to any modern kitchen.

When choosing the best appliances for your kitchen, it’s important to consider your cooking habits, space, and budget. Investing in high-quality smart appliances can not only enhance your culinary skills but also create a more efficient, enjoyable cooking environment. The future of cooking is here, and it’s smarter than ever before.

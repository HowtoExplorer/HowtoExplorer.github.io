DIY Wall Hangings for Boho Home Decor


Bohemian, or boho, home decor is all about embracing creativity, individuality, and a laid-back vibe. One of the key elements that can elevate the boho aesthetic of your living space is unique wall hangings. Creating your own DIY wall hangings allows you to add a personal touch to your decor while also unleashing your artistic side. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of DIY wall hanging ideas that are perfect for achieving that bohemian look in your home.

Macrame Wall Hanging

Materials Needed:

  • Macrame cord
  • Wooden dowel
  • Scissors
  • Tape measure

Instructions:

  1. Cut several pieces of macrame cord to your desired length.
  2. Fold each piece in half and loop it over the wooden dowel.
  3. Use a simple knot to secure the cord to the dowel.
  4. Continue this process with all the pieces of cord until the dowel is covered.
  5. Trim the ends of the cords to create a neat, even look.
  6. Hang your macrame wall hanging on a prominent wall for a boho statement piece.

Yarn Wall Hanging

Materials Needed:

  • Yarn in various colors
  • Wooden dowel or branch
  • Scissors
  • Comb

Instructions:

  1. Cut several lengths of yarn in different colors.
  2. Fold each piece of yarn in half and loop it over the dowel.
  3. Use a simple knot to attach the yarn to the dowel.
  4. Continue adding yarn in various colors and lengths to create a textured look.
  5. Use a comb to brush out the yarn for a fuller appearance.
  6. Hang your yarn wall hanging to add warmth and texture to your boho decor.

Woven Wall Hanging

Materials Needed:

  • Weaving loom
  • Yarn in assorted colors
  • Weaving needle
  • Scissors

Instructions:

  1. Set up your weaving loom according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  2. Begin weaving the base of your wall hanging using a simple over-under pattern.
  3. Once the base is complete, start adding in different colors of yarn to create patterns and textures.
  4. Use the weaving needle to secure the yarn in place.
  5. Continue weaving until your desired size is reached.
  6. Remove the finished piece from the loom and trim any excess yarn.
  7. Hang your woven wall hanging to showcase your intricate handiwork in your boho-inspired space.

Fabric Wall Hanging

Materials Needed:

  • Fabric scraps
  • Wooden dowel
  • Scissors
  • Twine

Instructions:

  1. Cut your fabric scraps into strips or geometric shapes.
  2. Tie the fabric pieces onto the wooden dowel using twine.
  3. Arrange the fabric in a visually appealing pattern.
  4. Trim any excess fabric for a clean finish.
  5. Hang your fabric wall hanging to add a pop of color and texture to your boho decor scheme.

DIY wall hangings are a fantastic way to infuse your home with bohemian charm and showcase your creativity. Whether you prefer the intricate knotting of macrame or the colorful weaving of yarn, there is a DIY wall hanging project for every skill level. Experiment with different materials, colors, and textures to create a one-of-a-kind piece that reflects your personal style. Let your imagination run wild and watch as your DIY wall hangings transform your living space into a boho paradise.

Remember, the beauty of boho decor lies in its eclectic and free-spirited nature, so don’t be afraid to try new techniques and incorporate unexpected elements into your wall hangings. With a little creativity and a lot of love, you can turn your walls into stunning works of art that embody the bohemian aesthetic. Happy crafting!

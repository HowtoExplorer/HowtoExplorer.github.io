The Best Food Subscription Boxes to Try This Year


In recent years, food subscription boxes have transformed the way we think about meal preparation and grocery shopping. With the convenience of having fresh ingredients and exciting recipes delivered right to your doorstep, these services cater to a variety of diets, preferences, and culinary skills. Whether you’re a busy professional looking to save time, a foodie eager to explore new flavors, or someone trying to eat healthier, there’s a subscription box out there for you. This year, we’ve compiled a list of some of the best food subscription boxes that you should consider trying.

1. HelloFresh

HelloFresh has firmly established itself as one of the leading meal kit delivery services. They offer a diverse range of meal plans designed to accommodate different dietary needs, including vegetarian, family-friendly, and calorie-smart options. Each week, subscribers can choose from a selection of delicious recipes featuring fresh, seasonal ingredients.

Why Choose HelloFresh?

  • Variety: With new recipes every week, you’ll never get bored.
  • Flexibility: You can modify your delivery schedule and skip weeks as needed.
  • Sustainability: HelloFresh is committed to reducing food waste by providing pre-portioned ingredients.

Pricing

HelloFresh’s pricing is competitive, with plans starting at around $8 per serving. The number of meals you choose per week can affect the overall cost, making it adaptable to your budget.

2. Blue Apron

Blue Apron was one of the pioneers in the meal kit industry and continues to impress with its commitment to quality. Their meals are crafted by professional chefs, and the ingredients are sourced from sustainable farms. Blue Apron offers a unique feature: Wine Pairing which allows you to enjoy curated wines delivered alongside your meals.

Why Choose Blue Apron?

  • Chef-Designed Recipes: Enjoy gourmet meals that are easy to prepare.
  • Wine Pairing: Elevate your dining experience with expertly paired wines.
  • Sustainability Focus: Blue Apron emphasizes eco-friendly practices in sourcing and packaging.

Pricing

Blue Apron’s pricing starts at approximately $7.49 per serving, depending on the plan you select. They offer a variety of plans, including two-person and family plans.

3. Sun Basket

For health-conscious eaters and those with specific dietary restrictions, Sun Basket stands out as a fantastic option. They provide organic and clean ingredients, and their meal plans cater to various diets, including paleo, keto, and Mediterranean.

Why Choose Sun Basket?

  • Health-Focused: All ingredients are organic and sustainably sourced.
  • Customizable Plans: Choose meals that fit your dietary preferences.
  • Quick Prep Options: Many meals can be prepared in 30 minutes or less.

Pricing

Sun Basket’s pricing typically starts around $11.99 per serving, but this can vary based on the meal plan you select.

4. EveryPlate

If you’re looking for an affordable meal kit option without compromising on quality, EveryPlate is an excellent choice. They provide simple, hearty recipes that are easy to prepare and budget-friendly.

Why Choose EveryPlate?

  • Affordability: One of the most cost-effective meal kits on the market.
  • Simplicity: Recipes are designed to be straightforward, making cooking accessible for everyone.
  • Variety: A rotating menu ensures you’ll always have new options to try.

Pricing

EveryPlate’s pricing starts at about $4.99 per serving, making it perfect for families or anyone looking to save on meal costs.

5. Green Chef

Green Chef is a USDA-certified organic meal kit service that focuses on healthy eating. They offer multiple meal plans, including keto, paleo, and balanced living. The recipes are designed to be nutritious without sacrificing flavor.

Why Choose Green Chef?

  • Health-Centric: All meals are crafted with organic ingredients and focus on balanced nutrition.
  • Flexibility: Choose from various meal plans to suit your dietary needs.
  • Easy Cooking: Recipes are easy to follow, even for novice cooks.

Pricing

Green Chef typically starts at around $11.99 per serving, but prices may vary based on the chosen plan.

6. Freshly

If you prefer ready-made meals rather than cooking from scratch, Freshly is the go-to subscription box. They deliver fully prepared meals that just need to be heated, making it an ideal option for those with busy lifestyles.

Why Choose Freshly?

  • Convenience: Ready-to-eat meals save time on preparation and cleanup.
  • Balanced Nutrition: Meals are crafted by chefs and nutritionists to ensure healthy options.
  • Dietary Flexibility: Offers a range of meals catering to various dietary preferences.

Pricing

Freshly’s pricing starts at around $8.99 per meal, with options to select different meal plans based on your needs.

7. Terra’s Kitchen

For those who want to minimize food waste while enjoying fresh meals, Terra’s Kitchen is a unique choice. Their meals come in a reusable vessel, and they focus on sustainability. The recipes are chef-designed, and the ingredients are all fresh and organic.

Why Choose Terra’s Kitchen?

  • Eco-Friendly Packaging: Reusable vessels reduce waste and promote sustainability.
  • Fresh Ingredients: All ingredients are fresh and ethically sourced.
  • Quick Meals: Most recipes can be prepared in 30 minutes or less.

Pricing

Terra’s Kitchen pricing starts at around $9.99 per serving, focusing on fresh and sustainable options.

8. Snap Kitchen

For those who are constantly on the go, Snap Kitchen offers a selection of healthy, chef-prepared meals that are perfect for a busy lifestyle. You can find a variety of options, including gluten-free, vegetarian, and whole30 meals.

Why Choose Snap Kitchen?

  • Ready-to-Eat Meals: Perfect for those who don’t have time to cook.
  • Variety: A wide range of options caters to different dietary needs.
  • Nutritionally Balanced: Meals are crafted by chefs with a focus on health.

Pricing

Snap Kitchen prices vary based on location and meal selection, but you can expect to pay around $8 to $12 per meal.

9. Home Chef

Home Chef is known for its versatility and customization. They offer meal kits that allow you to mix and match proteins, sides, and sauces, giving you the freedom to create meals that suit your taste.

Why Choose Home Chef?

  • Customization: Tailor your meals to your preferences with a variety of options.
  • Flexible Plans: Skip weeks or change your delivery frequency as needed.
  • Variety of Recipes: Enjoy a rotating menu with new dishes each week.

Pricing

Home Chef pricing starts around $7.99 per serving, making it a budget-friendly option with plenty of flexibility.

10. SnackCrate

For those who love to snack, SnackCrate is a fun subscription box that delivers snacks from around the world. Each month, you can explore a new country’s treats, making it an exciting way to discover international flavors.

Why Choose SnackCrate?

  • Global Variety: Experience snacks from different countries each month.
  • Perfect for Sharing: Great for families or as a gift for friends.
  • Unique Treats: Many items are hard to find in local stores.

Pricing

SnackCrate pricing varies based on the size of the box you choose, with options starting at around $14.99 per month.

Conclusion

Food subscription boxes are more than just a trend—they’re a convenient and exciting way to enhance your culinary experience at home. Whether you prefer cooking from scratch or simply warming up a meal, there’s a service that fits your lifestyle and dietary needs. As you explore the options, consider your preferences for meal types, cooking time, and budget. With so many fantastic choices available, this is the year to dive into the world of food subscription boxes and elevate your dining experience.

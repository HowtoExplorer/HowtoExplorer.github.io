Who Run the World? Girls: Celebrating Female Dance Legends


In the vast landscape of music, dance holds a central position. From ballet to club bangers, dance music has continuously evolved, capturing hearts and souls worldwide. Amidst the predominantly male-dominated industry, female dance legends have left an indelible mark. They have forged a path for today’s dance scene and inspired countless artists to follow in their impressive footsteps. In this blog post, we will be celebrating these female dance legends who have run the world.

Grace Jones - The Iconic Versatile Force

Often remembered for her eccentric appearances and transformative performances, Grace Jones took the dance world by storm in the 1970s and 80s. Her synthpop, new wave, and reggae-infused music set the stage for groundbreaking artists. With her stage presence, Jones revolutionized music videos and visually captivating live performances.

Vanessa Daou – Arabesque Sensuality

A true innovator in the world of electronic dance music, Vanessa Daou’s unique blend of Arabic and European influences created mesmerizing rhythms and haunting melodies. Her debut album, “Mystic Rose”, showcased her captivating and sensual Arabic voice within a grounded electronic dance music foundation. Despite limited mainstream exposure, Daou remains a prominent figure in the experimental world of dance music.

Chaka Khan – Queen of Jazz-Funk

Chaka Khan needs no introduction. The iconic artist brought soul and funk elements to the forefront, shaping the musicaide range as well as her charismatic stage presence. Khan’s unique vocal stylings and passionate performances influenced generations and remain an essential part of dance music history.

Jill Scott – Neo-Soul Seductress

With her rich, soulful voice and smooth rhythmic melodies, Jill Scott’s neo-soul vibes stirred emotions and hearts across the globe. As one half of the duo “SCOTTATTA”, her collaboration with producer Dirk Powell brought jazz, blues, and folk to the forefront of the electronic dance scene. Jill’s innovative approach to neo-soul continue to inspire other female artists in the pursuit of musical creativity.

Sasha & Charlatans – Unforgettable Vocal Duets

Sasha CARINA a well-known DJ, led a groundbreaking collaboration with the Charlatans, a popular British band. The result was a collection of unforgettable vocal duets that showcased another facet of dance music -deep, soulful, and introspective. Their captivating chemistry and daring musical arrangements pushed boundaries and established a new, open-minded perspective on electronic dance music.

Amelie Lens – Feminine Force of Techno

In recent years, Amelie Lens, the techno titan, asserts her presence within the male-dominated techno scene. Her high-energy sets and roaring, powerful soundscapes remind listeners that women can lead with intensity just as men can. The Belgian DJ and producer has become a role model for young female artists entering the world of techno with her fierce determination and uncompromising style.

Tina Turner – Rock N’ Roll Rebel

An undeniable rock n’ roll rebel, Tina Turner’s powerful and electrifying performances have graced stages across the globe. A force of nature, she energized dance floors with her magnetic stage presence and passionate, powerhouse vocals. The Turner’s fearless exploration and vivid interpretation of rock, soul, and dance music made her a legendary figure in the world of music.

Deborah Cox – Soulful House Queen

While Canadian singer Deborah Cox made a name for herself in pop and R&B, her soul-stirring vocals have also captivated house music lovers worldwide. Best known for her hit single “Thought It Through”, Cox’s sultry voice and raw emotion have set her apart in the house music genre. Her commitment to captivating storytelling within dance music is a testament to her talent and enduring influence.

These boundary-breaking female dance legends have shaped the course of dance music and opened doors for future generations. Their innovative work, unwavering commitment, and passion for pushing creative boundaries have left a lasting legacy that influences the current and future prime movers in the dance music industry. Let us continue to celebrate and honor their spirits and look forward to what young talented female artists will bring to this evolving universe.

The Best Family-Friendly Subscription Boxes to Try


In recent years, subscription boxes have soared in popularity, offering families the perfect way to stay entertained, educated, and engaged—right from the comfort of home. The beauty of family-friendly subscription boxes lies in their diversity. Whether you’re seeking activities to spark your children’s creativity, tools to promote hands-on learning, or just a monthly dose of fun to share as a household, there’s something for everyone. This guide highlights some of the best family-friendly subscription boxes that cater to a wide range of interests and age groups, making it easy to find the perfect fit for your family.

Why Choose a Family-Friendly Subscription Box?

Balancing work, school, and family time can be a challenge. Subscription boxes provide a convenient way to carve out meaningful moments, offering carefully curated activities designed to delight and bond families. They often deliver the triple benefit of convenience, education, and entertainment, and they arrive straight to your doorstep, saving you valuable time and energy.

These boxes are also incredibly versatile—whether you want to inspire greater curiosity in science, nurture a budding artist, or simply spend quality time together, they deliver in spades. With options geared toward children of all ages, as well as some that involve the entire family, subscription boxes make family bonding easy and fun.

1. STEM and Learning-Focused Subscription Boxes

Educational subscription boxes empower children to learn through hands-on projects, making subjects like science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) exciting and accessible. They’re ideal for parents looking to supplement their kids’ schooling with engaging, extracurricular content.

KiwiCo
KiwiCo offers curated STEM and art projects for kids of all ages. From toddlers to teens, you’ll find activity crates tailored to different developmental levels. Younger kids can explore simple engineering with the Panda Crate, while older kids can tackle more advanced projects like building robotic arms or exploring chemical reactions with the Tinker Crate. Every crate comes with step-by-step instructions and materials, separating it from traditional activity kits.

Think Outside
Designed to inspire a love of the outdoors, Think Outside encourages children to develop practical skills while appreciating nature. Each box includes a mix of outdoor survival tools and activity guides. It’s an excellent choice for families looking to get away from screens and reconnect with the natural world.

Spangler Science Club
From the brilliant mind of Steve Spangler, this subscription box makes science experiments accessible right at home. Each monthly delivery features awe-inspiring experiments that encourage kids to hypothesize, test, and learn through discovery. The subscription is educational and a blast to work through together as a family.

2. Creative and Craft Subscription Boxes

Arts and crafts subscription boxes provide the perfect outlet for creative expression. They’re excellent for rainy days, weekends, or any time your family wants to get hands-on and imaginative.

Craftsy Crate
Craftsy Crate targets all age groups and skill levels, offering DIY projects that range from beginner-friendly to complex masterpieces. Whether your child loves painting, sewing, or sculpting, this box delivers on variety and creativity while providing easy-to-follow guidelines.

We Craft Box
Designed for younger kids, We Craft Box provides themed craft activities that foster creativity while reducing the burden of prep work on parents. Every box includes all materials needed for up to two children, ensuring zero trips to the store for supplies. Projects focus on storytelling, encouraging kids to use their creations in imaginative play.

Maker Crate by KiwiCo
For older kids and teens with a love for design, Maker Crate delivers sophisticated, stylish DIY projects like macramé wall hangings, resin art, and handmade ceramics. Ideal for independent work or family crafting sessions, this box supports lifelong creative skills.

3. Family Activity Subscription Boxes

Looking for something that involves every member of the family? These subscription boxes are designed with group participation in mind, making them perfect for game nights, weekend bonding, or shared educational experiences.

UnboxBoardom
A subscription that delivers family game night right to your doorstep, UnboxBoardom sends out a new board game monthly or quarterly, depending on your choice. The games range from cooperative adventures to strategy-based challenges, ensuring hours of fun for both parents and kids alike.

Adventure Challenge: Family Edition
Adventure Challenge offers a scratch-off activity book paired with supplies designed to facilitate unique family bonding experiences. Each kit encourages you to put down devices and enjoy meaningful, memory-making activities, from backyard scavenger hunts to DIY theater productions.

Universal Yums
Want to explore new flavors from around the world? Universal Yums sends out snacks from a different country each month, turning mealtime or snack time into an immersive cultural experience. Each box includes trivia and educational materials about the featured country, making it both fun and informative.

4. Book Subscription Boxes

For families with a passion for reading, book subscription boxes are a fantastic way to expand your library and instill a love of literature in your little ones. From picture books to middle-grade reads, these boxes cater to every reading level.

Little Fun Club
Ideal for fostering a love of reading in young children, Little Fun Club personalizes its book selections based on age and interests. Each box contains two to three books, along with accompanying activities that encourage deeper engagement with the stories.

OwlCrate Jr.
Geared toward middle-grade readers, OwlCrate Jr. delivers captivating novels, exclusive author content, and an assortment of fun extras. Perfect for tweens who have a passion for immersive storytelling, this subscription adds a touch of magic to reading time.

Story Box
Targeting the earliest readers, Story Box combines enchanting board books with literacy-building activities. Its simple yet effective approach encourages parents to sit down with their kids and explore the joys of reading together.

5. Food and Cooking Subscription Boxes

Food subscription boxes offer families the opportunity to explore cooking together while introducing kids to new cuisines and culinary skills. These are perfect for those who enjoy spending time in the kitchen or want to teach children healthy eating habits.

Raddish Kids
This popular cooking club for kids provides child-friendly recipes, pre-measured ingredients, and culinary learning materials. With themes like “Farm to Table” or “Around the World,” Raddish Kids makes cooking a fun, educational activity the entire family can enjoy.

Kidstir
Kidstir combines easy-to-follow recipes with fun cooking tools to help kids develop practical kitchen skills. Each box has a theme, such as breakfast bakes or pizza night, allowing families to whip up delicious meals while creating cherished memories.

SnackCrate
For families less inclined to cook but eager to expand their palate, SnackCrate sends a monthly box of international snacks. From Japanese mochi to Swiss chocolates, it’s a tasty way to introduce kids to global flavors and cultures.

Choosing the Best Subscription Box for Your Family

Selecting the right subscription box comes down to your family’s unique interests and needs. Consider the following:

  • Age Range: Opt for boxes tailored to the developmental stage and skill level of your child.
  • Frequency: Decide whether you’d prefer a monthly box or a quarterly delivery with more substantial items.
  • Focus: Whether your goal is educational enrichment, creative expression, or simply having fun, choose a box that aligns with your priorities.

Conclusion

Family-friendly subscription boxes are more than just monthly deliveries—they’re miniature opportunities to laugh, learn, and come together as a family. From educational STEM kits to hands-on cooking adventures, these boxes make shared experiences easy and meaningful.

By incorporating one or more of these boxes into your routine, you’re not just simplifying family time—you’re enriching it. Whether exploring new hobbies, learning new skills, or simply creating precious memories, these subscription boxes are sure to bring joy and value to any household.

The Best Real Estate Investment Podcasts to Follow


Real estate investing has always been a popular way to grow wealth, whether you’re a seasoned investor or just getting started. With the rise of podcasts, the real estate investment space has become more accessible, offering valuable insights and actionable advice to help investors navigate the complexities of the market. If you’re looking for guidance, tips, and inspiration, following the right real estate investment podcasts can make all the difference. In this post, we’ll dive into the best real estate investment podcasts you should follow for expert knowledge, trends, and strategies to enhance your investment journey.

1. BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcast

One of the most popular and trusted names in real estate investment podcasts is the BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcast. With hundreds of episodes featuring seasoned investors, successful entrepreneurs, and real estate experts, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, from beginner-level investment tips to advanced strategies for scaling your real estate portfolio.

Why It’s Great

  • Diverse Topics: Whether you’re interested in house flipping, rental properties, or commercial real estate, BiggerPockets has you covered. The hosts, Brandon Turner and David Greene, delve into the most relevant trends and issues in real estate investing, sharing tips for overcoming challenges and maximizing profits.
  • Expert Guests: The podcast frequently features interviews with successful real estate investors who share their stories and provide valuable insights.
  • Community Engagement: BiggerPockets fosters a sense of community where listeners can join discussions and connect with others in the industry.

Best for:

Investors of all levels, from beginners to advanced. Whether you are just starting or looking to grow your portfolio, this podcast offers useful strategies and success stories to guide you.

2. The Real Estate Guys Radio Show

The Real Estate Guys Radio Show has been a staple in the real estate podcasting world for over 20 years. Hosted by Robert Helms and Russell Gray, this podcast dives deep into the principles and strategies behind successful real estate investing. From tax strategies and asset protection to market trends and financing, the show provides listeners with comprehensive insights into the world of real estate.

Why It’s Great

  • Timeless Advice: With decades of experience, the hosts provide advice that has stood the test of time. Their content doesn’t just focus on current trends; it offers principles that remain relevant even during market shifts.
  • Expert Panels: The podcast regularly features experts from various fields, including finance, legal, and tax professionals, providing a holistic approach to real estate investing.
  • Market Specifics: They offer deep dives into different types of real estate investments and strategies, including multifamily, single-family, and international opportunities.

Best for:

Those looking for a more structured and strategic approach to real estate investing. If you prefer listening to expert panels and discussions about the economic, financial, and legal aspects of investing, this is the podcast for you.

3. The Best Ever Real Estate Podcast

Hosted by Joe Fairless, The Best Ever Real Estate Podcast stands out as one of the most comprehensive podcasts in the real estate space. It’s designed for both beginners and experienced investors and provides actionable advice from some of the most successful people in real estate.

Why It’s Great

  • Daily Content: Unlike many real estate podcasts that post once a week, this podcast publishes daily episodes, giving you a steady stream of knowledge to stay updated on the latest industry trends.
  • Top-tier Guests: Joe interviews prominent investors, from self-made millionaires to billionaires, sharing their stories and strategies for achieving success in the real estate sector.
  • Actionable Advice: Each episode is packed with actionable tips, from finding deals and raising capital to scaling a business and managing properties efficiently.

Best for:

Investors who prefer quick, actionable advice delivered consistently. If you’re eager to learn from successful investors daily and implement those strategies, The Best Ever Real Estate Podcast is an excellent resource.

4. Real Estate Investing for Cash Flow

For those who are looking to invest specifically for cash flow, Real Estate Investing for Cash Flow by Kevin Bupp offers expert advice on building a passive income stream through real estate. The podcast focuses on investing in mobile home parks, multifamily units, and other high-cash-flow properties that are often overlooked by traditional investors.

Why It’s Great

  • Cash Flow Focused: The primary focus is on generating consistent cash flow, a crucial aspect for investors looking for stability and long-term wealth.
  • Niche Expertise: Kevin’s expertise in mobile home parks and other non-traditional investment assets offers valuable insights that aren’t typically covered in mainstream real estate podcasts.
  • Expert Interviews: Guests on the podcast are experts in cash flow-generating properties, sharing strategies to maximize returns and mitigate risk.

Best for:

Investors interested in cash flow properties and unconventional real estate investment opportunities. This podcast is especially valuable for those looking for a passive income stream through investments like mobile home parks and multifamily buildings.

5. The Real Wealth Show

The Real Wealth Show is a great resource for both new and experienced real estate investors looking for sound advice on building wealth through real estate. Hosted by Kathy Fettke, this podcast focuses on helping listeners create financial independence and wealth using real estate as a tool.

Why It’s Great

  • Comprehensive Learning: The podcast covers all aspects of real estate investing, from financing and property management to finding great markets and understanding tax benefits.
  • Market Focus: Kathy regularly focuses on markets that offer the best investment opportunities, helping investors make informed decisions on where to invest.
  • Real Estate Strategies: The podcast emphasizes building long-term wealth through real estate by leveraging strategies like passive investing and out-of-state investing.

Best for:

Those seeking a clear path to financial independence using real estate as a wealth-building tool. The Real Wealth Show is ideal for anyone wanting to learn how to make sound investment decisions based on market research and financial strategies.

6. The Real Estate Syndication Show

If you’re interested in real estate syndications, The Real Estate Syndication Show is a must-listen podcast. Hosted by Whitney Sewell, this show focuses on helping investors learn how to get started in syndication, where multiple investors pool their resources to invest in large real estate projects, such as apartment complexes and commercial properties.

Why It’s Great

  • Syndication Focus: This podcast is perfect for those looking to invest in larger real estate deals without having to take on the full financial responsibility themselves.
  • Detailed Education: Whitney provides in-depth information on how syndications work, including how to find investors, structure deals, and manage properties.
  • Guest Insights: The show regularly features successful syndicators who share their experiences, challenges, and lessons learned in the syndication world.

Best for:

Investors looking to scale their portfolios by participating in larger real estate deals through syndications. Whether you’re new to syndication or an experienced investor looking to refine your strategy, this podcast offers valuable insights.

7. Cashflow Ninja

For those who want to build wealth and financial freedom through real estate, Cashflow Ninja is a podcast that delves into strategies for creating passive income through smart investing. Hosted by M.C. Laubscher, this show covers a variety of topics, from real estate to other forms of income-generating assets.

Why It’s Great

  • Passive Income Focus: Cashflow Ninja focuses heavily on building passive income streams, which is ideal for real estate investors looking for financial independence.
  • Diverse Investment Strategies: While real estate is a primary focus, the podcast also delves into other ways to build wealth, such as business investments, crypto, and life insurance.
  • Actionable Strategies: M.C. Laubscher provides actionable insights on how to leverage your investments to generate income and build wealth over time.

Best for:

Investors looking to create long-term wealth through passive income strategies, with a focus on real estate and alternative investment vehicles.

8. Real Estate Unfiltered

For those who prefer no-nonsense, unfiltered real estate discussions, Real Estate Unfiltered offers raw, authentic insights into the world of real estate investing. Hosted by seasoned investor Kyle Stanley, the podcast offers a realistic look at the challenges and opportunities within the real estate market.

Why It’s Great

  • Honest Approach: Kyle brings a no-fluff, candid perspective on what it takes to succeed in real estate. He discusses both the highs and the lows of investing, providing a balanced view.
  • Actionable Tips: With each episode, listeners walk away with practical advice that can be implemented immediately.
  • Success Stories: The podcast also features interviews with successful real estate investors who share their journeys and insights.

Best for:

Investors who prefer a straightforward, no-nonsense approach to real estate investing. If you’re looking for actionable advice without the hype, Real Estate Unfiltered is the perfect choice.

9. Wealth Formula Podcast

The Wealth Formula Podcast, hosted by Buck Joffrey, focuses on wealth-building strategies through real estate and other forms of alternative investments. The podcast covers a broad range of topics, including asset protection, tax strategies, and the mindset required for successful investing.

Why It’s Great

  • Broad Investment Focus: While real estate is a significant topic, the podcast also covers other types of investments that complement real estate strategies.
  • Tax and Legal Advice: Buck often brings in tax professionals and legal experts to discuss strategies for minimizing taxes and protecting your assets, which are essential topics for any real estate investor.
  • Interviews with Industry Leaders: The podcast features high-profile interviews with investors and experts who share their wealth-building insights.

Best for:

Investors looking for a comprehensive approach to wealth-building that includes real estate and other alternative investments, with a strong focus on tax and asset protection strategies.

Conclusion

Whether you’re a novice investor just starting or a seasoned pro looking to scale your portfolio, these real estate investment podcasts offer valuable insights and strategies that can help you achieve your goals. From passive income and cash flow to syndications and market research, the range of topics covered by these podcasts ensures that every investor will find something useful. Stay informed, learn from the experts, and use the strategies shared on these shows to enhance your real estate investing journey.

The Best Fashion Subscription Boxes to Try


In today’s fast-paced world, convenience is king, and fashion is no exception. Subscription boxes have become a game-changer in the way we shop for clothes, offering personalized styles delivered straight to your doorstep. Whether you’re looking to refresh your wardrobe, explore new styles, or simply enjoy the thrill of receiving new items, fashion subscription boxes provide an excellent solution.

These services cater to various styles, budgets, and preferences, making them perfect for anyone who loves fashion but doesn’t have the time or energy to spend hours shopping. In this post, we’ll dive into the best fashion subscription boxes that are worth trying, each offering something unique for different fashion tastes.

1. Stitch Fix

Stitch Fix is one of the most popular and well-known fashion subscription services, and for a good reason. It offers a highly personalized shopping experience based on your style, size, and preferences. After filling out a comprehensive style quiz, a personal stylist selects pieces tailored just for you. You can specify your clothing type preferences, budget, and even set your delivery frequency.

Each box contains five items, which may include tops, dresses, pants, and accessories. The best part? You only pay for what you decide to keep, and you can try everything on in the comfort of your home. The styling fee is credited toward any purchase, and if you buy all five items in the box, you’ll receive a discount.

Key Features:

  • Personalized styling based on preferences and style quiz
  • Try on clothes at home
  • Styling fee credited to purchases
  • 25% off if you buy all five items

2. Trunk Club

Trunk Club, owned by Nordstrom, is another top contender in the fashion subscription box industry. Unlike some other services, Trunk Club offers a blend of high-end and everyday brands, making it ideal for those who want quality pieces but don’t want to break the bank. The service begins with a personal consultation where a stylist gets to know your taste, size, and needs. Then, you receive a “trunk” (a larger box) filled with 8-12 handpicked items that match your preferences.

What sets Trunk Club apart is its emphasis on working with stylists who are experienced in selecting a mix of fashion-forward and classic pieces. If you’re someone who enjoys experimenting with trends but still wants timeless staples in your wardrobe, Trunk Club might be the service for you.

Key Features:

  • Personalized styling from professional stylists
  • A mix of premium and accessible brands
  • Up to 12 items per trunk
  • Free shipping and returns

3. Le Tote

Le Tote is a fashion subscription box that focuses on rentals, offering a unique twist on the typical subscription service. With Le Tote, you can rent an unlimited number of items each month, including clothing, jewelry, and accessories. The service allows you to choose pieces you love, wear them, and then return them once you’re ready for something new.

Le Tote works similarly to a traditional subscription box in that you complete a style profile. However, instead of keeping items, you’re renting them for a set period, making it ideal for those who love variety but don’t want to keep clothes long-term. This service is perfect for people looking for trendy, high-quality pieces without the commitment of ownership.

Key Features:

  • Rent clothing and accessories instead of buying
  • Unlimited exchanges per month
  • Wide range of sizes, including maternity wear
  • Option to purchase pieces at discounted rates

4. Fabletics

Fabletics is a subscription service tailored specifically to those who love activewear. Founded by actress Kate Hudson, Fabletics has become a go-to brand for stylish, comfortable, and functional athletic wear. The subscription model offers customers exclusive access to new collections, and members receive personalized recommendations based on their preferences.

Each month, members can browse Fabletics’ curated collection of activewear and select items to add to their wardrobe. If you’re looking for leggings, sports bras, workout tops, or even swimwear, Fabletics offers everything you need to stay fashionable while hitting the gym or running errands.

Key Features:

  • High-quality activewear for all activities
  • Personalized shopping recommendations
  • VIP membership discounts and perks
  • Flexibility to skip months without penalty

5. Rent the Runway

For those who need designer pieces for special occasions but don’t want to spend a fortune, Rent the Runway is a perfect choice. This fashion subscription box lets you rent high-end clothing and accessories for a fraction of the retail price. From cocktail dresses to elegant gowns, Rent the Runway has an extensive collection of formal wear, ideal for weddings, parties, and other events.

In addition to event wear, Rent the Runway offers everyday essentials like tops, pants, and outerwear. You can choose from different membership tiers, depending on how often you want to receive new items and how many pieces you want per month. For a premium experience, Rent the Runway also allows you to purchase items at a discount if you fall in love with them.

Key Features:

  • Rent designer pieces for a fraction of the price
  • Wide range of formal and casual wear
  • Flexible membership options
  • Option to purchase rental items at a discount

6. Wantable

Wantable is another fashion subscription box service that offers personalization at its best. Similar to Stitch Fix, Wantable provides a style quiz that gathers information about your style, preferences, and sizing. The difference is that Wantable has several different “Edit” options, including the Style Edit, Active Edit, and Sleep & Body Edit. This makes Wantable a great choice for a wide variety of styles and needs.

Each box contains 7-8 items, carefully curated to match your preferences. The service is flexible, allowing you to skip boxes and only receive items when you feel ready to add new pieces to your wardrobe. Wantable also offers a fitness-focused edit, which is perfect for those who enjoy an active lifestyle but still want stylish options for their workouts.

Key Features:

  • Multiple edits for different needs (Style, Active, Sleep & Body)
  • 7-8 items per box
  • Ability to skip boxes and set preferences
  • Personal stylist to curate selections

7. Express Style Trial

Express is a popular brand known for its trendy and affordable clothing. Their Style Trial subscription box is a great option for people who love to shop but want to experience curated selections without the hassle. With this service, you’ll receive a box of up to 8 pieces of clothing and accessories. Each item is selected based on your size, preferences, and style profile.

What makes Express Style Trial stand out is its focus on offering versatile and on-trend pieces that can easily transition from work to weekend wear. The clothing is generally more affordable than some other high-end services, making it perfect for those who want stylish options without the designer price tag.

Key Features:

  • Affordable, stylish clothing and accessories
  • Versatile items that can be worn for work or casual outings
  • Easy returns and exchanges
  • Personalized selections based on style preferences

8. Dia & Co

Dia & Co is a fashion subscription box for plus-size women, providing stylish, flattering clothing in sizes 14 and up. Similar to Stitch Fix, Dia & Co gives customers the opportunity to fill out a style quiz that helps stylists select pieces tailored to their taste, budget, and sizing. The box typically includes 5 items, ranging from tops and dresses to jeans and accessories.

Dia & Co focuses on making women feel confident and beautiful by curating outfits that celebrate all body types. This service is particularly popular among plus-size women who want to update their wardrobes with trendy yet comfortable pieces that fit well and flatter their shape.

Key Features:

  • Plus-size clothing for women sizes 14+
  • Personalized styling by experienced professionals
  • Trendy yet comfortable clothing selections
  • Free shipping and returns

9. The Ms. Collection

For women who love luxurious and high-quality clothing, The Ms. Collection is an excellent fashion subscription box to try. This service offers premium items with an emphasis on luxury, sophistication, and elegance. After filling out a detailed profile, you’ll receive curated selections that reflect your personal style and preferences.

The Ms. Collection focuses on upscale wardrobe staples, such as tailored blazers, dresses, and statement accessories. If you want to build a closet filled with timeless and high-quality pieces, this service is worth considering.

Key Features:

  • Luxury and high-quality clothing
  • Personalized selections based on style preferences
  • Focus on timeless, elegant pieces
  • Free shipping and easy returns

10. Gwynnie Bee

Similar to Le Tote, Gwynnie Bee is a clothing rental service specifically designed for plus-size women. This service allows you to rent a variety of stylish pieces, including dresses, tops, skirts, and pants. Gwynnie Bee is perfect for those who want a flexible, no-commitment way to enjoy new clothes without the pressure of buying them.

With Gwynnie Bee, you can keep pieces as long as you’d like, and the service will send new items once you’re ready to return your current selections. The box offers a range of items that cater to both casual and more formal occasions, making it versatile and perfect for various events and daily wear.

Key Features:

  • Rent stylish pieces for plus-size women
  • No time limit on rentals
  • Flexible subscription plans
  • Wide selection of sizes, from casual to formal wear

Conclusion

Fashion subscription boxes are a fantastic way to refresh your wardrobe without the stress of traditional shopping. From personalized styling services to clothing rentals, these services provide a variety of options to suit different tastes and budgets. Whether you’re looking for high-end designer pieces or trendy activewear, there’s a fashion subscription box for everyone.

As with any service, it’s important to try a few options and see what fits your style best. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different boxes to find the one that suits your lifestyle, needs, and fashion preferences. So why not treat yourself to a little surprise each month and let a personal stylist curate your wardrobe?

The Role of Feedback in Continuous Project Improvement


Project success and failure are often driven by a single factor: the ability to adapt to change and the continuous improvement process. At the center of this process lies a crucial element – feedback. This intangible entity holds significant power to either stabilize or propel a project forward. In this article, we'll explore the intricate mechanisms of feedback, discussing its types, the impact it has on project outcome, and ultimately highlighting the mechanisms to utilize feedback optimally for continuous project improvement.

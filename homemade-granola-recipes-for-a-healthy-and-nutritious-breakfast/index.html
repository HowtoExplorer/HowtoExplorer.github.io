Homemade Granola Recipes for a Healthy and Nutritious Breakfast


Granola is a versatile and nutritious breakfast option that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Whether you prefer it with yogurt, milk, or as a topping for smoothie bowls, homemade granola allows you to control the ingredients and customize the flavors to suit your taste. In this blog post, we will share three delicious and easy homemade granola recipes that will kickstart your day with a healthy and nutritious breakfast.

Recipe 1: Classic Honey and Almond Granola

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raw almonds, chopped
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the rolled oats and chopped almonds.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the honey, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until well combined.
  4. Pour the honey mixture over the oats and almonds, and stir until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Spread the granola mixture onto the prepared baking sheet in an even layer.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the granola is golden brown and fragrant.
  7. Allow the granola to cool completely before storing it in an airtight container.

Recipe 2: Chocolate and Peanut Butter Granola

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsalted peanuts
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the rolled oats and peanuts.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, honey, peanut butter, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
  4. Pour the cocoa mixture over the oats and peanuts, and stir until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Spread the granola mixture onto the prepared baking sheet in an even layer.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the granola is crisp and fragrant.
  7. Allow the granola to cool completely before storing it in an airtight container.

Recipe 3: Tropical Coconut and Pineapple Granola

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 1/2 cup dried pineapple, chopped
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the rolled oats, coconut flakes, and dried pineapple.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the honey, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
  4. Pour the honey mixture over the oats, coconut flakes, and dried pineapple, and stir until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Spread the granola mixture onto the prepared baking sheet in an even layer.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the granola is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Allow the granola to cool completely before storing it in an airtight container.

Enjoy a Nutritious Breakfast with Homemade Granola

Now that you have three delicious homemade granola recipes at your fingertips, you can easily incorporate a nutritious breakfast into your daily routine. The great thing about these recipes is that you can experiment with different ingredients and flavors to create your own unique combinations. Whether you prefer the classic honey and almond, the indulgent chocolate and peanut butter, or the tropical coconut and pineapple, homemade granola is a versatile and healthy option that will keep you satisfied until lunchtime.

So, why settle for store-bought granola when you can make your own? Give these recipes a try and start your day with a delicious and nutritious breakfast that will fuel you for the day ahead. Bon appétit!

Note: Remember to adjust the quantities and baking times according to your preferences and oven. Feel free to add or substitute ingredients based on your dietary needs or personal taste.

The Top Cybersecurity Podcasts You Need to Listen to


In today’s digital age, cybersecurity is more important than ever. Whether you’re a cybersecurity professional, a tech enthusiast, or simply someone who wants to stay informed about online security, podcasts can be a valuable source of information and insight. With that in mind, we’ve compiled a list of the top cybersecurity podcasts that you need to add to your listening lineup.

Darknet Diaries

Hosted by Jack Rhysider, Darknet Diaries is a captivating podcast that delves into the darker side of the internet. Each episode explores real-life cybercrime stories, hacking incidents, and cybersecurity breaches. Rhysider’s storytelling abilities and in-depth research make this podcast a must-listen for anyone interested in the world of cybersecurity.

CyberWire

If you’re looking for a comprehensive overview of the latest cybersecurity news, CyberWire is the podcast for you. With daily episodes that cover industry developments, threat intelligence, and interviews with cybersecurity experts, this podcast keeps you up to date with the rapidly evolving cybersecurity landscape.

Security Now

Hosted by tech guru Steve Gibson and cybersecurity expert Leo Laporte, Security Now provides a deep dive into the technical aspects of cybersecurity. The hosts discuss vulnerabilities, security protocols, and emerging threats, offering valuable insights for both beginners and seasoned professionals in the field.

Risky Business

Risky Business is a podcast that combines cybersecurity news, analysis, and interviews with a healthy dose of humor. Hosted by Patrick Gray, this podcast offers a lighter take on the often serious subject of cybersecurity, making it an enjoyable and informative listen for a wide audience.

Smashing Security

If you prefer a more lighthearted approach to cybersecurity, Smashing Security is the podcast for you. Hosted by veteran security experts Graham Cluley and Carole Theriault, this podcast covers cybersecurity news and developments with a humorous twist. The hosts also address common security myths and offer practical tips for staying safe online.

Recorded Future

Recorded Future is a podcast that focuses on threat intelligence and cybersecurity analysis. With episodes that feature discussions on emerging threats, cyber espionage, and security research, this podcast provides valuable insights for cybersecurity professionals and enthusiasts alike.

The Privacy, Security, & OSINT Show

For those interested in online privacy and open-source intelligence, The Privacy, Security, & OSINT Show is an invaluable resource. Hosted by Michael Bazzell, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, including digital privacy tools, investigative techniques, and cybersecurity best practices.

Conclusion

With the ever-present threat of cyber attacks and data breaches, staying informed about cybersecurity is crucial. These top cybersecurity podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge, insights, and entertainment for anyone interested in the world of digital security. Whether you’re a seasoned cybersecurity professional or a curious listener, adding these podcasts to your playlist will provide you with a deeper understanding of the evolving cyber landscape and help you stay one step ahead of potential threats. So, tune in, stay informed, and enhance your cybersecurity knowledge with these top-notch podcasts.

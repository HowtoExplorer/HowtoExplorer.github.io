10 Delicious Recipes for Your Instant Pot


The Instant Pot has revolutionized the way we cook, making meal preparation quicker and more convenient without sacrificing flavor. With its multifunctionality, you can pressure cook, slow cook, sauté, steam, and even make yogurt. Here, we present ten delicious recipes that will not only satisfy your taste buds but also make your cooking effortless. Let’s dive into these mouthwatering dishes that are perfect for any occasion!

1. Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics: Turn on the Instant Pot and select the ‘Sauté’ function. Add a splash of oil and sauté the onion until translucent. Add garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute.
  2. Add Chicken and Spices: Stir in the chicken pieces along with garam masala, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes until the chicken is lightly browned.
  3. Pressure Cook: Pour in the crushed tomatoes and coconut milk. Seal the lid and set the pot to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 10 minutes.
  4. Release and Serve: Allow for a natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

2. Instant Pot Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 4 carrots, chopped
  • 3 potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Brown the Beef: Use the ‘Sauté’ function to brown the beef cubes in batches. Remove and set aside.
  2. Sauté Vegetables: Add onion to the pot and sauté until soft. Stir in tomato paste and cook for one minute.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Return the beef to the pot, and add carrots, potatoes, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pressure Cook: Secure the lid and set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 35 minutes. Allow for a natural release.
  5. Serve: Stir well and adjust seasoning as needed before serving.

3. Instant Pot Quinoa and Black Beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lime for serving
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Combine Ingredients: In the Instant Pot, combine quinoa, black beans, vegetable broth, bell pepper, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Pressure Cook: Seal the lid and set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 1 minute. Allow for a natural release.
  3. Fluff and Serve: Fluff with a fork, serve with lime wedges and garnish with cilantro.

4. Instant Pot Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (14 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Onions and Garlic: Use the ‘Sauté’ function to cook onions until soft, then add garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Add Tomatoes and Broth: Stir in crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, and sugar. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Pressure Cook: Seal the lid and set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 10 minutes. Allow for a quick release.
  4. Blend and Serve: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup. Stir in heavy cream before serving, garnished with fresh basil.

5. Instant Pot Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook Pasta: Add macaroni and water to the Instant Pot. Seal the lid and set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 4 minutes. Quick release the pressure.
  2. Make Cheese Sauce: Stir in butter, cheese, and milk. Mix until creamy, adding salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Serve: Enjoy your creamy macaroni and cheese hot from the pot!

6. Instant Pot Vegetable Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Onions and Garlic: Using the ‘Sauté’ function, heat olive oil and sauté onion until soft. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Add Rice and Broth: Stir in Arborio rice and cook for 2 minutes. Add vegetable broth and seal the lid.
  3. Pressure Cook: Set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 6 minutes. Allow for a natural release.
  4. Finish Risotto: Stir in peas and parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper before serving.

7. Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Sauté Chicken: Set the Instant Pot to ‘Sauté’ and add olive oil. Brown chicken breasts on both sides, then remove.
  2. Make Sauce: Add garlic, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  3. Cook Chicken: Return chicken to the pot, seal the lid, and set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 10 minutes. Quick release the pressure.
  4. Serve: Enjoy this flavorful chicken with a side of vegetables or rice.

8. Instant Pot Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Brown Meat: Use the ‘Sauté’ function to cook the ground meat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add Vegetables and Spices: Stir in onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook for 3 minutes until onion is soft.
  3. Combine Remaining Ingredients: Add beans and diced tomatoes. Seal the lid and set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve: Allow for a natural release. Serve with your favorite toppings.

9. Instant Pot Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Apples: Place sliced apples in the bottom of the Instant Pot. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  2. Mix Topping: In a bowl, combine oats, brown sugar, flour, and melted butter until crumbly. Spread over the apples.
  3. Add Water and Cook: Pour water into the pot, seal the lid, and set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 8 minutes.
  4. Serve: Quick release the pressure and serve warm, ideally with ice cream.

10. Instant Pot Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup pesto
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook Pasta: Add pasta and water to the Instant Pot. Seal the lid and set to ‘Pressure Cook’ for 4 minutes. Quick release the pressure.
  2. Combine with Pesto: Stir in pesto and parmesan cheese. Mix well until the pasta is coated.
  3. Serve: Enjoy your flavorful pasta dish with a sprinkle of extra parmesan.

Conclusion

These ten delicious recipes highlight the versatility and convenience of the Instant Pot, allowing you to whip up hearty meals with minimal effort. From comforting soups to savory stews, each recipe offers a unique flavor experience. Experiment with these dishes, and soon you’ll find your favorites while enjoying the ease of cooking with your Instant Pot!

Satisfying and Healthy Mediterranean Wrap Recipes for Lunch


When it comes to a satisfying and healthy lunch option, Mediterranean wraps are a perfect choice. These flavorful wraps are not only delicious but also packed with nutritious ingredients that will keep you feeling energized throughout the day. In this blog post, we will explore some mouthwatering Mediterranean wrap recipes that are easy to make and will surely satisfy your taste buds.

1. Greek Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat wrap
  • 4 ounces grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Lay the whole wheat wrap flat on a clean surface.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the Greek yogurt, fresh dill, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the Greek yogurt mixture evenly on the wrap.
  4. Layer the grilled chicken breast, diced tomatoes, sliced cucumber, and crumbled feta cheese on top of the Greek yogurt mixture.
  5. Roll the wrap tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Slice the wrap in half and serve.

2. Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat wrap
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread the hummus evenly on the whole wheat wrap.
  2. Layer the sliced bell peppers, red onion, black olives, crumbled feta cheese, and fresh parsley on top of the hummus.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly, ensuring all the ingredients are enclosed.
  5. Slice the wrap in half and enjoy.

3. Falafel Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole wheat wrap
  • 4 falafel balls
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumbers
  • 2 tablespoons tahini sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Warm the falafel balls according to the package instructions.
  2. Lay the whole wheat wrap flat on a clean surface.
  3. Spread the tahini sauce evenly on the wrap.
  4. Place the falafel balls, diced tomatoes, diced cucumbers, and fresh mint on top of the tahini sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Roll the wrap tightly, securing all the ingredients inside.
  7. Slice the wrap in half and serve.

Conclusion

Mediterranean wraps offer a delicious and healthy option for a satisfying lunch. These recipes are versatile and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. Whether you’re a fan of grilled chicken, fresh veggies, or flavorful falafel, there is a Mediterranean wrap recipe for everyone. So, next time you’re looking for a lunch idea that is both satisfying and nutritious, give these Mediterranean wraps a try. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!

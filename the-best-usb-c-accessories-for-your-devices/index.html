The Best USB-C Accessories for Your Devices


USB-C has quickly become the standard for most modern devices, from smartphones and laptops to tablets and gaming consoles. With its versatility, fast charging capabilities, and high-speed data transfer, it’s no wonder that USB-C ports are everywhere today. But with so many accessories available, it can be difficult to choose the right ones for your needs. In this guide, we’ll explore the best USB-C accessories that can enhance your devices, from chargers to hubs, cables, adapters, and more.

1. USB-C Chargers: Fast and Reliable Power Delivery

The first accessory that every USB-C user should consider is a high-quality USB-C charger. USB-C chargers have revolutionized the way we power our devices by offering faster charging speeds and more efficient power delivery. Here are some options to consider when shopping for a USB-C charger:

Anker PowerPort Atom PD 2

Anker is known for producing reliable charging accessories, and the PowerPort Atom PD 2 is no exception. This charger offers a 60W output, which is more than enough to charge laptops, tablets, and smartphones quickly. Its compact size makes it perfect for travelers who need to carry an efficient yet powerful charger without taking up too much space.

RAVPower 61W PD Charger

RAVPower’s 61W PD Charger is another excellent option, featuring an ultra-compact design and a foldable plug for easy storage. It supports Power Delivery (PD) 3.0 technology, ensuring that your devices charge as quickly as possible. The 61W output is ideal for charging laptops, tablets, and other USB-C devices.

Aukey Omnia 65W PD Charger

Aukey’s Omnia 65W charger packs a lot of power in a small package. This charger supports both Power Delivery and Quick Charge technology, making it versatile enough to handle a variety of devices. It’s perfect for those who need to power up their laptop, phone, and other USB-C devices in one go.

2. USB-C Hubs: Expanding Connectivity

One of the key advantages of USB-C is its ability to support various types of connections in a single port. However, many devices come with only a few USB-C ports, so a USB-C hub can expand your connectivity options. Whether you’re looking to add more USB ports, HDMI outputs, or SD card readers, a USB-C hub can enhance the versatility of your device.

Sabrent USB-C 4-Port Hub

The Sabrent USB-C 4-Port Hub is perfect for anyone who needs extra USB-A ports. This compact hub connects to your device via USB-C and gives you four additional USB 3.0 ports for connecting peripherals like a mouse, keyboard, flash drive, or external hard drive. It’s a great way to maximize your device’s port options without having to worry about carrying bulky adapters.

Anker PowerExpand+ 7-in-1 USB-C Hub

If you’re looking for a more robust USB-C hub, the Anker PowerExpand+ 7-in-1 USB-C Hub offers everything you need to expand your device’s connectivity. This hub includes HDMI support for external displays, SD and microSD card readers, multiple USB-A ports, and an Ethernet port. It’s a great all-in-one solution for users who need to connect multiple devices at once.

HyperDrive 6-in-1 USB-C Hub

The HyperDrive 6-in-1 USB-C Hub is a sleek, ultra-portable hub that includes a variety of ports, including an HDMI port for 4K video output, USB-A ports, and an SD card reader. It’s perfect for on-the-go professionals who need to connect to external monitors, cameras, and other devices.

3. USB-C Cables: Essential for Data and Charging

USB-C cables are essential for transferring data and charging devices, but not all cables are created equal. Depending on your device and needs, it’s important to choose a high-quality cable that supports the right data transfer speeds and charging capabilities. Here are a few top options for USB-C cables:

Anker PowerLine II USB-C to USB-C Cable

Anker’s PowerLine II USB-C to USB-C Cable is a durable, high-speed cable that supports 60W power delivery for fast charging. It’s built to last with reinforced construction, ensuring that the cable won’t fray or break easily. Whether you’re charging your phone or transferring data between devices, this cable is an excellent choice.

Aukey USB-C to USB-C Cable

Aukey’s USB-C to USB-C cable supports fast charging and data transfer speeds of up to 5Gbps. It’s perfect for charging smartphones, tablets, and laptops, and it offers a tangle-free design that makes it easy to store and use. Aukey is known for producing reliable, cost-effective cables that get the job done without breaking the bank.

Belkin USB-C to USB-A Cable

For those who need to connect USB-C devices to older USB-A ports, the Belkin USB-C to USB-A cable is an ideal solution. This cable supports fast data transfer speeds and charging capabilities, ensuring that you don’t lose performance when switching between different types of ports.

4. USB-C Adapters: Versatility for Older Devices

While USB-C is becoming the new standard, many devices still use older connectors like USB-A, HDMI, or VGA. USB-C adapters can bridge the gap, allowing you to connect your modern USB-C device to older hardware or accessories.

UGREEN USB-C to HDMI Adapter

For users who need to connect their USB-C laptop or tablet to an HDMI-enabled display, the UGREEN USB-C to HDMI adapter is a great option. It supports 4K resolution and ensures that your video output is sharp and clear. Whether you’re giving a presentation or streaming content, this adapter allows for easy HDMI connectivity.

Anker USB-C to Ethernet Adapter

For users who prefer a wired internet connection, the Anker USB-C to Ethernet Adapter is a must-have accessory. It offers a reliable, fast, and stable Ethernet connection, making it ideal for work environments where Wi-Fi may not be sufficient or reliable. It’s also great for gamers who need the low-latency performance that only a wired connection can provide.

Cable Matters USB-C to VGA Adapter

If you’re using older monitors or projectors that still rely on VGA, the Cable Matters USB-C to VGA Adapter can save the day. This adapter supports resolutions up to 1080p, allowing you to connect your USB-C device to VGA-equipped displays. It’s compact, lightweight, and perfect for professionals on the go.

5. USB-C External Drives: Portable Storage Solutions

External drives are essential for expanding your storage capacity, and many modern external drives now support USB-C for faster data transfer speeds. Whether you’re backing up important files, transferring large media files, or storing games, a USB-C external drive is a convenient solution.

Samsung T7 Portable SSD

The Samsung T7 Portable SSD offers ultra-fast read and write speeds, making it perfect for transferring large files or running applications directly from the drive. It supports USB 3.2 Gen 2 for speeds up to 1,050MB/s, which is ideal for users who need to move data quickly and efficiently. With its sleek design and durability, it’s a great option for both personal and professional use.

SanDisk Extreme Portable SSD

SanDisk’s Extreme Portable SSD is another excellent external storage option that supports USB-C for fast data transfer speeds. It’s shock-resistant, making it perfect for on-the-go use, and offers up to 1TB of storage, which is more than enough for most users. Whether you’re using it for media storage, backups, or file sharing, this portable SSD is a reliable and high-performance choice.

Seagate One Touch USB-C External Hard Drive

For users who need more traditional hard drive storage, the Seagate One Touch USB-C External Hard Drive offers up to 5TB of space. It provides reliable and fast storage with USB-C connectivity, making it ideal for storing large amounts of data. Its compact design makes it easy to take with you, whether you’re traveling or working from home.

6. USB-C Docking Stations: All-in-One Solutions

If you’re using a laptop as your primary device, a USB-C docking station can provide a full desktop experience. These docking stations allow you to connect multiple monitors, external drives, and other peripherals to your laptop with a single cable. Here are some of the best USB-C docking stations to consider:

CalDigit TS3 Plus Thunderbolt 3 Dock

The CalDigit TS3 Plus Thunderbolt 3 Dock is one of the most powerful docking stations available. It offers a wide array of ports, including USB-A, USB-C, Ethernet, DisplayPort, and SD card readers, making it perfect for users who need to connect multiple devices at once. It also supports dual 4K displays, making it ideal for professionals who work with multiple monitors.

Plugable USB-C Triple Display Docking Station

If you need to connect three external monitors to your laptop, the Plugable USB-C Triple Display Docking Station is a great option. It supports up to three displays at 1080p resolution and offers plenty of USB ports for your peripherals. It’s an excellent solution for users who need extensive connectivity and multitasking capabilities.

Kensington SD5700T Thunderbolt 4 Docking Station

For users who need a more future-proof docking solution, the Kensington SD5700T Thunderbolt 4 Docking Station supports Thunderbolt 4 for faster data transfer speeds and higher display resolutions. It supports dual 4K or single 8K displays and offers multiple USB-A and USB-C ports for a variety of peripherals. It’s a great option for high-end laptops and workstations.

Conclusion: Enhancing Your Devices with the Best USB-C Accessories

USB-C accessories have made it easier to power, connect, and expand the functionality of modern devices. Whether you need to charge your laptop quickly, connect to external displays, or store large amounts of data, there’s a USB-C accessory that can meet your needs. By investing in high-quality accessories, you can optimize your device usage and ensure that you’re ready for anything that comes your way.

From chargers and hubs to adapters and docking stations, these accessories will help you get the most out of your devices. As USB-C continues to evolve, we can expect even more innovative accessories that will further enhance our tech experience.

Delicious Recipes for Vegan Pies


If you’re a fan of pies but follow a vegan diet, you might think that your options are limited. However, there are plenty of creative and delicious ways to make vegan pies that will satisfy your cravings and delight your taste buds. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan baker or just dipping your toes into the world of plant-based desserts, these recipes are sure to impress your friends and family. From classic fruit-filled pies to rich and creamy creations, there’s something for every pie lover in this collection.

1. Classic Vegan Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 6-7 medium apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 pre-made vegan pie crusts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, brown sugar, lemon juice, cornstarch, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.
  3. Line a pie dish with one of the pie crusts. Pour the apple filling into the crust.
  4. Cover the pie with the second crust, crimping the edges to seal. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  5. Bake the pie for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  6. Allow the pie to cool before serving.

2. Silky Vegan Chocolate Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews, soaked in water for at least 4 hours
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pre-made vegan graham cracker crust

Instructions:

  1. Drain the soaked cashews and place them in a blender with the coconut cream, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the chocolate filling into the graham cracker crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  3. Refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or until set.
  4. Serve chilled, optionally garnished with vegan whipped cream and chocolate shavings.

3. Zesty Vegan Key Lime Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews, soaked in water for at least 4 hours
  • 3/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 3/4 cup agave nectar
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1 pre-made vegan graham cracker crust

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the soaked cashews, lime juice, lime zest, coconut cream, agave nectar, and melted coconut oil. Blend until silky smooth.
  2. Pour the lime filling into the graham cracker crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  3. Refrigerate the pie for at least 6 hours, or until set.
  4. Garnish with additional lime zest before serving.

Conclusion

These vegan pie recipes prove that plant-based desserts can be just as indulgent and satisfying as their traditional counterparts. Whether you’re craving the comforting aroma of a freshly baked apple pie, the rich decadence of a chocolate pie, or the refreshing tang of a key lime pie, these recipes offer a delightful array of flavors and textures. Experiment with different fruits, spices, and crust variations to make these recipes your own. So, roll up your sleeves, preheat the oven, and get ready to create some unforgettable vegan pies that will have everyone asking for seconds!

Sizzling Summer Seafood BBQ Recipes


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the barbecue and enjoy the delicious flavors of seafood. Whether you’re hosting a backyard gathering or simply craving a taste of the ocean, seafood BBQ recipes are a fantastic way to elevate your outdoor cooking experience. From succulent shrimp skewers to flavorful grilled fish, there’s a wide array of mouthwatering dishes to explore. In this blog post, we’ll dive into some tantalizing seafood BBQ recipes that are sure to impress your guests and tantalize your taste buds.

Grilled Garlic Butter Lobster Tails

Ingredients:

  • 4 lobster tails
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Using kitchen shears, carefully cut the top of the lobster shell down to the tail.
  3. Gently pull the shell apart and lift the meat, resting it on top of the shell.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Brush the garlic butter mixture over the lobster meat.
  6. Place the lobster tails on the grill, shell side down, and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the meat is opaque and slightly charred.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

These succulent grilled garlic butter lobster tails are a showstopper at any summer BBQ. The rich, buttery flavor combined with the sweet, tender lobster meat creates a truly indulgent dish that will leave your guests craving more.

Citrus Grilled Shrimp Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon zest, lime zest, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat evenly. Marinate for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  4. Thread the marinated shrimp onto the soaked skewers.
  5. Grill the shrimp skewers for 2-3 minutes per side, or until they are pink and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the citrus grilled shrimp skewers with a squeeze of fresh lemon or lime juice.

These vibrant and zesty citrus grilled shrimp skewers are a refreshing addition to any summer BBQ spread. The bright flavors of the citrus marinade perfectly complement the sweet, succulent shrimp, creating a dish that’s bursting with freshness.

Smoky Grilled Salmon with Dill Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Dill Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Brush the mixture over the salmon fillets.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on the grill, skin side down, and cook for 6-8 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the Greek yogurt, fresh dill, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to create the dill sauce.
  5. Serve the grilled salmon with a dollop of the dill sauce and a sprinkle of fresh dill.

This smoky grilled salmon with dill sauce is a delightful dish that captures the essence of summer. The robust, smoky flavor of the salmon pairs beautifully with the creamy, herbaceous dill sauce, creating a culinary masterpiece that’s sure to be a hit at your BBQ gathering.

Conclusion

Elevate your summer BBQ game with these sensational seafood recipes that are bursting with flavor and freshness. Whether you’re a seafood enthusiast or looking to impress your guests, these sizzling seafood BBQ recipes are guaranteed to take your outdoor cooking experience to the next level. So, fire up the grill, embrace the sunshine, and savor the delightful flavors of the ocean with these delectable dishes. Cheers to a summer filled with unforgettable seafood feasts!

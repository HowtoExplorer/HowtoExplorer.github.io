The Best Online Courses for Public Speaking Skills


Public speaking is one of the most valuable skills anyone can develop. Whether you’re presenting at work, speaking at an event, or communicating with a group of friends, being able to express yourself clearly and confidently can make a world of difference. Fortunately, there are numerous online courses available to help individuals improve their public speaking abilities, from beginners to seasoned professionals looking to refine their skills. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best online courses for enhancing your public speaking.

1. Dale Carnegie’s Public Speaking Course

Dale Carnegie is a name that resonates with leadership and communication skills, and their Public Speaking course is no exception. This program has been a trusted resource for many years, offering practical techniques and exercises to help individuals overcome stage fright and master public speaking.

Key Features:

  • Confidence Building: The course focuses on eliminating nervousness, enabling you to speak with assurance and poise.
  • Audience Engagement: Learn how to connect with your audience and keep them engaged throughout your presentation.
  • Real-World Application: Participants practice their speaking skills in front of others to gain constructive feedback.

Why It’s Great:

Dale Carnegie’s program is ideal for anyone looking to build long-term confidence in speaking engagements. The course is highly interactive, allowing participants to practice and apply the techniques they learn in real-time.

2. Toastmasters International – Pathways Program

Toastmasters International has long been a leader in the world of public speaking. Their Pathways program offers various tracks that cater to different speaking needs, such as leadership, persuasive speaking, and public speaking. The course is designed to develop both verbal and non-verbal communication skills, making it a comprehensive option for anyone seeking to enhance their speaking abilities.

Key Features:

  • Customizable Learning Paths: Toastmasters offers a range of learning paths tailored to specific goals, including dynamic leadership and engaging humor.
  • Global Community: Join a supportive network of public speakers from around the world, receiving feedback and tips from experienced members.
  • Ongoing Evaluation: Regular evaluations help you track your progress and make necessary adjustments to your speaking style.

Why It’s Great:

Toastmasters stands out due to its peer-based feedback system. You get direct feedback from other members, allowing you to refine your skills in a supportive environment.

3. Udemy: Complete Public Speaking Masterclass for Every Occasion

Udemy is known for offering a variety of online courses, and its public speaking masterclass is one of the most popular. This comprehensive course covers everything from overcoming fear to mastering body language, tone, and vocal variety.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Covers everything from the basics to advanced techniques, ensuring it’s suitable for all experience levels.
  • Video Lessons: Learn through pre-recorded lessons, which you can revisit as needed.
  • Practical Exercises: Gain hands-on experience through assignments that help you improve your delivery.

Why It’s Great:

Udemy’s course is great for people who want a self-paced, flexible approach. The course content is well-structured, and the video format allows you to review the material at your convenience.

4. Coursera: Dynamic Public Speaking by the University of Washington

Coursera offers a top-tier course on dynamic public speaking from the University of Washington. This program teaches participants how to craft persuasive speeches, speak with clarity, and connect with an audience. It’s designed to help learners improve their speaking skills in a wide variety of settings, whether in front of large audiences or in small team meetings.

Key Features:

  • Expert Instruction: The course is taught by experienced university professors who bring a wealth of knowledge to the table.
  • Peer Interaction: Coursera’s platform allows you to interact with peers, receive feedback, and gain new insights from fellow learners.
  • Certificate: Upon completion, you’ll receive a certificate that you can add to your resume or LinkedIn profile, providing tangible proof of your skills.

Why It’s Great:

Coursera’s Dynamic Public Speaking course is perfect for those who want a formal, structured learning experience. The university-level instruction ensures you gain comprehensive knowledge of public speaking.

5. LinkedIn Learning: Public Speaking Foundations

LinkedIn Learning offers a course that focuses on the foundations of public speaking, with a concentration on practical techniques that can be used immediately. It’s an excellent choice for professionals who want to polish their presentation skills for the workplace.

Key Features:

  • Concise Modules: Each module is short and focused, making it easy to fit into your busy schedule.
  • Expert Instructors: Learn from experienced professionals who share industry-specific tips and strategies.
  • Wide Variety of Topics: The course covers topics such as voice control, body language, and engaging an audience.

Why It’s Great:

For professionals looking to improve their speaking skills quickly, LinkedIn Learning’s course offers a perfect balance between depth and brevity. The platform’s flexibility allows you to access lessons anytime, and the short videos make it easy to learn in a fragmented schedule.

6. MasterClass: Robin Roberts Teaches Effective Storytelling

MasterClass offers a unique opportunity to learn public speaking through storytelling with Robin Roberts, one of America’s most respected broadcasters. In this course, Roberts teaches you how to connect with your audience through powerful storytelling techniques, giving you the tools to craft memorable, impactful speeches.

Key Features:

  • Focus on Storytelling: Learn the art of storytelling to keep your audience engaged.
  • Practical Advice: Gain insights on how to remain authentic and compelling while speaking.
  • Celebrity Instructor: Learn from a highly respected figure in the world of public speaking and media.

Why It’s Great:

MasterClass takes public speaking to the next level by emphasizing storytelling. This approach works well for individuals who want to captivate their audiences with personal narratives or powerful anecdotes.

7. The Great Courses: Public Speaking: How to Give a Great Speech

The Great Courses offers an extensive public speaking course that provides a step-by-step guide on how to deliver great speeches. Taught by expert instructor Dr. John R. Gallagher, this course is perfect for those who want a deep dive into speech crafting and delivery techniques.

Key Features:

  • Structured Learning: The course is broken down into 24 lessons, each focused on a specific aspect of public speaking.
  • Actionable Techniques: Learn practical methods that you can use right away to improve your speaking skills.
  • Expert Advice: Dr. Gallagher’s years of experience as a public speaking coach shine through in the lessons.

Why It’s Great:

If you’re someone who thrives in a more formal, academic setting, The Great Courses’ public speaking course offers a thorough and structured approach to learning. The detailed instruction ensures you understand the nuances of speech delivery.

8. Skillshare: Public Speaking for Beginners

Skillshare offers a fantastic public speaking course aimed at beginners. Whether you’re looking to build confidence or need guidance on how to deliver a presentation, this course offers a solid foundation for anyone new to public speaking.

Key Features:

  • Beginner-Friendly: Focuses on building confidence, structuring speeches, and delivering them effectively.
  • Project-Based Learning: Apply what you learn by completing hands-on projects, which will help reinforce the skills you acquire.
  • Engaging Community: Interact with other learners to exchange ideas and receive constructive feedback.

Why It’s Great:

Skillshare’s public speaking course is ideal for beginners who want to start at the very basics and build their skills gradually. The platform’s community-driven approach ensures you can learn at your own pace and get feedback from fellow students.

9. Public Speaking Academy: The Ultimate Public Speaking Course

The Public Speaking Academy offers a complete package for those serious about mastering public speaking. This online course is comprehensive and covers all the essential skills you need to become a confident speaker, whether you’re presenting in small meetings or delivering speeches to large audiences.

Key Features:

  • Step-by-Step Approach: The course starts with the basics and builds up to advanced techniques, making it suitable for beginners and more experienced speakers alike.
  • Personalized Coaching: Receive one-on-one coaching sessions to refine your presentation style.
  • Live Workshops: Participate in live workshops to practice your skills in a real-world environment.

Why It’s Great:

If you’re looking for a complete, personalized approach to learning public speaking, the Public Speaking Academy’s course offers a highly tailored experience that includes live workshops and one-on-one coaching.

10. Speak Like a Leader by Mawi Learning

Mawi Learning offers a course aimed at developing leadership communication skills, including public speaking. This course is designed for those who wish to enhance their speaking abilities in a professional context, specifically for leaders and executives.

Key Features:

  • Leadership Focus: The course emphasizes communication strategies that leaders use to inspire and motivate their teams.
  • Practical Techniques: Learn how to craft messages that resonate with audiences and deliver them with confidence.
  • Professional Growth: Gain tools that will help you enhance your public speaking and grow your leadership potential.

Why It’s Great:

For individuals looking to hone their public speaking in a leadership context, this course provides practical tools for communicating effectively in a professional setting.

Conclusion

Improving your public speaking skills can be a game-changer in both your personal and professional life. Whether you’re looking to build confidence, refine your delivery, or learn specific techniques, there’s an online course to fit your needs. From beginner courses to advanced masterclasses, these programs offer valuable resources to help you grow as a public speaker. Take the time to explore the options available and choose the course that best aligns with your goals and speaking style. With dedication and practice, you’ll be well on your way to becoming a more confident and effective communicator.

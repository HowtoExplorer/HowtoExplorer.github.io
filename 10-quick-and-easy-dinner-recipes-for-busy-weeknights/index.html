10 Quick and Easy Dinner Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Looking for ways to save time yet satisfy your taste buds during a busy weeknight? Look no further! These fast and satisfying dinner recipes will have you out of the kitchen and enjoying a delicious meal in no time. Who needs takeout or fast food when you can have a quality home-cooked meal?

Recipe #1: One-Pot Chicken Fried Rice

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 20 minutes Serves: 4

Why go out when you can make your own chicken fried rice right at home? This easy dinner recipe calls for ingredients you already have.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked, diced chicken
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • Vegetable stir-in options: peas, carrots, bell peppers

Directions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken, followed by the vegetables of your choice.
  4. Stir in the rice and beaten eggs.
  5. Drizzle with soy sauce and cook until heated through.

Recipe #2: Sheet Pan Lemon Herb Chicken

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 25 minutes Serves: 4

Make a healthy, delicious chicken dinner with minimal effort using a sheet pan.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • Mixed herbs: rosemary, thyme, parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (204°C).
  2. Arrange the chicken on a sheet pan and season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon slices.
  4. Roast in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until the chicken is browned and cooked through.

Recipe #3: Classic Baked Spaghetti

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 30 minutes Serves: 4

Instead of lasagna, try this equally satisfying baked spaghetti dish for a family-friendly meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente; drain.
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground beef with the onion and garlic. Add the tomato paste and marinara sauce.
  3. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the meat sauce.
  4. Pour the mixture into a baking dish and top with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Recipe #4: One-Pot Creamy Tomato Zucchini Pasta

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 25 minutes Serves: 4

Quickly whip up this creamy tomato zucchini pasta for a satisfying one-pot meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound zoodles (sliced zucchini)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 28-ounce canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper

Directions:

  1. Cook the zoodles in boiling water for 1-2 minutes; drain.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and heavy cream; simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Return the zoodles to the pot and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook until heated through.

Recipe #5: Easy Taco Bowl

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 0 minutes Serves: 4

Set up a build-your-own taco bowl bar for a fun and effortless family dinner.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef or turkey
  • Taco seasoning
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, avocado slices, hot sauce, chopped onions

Directions:

  1. Cook and season the ground meat with taco seasoning.
  2. Set up a serving bar with the desired toppings.
  3. Dish up rice or lettuce bowls and top with taco meat and desired toppings.

Recipe #6: Thirty-Minute Honey Garlic Shrimp

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 20 minutes Serves: 4

Enjoy restaurant-quality honey garlic shrimp at home in half an hour.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.
  2. Marinate the shrimp in this mixture for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; add the garlic.
  4. Add the shrimp and marinade; cook until pink and cooked through.
  5. Garnish with green onions.

Recipe #7: Skillet Chicken and Seasoned Rice

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 30 minutes Serves: 4

Make a complete meal using just one skillet.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • Mixed seasonings: paprika, basil, garlic powder, salt, pepper
  • Cooked rice
  • Salsa, for serving

Directions:

  1. Season the chicken with your choice of seasonings.
  2. In a hot skillet, cook the chicken until browned and cooked through.
  3. Serve chicken over seasoned rice and top with salsa.

Recipe #8: Sheet Pan Lemon Butter Salmon

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 25 minutes Serves: 4

Upgrade your salmon dinner with lemon butter flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (204°C).
  2. Mix together the melted butter, lemon juice, minced garlic, and seasoning to create a lemon butter sauce.
  3. Drizzle the sauce over the salmon fillets.
  4. Place the salmon on a sheet pan and bake for 25 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.

Recipe #9: Hearty Vegetarian Chili

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 30 minutes Serves: 4

Warm up with a hearty vegetarian chili that even meat eaters will enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 cups kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper

Directions:

  1. Sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the kidney beans, crushed tomatoes, chili powder, and cumin.
  3. Simmer for 30 minutes.

Recipe #10: Chicken Stir-Fry with Peanut Sauce

Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 20 minutes Serves: 4

Serve a tropical flavor experience with this chicken stir-fry dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • PB2 or powdered peanut butter (optional)
  • Brown sugar (optional)

Directions:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook the chicken until browned.
  2. Add the onion and garlic; cook until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the broccoli and cook until tender.
  4. In a separate pan, heat the peanut butter in the chicken broth or with the powdered peanut butter and brown sugar.
  5. Mix the peanut sauce with the chicken and vegetables.

Now, you have ten quick and easy dinner recipes to save you time and satisfy your taste buds on busy weeknights. Enjoy!

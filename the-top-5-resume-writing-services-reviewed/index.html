The Top 5 Resume Writing Services Reviewed


In today’s competitive job market, a well-crafted resume is the key to standing out from the crowd and securing your dream job. A resume isn’t just a document that lists your qualifications; it’s a marketing tool that needs to capture the attention of hiring managers and recruiters within seconds. That’s where professional resume writing services come in. These services can help you tailor your resume to highlight your strengths, experiences, and skills in the best possible way.

Choosing the right resume writing service, however, can be overwhelming due to the number of options available. In this post, we will review the top five resume writing services, covering their offerings, pricing, pros, cons, and who each service is best suited for. Let’s dive in.

1. TopResume

Overview
TopResume is one of the most popular resume writing services in the industry, known for its experienced team of writers who specialize in creating professional, customized resumes. With a team of expert writers and a straightforward, user-friendly process, TopResume offers personalized resume services to job seekers across a wide range of industries.

What They Offer
TopResume offers a range of services including resume writing, LinkedIn profile optimization, cover letters, and executive resume writing. Their process begins with a free resume review, where one of their experts will evaluate your current resume and provide feedback. Based on this assessment, you can choose from a variety of packages to get a resume that suits your needs. The company also offers a satisfaction guarantee, meaning that if you’re not happy with the final product, they’ll revise it for free.

Pricing

  • Entry-Level Resume: $149
  • Professional Resume: $219
  • Executive Resume: $349

Pros

  • Free resume review and feedback
  • Satisfaction guarantee with unlimited revisions
  • Expert writers with experience in various industries
  • Excellent customer service with quick responses

Cons

  • Higher pricing compared to some competitors
  • Turnaround time can take up to a week

Best For
TopResume is an excellent choice for job seekers at all career levels who want a comprehensive and professional resume crafted by experts. Their satisfaction guarantee and free review make it appealing for those who want to ensure the quality of their resume before committing.

2. ZipJob

Overview
ZipJob is another top-tier resume writing service that offers a unique approach to resume creation. They focus on optimizing resumes to pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), the software that many companies use to screen resumes before they reach a human recruiter. By using ATS-friendly strategies, ZipJob helps ensure that your resume is seen by hiring managers.

What They Offer
ZipJob offers resume writing services, ATS optimization, and LinkedIn profile makeovers. The service is particularly valuable for those whose resumes are not getting noticed due to ATS filters. ZipJob’s expert writers not only create resumes that are designed to beat ATS but also provide personalized feedback to job seekers on how to improve their resumes and job search strategies.

Pricing

  • Basic Resume: $139
  • Professional Resume: $229
  • Executive Resume: $349

Pros

  • Specializes in ATS optimization
  • Quick turnaround (usually within 5 business days)
  • Offers a free resume review with a detailed analysis
  • Provides job search tips and advice

Cons

  • More expensive than other services
  • Some customers report inconsistencies in quality based on the writer assigned

Best For
ZipJob is an ideal service for professionals in industries where ATS is frequently used, such as technology, finance, and healthcare. If you’re struggling to get your resume noticed due to ATS filters, ZipJob’s optimization expertise will give your resume a significant boost.

3. Resumake

Overview
Resumake is a great choice for job seekers who want a budget-friendly yet professional resume writing service. Known for its simplicity and ease of use, Resumake offers quick and affordable resume writing services for job seekers who need a polished resume without breaking the bank.

What They Offer
Resumake offers an online platform where you can easily build and download your resume. Their basic service is free, allowing users to create and customize resumes with various templates. For a more personalized experience, users can opt for paid packages that include professional writing assistance and resume optimization. The company’s services also extend to LinkedIn profile writing and cover letter writing.

Pricing

  • Basic Resume Template (Free)
  • Standard Resume (One-time payment of $99)
  • Premium Resume (One-time payment of $149)

Pros

  • Affordable pricing options
  • Simple, easy-to-use interface for building resumes
  • Great for entry-level professionals or anyone on a budget
  • Fast turnaround time

Cons

  • Limited customization options in free version
  • Not as in-depth as more expensive services
  • Limited support for complex career histories

Best For
Resumake is perfect for entry-level job seekers or those on a tight budget who need a simple, professional resume quickly. If you don’t need extensive customization or complex formatting, this service will provide a cost-effective and efficient solution.

4. ResumeWriters.com

Overview
ResumeWriters.com has built a solid reputation over the years for its experienced team of certified writers who specialize in resumes, cover letters, and LinkedIn profiles. The company offers tailored services to job seekers in a variety of industries, including technology, healthcare, marketing, and more. They are also known for their personalized customer service, providing clients with a clear understanding of their resume’s strengths and weaknesses.

What They Offer
In addition to resume writing, ResumeWriters.com offers career coaching, cover letter writing, and LinkedIn profile optimization. Their unique service offerings include specialized packages for military veterans transitioning to civilian careers, as well as executive-level resume writing. They also provide a satisfaction guarantee, ensuring that customers are happy with the final product.

Pricing

  • Entry-Level Resume: $170
  • Professional Resume: $250
  • Executive Resume: $375

Pros

  • Strong focus on customer service
  • Experienced writers with industry-specific expertise
  • Satisfaction guarantee with unlimited revisions
  • Offers career coaching and job search advice

Cons

  • Pricing can be high for entry-level services
  • Turnaround time can be longer than some competitors

Best For
ResumeWriters.com is ideal for job seekers at the executive or mid-career levels who need a highly customized, professionally written resume. Their specialty packages, like those for veterans or senior professionals, make them an excellent choice for specific career needs.

5. LiveCareer

Overview
LiveCareer is a comprehensive platform offering resume writing, cover letter writing, and interview preparation services. The platform also includes job search tools, career tests, and resume templates to help users create a polished and professional resume. LiveCareer has been in business for many years, and its reputation is built on a blend of high-quality writing services and innovative career tools.

What They Offer
LiveCareer offers a wide variety of resume-related services, including resume building tools, professional writing, and LinkedIn profile optimization. They also provide access to personalized career advice, including tips on salary negotiation and job interviews. One of the standout features of LiveCareer is its online resume builder, which allows users to easily create resumes by choosing from a variety of templates and formats.

Pricing

  • Resume Builder (Free trial available)
  • Professional Resume Writing: $179.95
  • LinkedIn Profile Optimization: $89.95

Pros

  • Wide range of career-related services
  • Easy-to-use resume builder
  • Offers a free trial with the resume builder
  • Provides additional career tools like job search advice and interview prep

Cons

  • The professional writing service is relatively expensive
  • Limited customization options compared to higher-end services

Best For
LiveCareer is perfect for job seekers who want a comprehensive set of tools for job searching and resume writing. If you prefer a do-it-yourself approach but also need professional writing help, LiveCareer provides a great combination of resources.

Conclusion

Choosing the right resume writing service can make all the difference in your job search. Whether you need a simple resume or a highly customized, ATS-optimized document, there is a service that fits your needs. TopResume and ZipJob are great for those who need expert help, while Resumake offers affordable, budget-friendly solutions. ResumeWriters.com excels for mid-career and executive professionals, and LiveCareer is a great choice for those looking for an all-in-one career solution.

When selecting a service, consider your budget, your career level, and the specific type of resume or service you require. A well-crafted resume can help you land your dream job, and these top services will make sure that your resume stands out in the competitive job market.

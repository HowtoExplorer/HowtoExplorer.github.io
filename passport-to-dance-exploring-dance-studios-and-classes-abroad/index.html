Passport to Dance: Exploring Dance Studios and Classes Abroad


Embarking on a journey to explore the world of dance studios and classes abroad is an experience like no other. Beyond just taking a course, it’s a passport to uncover the beauty of diverse cultures, their traditions, and their art forms through dance. In this blog post, we’ll take you on a delightful tour around the globe to discover the unique attractions of exploring dance studios and classes abroad.

1. The Magic of Ballet in Russia

Russia is synonymous with world-class ballet. The country’s illustrious tradition in classical dance offers visitors a chance to witness or learn from beauty, rigor, and passion. The Bolshoi Ballet Academy, in Moscow and affiliated with the famous Bolshoi Theatre, is a must-visit for aspiring dancers. Immerse yourself in the magic of ballet, take classes, or enroll in the renowned conservatories, and discover the essence of Russian dance.

2. Flamenco: The Soul of Spain

Dive into the heart of Spain and experience the vibrant world of Flamenco. This passionate form of dance-music theater has its roots in Andalusia and will leave you captivated by its elements of sadness, joy, and confidence. Whether you sign up for a Flamenco class in Seville or Granada, or attend a lively show in Madrid, the raw experience of this art form is sure to seep deep into your soul.

3. Swaying to the Rhythm of Samba in Brazil

The rhythm of Brazil is impossible to resist, and its dance forms are no exception. The country is famous for its various dance styles, but it’s the Samba that steals the show. The vibrant, lively genre is celebrated during the annual Rio Carnival, and what better way to experience it than by joining a class or dancing under the sun in Rio de Janeiro?!

4. The Exotic Beauty of Indian Classical Dance

India’s rich cultural heritage is reflected profoundly in its dance forms. From the grace of Bharatanatyam to the intricate Katthak, Indian classical dances are a delight to witness and learn. Visit institutes in the southern city of Chennai or the cultural capital, Kolkata, where you can take courses and be mesmerized by the elegance of these traditional dances.

5. Hip-Hop and Street Dance in United States

Imagine learning from the masters themselves, as you explore the booming hip-hop and street dance scene in the United States. Cities like New York, Los Angeles, and Las Vegas offer a plethora of opportunities to learn from professionals. From Hip-Hop to Breakdancing, Popping, and even Gliding, there’s a whole universe of street dance for you to discover.

6. The Power of Waacking in Germany

Germany is not just known for its techno beats; it’s also a hub for underground dance styles originating from Southern California in the 1970s. Waacking, which is a versatile dance style combining smooth, sharp movements with energy, now enjoys a flourishing scene in Berlin – the perfect destination for a dose of funk and soul.

7. Discovering Contemporary Dance in Stockholm

Sweden’s capital, Stockholm, is known for its progressive, artistic culture. The field of contemporary dance flourishes there, celebrating creativity, expression, and individuality. Sign up for workshops, attend performances of renowned choreographers, or even explore the intimacy of smaller studios – the city is an oasis for contemporary dance lovers.

Concluding Thoughts

Exploring dance studios and classes abroad can be an incredible way to immerse yourself in the local culture and art. From experiencing age-old traditions to embracing the new and experimental, travel can open up new doors to self-expression through dance. So don your dancing shoes, grab your passport, and let the rhythm of the world guide you to a journey of a lifetime!

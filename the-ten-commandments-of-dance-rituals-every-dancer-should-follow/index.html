The Ten Commandments of Dance: Rituals Every Dancer Should Follow


Dancing is a form of art that goes beyond the physical movement of the body. It is an expression of emotions, emotions, and inner passion. With roots in various cultures and practices worldwide, dance has transcended barriers and united people across the globe. For those who possess the inner calling for dance, it is not just a hobby or a career, but a way of life. To recognize and cultivate this intrinsic relationship, it is essential to follow the Ten Commandments of Dance – a guide every dancer should adopt to fully embrace their love for dance.

  1. Believe in Thyself

The first commandment mandates dancers to believe in themselves. Every choreographer and dancer must acknowledge their personal worth and unique abilities to deliver exceptional performances consistently. A strong belief in oneself imbues confidence in every step taken on the dance floor, engaging the audience and leaving an indelible impression.

  1. Follow Your Dance Heart

At the core of every dancer lies their true love – their favorite dance form. Actively seeking out opportunities to pursue the dance style that stirs the soul helps foster personal growth and self-expression. Dancers should not be swayed by external pressures or societal expectations to change their love for dance but should pursue their dance heart passionately.

  1. Respect Your Dance Space

Respecting other dancers, as well as the studio, stage, or performance area, forms the third commandment. Dancers should treat their dance spaces with the utmost respect by maintaining cleanliness, tidiness, and cohesiveness. By setting a positive example, dancers significantly contribute to a lively dance environment that stimulates growth and learning.

  1. Commit to Your Practice

The ballet barre, the stage lights, the adrenaline pumping— for many, dance is life. Hence, dancers should commit to regular practice with persistence and passion. Consistent effort and hard work not only refine technical skills but also yield increased fluidity, grace, and poise in performance.

  1. Free Thyself

Freedom and creativity are fundamental to dance. Dancers should strive to break free from inhibitions and limiting self-doubt by practicing regularly. Embracing flexibility in expression and experimentation through movement enables dancers to discover their authentic style, making performances much more colorful and vibrant.

  1. Control Thy Technique

Mastering technique is crucial to effective dance. Dancers should dedicate time and effort to understand, internalize, and refine details of their technique – be it in ballet or hip hop, Kathak or contemporary. Precision in movements and flawless execution make for spellbinding dance performances.

  1. Be in Sync with Thine Partner

Dancing often requires, or brings joy through, partnering up. Whether it is a tango, waltz or an energetic salsa, dancers should strive to be in sync with their partners. Conversely, respecting each other’s space, rhythm, and style of dancing fosters a harmonious and successful collaboration on stage.

  1. Dance LOUD

Audiences love to see a dance performance full of life and energy. Dancers are encouraged to shower their performances with passion and vigor, refusing to be passive or monotonous. Each dance should speak volumes about the emotions, feelings, and expressions of the dancers, enticing spectators to revel in a world of excitement.

  1. Be True to Thine Dance Community

Dance communities all over the world are bonded in the unwavering pursuit of love and understanding for the art. Actively participating in workshops, dance exchange programs, and networking opportunities is essential in nurturing the rich diversity of dance. It is also crucial to practice humility and thankfulness towards fellow dancers and mentors who have helped in the journey of self-improvement and growth.

  1. Share Thy Dance

Finally, the last commandment mandates dancers to share their passion with the world. Whether it’s teaching a class, choreographing a routine for a friend, or organizing a dance event, dancers must promote and enhance the appreciation of dance in society. Sharing dance brings unparalleled joy and enriches the lives of others.

Conclusion

Following these Ten Commandments of Dance creates a beautiful journey for every dancer and brings forth an unparalleled charm that captivates the hearts of onlookers everywhere. By embracing these commandments, dancers can elevate their artistry and collaborate more effectively, creating harmonious, spellbinding, and mesmerizing performances that live on in the hearts and minds of their audiences.

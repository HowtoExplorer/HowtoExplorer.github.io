Plant-Based Protein Recipes for Meatless Meals


Are you looking for delicious and protein-packed recipes to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet? From satisfying breakfast options to hearty dinner dishes, these plant-based protein recipes are sure to please. So, let’s dive right in and explore some incredible meatless alternatives!

  1. Smoky Tofu Scramble

Prep time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 15 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 block (14 oz) firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled and sliced
  • 1 medium mushroom, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

  2. Add onion and bell pepper, cook for 3-4 minutes or until tender.

  3. Add zucchini, carrot, and mushroom; cook for 5 minutes.

  4. In a small bowl, combine tofu, garlic, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.

  5. Add tofu mixture to the skillet, and stir to combine.

  6. For extra protein, stir in lightly beaten eggs (optional).

  7. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  8. Serve and enjoy!

  9. Lentil and Quinoa Salad

Prep time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 30 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow or green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring 3 cups of water to a boil. Add lentils, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until tender.

  2. In another saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add quinoa, reduce heat, and simmer for 18 minutes, or until tender.

  3. In a large bowl, combine cooked lentils and quinoa, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and parsley.

  4. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

  5. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently.

  6. If desired, top with crumbled feta cheese.

  7. Serve and savor the flavors!

  8. Spinach and Chickpea Curry

Prep time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 30 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, saut\a e onion, garlic, and ginger in oil until tender.
  2. Add tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and spices (curry powder, garam masala, turmeric, and cayenne pepper). Stir well.
  3. Add chickpeas and spinach, stirring gently.
  4. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, allowing flavors to meld.
  5. Adjust seasoning as needed, and serve hot.
  6. For a creamy touch, top with a dollop of Greek yogurt (optional).

Explore plant-based protein recipes and delight in the versatility of meatless meals. They are both nutritious and delicious, making it easy to maintain a healthy diet and reduce your environmental footprint. Bon appétit!

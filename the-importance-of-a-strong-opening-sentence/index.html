The Importance of a Strong Opening Sentence

Good opening sentences dig deep into our unconscious, speaking directly to the parts of our minds that crave mystery, asks questions, or want to escape into someone else's reality. This gentle caress seeps into our collective consciousness and leaves an impression on the reader.

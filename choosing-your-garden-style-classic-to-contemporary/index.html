Choosing Your Garden Style: Classic to Contemporary


When it comes to garden design, there are numerous styles to choose from. Some prefer the elegance of a classic English garden, while others enjoy the creativity of a modern Japanese garden. Whatever your style, your garden should reflect your unique taste and lifestyle. Let’s explore some key garden styles so that you can find your perfect match.

Classic Garden Style

The classic garden style exudes timeless beauty and charm. Inspired by the grand estates of 18th-century England, this style involves well-manicured lawns, geometric borders, and meticulously trimmed hedges. A classic garden is an excellent choice for those who appreciate a refined and sophisticated atmosphere.

Key Elements:

  1. Symmetry and order
  2. Use of formal landscaping techniques
  3. Traditional plant varieties
  4. Ornate garden features, such as fountains and statues

English Country Garden Style

The English country garden style is an amalgamation of classic and cottage gardening principles. It combines the formality of classic garden design with the relaxed and informal nature of cottage-style planting. This style is perfect for those who love the classic garden but also yearn for a more natural, wildflower feel.

Key Elements:

  1. Mixture of formal and informal planting
  2. Traditional English plants, such as roses, foxgloves, and lavender
  3. Rustic features, such as thatched sheds and picket fences
  4. A sense of harmonious integration with the surrounding landscape

French Formal Garden Style

The French formal garden harks back to the opulence and grandeur of Versailles. This style involves an impressive layout with terraces, parterres, and box hedges. It’s a perfect choice for those looking for a sense of elegance and sophistication.

Key Elements:

  1. Exquisite geometry
  2. Use of gravel or gravel-like materials
  3. Architectural features, such as arbors and treillage
  4. Paved pathways and meticulously maintained plantings

Contemporary Garden Style

The contemporary garden style is all about innovation and creativity. It breaks the mold of traditional garden design and embraces a more eclectic mix of materials, textures, and colors. This style is ideal for those who crave originality and a unique garden experience.

Key Elements:

  1. Unorthodox plant choices
  2. Use of industrial materials, such as metal and concrete
  3. Dramatic lighting and water features
  4. Creative use of landscaping techniques to create striking contrasts

Eclectic Garden Style

The eclectic garden style celebrates individuality and the art of mixing and matching different styles and elements. It’s a perfect choice for those who enjoy experimenting with various gardening styles and don’t want to be bound by any particular garden aesthetic.

Key Elements:

  1. Mixture of different garden styles and design elements
  2. An amalgamation of various plant varieties
  3. Unique garden features and ornaments
  4. A sense of personal expression and creativity

Ultimately, your choice of garden style should reflect your personality and lifestyle. Make an effort to rethink your garden space and create an outdoor oasis that brings joy and satisfaction for years to come.

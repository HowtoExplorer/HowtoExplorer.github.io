The Best Fashion Documentaries to Watch Right Now


Fashion documentaries offer a captivating look into the world of style, design, and creativity. They provide insight into the lives of renowned designers, the fashion industry’s evolution, and the impact of fashion on society. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast or simply intrigued by the artistry behind clothing, these documentaries are a must-watch. Here are some of the best fashion documentaries to add to your watchlist:

1. “The First Monday in May”

Directed by Andrew Rossi, “The First Monday in May” takes you behind the scenes of the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s Costume Institute and its annual gala. This documentary follows the creation of the highly anticipated fashion exhibition, exploring the intersection of fashion, art, and celebrity culture. With appearances from industry icons such as Anna Wintour and Karl Lagerfeld, this film offers an exclusive look into the fashion world’s most glamorous event.

2. “Dior and I”

“Dior and I” provides an intimate glimpse into the world of haute couture through the eyes of Belgian fashion designer, Raf Simons. Directed by Frédéric Tcheng, this documentary showcases Simons’ journey as he takes the helm at Christian Dior and prepares his debut collection. From the atelier to the runway, viewers get a behind-the-scenes look at the meticulous craftsmanship and creative process that goes into creating a Dior collection.

3. “The September Issue”

Directed by R.J. Cutler, “The September Issue” is a captivating documentary that delves into the making of Vogue’s September 2007 issue, known as the fashion bible’s most important edition. The film offers an inside look at the dynamic relationship between Vogue’s Editor-in-Chief, Anna Wintour, and Creative Director, Grace Coddington. Through their collaboration, viewers witness the creative vision and high-pressure environment that shapes the iconic magazine.

4. “Valentino: The Last Emperor”

“Valentino: The Last Emperor” follows the final two years of legendary fashion designer Valentino Garavani’s career. Directed by Matt Tyrnauer, this documentary showcases Valentino’s passion for fashion, his meticulous attention to detail, and the profound impact he has had on the industry. With exclusive access to the designer’s personal and professional life, viewers get an intimate look at the man behind the iconic fashion house.

5. “McQueen”

Directed by Ian Bonhôte and Peter Ettedgui, “McQueen” is a powerful documentary that explores the life and work of British fashion designer Alexander McQueen. This film delves into McQueen’s dark and creative mind, highlighting his groundbreaking designs, controversial shows, and personal struggles. Through archival footage and interviews with friends, family, and collaborators, “McQueen” provides a poignant look at the visionary designer’s immense talent and tragic demise.

6. “Bill Cunningham New York”

“Bill Cunningham New York” pays homage to the late, great street fashion photographer Bill Cunningham. Directed by Richard Press, this documentary chronicles Cunningham’s career at The New York Times, where he captured the essence of style on the streets of the city. With his bicycle and camera in hand, Cunningham documented fashion trends and celebrated individuality, leaving an indelible mark on the fashion industry and inspiring countless photographers and fashion enthusiasts.

7. “Iris”

Directed by Albert Maysles, “Iris” is a delightful documentary that celebrates the life and style of fashion icon Iris Apfel. Known for her eclectic fashion sense and larger-than-life personality, Apfel has become an inspiration for embracing individuality and personal style. This film takes viewers on a journey through Apfel’s colorful career, her unique approach to fashion, and her impact on the industry at any age.

Fashion documentaries offer a glimpse into the world of style, creativity, and self-expression. They showcase the dedication, passion, and innovation that drive the fashion industry forward. Whether you’re seeking inspiration, knowledge, or simply entertainment, these films are sure to captivate and leave you with a deeper appreciation for the artistry behind fashion. So grab some popcorn, get cozy, and prepare for a fashionable movie night!

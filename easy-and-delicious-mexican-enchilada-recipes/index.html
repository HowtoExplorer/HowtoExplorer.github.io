Easy and Delicious Mexican Enchilada Recipes


Enchiladas are a classic Mexican dish that never fails to satisfy. With their rich flavors and comforting textures, they are perfect for a hearty dinner or a weekend gathering with friends and family. In this blog post, we will explore some easy and delicious Mexican enchilada recipes that you can try at home. These recipes are simple to make and packed with authentic Mexican flavors. So, let’s get started!

Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 10 tortillas
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, onions, bell peppers, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable.
  4. Spoon the chicken mixture onto each tortilla and roll it up tightly.
  5. Place the rolled tortillas in a baking dish, seam side down.
  6. Pour the enchilada sauce over the tortillas, making sure they are fully covered.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve hot and garnish with additional cilantro if desired.

Beef Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 10 tortillas
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the onions, bell peppers, cilantro, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable.
  5. Spoon the beef mixture onto each tortilla and roll it up tightly.
  6. Place the rolled tortillas in a baking dish, seam side down.
  7. Pour the enchilada sauce over the tortillas, making sure they are fully covered.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the top.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Serve hot and garnish with additional cilantro if desired.

Vegetarian Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked black beans
  • 1 cup cooked corn kernels
  • 10 tortillas
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the black beans, corn kernels, onions, bell peppers, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable.
  4. Spoon the bean and corn mixture onto each tortilla and roll it up tightly.
  5. Place the rolled tortillas in a baking dish, seam side down.
  6. Pour the enchilada sauce over the tortillas, making sure they are fully covered.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve hot and garnish with additional cilantro if desired.

Conclusion

These easy and delicious Mexican enchilada recipes are a great way to bring the flavors of Mexico into your home. Whether you prefer chicken, beef, or a vegetarian option, there is a recipe here for everyone. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to enjoy a taste of Mexico with these mouthwatering enchiladas. Buen provecho!

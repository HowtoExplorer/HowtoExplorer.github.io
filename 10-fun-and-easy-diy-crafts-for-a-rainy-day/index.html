10 Fun and Easy DIY Crafts for a Rainy Day


Are you stuck at home on a rainy day with nothing to do? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! Here are 10 fun and easy DIY crafts that will keep you entertained and creative while the rain pours outside.

1. Paper Mache Bowl

Create a unique and colorful bowl using paper mache. All you need is some old newspapers, water, flour, and a bowl as a mold. Tear the newspapers into small strips, dip them in the flour-water mixture, and layer them on the bowl. Let it dry overnight, and voila! You have a beautiful paper mache bowl to store your trinkets.

2. Painted Rocks

Take a stroll outside and collect some smooth rocks. Clean them thoroughly and let them dry. Now, grab your favorite acrylic paints and let your imagination run wild! Paint cute animals, inspirational quotes, or intricate patterns on the rocks. Once they dry, you can use them as paperweights or even garden decorations.

3. DIY Terrarium

Bring a piece of nature into your home with a DIY terrarium. Find a glass jar or container, fill the bottom with small rocks for drainage, add a layer of activated charcoal to keep it fresh, and top it off with potting soil. Now, it’s time to plant! Choose small succulents or air plants and arrange them creatively. Place your terrarium near a window, and enjoy the greenery even on rainy days.

4. Homemade Candles

Create a cozy atmosphere with homemade candles. Gather some wax flakes, a candle wick, and your favorite essential oils. Melt the wax in a double boiler, add a few drops of essential oil, and pour it into a heat-resistant container. Place the wick in the center and let it cool. Trim the wick, light your candle, and bask in its warm glow.

5. Upcycled T-Shirt Tote Bag

Give new life to old t-shirts by turning them into trendy tote bags. Cut off the sleeves and neckline, leaving the bottom intact. Sew the bottom closed, and you have a simple tote bag ready for groceries or a trip to the beach. Get creative and decorate it with fabric markers or patches to make it truly unique.

6. DIY Dreamcatcher

Channel your inner bohemian spirit by making your own dreamcatcher. Start with a metal or wooden hoop, wrap it with colorful yarn or twine, and weave a web in the center using string or embroidery thread. Add decorative elements like feathers, beads, or ribbons. Hang your dreamcatcher near your bed to catch bad dreams and let the good ones pass through.

7. Personalized Photo Coasters

Protect your coffee table in style with personalized photo coasters. Print out your favorite photos, cut them to fit the size of the coasters, and glue them onto cork or tile coasters. Seal them with a waterproof sealant, and you’ll have a set of coasters that will bring back cherished memories with every sip.

8. DIY Bath Bombs

Turn bath time into a luxurious experience with DIY bath bombs. Mix together baking soda, citric acid, Epsom salt, and your choice of essential oils. Add a few drops of food coloring if desired. Shape the mixture into balls or use molds, and let them dry overnight. Drop one into your bath, and enjoy a fizzy, fragrant soak.

9. Yarn-Wrapped Letters

Add a personal touch to your home decor with yarn-wrapped letters. Choose your initials or spell out a meaningful word. Wrap colorful yarn around each letter, securing it with glue as you go. Hang your yarn-wrapped letters on the wall or place them on a shelf for a charming and personalized touch.

10. DIY Herb Garden

Bring a touch of nature and freshness into your home by creating a DIY herb garden. Use small pots or repurpose old containers, fill them with potting soil, and plant your favorite herbs like basil, rosemary, or mint. Place your herb garden near a sunny window, water it regularly, and enjoy the satisfaction of cooking with your own fresh herbs.

So, the next time you find yourself stuck indoors on a rainy day, don’t let the weather dampen your spirits. Try out these 10 fun and easy DIY crafts, and let your creativity shine. Happy crafting!

The Best Crafting Magazines to Inspire Your Creativity


Crafting is more than just a hobby; it’s a way to express yourself, unwind, and create something beautiful. Whether you’re a seasoned crafter or just starting, finding inspiration can sometimes be a challenge. One of the best ways to spark your creativity is through crafting magazines. These publications are filled with innovative ideas, step-by-step tutorials, and expert tips that can help you elevate your crafting game. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best crafting magazines available today that are sure to inspire your creativity.

1. Mollie Makes

Mollie Makes is a delightful magazine that focuses on modern craft and DIY projects. Each issue is packed with vibrant photography and a variety of projects ranging from knitting and sewing to home décor and paper crafts. The magazine often features interviews with contemporary crafters, providing insights into their creative processes and inspirations. Mollie Makes is perfect for those who appreciate a blend of traditional crafting techniques with a modern twist.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Diverse Projects: Each issue includes a mix of projects suitable for all skill levels.
  • Inspiring Stories: The interviews with crafters can motivate you to explore new techniques.
  • Stylish Aesthetic: The magazine’s design is visually appealing, making it a joy to read.

2. Crafts Beautiful

Crafts Beautiful is a well-established magazine that covers a wide range of crafting topics, including card making, scrapbooking, and home crafts. Each issue features a variety of projects, tips, and techniques that cater to both beginners and experienced crafters. The magazine also includes a section dedicated to seasonal crafts, ensuring you have fresh ideas for every occasion.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Step-by-Step Guides: Clear instructions make it easy to follow along with projects.
  • Seasonal Inspiration: Get ideas for holidays and special events throughout the year.
  • Community Focus: The magazine often highlights reader submissions, fostering a sense of community among crafters.

3. Better Homes & Gardens: Crafts

Better Homes & Gardens is a household name, and their crafting section is no exception. This magazine offers a blend of home improvement, gardening, and crafting ideas. The crafting section features a variety of projects that are practical and stylish, making it a great resource for those looking to enhance their living spaces with handmade items.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Home-Centric Projects: Focus on crafts that can beautify your home.
  • Expert Tips: Benefit from the knowledge of experienced crafters and designers.
  • Variety of Topics: Explore a range of subjects beyond just crafting, including gardening and home décor.

4. Creative Knitting

For knitting enthusiasts, Creative Knitting is a must-read. This magazine is dedicated entirely to knitting, offering a plethora of patterns, techniques, and tips. Each issue features a variety of projects, from garments to accessories, catering to knitters of all skill levels. The magazine also includes articles on yarn selection and care, helping you make informed choices for your projects.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Exclusive Patterns: Discover unique designs that you won’t find elsewhere.
  • Skill-Building Articles: Learn new techniques to enhance your knitting skills.
  • Yarn Reviews: Stay updated on the latest yarn trends and products.

5. Simply Crochet

Simply Crochet is a vibrant magazine that celebrates the art of crochet. Each issue is filled with colorful patterns, tips, and inspiration for crocheters of all levels. The magazine often features themed projects, making it easy to find something that resonates with your personal style. Additionally, Simply Crochet includes interviews with designers, providing insight into their creative processes.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Colorful Designs: The magazine’s aesthetic is bright and cheerful, reflecting the joy of crochet.
  • Variety of Projects: From home décor to fashion, there’s something for everyone.
  • Designer Insights: Learn from the best in the industry through exclusive interviews.

6. The Knitter

The Knitter is a premium magazine that caters to serious knitting enthusiasts. Each issue features high-quality patterns, in-depth articles, and expert advice. The magazine often collaborates with renowned designers, offering exclusive patterns that are both challenging and rewarding. The Knitter is perfect for those looking to take their knitting skills to the next level.

Why You’ll Love It:

  • High-Quality Patterns: Access exclusive designs from top designers in the knitting world.
  • In-Depth Articles: Gain insights into knitting techniques and trends.
  • Challenging Projects: Perfect for those looking to push their skills further.

7. PaperCraft Inspirations

If you love working with paper, PaperCraft Inspirations is the magazine for you. This publication focuses on card making, scrapbooking, and other paper crafts.

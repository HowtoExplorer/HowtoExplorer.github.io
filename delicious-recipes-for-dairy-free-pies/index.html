Delicious Recipes for Dairy-Free Pies


Whether you’re lactose intolerant, following a dairy-free diet, or simply looking to explore new flavors, dairy-free pies are a delightful treat that everyone can enjoy. From creamy fillings to flaky crusts, there are endless possibilities for creating delectable dairy-free pies that will tantalize your taste buds. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering dairy-free pie recipes that are sure to become a hit at any gathering or simply for a cozy night in.

Vegan Chocolate Silk Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 ¼ cups raw cashews, soaked overnight
  • 1 ½ cups full-fat coconut milk
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • ¾ cup maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 pre-baked dairy-free pie crust

Instructions:

  1. Drain the soaked cashews and place them in a blender.
  2. Add the coconut milk, cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt to the blender.
  3. Blend the ingredients until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour the filling into the pre-baked dairy-free pie crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Refrigerate the pie for at least 4 hours, or until the filling is set.
  6. Serve chilled and garnish with dairy-free whipped topping or fresh berries.

Coconut Key Lime Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut cream
  • ¾ cup fresh key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon key lime zest
  • ½ cup agave nectar
  • 1 pre-baked dairy-free graham cracker crust

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the coconut cream, key lime juice, key lime zest, and agave nectar until well combined.
  2. Pour the filling into the pre-baked dairy-free graham cracker crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  3. Refrigerate the pie for at least 2 hours, or until the filling is firm.
  4. Once set, slice the pie and garnish with additional lime zest or coconut flakes.

Blueberry Almond Crumble Pie

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 pre-baked dairy-free pie crust
  • For the crumble topping:
    • 1 cup rolled oats
    • ½ cup almond flour
    • ¼ cup coconut oil, melted
    • ¼ cup maple syrup
    • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
    • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, gently toss the blueberries with granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and almond extract.
  3. Pour the blueberry filling into the pre-baked dairy-free pie crust.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond flour, melted coconut oil, maple syrup, cinnamon, and salt to make the crumble topping.
  5. Sprinkle the crumble mixture evenly over the blueberry filling.
  6. Bake the pie for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  7. Allow the pie to cool before slicing and serving.

These dairy-free pie recipes are just a taste of the endless possibilities for indulgent and satisfying desserts without the need for dairy. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, these recipes are guaranteed to impress. Experiment with different flavors and ingredients to create your own dairy-free pie masterpieces, and don’t be afraid to let your creativity shine in the kitchen. Enjoy the delightful world of dairy-free pies and treat yourself to a slice of bliss!

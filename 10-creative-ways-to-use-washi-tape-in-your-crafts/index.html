10 Creative Ways to Use Washi Tape in Your Crafts


Washi tape has become a must-have accessory in the crafting world. Thanks to its versatility, eye-catching designs, and ease of use, it has taken creative projects to a whole new level. Whether you’re new to crafting or a seasoned DIY enthusiast, this decorative adhesive tape can add charm, color, and personality to everything you create. Below are ten inventive ways to use washi tape that will inspire and transform your artistic endeavors.

1. Customize Journals and Planners

One of the most popular uses for washi tape is decorating journals and planners. The tape’s vibrant colors and patterns can turn a plain planner into a personalized masterpiece. Use it to create borders, highlight important dates, or design sections. For bullet journaling, washi tape can be used as headers, dividers, or to establish a theme for your monthly spreads. Mix and match colors and patterns to reflect your mood or the season. Its removable nature makes it easy to revise designs without damaging the pages.

2. Create Unique Greeting Cards

If you enjoy gifting handmade cards, washi tape can help add flair without much effort. Whether for birthdays, holidays, or special occasions, you can use strips of washi tape to create borders, add geometric patterns, or form delightful images like trees, balloons, or stripes. Layer different designs to add dimension, or fold it into rosettes or ribbons for a more three-dimensional effect. The possibilities are endless, and the results are always professional-looking.

3. Personalized Phone Cases

Give your smartphone an instant makeover using washi tape. Instead of purchasing expensive custom phone cases, use washi tape to design a unique and trendy cover. Take a clear phone case and apply strips of washi tape to the back in vertical, horizontal, or diagonal patterns. You can create abstract designs or go for something specific like florals, polka dots, or stripes. Once complete, trim any excess tape, and you’ll have a custom phone case tailored to your style.

4. Transform Home Décor Items

Washi tape isn’t just for small projects; it can be used to elevate home décor as well. Add stylish borders to plain photo frames, decorate flowerpots with fun geometric designs, or even jazz up light switch covers. Another great idea is to create wall art by arranging washi tape strips in a specific pattern or forming shapes like triangles, hearts, or chevrons. Washi tape is also ideal for seasonal decorations since it can be removed easily without damaging surfaces.

5. Gift Wrapping and Tag Design

Why stick to plain gift wrap when you can use washi tape to make your presents pop? Add strips of washi tape to kraft paper, or layer tapes with complementary designs for a stunning effect. You can also create your gift tags by cutting cardstock into shapes and embellishing them with pieces of tape. Personalizing your gift wrap and tags not only enhances the aesthetic but also shows the recipient how much thought you’ve put into the gift.

6. DIY Bookmarks

For book lovers, bookmarks made with washi tape are not only practical but also stylish. Simply take a piece of firm cardstock or paper and cover it with your favorite washi tape designs. Punch a hole at the top and add a tassel or ribbon for extra flair. These bookmarks can also make great gifts for friends who enjoy reading. Even better—because they’re lightweight and easy to make, they’re perfect for crafting on a budget.

7. Embellish Envelopes and Stationery

Enhance your correspondence by decorating envelopes and stationery with washi tape. Use the tape to seal envelope flaps, outline the edges, or create fun designs on the front. On stationery, you can use strips of tape as borders or accents. This is a great way to add a personal touch to letters or invitations. When creating DIY thank-you cards or wedding invites, washi tape can make your correspondence stand out while saving costs on expensive paper.

8. Organize and Label Items

Washi tape is an excellent tool for organization. Use it to label storage boxes, jars, or binders with colorful tags. Write directly on lighter-colored tape with a permanent marker to identify contents while keeping them visually appealing. You can also use washi tape to color-code cables, electronic gadgets, and kitchen containers. Whether for home, work, or hobbies, washi tape brings creativity and function together.

9. Create Washi Tape Artwork

Channel your inner artist by creating one-of-a-kind artwork using washi tape. Instead of traditional paints or drawing tools, use the tape to design abstract art, landscapes, or portraits. You can even replicate famous art pieces with washi tape strips. Layer different shades and textures to bring your vision to life. This unconventional medium allows for limitless creative expression without the mess of paint or markers.

10. Brighten Up Office Supplies

Add personality to your workspace by decorating your office supplies with washi tape. Cover pencils, pen barrels, notebooks, or binder clips with colorful tape. Transform basic office essentials like staplers or scissors into conversation pieces with a quick application of washi tape. These small changes can make a significant difference in motivating you to stay productive and creative throughout the day.

Washi tape is an essential tool for crafters thanks to its versatility, affordability, and aesthetic appeal. From personalizing your belongings to creating unique gifts, there’s no limit to what you can achieve with washi tape. By incorporating these ten creative ideas into your projects, you’ll not only add beauty and fun to your crafts but also discover new ways to express your individuality. Let your imagination run wild, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different colors, patterns, and combinations—you’re sure to craft something spectacular!

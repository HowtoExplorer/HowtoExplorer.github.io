10 Easy Baking Recipes for Beginners


Are you new to the world of baking and looking to try your hand at creating delicious treats in your own kitchen? Baking can be a rewarding and enjoyable experience, especially when you start with simple and easy recipes that are perfect for beginners. Whether you have a sweet tooth or prefer savory delights, we’ve got you covered with 10 easy baking recipes that will have you impressing your friends and family in no time.

1. Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually add to the creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop dough by rounded tablespoon onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until lightly golden brown.

2. Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mash the ripe bananas in a mixing bowl.
  3. Stir in the melted butter.
  4. Mix in the baking soda and salt.
  5. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  6. Mix in the flour.
  7. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan.
  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

3. Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Pizza sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve sugar in warm water and stir in the yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Combine flour and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Stir in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  4. Knead the dough until smooth, then let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out the dough, add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.

4. Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture alternately with milk.
  6. Pour batter into cupcake liners.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

5. Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Combine flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. Place vegetable oil into a 1 cup measuring cup; add the egg and enough milk to fill the cup.
  4. Mix this with flour mixture.
  5. Fold in blueberries.
  6. Fill muffin cups right to the top.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

6. Cheesy Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 French baguette
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the baguette in half lengthwise.
  3. Mix butter, garlic, and parsley together.
  4. Spread the mixture on the cut sides of the bread.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the top.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

7. Lemon Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Zest of one lemon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mix 1 cup flour and powdered sugar; cut in butter until crumbly.
  3. Press into the bottom of an ungreased 9x9-inch baking pan.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes.
  5. In another bowl, whisk eggs, white sugar, 2 tbsp flour, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
  6. Pour over the baked crust.
  7. Bake for an additional 20 minutes.
  8. Cool before cutting into bars.

8. Fudgy Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  5. Beat in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  6. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes.

9. Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened
  • Cream cheese icing

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm milk and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in sugar, butter, salt, eggs, and flour.
  3. Knead the dough and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out the dough, spread with softened butter, cinnamon, and sugar.
  5. Roll up the dough and cut into rolls.
  6. Place in a greased pan and let rise for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes.
  8. Drizzle with cream cheese icing.

10. Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Arrange sliced apples in the bottom of a greased baking dish.
  3. Mix flour, oats, sugars, and cinnamon.
  4. Cut in the softened butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  5. Sprinkle over the apples.
  6. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the apples are tender.

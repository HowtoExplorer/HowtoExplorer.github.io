The Top Skincare Habits I Learned in Quarantine: A Guide for Enhanced Well-being


Quarantine has been a bizarre and unpredictable period for many. However, it has given us the unique opportunity to slow down and truly focus on our well-being, especially when it comes to our skincare routines. Perhaps because we have more time on our hands, or maybe because we’re also trying to keep our dermatologist appointment anxiety under control, we’ve been optimizing and experimenting with new skincare habits that have turned out to be surprisingly effective.

Through trial and error, and gathering advice from experts, we’ve discovered some incredible skincare habits that have not only worked wonders for our skin but have also contributed to our overall health and well-being. So, without further ado, here’s the ultimate guide to transforming your skincare, right from the comfort of your home.

  1. Narrow Down Your Skincare Routine

The first skincare habit we quickly learned during quarantine is that less is indeed more. With so much downtime at home, we finally had the opportunity to test a variety of skincare products and methods, only to realize that multiple layers of serums, masks, and creams were not only overwhelming but also confusing for our skin. By narrowing down our skincare routine, we not only saved time, but we also helped our skin start to recognize and better absorb the active ingredients.

  1. Embrace the Power of True Restorative Sleep

Believe it or not, quarantine has given us a newfound appreciation for sleep, with our bedtime routines becoming more consistent and our sleep quality much improved. We found that the secret to healthy, glowing skin lies in prioritizing good sleep. Our skin regenerates while we’re asleep, so it’s essential to give our body the rest and hydration it needs to do so effectively.

  1. Start a Skin-Diet Movement

As they say, you are what you eat. The same rule applies to your skin. During quarantine, we realized just how much our overall health affects our skin. So, we kicked off a skin-diet movement by focusing on incorporating foods that nourish our skin from within. We began consuming loads of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins to fuel our bodies with essential vitamins and minerals, which allowed our skin to further thrive.

  1. Create a Personalized Hydration Plan

Hydration is vital for living a healthy lifestyle, but it’s especially true for maintaining beautiful, glowing skin. Drinking more water, limiting caffeine and sugary drinks, and replenishing electrolytes (such as by using coconut water) became daily priorities during quarantine, and we could definitely see the payoff in our skin. By creating a system and staying disciplined with our hydration, we experienced not only clearer and dewier skin but also overall improved well-being.

  1. Schedule in-quality “Me” Time

When the world outside is in chaos, it’s crucial to have designated “me” time, especially when it comes to skincare. Giving ourselves uninterrupted stretches of time to practice self-care has been enormously beneficial in improving our skincare routines and overall well-being. From shorter pampering sessions (like face masks or deep conditioning hair treatments) to more extended indulgences (such as long, hot baths), these moments of self-care not only help us unwind but also have integral effects on our skincare rituals.

  1. Embrace Natural and Organic Ingredients

As we spent more time at home during quarantine, we became increasingly conscious about the products we were using on our skin. We searched for natural, cruelty-free, and organic products, paying close attention to the ingredients. Using botanicals such as aloe vera, chamomile, and vitamin E, we began to witness improvements in our skin’s texture, tone, and clarity. By swapping out harsh chemicals for gentle, organic alternatives, our skin has never felt or looked better.

  1. Listen to Your Skin

This might be the most essential habit to adopt amidst quarantine. Listening to our skin’s needs and responding to its cues has made it possible to pinpoint exactly what it craves: whether it’s a gentle exfoliant, an extra serum, or a tropical getaway (ideally, we can indulge in all three, but in reality, only one). In this unprecedented time, listening to our skin has transformed it into a glowing, healthy canvas that continues to evolve and adapt.

In conclusion, quarantine has been a time of self-discovery and growth. By refining our skincare habits, we’ve not only found new ways to enhance our physical well-being but have also acquired additional tools for emotional resilience and self-care. Whether it’s simplifying our routines, enhancing our sleep, or embracing natural ingredients, our skin has never looked better. So, take advantage of this time at home and enhance your well-being – starting with your skincare.

Top 10 Dance YouTube Channels for Learning New Moves


Dancing is not just an art; it’s a form of expression, a way to connect with others, and a fantastic workout. With the rise of digital platforms, YouTube has become a treasure trove for dance enthusiasts of all skill levels. Whether you’re a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced dancer aiming to refine your technique, there’s something for everyone. In this blog post, we explore the top 10 YouTube channels dedicated to dance tutorials that can help you groove to your favorite tunes and discover new moves.

1. Dance Tutorials Live

Dance Tutorials Live stands out for its extensive library of dance styles, ranging from hip-hop to ballet. The channel features a diverse lineup of professional dancers who break down complex moves into easy-to-follow steps. Their “Learn the Dance” series is particularly popular, allowing viewers to learn choreography from trending songs. This channel not only focuses on the moves but also on the musicality behind them, helping dancers understand how to connect with the rhythm.

Why Subscribe?

  • Variety of Styles: Hip-hop, contemporary, and even jazz.
  • Engaging Choreography: Tutorials are often set to current music hits.

2. Kyle Hanagami

Kyle Hanagami is one of the most sought-after choreographers in the dance scene today. His channel is filled with energetic tutorials that make learning exciting. Kyle’s teaching style emphasizes the importance of performance quality, encouraging dancers to express themselves fully. His choreography often features in music videos and live performances, making his tutorials incredibly relevant.

Why Subscribe?

  • High-Quality Production: Professional-level choreography taught in clear steps.
  • Celebrity Collaborations: Regular appearances by well-known dancers and artists.

3. The Fitness Marshall

If you’re looking to combine fitness with fun dance routines, The Fitness Marshall is the perfect channel. This energetic channel puts a unique spin on dance workouts by creating choreography that feels like a party. Each session is designed not only to teach dance moves but also to provide an effective cardio workout, making it great for those who want to stay fit while dancing.

Why Subscribe?

  • Effective Workouts: Dance routines that also burn calories.
  • Fun Atmosphere: High-energy music and engaging personalities keep you motivated.

4. 1MILLION Dance Studio

Based in South Korea, 1MILLION Dance Studio is renowned for its world-class choreography and professional dancers. Their YouTube channel features a myriad of dance styles, emphasizing street dance and K-pop routines. Each video showcases detailed tutorials, often accompanied by behind-the-scenes footage that gives insights into the creative process.

Why Subscribe?

  • Professional Tutorials: Learn from some of the best dancers in the world.
  • K-Pop Influence: Perfect for fans of K-Pop wanting to learn their favorite idol’s choreography.

5. Matt Steffanina

Matt Steffanina is a popular figure in the dance community, known for his fun and approachable teaching style. His YouTube channel offers a variety of hip-hop dance tutorials, with a focus on both choreography and freestyle techniques. Matt also frequently collaborates with other dancers, providing fresh perspectives and diverse styles in his tutorials.

Why Subscribe?

  • Accessible for Beginners: Tutorials that break down moves into manageable steps.
  • Diverse Collaborations: Exposure to various dance styles and techniques.

6. DancewithMaddie

For younger dancers or those young at heart, DancewithMaddie is a fantastic channel that offers engaging tutorials aimed specifically at kids and beginners. Maddie’s bubbly personality and clear explanations make learning fun and accessible. The channel covers a range of styles, from ballet to hip-hop, ensuring that young dancers can explore their interests.

Why Subscribe?

  • Kid-Friendly Content: Engaging and fun for younger audiences.
  • Easy-to-Follow Tutorials: Perfect for beginners looking to start their dance journey.

7. Dancing With The Stars

This channel takes the excitement of the hit TV show and brings it to YouTube. Featuring professional dancers from the show, viewers get an insider’s look at popular ballroom dances such as the waltz, salsa, and tango. Tutorials often include tips on posture, technique, and performance, making it ideal for anyone looking to learn partner dancing.

Why Subscribe?

  • Ballroom Focus: Perfect for those interested in classic dance styles.
  • Celebrity Choreography: Learn routines that have been performed on a major stage.

8. Coco and Breezy

Sisters Coco and Breezy are not only successful eyewear designers but also passionate dancers who share their love for dance through their YouTube channel. This channel is a mix of dance tutorials, personal stories, and lifestyle content that showcases their unique style. The tutorials range from hip-hop to contemporary, often infused with a sense of fun and individuality.

Why Subscribe?

  • Unique Perspective: Personal stories and insights from successful entrepreneurs.
  • Eclectic Dance Styles: A blend of various genres with a creative twist.

9. The School of Dance

This channel is perfect for those who prefer a more structured approach to learning dance. The School of Dance offers comprehensive tutorials spanning various dance styles, including ballet, jazz, and contemporary. The instructors break down each move in detail, making it easier for dancers to master complex techniques.

Why Subscribe?

  • Structured Learning: Ideal for those wanting a more education-focused approach.
  • Wide Range of Styles: Opportunities to learn different dance genres.

10. Dance Moms

While primarily known for its reality TV roots, the Dance Moms YouTube channel features many dance tutorials led by experienced instructors. The channel captures the drama and dedication of the original series while providing valuable insights into various dance techniques. Viewers can learn routines that have appeared on the show, giving them a taste of competitive dance.

Why Subscribe?

  • Reality TV Appeal: Insight into the world of competitive dance.
  • Expert Instruction: Learn from renowned dance teachers featured on the show.

Conclusion

With the plethora of dance channels available on YouTube, there’s no shortage of resources to help you learn new moves and styles. Each of these channels offers unique teaching styles, diverse genres, and engaging content that caters to dancers of all levels. Whether you’re looking to improve your skills for fun, fitness, or a future career in dance, these top 10 channels have you covered.

So, grab your dancing shoes, hit play, and let the rhythm guide you as you embark on your dance journey! Happy dancing!

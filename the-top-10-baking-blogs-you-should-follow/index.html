The Top 10 Baking Blogs You Should Follow


As a baking enthusiast, there's nothing quite like getting lost in the world of recipes, techniques, and endless possibilities that this sweet and savory world has to offer. With the advent of the internet, there are now countless resources available to help you improve your baking skills, from informative blogs to beautifully-shot tutorials. However, with so many options out there, it can be daunting to know where to start. That's why we've compiled a list of the top 10 baking blogs that you simply must follow. These blogs offer a wealth of knowledge, expertise, and inspiration to help you take your baking to the next level.

  1. The King Arthur Flour Company Blog First on our list is the renowned King Arthur Flour Company Blog. This sister blog to the famous flour company is a treasure trove of baking knowledge, with recipes, tutorials, and expert advice on all things baking. From traditional breads to modern desserts, the King Arthur Flour Company Blog has something for every level of baker.
  2. Smitten Kitchen Sara Moulton's popular Smitten Kitchen blog is a favorite among foodies and bakers alike. With a focus on creative ingredients and innovative recipes, Smitten Kitchen is the perfect destination for those looking to push the boundaries of traditional baking.
  3. The Cake Blog The Cake Blog is a comprehensive resource for all things cake. With tutorials, recipes, and expert advice, this blog is a must-follow for anyone looking to master the art of cake decorating and baking.
  4. Baking a Moment For fans of sweet and indulgent treats, Baking a Moment is the perfect destination. This blog features a stunning array of cupcakes, cakes, and other sweet delights, with clear instructions and eye-catching photography to inspire your next baking project.
  5. Joy the Baker Joy the Baker is a vibrant and engaging blog that showcases the joy of baking in all of its forms. With a focus on creative and innovative recipes, this blog is a must-follow for anyone looking to add some excitement to their baking routine.
  6. How to Celebrate Everything For those looking to bring a touch of elegance to their baking, How to Celebrate Everything is the perfect destination. This blog showcases beautifully-designed cakes, cookies, and other treats that are sure to impress at any gathering.
  7. A Cozy Kitchen A Cozy Kitchen is a warm and inviting blog that celebrates the joy of baking and cooking. With a focus on comforting, homemade treats, this blog is the perfect destination for anyone looking to add some warmth to their recipe repertoire.
  8. Tieghan Gerard Tieghan Gerard's blog, Half Baked Harvest, is a stunning resource for creative and innovative baking and cooking recipes. With a focus on seasonal ingredients and eye-catching photography, this blog is a must-follow for anyone looking to bring some excitement to their kitchen.
  9. Sweet Amaris Sweet Amaris is a vibrant and engaging blog that showcases the beauty and simplicity of classic desserts. With a focus on traditional baking techniques and high-quality ingredients, this blog is the perfect destination for anyone looking to improve their baking skills.
  10. Bravely Baking Last on our list is the inspiring Bravely Baking blog. Hosted by Rachel Morsch, this blog is a must-follow for anyone looking to improve their baking skills and build confidence in the kitchen. With clear instructions, expert advice, and beautiful photography, Bravely Baking is the perfect destination for anyone looking to take their baking to the next level. In conclusion, these 10 baking blogs offer a wealth of knowledge, expertise, and inspiration to help you take your baking to the next level. Whether you're a seasoned baker or just starting out, each of these blogs has something to offer. Follow these blogs today and discover the joy of baking like never before.

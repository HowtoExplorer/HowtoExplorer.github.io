The Best Dance Documentaries That Will Inspire You


Are you a dance enthusiast craving a deeper connection with the world of movement and rhythm? Look no further! Dive into the captivating realm of dance documentaries, where stories of passion, perseverance, and creativity await you. These films will ignite your passion for dance, reveal the struggles and triumphs of dancing legends, and leave you motivated to take your own dancing journey to the next level.

  1. Steps Through Time Head to the Soviet era for a journey through the history of Soviet ballet in the captivating documentary "Steps Through Time." Born out of the world's top ballet schools, Soviet ballet witnessed a revolution in dance history, with ballet breaking away from strict classical tradition and embracing innovators such as Natalia Makarova and Marge Champion. Every frame of this film emphasizes the artistic courage and zest of the dancers who formed the core of Russian ballet, exploring moments in time when technique merged with artistic expression.
  2. Made in Glasgow Dance isn't just limited to professional studios; for one RCA artist, the streets are her canvas. Observing dancer and filmmaker Ruth Aylett dance through the vibrant streets of Glasgow allows audiences to observe Scotland through a different lens.

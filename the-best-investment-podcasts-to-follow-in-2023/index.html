The Best Investment Podcasts to Follow in 2023


In recent years, the investing world has witnessed a renaissance of accessible financial education, sparked primarily by the rapid growth of podcasting. Investment podcasts have become a cornerstone for individuals seeking financial independence, aspiring to master the stock market, or simply striving to build generational wealth. Whether you’re a beginner just starting on your investing journey or an experienced trader seeking new insights, the right investment podcast can play a pivotal role in your financial growth.

To help you navigate the ever-expanding universe of financial content, we’ve curated a list of the best investment podcasts to follow in 2023. These podcasts, hosted by seasoned experts and industry veterans, deliver sharp insights into stocks, real estate, cryptocurrency, personal finance, economic trends, and much more. Let’s dive into the top picks.

1. We Study Billionaires

If there’s one podcast that consistently tops the charts in the investment category, it’s We Study Billionaires. Hosted by Preston Pysh and Stig Brodersen, this podcast focuses on analyzing the strategies of the world’s most successful investors—Warren Buffett, Charlie Munger, Ray Dalio, and others. Episodes cover key lessons in value investing, behavioral economics, and market trends.

One of the standout qualities of this podcast is its balance between advanced concepts and beginner-friendly explanations. The hosts often pair technical topics with actionable takeaways, ensuring listeners leave with both knowledge and practical steps to apply. Additionally, those who are interested in Bitcoin and decentralized finance will enjoy Preston Pysh’s deep dives into cryptocurrency.

2. Motley Fool Money

Motley Fool has long been a trusted name in investing, and their Motley Fool Money podcast continues to deliver valuable insights to its audience. Featuring a roundtable discussion format, the show brings together seasoned analysts to break down stock trends, earnings reports, and market predictions.

The conversational tone makes it easy to digest complex investing ideas, while the humor and banter of the hosts keep the episodes engaging. The podcast also covers broader economic topics, such as inflation, GDP growth, and Fed interest rate decisions, making it an excellent resource for understanding the macroeconomic factors that influence investments.

3. Invest Like the Best

Patrick O’Shaughnessy’s Invest Like the Best is widely regarded as one of the most insightful podcasts in the investment space. Patrick invites top investors, CEOs, and thought leaders to discuss their strategies, frameworks, and philosophies for building wealth and driving innovation. The emphasis here is on long-term thinking and mastering mental models that work in various environments.

This podcast is a treasure trove for entrepreneurs, venture capitalists, and growth-oriented investors. Patrick’s knack for asking incisive questions often leads to fascinating, in-depth discussions about industries such as technology, real estate, and private equity.

4. The Dave Ramsey Show

For those focused on personal finance and investing as part of a broader financial strategy, The Dave Ramsey Show is a must-listen. Ramsey’s straightforward advice on budgeting, debt elimination, and retirement planning has helped countless listeners get back on track financially.

What sets this podcast apart is the relatable, real-world perspective it offers. Listeners frequently call in with their own financial struggles, and Ramsey provides guidance tailored to their specific situations. His investment advice is rooted in simplicity and consistency, making this podcast ideal for those who want to keep things practical.

5. Mad Money with Jim Cramer

A household name in financial media, Jim Cramer brings his energetic style and encyclopedic market knowledge to his podcast, Mad Money. This show is perfect for investors who want to stay updated on the latest stock picks, earnings reports, and market-moving news.

While Jim’s personality is larger than life, his insights are rooted in decades of investing experience. He doesn’t shy away from controversial or bold opinions, making the podcast an entertaining and educational listen for anyone looking to gain an edge in the markets.

6. Money For the Rest of Us

In Money For the Rest of Us, David Stein explains how to invest and live without worrying about running out of money. The podcast covers traditional stock investing, bonds, real estate, and even unconventional options like peer-to-peer lending. What makes this show stand out is its holistic approach to investing and financial independence.

David emphasizes the importance of understanding economic principles and using them to make better decisions. His storytelling style is both engaging and accessible, making this podcast a valuable resource for anyone who wants to better manage risk and navigate financial uncertainties.

7. BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcast

For those interested in real estate investing, the BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcast is the gold standard. Hosted by industry experts David Greene and Rob Abasolo, this podcast is a one-stop shop for advice on buying properties, scaling your portfolio, and building passive income through real estate.

The show features interviews with investors of all experience levels, from novices to multi-million-dollar property owners. It also provides actionable strategies for navigating real estate market cycles and identifying profitable opportunities in both residential and commercial spaces.

8. Animal Spirits

Barry Ritholtz and Ben Carlson bring humor and hard-hitting analysis to Animal Spirits. This podcast blends behavioral finance with investing insights, offering valuable context around market trends, investment strategies, and personal finance principles.

Both hosts are highly knowledgeable and have a knack for explaining complex topics like asset allocation, diversification, and financial planning. Whether you’re a fan of macroeconomic discussions or looking for tips on building an investment portfolio, Animal Spirits offers content to suit your needs.

Why Podcasts Are an Essential Tool for Investors

The rise of podcasts as a financial education tool represents the democratization of knowledge. Traditional gatekeepers like Wall Street analysts and institutional managers no longer hold a monopoly on investment insights. By tuning in to the right podcasts, everyday investors gain access to the same expertise and strategies previously reserved for professionals.

One of the major benefits of podcasts is their convenience. Whether you’re commuting, working out, or taking a break, you can seamlessly integrate learning into your routine. Unlike books or lengthy articles, podcasts allow you to absorb knowledge passively, making them a perfect fit for busy lifestyles.

Additionally, many podcasts feature guest speakers who are leaders in their respective fields, offering fresh perspectives and cutting-edge ideas. These guest-driven episodes often become mini-masterclasses in specific topics, ranging from tech innovation to real estate flipping.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Investment Podcasts

To make the most of investment podcasts, consider the following tips:

  1. Choose Podcasts Aligning with Your Goals: Whether you’re focused on stocks, real estate, or personal finance, pick podcasts that cater to your specific interests.
  2. Take Notes: Jot down actionable insights or book recommendations to reinforce your learning.
  3. Be Consistent: Regular listening builds deeper understanding over time.
  4. Fact-Check: Cross-reference advice with trusted sources before acting on it.
  5. Engage with Community: Many podcasts have active communities where you can ask questions and exchange ideas.

Final Thoughts

Investment podcasts have transformed how people learn about wealth building. The medium provides an unprecedented level of access to the industry’s brightest minds, fostering a culture of self-education and financial empowerment. As 2023 unfolds, consider adding these standout podcasts to your rotation to sharpen your investing acumen and achieve your financial goals.

Effective investing requires both knowledge and action. Start by tuning into one of the podcasts mentioned above and see how their advice can shape your approach to building wealth. Whether you’re saving for retirement, buying your first rental property, or exploring cryptocurrency, there’s something for everyone in the world of investment podcasts.

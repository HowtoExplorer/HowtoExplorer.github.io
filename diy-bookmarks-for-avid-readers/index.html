DIY Bookmarks for Avid Readers


Creating your own DIY bookmarks is both a fun and practical project for avid readers, providing a customized way to mark one’s place in a book while adding a personal touch. These functional and creative bookmarks make ideal gifts for bookworms and are perfect for pairing with newly acquired novels or for your very own reading pleasure.

Materials Needed:

  • Cardstock or lightweight cardboard
  • Assorted colorful papers, such as wrapping paper, tissue paper, or unique scraps
  • Scissors
  • Hole puncher
  • Ribbon, twine, or string
  • Clear adhesive gem stickers (optional)
  • Pencil or measure tape

Step 1: Choose Your Design

Before diving into the crafting process, decide on your bookmark’s design. Consider the book’s theme or your reader’s preference. Trends include basic rectangles or circles, nature-inspired motifs, book-related images, such as bookshelves or open books, or elaborate patterns.

Step 2: Gather Supplies and Prepare Workspace

Gather the supplies and set up a workspace where you can comfortably cut and assemble your bookmarks. Having all the required materials at hand can make a huge difference in avoiding interruptions and streamlining the creation process.

Step 3: Outline and Cut Bookmarks

Begin by outlining your chosen design on your cardstock. To create a simple rectangle, for instance, use a pencil or ruler to draw lines on the cardstock. Measure and mark 5-6 inches long and 1-2 inches wide. Children might prefer playing with shapes and sizes to create unique markers suited to their preferences.

Step 4: Cut Out the Bookmarks

Carefully cut out the outlined designs using a scissors. Ensure you’ve accurately measured and outlined to avoid wasting material. Remember to keep the markings on the cardstock and cut parallel to them to maintain the accurate desired size.

Step 5: Poke Holes for String

Place your bookmark flat on a working surface and punch holes through the top edges using a hole puncher. Take care not to poke all the way through, but create a small hole small enough for your chosen string or ribbon to fit comfortably.

Step 6: Assemble the Bookmark

Cut the chosen ribbon, twine, or string to match the length of your bookmark. Attach the string to your bookmarks by weaving it through the holes using a tapestry needle. You may also opt for using hot glue to securely fix the ends. If your bookmark design requires additional embellishments, such as gem stickers, apply them now.

Step 7: Finalizing Your Bookmark

After completing your bookmark, let your reader enjoy their favorite reads with the bookmark as a companion. Your bookmark serves as an accessory that not only marks pages but as a personalized reminder of your thoughtfulness and creativity.

DIY bookmarks provide endless possibilities to reflect a reader’s personality and interests while promoting a sense of creativity. These beautifully created and functional bookmarks will not only help readers stay engaged within their reading material but also carry short messages, inspiring quotes, or alluding to the book’s theme. Go ahead, grab your supplies, and let your imagination run wild as you create unique and unforgettable bookmarks that express your individuality.

The Best Crafting Blogs to Follow for Inspiration


In a world where creativity thrives, crafting has emerged as a hobby that transcends age, skill level, and purpose. Whether you’re an experienced artisan or someone picking up scissors and glue for the very first time, the crafting community has something valuable to offer everyone. The internet has opened the floodgates to an endless stream of inspiration, tutorials, and insights into the world of DIY projects. From intricate home décor pieces to handmade gifts, the right crafting blog can serve as both a learning hub and an endless source of motivation.

To help spark your next creative endeavor, we’ve curated a list of some of the best crafting blogs brimming with tutorials, tips, and inspiration for all levels of expertise. These blogs cover a diverse range of crafting niches, including paper crafts, sewing, upcycling, woodworking, and more. Dive in and discover your next obsession.

1. A Beautiful Mess

If bold, colorful, and modern designs are your style, “A Beautiful Mess” is the perfect blog to add to your bookmarks. Run by sisters Elsie Larson and Emma Chapman, this vibrant blog covers a variety of craft projects, including DIY home upgrades, handmade gifts, and art pieces. Beyond crafting, the blog also features home décor and photography tips, making it a one-stop-shop for creative inspiration.

What sets “A Beautiful Mess” apart is its approachable technique. Many of the projects are easy to follow with step-by-step instructions and a laid-back tone that lets beginner and intermediate crafters feel equally welcome. From creating quirky wall art to repurposing household items, this blog is filled with versatile ideas that can be adapted to suit anyone’s tastes.

2. Lia Griffith

For those who love intricate, professional-looking crafts, Lia Griffith’s blog is an absolute treasure trove. Lia is best known for her phenomenal paper flowers and gift wrapping ideas, which are often so realistic or artful that you might do a double-take. But her talents don’t stop there; the site also showcases felt creations, general paper crafting, and home décor projects.

One of the blog’s standout features is its comprehensive patterns and templates, which allow crafters to replicate her designs with ease. Lia even includes tutorials for creating new tools or learning advanced crafting methods, making this blog ideal for anyone experienced or looking to step up their skills.

3. The Craft Patch

If practical yet stunning craft projects are more your speed, then “The Craft Patch” is a must-visit. Designed for hobbyists with busy lives, this blog features countless tutorials for seasonal decorations, kid-friendly crafts, and budget-friendly DIYs. The author, Jennifer, excels at finding creative uses for everyday materials, ensuring that no project feels out of reach.

What makes “The Craft Patch” especially engaging is its focus on practicality. Most of the projects are straightforward yet beautiful, allowing crafters to feel accomplished without spending an excessive amount of time or money. The blog also frequently highlights affordable craft supplies and clever hacks to make crafting accessible to all.

4. Make and Takes

If you’re a parent looking for crafting activities to do with your children or a teacher searching for educational DIYs, “Make and Takes” will fit the bill. This blog is filled with family-friendly projects, seasonal craft ideas, and simple how-to guides for creating memories with your kids while making something unique.

The blog owner, Marie, takes an intuitive approach by categorizing her tutorials based on holidays, seasons, and age groups, making it easy to find the perfect craft for any situation. Crafting with kids can sometimes get messy, but her suggestions for easy-to-clean projects and minimal supplies simplify the process. Whether crafting miniature fairy gardens or homemade cards, this blog is all about bonding while making art.

5. Oh Happy Day

Celebrations become even more special with the delightful DIY ideas from “Oh Happy Day.” Specializing in party crafts, decor, and unique gift ideas, this blog is a dream for anyone who loves throwing events with a personal touch. Think fun piñatas, stunning balloon installations, and quirky dinner table settings.

Jordan Ferney, the creative force behind the blog, has an eye for playful, youthful aesthetics that make every design pop. Many of the tutorials use inexpensive materials, ensuring you can create show-stopping looks without breaking the bank. If you love vibrant colors and memorable touches, you’ll definitely want “Oh Happy Day” on your list of must-follow blogs.

6. We Are Scout

For crafters who draw inspiration from design, lifestyle, and stylish minimal aesthetics, “We Are Scout” deserves a spot in your creative arsenal. Run by Lisa Tilse, the blog provides exquisite tutorials for an assortment of crafts, from papercrafts and embroidery to jewelry-making and custom room décor.

What stands out about this blog is its ability to combine contemporary trends with timeless crafting techniques. Lisa’s step-by-step guides are highly detailed and accompanied by easy-to-follow images, ensuring that even the most intricate designs remain approachable. “We Are Scout” caters to those looking for chic, elevated craft projects that can easily double as home décor or boutique-quality gifts.

7. Honestly WTF

Part crafting corner, part design haven, “Honestly WTF” strikes a balance between creativity and personal style. Founded by Erica Chan Coffman, this blog is home to a range of DIY tutorials that are often trendy, unique, and incredibly fun to make. You’ll find an eclectic mix of accessories, jewelry, and home décor tutorials inspired by global and bohemian aesthetics.

The projects often explore the unconventional use of materials, which encourages readers to think outside the box and experiment. The authentic storytelling and photos accompanying every tutorial make this blog an inspiring journey as much as it is an instructional platform.

8. One Dog Woof

Crochet enthusiasts and yarn devotees, rejoice! “One Dog Woof” is a crochet-centric blog that offers everything from patterns for adorable amigurumi to functional home accessories like baskets and blankets. ChiWei, the creator behind the blog, also incorporates tips for beginners and detailed tutorials on enhancing specific techniques.

Each project is thoughtfully designed to cater to various skill levels, so whether you’re just starting or an advanced crocheter, there’s something here to challenge and delight you. Beyond crochet, ChiWei also dabbles in other crafts, making this blog a versatile resource for crafters of all kinds.

9. Happy Hour Projects

For those who find joy in express crafting, Adrianne’s “Happy Hour Projects” is ideal. The blog specializes in crafts and DIY projects that can be completed in an hour or less, perfect for crafters who want quick, satisfying results.

Catering to all skill levels, the blog includes an array of tutorials for jewelry-making, home décor, cookbooks, and even beginner woodworking. Adrianne’s approachable voice and simple instructions ensure that crafting is a stress-free experience for all visitors looking for instant gratification.

10. Crafty Morning

Creative minds who love discovering ways to reuse everyday objects in exciting new ways will find a home at “Crafty Morning.” From turning old jars into adorable seasonal decorations to designing fun crafts for kids, the site focuses on practical yet imaginative projects.

Crafty Morning is an especially great blog for school assignments, quick gifts, and budget-friendly décor that won’t feel like an afterthought. Many tutorials also include tips for upcycling, making it an excellent resource for crafting sustainably.

These crafting blogs open the door to boundless creativity. Whether you’re interested in gaining new skills, reconnecting with an old hobby, or just browsing for inspiration, keeping up with these creators can spark new ideas for projects you’ll love. Follow, explore, and rediscover the joy and therapeutic magic of crafting!

7 TED Talks to Ignite Your Professional Development Flame


Professional development is a lifelong journey that never truly ends. Whether you’re a fresh-faced newbie in the workforce or a seasoned veteran with decades of experience, there’s always something new to learn and apply to your career. One effective way to fuel your professional growth and discover fresh ideas is by consuming thought-provoking TED Talks. Here are seven TED Talks that can help you jumpstart your journey of continuous learning and elevate your professional development:

  1. “Great Leaders Coach, Don’t Compel” by Bill Hawkins

Did you know that a weak link in your team can negatively affect your company’s overall prosperity? Bill Hawkins reveals why the winning quality of a successful leader is their ability to coach others instead of merely commanding them to act. In short, great leaders empower others to achieve their full potential, ultimately leading to the success of their entire organization.

  1. “How Great Teams Fall and How They Can Get Back Up” by Ee Wei Shaun Teo

Finding ways to sustain excellence and collaborate effectively among team members can be challenging in the workplace. Shaun Ee Wei Teo presents how exceptional teams can stumble in their journey, and how to overcome those falls and rebuild, creating stronger and more resilient teams in the process.

  1. “Connormalize” by Ari Wallach

This talk is a game-changer when it comes to thinking outside the box in the professional world. Ari Wallach presents us with an idea to look at the world through a different lens: the concept of “connormalizing” actions. This perspective encourages us to embrace the unknown, face challenges innovatively, and ultimately prosper in a rapidly changing environment.

  1. “Smarter Than the Average Bear? Think Again” by Chris Berka

In a highly competitive world, one primary quality sets some individuals apart: emotional intelligence. Chris Berka’s TED Talk dives into the reasons why emotionally intelligent people are better equipped to handle the stresses of modern life and suggests practical ways to boost emotional intelligence at work.

  1. “Fearless” by Michelle Poler

One of the biggest blockers in the professional world is undoubtedly fear. In her TED Talk, Michelle Poler shares her inspiring journey, conquering her fears through 100 consecutive challenges. Her talk encourages us to accept and face our fears, providing priceless wisdom on personal and professional growth.

  1. “The Skill of Selfless Service” by Jerry Colonna

Jerry Colonna’s powerful TED Talk delves into the concept of selfless service, which he claims spearheads a healthy work culture. According to his thoughts, taking care of your colleagues can lead to a more prosperous workplace where employees are preventive of each other’s burdens and motivated to deliver their best.

  1. “Your Body Language May Shape Who You Are” by Amy Cuddy

Lastly, Amy Cuddy’s TED Talk unveils the psychology behind body language and its deep connection with success and confidence. She imparts practical tips on improving our physical presence and explains why power postures can accelerate personal and professional success.

These superb TED Talks serve as great starting points for igniting your professional development flame. They can excite your passion, sharpen your work ethic, and make you determined to achieve your higher professional aspirations. So, grab your favorite beverage, get comfortable, and let the wisdom of these speakers shine an enlightening beam on your path of continuous development and growth.

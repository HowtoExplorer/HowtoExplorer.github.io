The Best Scenic Drives in Europe


Europe, a continent renowned for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture, is a paradise for road trippers and nature lovers. With over 50 countries to choose from, the possibilities for an unforgettable scenic drive are endless. From winding mountain roads to picturesque coastal routes, we've carefully curated a list of the most spectacular drives in Europe that will leave you in awe. The Amalfi Coast Drive, Italy Twisting and turning through the hills of Italy's Amalfi Coast is one of the most breathtaking drives in the world. The SS163 road, which connects Sorrento to Salerno, offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea, picturesque towns perched on cliffs, and scenic bays. Pass through Positano, Amalfi, and Ravello, and enjoy the mythical architecture, stunning gardens, and tranquil beaches. Don't forget to stop at the promontory of Conca dei Marini and the stunning harbor of Marina Grande. The Great Ocean Drive, Ireland The Irish countryside is a treat for the senses, with its rolling hills, sparkling lakes, and rugged coastlines. The Great Ocean Drive, which spans over 130 kilometers, is a series of roads that hug the coastline, offering breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean, Atlantic coastline, and the Dingle Peninsula. Drive through the quaint villages, stop at the iconic Slea Head, and marvel at the Cliffs of Moher, a natural wonder that will take your breath away. The Icefields Parkway, Switzerland The Swiss Alps are a haven for road trippers, and the Icefields Parkway is the crown jewel. This 99-kilometer route runs through the heart of the Swiss mountains, offering panoramic views of glaciers, towering peaks, and serene lakes. Drive through the Lauterbrunnen Valley, stop at the famous Trümmelbach Falls, and marvel at the breathtaking scenery. Don't forget to breathe in the crisp mountain air and enjoy the tranquility of the Swiss countryside. The Pacific Coast Highway, Norway The dramatic coastline of Norway is a sight to behold, with fjords, waterfalls, and towering mountains that seem to touch the sky. The Pacific Coast Highway winds its way through the Land of the Midnight Sun, offering unparalleled views of the ocean, beaches, and quaint villages. Drive through Geirangerfjord, one of the world's most breathtaking fjords, and stop at the Troll Wall, a majestic rock formation that's a testament to nature's power. The Ring of Kerry, Ireland Killarney National Park, Ireland's oldest national park, is a treasure trove of natural wonders. The Ring of Kerry drive, which circumnavigates the park, offers a glimpse into the Emerald Isle's stunning landscapes. Pass through Killarney, Dingle, and Sneem, stopping at the majestic Lakes of Killarney and the Gap of Dunloe. Take a deep breath, and breathe in the serene atmosphere, an essence of traditional Irish charm. The Blue Lagoon Route, Iceland Iceland's volcanic landscapes are a dramatic backdrop for any drive. The Blue Lagoon Route, which spans over 200 kilometers, is a road trip like no other. Drive through the Golden Circle, stopping at the geothermal hotspot, Gullfoss, and marvel at the majestic waterfalls and black sand beaches. Visit the Blue Lagoon, a surreal oasis, where geothermal hot water flows from the earth, creating a turquoise beauty. End your journey in Reykjavik, the world's most sustainable city. The Granville-Laganotti Route, Andorra The tiny principality of Andorra is a surprise gem in the Pyrenees, offering breathtaking views of the border between Spain and France. The Granville-Laganotti route, which winds its way through the mountains and forests, is a haven for road trippers. Stop at the picturesque villages, such as Les Angoils, and marvel at the dramatic waterfalls and serene valleys. Visit the medieval town of U вике, and take a deep breath of the crisp mountain air. The Black Diamond Pass, Austria The Austrian Alps are a haven for road trippers, and the Black Diamond Pass is one of the most thrilling drives in Europe. This 670-meter pass road offers breathtaking views of the caves, mummified remains, and the magnificent Natterkil mountains. Pass through the Triebstein Mountain Preserve and the majestic Maria Wörth Lake. Enjoy the serene atmosphere and vast vistas of the Eastern Alps. Final Thoughts Each of these scenic drives offers a unique experience that will leave you awestruck. Take the scenic routes, enjoy the atmosphere, breathe in the fresh air, and tell the stories of your adventures for years to come. Whether you're a seasoned driver or a novice, these routes will test your limits and immerse you in the most breathtaking scenery the continent has to offer.

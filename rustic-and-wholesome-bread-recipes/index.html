Rustic and Wholesome Bread Recipes


Are you tired of store-bought bread that lacks the charm and character of homemade loaves? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore the world of rustic and wholesome bread recipes that will transport you to a cozy farmhouse kitchen with every bite.

The Art of Rustic Bread

Rustic bread is all about simplicity and embracing the rustic aesthetic. It celebrates the imperfect and the handmade, delivering a satisfying crunch on the outside and a soft, chewy interior. From sourdough boules to crusty baguettes, rustic bread is a delight to bake and even better to savor.

Sourdough Boules: A Classic Delight

Let’s start with a classic: the sourdough boule. This bread has a tangy flavor and a chewy texture that will transport you straight to a Parisian bakery. To make your own sourdough boules, you’ll need a sourdough starter, which can be easily created at home using flour and water.

Ingredients:

  • 500g bread flour
  • 350g water
  • 200g sourdough starter
  • 10g salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the bread flour and water. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Add the sourdough starter and salt. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let it rise for 4-6 hours, or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and place a Dutch oven inside to preheat as well.
  5. Shape the dough into a boule and place it on a piece of parchment paper. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Score the top of the boule with a sharp knife or bread lame.
  7. Carefully transfer the boule, along with the parchment paper, into the preheated Dutch oven.
  8. Cover the Dutch oven with its lid and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the lid and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown.
  9. Let the boule cool on a wire rack before slicing and enjoying!

Rustic Baguettes: A Crusty Delight

Next up, we have the iconic rustic baguettes. These long, slender loaves are perfect for tearing apart and dunking into a bowl of soup or slathering with butter and jam. With their crackly crust and airy crumb, homemade baguettes are a true delight.

Ingredients:

  • 500g bread flour
  • 350g water
  • 10g salt
  • 5g instant yeast

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the bread flour, water, salt, and instant yeast. Mix until a sticky dough forms.
  2. Transfer the dough onto a well-floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough back into the bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and place a baking stone or baking sheet inside to preheat as well.
  5. Divide the dough into three equal portions and shape each into a baguette shape.
  6. Transfer the shaped baguettes onto a well-floured peel or baking sheet.
  7. Let the baguettes rest for 30-45 minutes, allowing them to rise slightly.
  8. Score the tops of the baguettes with a sharp knife or bread lame.
  9. Carefully transfer the baguettes onto the preheated baking stone or baking sheet.
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the baguettes are golden brown and sound hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  11. Allow the baguettes to cool on a wire rack before slicing and enjoying!

Conclusion

There you have it – two delightful rustic bread recipes to bring warmth and joy to your kitchen. Whether you choose the tangy sourdough boules or the crusty baguettes, these recipes will surely satisfy your cravings for rustic, homemade goodness. So put on your apron, gather your ingredients, and let the aroma of freshly baked bread fill your home. Happy baking!

Note: Remember, baking bread is an art that requires practice and patience. Don’t be discouraged if your first attempts don’t turn out perfectly. With time, you’ll become a master of rustic bread baking.

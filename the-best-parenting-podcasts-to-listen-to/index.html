The Best Parenting Podcasts to Listen To


Parenting can feel like a never-ending journey of growth, learning, and discovering what works best for both you and your children. In today’s fast-paced world, it’s easy to become overwhelmed with the many challenges that come with raising children. One of the best ways to navigate this path is by tapping into the wealth of knowledge and advice available through podcasts. Parenting podcasts have become incredibly popular because they allow parents to gain insights, strategies, and support at their own convenience.

Whether you’re a new parent, a seasoned one, or looking for some expert advice, podcasts can offer valuable tips and guidance. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best parenting podcasts that cover everything from early childhood development to navigating the teenage years, creating a balanced family life, and fostering strong parent-child relationships.

1. The Longest Shortest Time

The Longest Shortest Time is a parenting podcast that is known for its unique blend of humor, storytelling, and expert advice. Hosted by Hilary Frank, the show tackles various aspects of parenthood, from the joy of new babies to the struggles of raising older children. One of the most remarkable things about this podcast is its inclusivity. It covers a wide range of topics like pregnancy, sleep deprivation, postpartum mental health, and parenting in the modern world. The show provides relatable stories from real parents and experts who discuss everything from difficult situations to delightful moments, offering listeners a balanced perspective.

Whether you’re looking for practical advice or just want to hear personal stories from other parents, The Longest Shortest Time offers an excellent mix of both. It’s a great choice for any parent, especially those who enjoy engaging, heartwarming stories.

2. The Mom Hour

Hosted by Meagan Francis and Sarah Powers, The Mom Hour is an incredibly popular parenting podcast that provides down-to-earth advice, expert tips, and practical strategies for modern moms. This podcast is perfect for busy moms who are juggling multiple responsibilities and want to find tips to simplify their parenting journey. The hosts bring a positive, calm, and real approach to parenting, focusing on issues such as time management, self-care, and managing family dynamics.

The Mom Hour covers a wide variety of topics, such as handling school routines, managing screen time, creating healthy family habits, and navigating parenting challenges with humor and compassion. It’s ideal for anyone looking for a fresh perspective on parenting and for those who need a reminder that they’re not alone on this journey.

3. The Happiness Lab with Dr. Laurie Santos

While The Happiness Lab with Dr. Laurie Santos isn’t specifically a parenting podcast, it is an incredible resource for parents looking to cultivate more happiness and well-being in their lives and families. Dr. Laurie Santos, a psychology professor at Yale University, focuses on research-based strategies to improve happiness. Her podcast provides scientifically-backed insights into building emotional resilience, cultivating gratitude, and creating better habits for emotional well-being.

Parenting can be stressful, and often, parents feel overwhelmed. The Happiness Lab offers practical advice for increasing happiness, which, in turn, can positively impact the way parents interact with their children and manage family life. This podcast is a must-listen for those who want to improve their overall well-being and create a positive, supportive environment for their families.

4. What Fresh Hell: Laughing in the Face of Motherhood

If you’re looking for a lighthearted and humorous take on parenting, What Fresh Hell: Laughing in the Face of Motherhood is the podcast for you. Hosted by comedians Margaret Ables and Amy Wilson, this podcast offers a witty, relatable look at the daily chaos that comes with motherhood. Each episode covers parenting topics in a fun and comedic way, from toddler tantrums to teenager rebellion.

What makes this podcast stand out is its ability to find humor in the most challenging aspects of parenting. Margaret and Amy provide advice, stories, and solutions while making their listeners laugh. It’s the perfect podcast for parents who need a break from the serious side of parenting and want a good laugh while still learning valuable insights.

5. Parenting Beyond Discipline

Parenting Beyond Discipline is a podcast hosted by Erin Royer, a child development expert, who focuses on positive discipline strategies. Unlike traditional approaches to parenting that often rely on punishment, this podcast emphasizes respectful communication and nurturing positive behavior in children. Erin offers practical tips on setting boundaries, creating healthy family structures, and fostering strong, trusting relationships with children.

This podcast is an excellent choice for parents who are interested in moving away from outdated discipline methods and want to focus on positive reinforcement and emotional intelligence. The insights offered by Erin can help parents foster a peaceful and supportive home environment, where both children and parents feel heard, understood, and respected.

6. The Modern Dads Podcast

The Modern Dads Podcast is an insightful and entertaining show designed for fathers who want to learn more about the challenges and rewards of modern fatherhood. Hosted by a group of dads who are actively involved in parenting, the podcast dives into topics such as work-life balance, the evolving role of fathers, and the emotional challenges of fatherhood.

One of the key strengths of The Modern Dads Podcast is its focus on making fatherhood a more inclusive and supportive experience. The hosts share their personal stories, provide practical advice, and talk about the importance of emotional involvement in parenting. This podcast is perfect for fathers who are looking for a place to relate to other dads and gain useful insights to help them navigate their parenting journey.

7. Your Parenting Mojo

For parents who are deeply invested in understanding child development and the science behind parenting, Your Parenting Mojo is a must-listen. Hosted by Jen Lumanlan, a mother and educator, this podcast combines the latest research in child development with practical parenting strategies. Jen brings evidence-based insights to the table, covering topics like positive discipline, attachment parenting, sleep, and language development.

What sets Your Parenting Mojo apart is its scientific approach to parenting, allowing parents to understand why certain strategies work and how to implement them effectively. This podcast is an excellent choice for parents who are eager to learn more about the underlying principles of child development and apply research-backed methods to their daily routines.

8. The Raising Curious Learners Podcast

If you want to provide your child with the tools to become a lifelong learner, The Raising Curious Learners Podcast is an ideal resource. Hosted by the experts at the Smithsonian Institution, this podcast explores ways to encourage curiosity, creativity, and learning in children. The episodes dive into topics like fostering problem-solving skills, encouraging exploration, and nurturing a love of learning from a young age.

This podcast is perfect for parents who want to create an environment that fosters intellectual growth and curiosity. It provides actionable strategies to help children develop critical thinking skills, explore the world around them, and become independent learners.

9. The Parent Cue Podcast

The Parent Cue Podcast is all about helping parents nurture their child’s spiritual, emotional, and social growth. Hosted by parents, church leaders, and child development experts, the podcast offers advice on raising children in a faith-based environment while addressing practical parenting topics. Whether you’re navigating the ups and downs of a child’s emotional development or looking for ways to connect more deeply with your children, The Parent Cue provides a helpful framework for parenting with intention.

This podcast is especially great for parents who want to incorporate values, character building, and spiritual guidance into their children’s lives. It’s a great resource for any parent seeking to be more intentional in their parenting journey.

10. The Motherly Podcast

Motherly is a leading parenting brand that celebrates motherhood and provides tools for parents at every stage of their journey. The Motherly Podcast features conversations with leading experts in the fields of psychology, health, and personal development. Host Liz Tenety discusses topics that range from mental health to self-care, making this podcast a great resource for parents looking for advice on balancing family life with personal growth.

Motherly’s approach is grounded in the idea that self-care is essential for being the best parent you can be. The podcast offers advice and encouragement to help parents take care of themselves while raising children. It’s an inspiring show that encourages mothers (and fathers) to find joy, balance, and growth in their parenting journey.

Conclusion

Parenting podcasts offer an incredible opportunity for parents to learn, grow, and connect with others on the same journey. Whether you are looking for advice on child development, seeking support for your mental health, or simply need a good laugh, there is a podcast out there for every parent. The best parenting podcasts provide valuable insights, expert advice, and real-life stories to help you navigate the ups and downs of parenting with confidence.

By listening to these shows, parents can gain new perspectives, discover helpful strategies, and be reminded that they are not alone in their challenges. So, the next time you’re looking for parenting advice or just need some inspiration, give one of these podcasts a listen. You’ll find that the journey of parenthood becomes a little bit easier when you’re equipped with the right tools and the support of a community.

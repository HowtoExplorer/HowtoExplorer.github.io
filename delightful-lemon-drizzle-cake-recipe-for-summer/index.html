Delightful Lemon Drizzle Cake Recipe for Summer


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in light and refreshing desserts, and what better way to satisfy your sweet tooth than with a delightful lemon drizzle cake? This tangy and moist treat is the epitome of summer flavors, and it’s surprisingly easy to make. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or simply want to enjoy a slice with your afternoon tea, this lemon drizzle cake recipe will surely impress your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

To create this mouthwatering lemon drizzle cake, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 200g unsalted butter, softened
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a 20cm (8-inch) round cake tin. Line the base with parchment paper for easy removal later.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and caster sugar until pale and fluffy. This can be easily done using an electric mixer or by hand with a wooden spoon.

  3. Gradually add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. This step ensures a light and airy texture in the final cake.

  4. Sift the self-raising flour into the bowl and gently fold it in using a spatula. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

  5. Add the lemon zest and juice to the batter, giving it a vibrant citrusy flavor. Stir until well combined.

  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake tin and smooth the top with the back of a spoon.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 40-45 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. While the cake is still warm, prepare the lemon drizzle by combining the juice of 1 lemon with a few tablespoons of granulated sugar. This will create a sweet and tangy syrup that will soak into the cake, adding an extra burst of flavor.

  9. Using a skewer or toothpick, poke several holes all over the surface of the cake. This will allow the drizzle to seep into the cake, making it incredibly moist and flavorful.

  10. Carefully pour the lemon drizzle over the warm cake, ensuring it covers the entire surface. Allow the cake to cool completely in the tin before removing.

  11. Once cooled, transfer the cake to a serving plate and dust with a sprinkle of icing sugar for a beautiful finishing touch.

Serving Suggestions

This delightful lemon drizzle cake is perfect on its own, but you can also get creative with your serving suggestions. Here are a few ideas to inspire you:

  • Serve a slice of lemon drizzle cake with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a decadent dessert.
  • Pair it with a cup of freshly brewed tea or iced lemonade for a refreshing summer treat.
  • Garnish each slice with a twist of lemon peel or a sprig of fresh mint for an elegant presentation.

Conclusion

There you have it – a simple yet incredibly delicious lemon drizzle cake recipe that will impress your family and friends this summer. The tangy lemon flavor combined with the moist texture makes it a perfect dessert for warm weather gatherings or as a special treat for yourself. So, why not give this recipe a try and indulge in a slice of delightful lemony goodness? Your taste buds will thank you!

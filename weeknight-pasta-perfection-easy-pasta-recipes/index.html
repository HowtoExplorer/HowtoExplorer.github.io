Weeknight Pasta Perfection: Easy Pasta Recipes


Are you in need of quick and delicious dinner ideas for busy weeknights? Look no further than these easy pasta recipes that are sure to become staples in your home. Whether you’re craving a creamy carbonara, a zesty marinara, or a light and fresh primavera, there’s a perfect pasta dish for every night of the week. Let’s dive into the world of weeknight pasta perfection with these mouthwatering recipes.

Creamy Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the diced chicken until no longer pink. Add minced garlic and sauté for an additional minute.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  4. Toss the cooked fettuccine in the creamy sauce until well coated. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve the creamy chicken Alfredo with a sprinkle of fresh parsley on top.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking until the garlic turns golden.
  3. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the garlic-infused oil, adding a splash of pasta water to create a silky sauce.
  4. Season with salt and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley before serving.

Lemon Asparagus Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 10 oz linguine
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the linguine in a large pot of boiling salted water. During the last 3 minutes of cooking, add the asparagus to the pot.
  2. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the linguine and asparagus.
  3. In the same pot, melt the butter and add the lemon zest and juice. Toss in the cooked pasta and asparagus, adding a splash of pasta water as needed.
  4. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is creamy. Season with salt and pepper before serving.

Pesto Tortellini Primavera

Ingredients:

  • 20 oz cheese tortellini
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup prepared pesto
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the cheese tortellini according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the cooked tortellini with cherry tomatoes, baby spinach, and prepared pesto.
  3. Add toasted pine nuts for a crunchy texture and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve the pesto tortellini primavera warm or at room temperature for a refreshing weeknight meal.

Conclusion

These easy pasta recipes are perfect for those hectic weeknights when you need a satisfying meal without spending hours in the kitchen. Whether you prefer a rich and indulgent Alfredo, a simple yet flavorful Aglio e Olio, a bright and citrusy lemon asparagus pasta, or a vibrant pesto tortellini primavera, there’s a dish to suit every palate. With minimal prep and cook time, you can enjoy a homemade pasta dinner that’s both convenient and delicious. Say goodbye to takeout and embrace the joy of weeknight pasta perfection!

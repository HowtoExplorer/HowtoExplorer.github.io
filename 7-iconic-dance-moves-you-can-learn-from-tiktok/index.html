7 Iconic Dance Moves You Can Learn from TikTok


With over a billion active users, TikTok has become a hub for dance enthusiasts and performers alike. From mesmerizing choreography to catchy soundtracks, the platform offers a treasure trove of dance inspirations. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or a beginner looking to learn new moves, TikTok has got you covered. In this post, we'll explore 7 iconic dance moves that you can learn and master from TikTok, and take your dance skills to the next level. The Renegade Dance Move The Renegade dance move took TikTok by storm in 2020 and has been viewed millions of times since then. This slick move involves a series of fluid footwork steps, including a "bouncy" step to the right and a hip-bump to the left. To master the Renegade, practice these steps in front of a mirror and focus on executing the footwork with precision and rhythm.

Step 1: Start with your feet together, with your weight evenly distributed between both feet. Step 2: Take a small step to the right with your right foot, keeping your left foot in place. Step 3: As you transfer your weight onto your right foot, bend your left knee and lift your left foot off the ground. Step 4: Quickly return your left foot to the ground and hop on your right foot, keeping your left foot in place.

Practice the Renegade dance move until you feel comfortable executing the footwork smoothly. Focus on keeping your movements sharp and precise, and don't forget to add your own flair to make the move your own! The Floss Dance Move Remember the Floss dance move from a few years ago? This iconic move originated on TikTok and has been viewed millions of times since then. The Floss dance move involves a series of smooth, flowing motions that look effortless to execute. To master the Floss dance move:

Step 1: Start with your feet shoulder-width apart, with your weight evenly distributed between both feet. Step 2: Tuck your arms close to your body, with your hands resting on your hips. Step 3: Swing your legs back and forth, keeping your knees straight. Step 4: As you swing your legs, bend your knees slightly and lower your body.

The key to mastering the Floss dance move is to focus on executing the smooth, flowing movements. Practice in front of a mirror to ensure you're executing the move correctly. The Milly Rock Dance Move The Milly Rock dance move is another iconic move that has been viewed millions of times on TikTok. This fun, upbeat move involves a series of hand claps and arm movements that are sure to get you grooving. To master the Milly Rock dance move:

Step 1: Start with your feet shoulder-width apart, with your weight evenly distributed between both feet. Step 2: Hold your arms straight out to the sides, with your hands in a gesture as if you're playing a drum. Step 3: As you clap your hands, bring your arms in towards each other, and then back out to the sides. Step 4: Continue clapping your hands and moving your arms in a fluid motion.

The key to mastering the Milly Rock dance move is to practice the hand claps and arm movements in front of a mirror to ensure you're executing the move correctly. The Zabadak Move The Zabadak dance move is a thrilling addition to any routine, originating on TikTok. This adept move involves a series of advanced footwork steps that require some practice to master. To master the Zabadak dance move:

Step 1: Start with your feet together, with your weight evenly distributed between both feet. Step 2: Take a small step to the left with your left foot, keeping your right foot in place. Step 3: As you transfer your weight onto your left foot, bend your right knee and lift your right foot off the ground. Step 4: Quickly return your right foot to the ground and hop on your left foot, keeping your right foot in place.

Practice the Zabadak dance move until you feel comfortable executing the footwork smoothly. Focus on keeping your movements sharp and precise, and don't forget to add your own flair to make the move your own! The Low Rider Move The Low Rider dance move originated in the 1970s and has been revived on TikTok, with millions of views. This fun move involves a series of smooth, swaying movements that look effortless to execute. To master the Low Rider dance move:

Step 1: Start with your feet shoulder-width apart, with your weight evenly distributed between both feet. Step 2: Tuck your arms close to your body, with your hands resting on your hips. Step 3: Sway your hips from side to side, keeping your upper body still. Step 4: As you sway your hips, bend your knees slightly and lower your body.

The key to mastering the Low Rider dance move is to focus on executing the smooth, flowing movements. Practice in front of a mirror to ensure you're executing the move correctly. The Butterfly Dance Move The Butterfly dance move is another iconic move that has taken TikTok by storm. This eye-catching move involves a series of fluid arm movements that look effortless to execute. To master the Butterfly dance move:

Step 1: Start with your feet shoulder-width apart, with your weight evenly distributed between both feet. Step 2: Hold your arms straight out to the sides, with your hands in a gesture as if you're spreading your wings. Step 3: As you "fly" your arms, bring them in towards each other, and then back out to the sides. Step 4: Continue "flying" your arms in a fluid motion.

The key to mastering the Butterfly dance move is to practice the arm movements in front of a mirror to ensure you're executing the move correctly. The Cupid Shuffle Dance Move The Cupid Shuffle dance move is a fun, upbeat move that originated on TikTok. This move involves a series of smooth, swaying movements that look effortless to execute. To master the Cupid Shuffle dance move:

Step 1: Start with your feet shoulder-width apart, with your weight evenly distributed between both feet. Step 2: Tuck your arms close to your body, with your hands resting on your hips. Step 3: Sway your hips from side to side, keeping your upper body still. Step 4: As you sway your hips, bend your knees slightly and lower your body.

The key to mastering the Cupid Shuffle dance move is to focus on executing the smooth, flowing movements. Practice in front of a mirror to ensure you're executing the move correctly. Mastering these iconic dance moves from TikTok takes practice, patience, and dedication. However, with persistence and a willingness to learn, you'll be dancing like a pro in no time.

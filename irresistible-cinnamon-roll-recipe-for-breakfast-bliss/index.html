Irresistible Cinnamon Roll Recipe for Breakfast Bliss


There’s nothing quite like waking up to the warm, sweet aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls. These fluffy, gooey treats are a breakfast favorite for many, and it’s no wonder why. With their irresistible combination of buttery dough, cinnamon-sugar filling, and sweet icing, cinnamon rolls are the perfect indulgence to start your day on a blissful note.

Ingredients

Before we dive into the recipe, let’s gather all the ingredients you’ll need to create these mouthwatering cinnamon rolls:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

For the icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix well.

  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk until warm (about 110°F). Remove from heat and add the melted butter. Stir until well combined.

  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the milk mixture. Add the eggs. Mix until a soft dough forms.

  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  5. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.

  6. Spread the softened butter evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.

  7. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle the mixture evenly over the buttered dough.

  8. Starting from one of the long sides, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Pinch the seam to seal.

  9. Using a sharp knife, cut the log into 12 equal-sized rolls.

  10. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each one. Cover with a clean kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  12. Bake the cinnamon rolls for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  13. While the rolls are baking, prepare the icing by whisking together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  14. Remove the cinnamon rolls from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Drizzle the icing over the warm rolls.

  15. Serve the cinnamon rolls warm and enjoy the blissful taste of homemade goodness!

Conclusion

With this irresistible cinnamon roll recipe, you can bring a little bit of breakfast bliss into your home. The soft, fluffy texture, the delightful swirl of cinnamon and sugar, and the sweet icing on top make these cinnamon rolls a true indulgence. Whether you enjoy them with a cup of coffee or as a special treat for brunch, these homemade cinnamon rolls are sure to bring a smile to your face and make your mornings a little bit sweeter. So go ahead, give this recipe a try and experience the bliss for yourself!

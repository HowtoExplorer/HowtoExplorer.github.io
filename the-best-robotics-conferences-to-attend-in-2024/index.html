The Best Robotics Conferences to Attend in 2024


As advancements in robotics continue to reshape industries and drive innovation, staying connected with the global robotics community is more important than ever. Robotics conferences offer a unique opportunity for researchers, professionals, and enthusiasts to collaborate, share cutting-edge discoveries, and explore the latest trends that will define the future. Below is a list of some of the best robotics conferences to attend in 2024, along with insights into their unique offerings and the value they bring to attendees.

1. International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)

ICRA remains a flagship event in the field of robotics and automation. Organized by the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society, this conference is renowned for its high-quality research presentations, workshops, and technical exhibitions. Scheduled to take place in Yokohama, Japan, from May 13–17, 2024, this event will attract researchers and industry leaders from across the world.

Attending ICRA provides unparalleled exposure to innovative work in robotics vision, AI integration, surgical robotic systems, and autonomous vehicles. A key highlight of this conference is its breadth of topics, which ensures that attendees from diverse domains can find value. From keynote speeches by pioneers in robotics to hands-on demonstrations from leading institutions, ICRA is an unmissable event for anyone serious about the field.

Who Should Attend: Academic researchers, robotics engineers, automation specialists, and tech entrepreneurs.

2. Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS)

RSS is another top-tier conference where leaders in robotics come together to present their latest research. Known for its rigorous selection process and high-impact papers, this conference is ideal for those seeking technical depth. Scheduled to be held from July 9–13, 2024, in Cambridge, UK, RSS brings together interdisciplinary work from both research and applied robotics innovation.

What sets RSS apart is its focus on fostering collaboration among roboticists across various disciplines, including computer science, mechanical engineering, and mathematics. Attendees can expect groundbreaking insights into topics such as robot learning, swarm robotics, robotic manipulation, and ethical considerations in AI-driven machines.

Who Should Attend: Researchers, academics, and data scientists focused on robotics and AI development.

3. International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)

IROS is a premier event for intelligent robotics systems, known for bridging academic research with real-world applications. Taking place in Abu Dhabi, UAE, from October 13–17, 2024, this conference is expected to draw participants from over 50 countries. It features a packed schedule of workshops, symposiums, and exhibitors showcasing the latest technologies.

With a strong emphasis on practical applications, IROS appeals to researchers, developers, and organizations eager to deploy robotics solutions. From advancements in sensor technologies to the progress in human-robot interaction, this event covers the full spectrum of intelligent systems.

Who Should Attend: Startups, tech innovators, researchers, and policy makers.

4. European Robotics Forum (ERF)

Scheduled for March 19–21, 2024, in Rimini, Italy, ERF has earned its reputation as the largest robotics networking event in Europe. Unlike traditional research-focused conferences, ERF provides a platform for industry leaders, SMEs, policymakers, and academia to converge and discuss the market’s future direction.

ERF is highly interactive, offering workshops, discussion panels, and business matchmaking opportunities that go beyond just knowledge sharing. This makes it an excellent forum for entrepreneurs and companies seeking partnerships in robotics and AI-driven solutions. Key areas of focus include the industrial deployment of robotics, ethical regulations around AI, and education in robotics.

Who Should Attend: Entrepreneurs, investors, and professionals in industrial robotics.

5. World Robot Conference (WRC)

Hosted annually in Beijing, China, the WRC showcases advancements in industrial, service, and specialized robotics. In 2024, WRC is set to return in August, bringing together tech enthusiasts, government representatives, and robotics companies to display their most exciting creations.

The exhibitions, alongside engaging workshops, highlight a variety of applications like smart logistics, medical robotics, and wearable exoskeletons. The conference’s global appeal ensures a vibrant mix of attendees, offering plenty of opportunities for international collaboration.

Who Should Attend: Engineers, government stakeholders, and anyone interested in the commercialization of robotics.

6. Automate

Automate, held annually in the United States, focuses on automation solutions and robotic technology designed for industrial and practical applications. Scheduled for May 6–9, 2024, in Chicago, Illinois, this event blends influential keynotes with technology demonstrations aimed directly at businesses.

The emphasis at Automate is on how robotics and AI can help optimize manufacturing processes, supply chain management, and operational efficiencies. Beyond the speaker sessions, attendees will benefit from hands-on training and educational opportunities tailored toward companies looking to integrate robotics into their workflows.

Who Should Attend: Manufacturing professionals, automation managers, and business leaders.

7. AI & Robotics Events Worth Exploring

In addition to the major robotics conferences, several interdisciplinary events also explore the synergy between robotics, artificial intelligence, and industry. Highlighted examples include the Conference on Robot Learning (CoRL) and TechCrunch Robotics. These conferences are worth considering if you’re looking to delve deeper into the intersection of AI and robotics with practical or academic applications.

Highlights of AI-Robotics Events in 2024:

  • CoRL will focus on the latest innovations in reinforcement learning for robotics, making it essential for students and researchers delving into robotic intelligence.
  • TechCrunch Robotics, with its blend of robotics showcases and startup-focus discussions, is a fantastic place to discover the future leaders in robotics-driven entrepreneurship.

Why Robotics Conferences Matter

Attending robotics conferences in 2024 offers far more than just an opportunity to listen to inspiring talks. These events serve as hubs for networking, collaboration, and career advancement. Whether you’re a student, researcher, industry leader, or entrepreneur, these conferences enable you to connect directly with the trailblazers in robotics and discover emerging technologies.

Moreover, the hands-on workshops and live technology demonstrations provide attendees with unique learning experiences that classrooms and online platforms cannot replicate. From cutting-edge AI-powered bots to sophisticated industrial robots, the exposure to real-world applications is invaluable.

Preparing for Robotics Conferences

To make the most of these events, thorough preparation is key:

  • Define Goals: Be clear on what you want to achieve by attending—be it networking, learning, or seeking partnerships.
  • Create a Schedule: With multiple tracks and concurrent sessions, identify those most relevant to your field or interests.
  • Engage with Speakers and Exhibitors: Prepare questions and actively participate in discussions.
  • Follow Event Updates: Most conferences have apps or platforms to connect attendees and share schedules.

As robotics continues to grow as a transformative field, the importance of staying informed and actively involved cannot be overstated. The best robotics conferences in 2024 offer unparalleled opportunities to learn, network, and stay ahead in this dynamic industry. Mark your calendars, prepare strategically, and make the most of these events to shape the future of robotics and automation.

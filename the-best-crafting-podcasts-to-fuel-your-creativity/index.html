The Best Crafting Podcasts to Fuel Your Creativity


Crafting is more than just a hobby – it’s a way of life for many people who find joy and relaxation in creating something beautiful with their own hands. Whether you’re into knitting, crocheting, sewing, woodworking, or any other craft, it’s a wonderful escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. But sometimes, the creative spark can wane, and that’s when the right inspiration becomes crucial. Crafting podcasts can be the perfect solution to reignite your passion and provide you with fresh ideas and techniques.

In this blog post, we’ll dive into some of the best crafting podcasts that are sure to fuel your creativity, inspire new projects, and keep you connected to a community of like-minded crafters.

1. CraftSanity Podcast

Hosted by Jennifer Ackerman-Haywood, the CraftSanity Podcast offers an insightful and engaging take on the world of crafting. Whether you’re a seasoned crafter or just starting out, Jennifer’s podcast is packed with interviews, stories, and tips that are sure to keep you inspired. The episodes cover a wide variety of crafting topics, from knitting and crochet to scrapbooking and DIY projects.

What sets CraftSanity apart is its focus on mental health and creativity. Jennifer often delves into how crafting can be a therapeutic outlet, which is incredibly valuable for anyone looking to unwind and destress. If you’re looking for a crafting podcast that balances creative tips with mindfulness, this one is a must-listen.

2. The Modern Sewciety Podcast

For sewing enthusiasts, The Modern Sewciety Podcast is a goldmine. Hosted by Stephanie, this podcast dives into the modern world of sewing, featuring interviews with fabric designers, pattern makers, and influencers in the sewing community. The podcast covers everything from beginner techniques to advanced sewing tips, so there’s something for everyone.

What makes The Modern Sewciety stand out is its emphasis on community. Each episode features conversations with people who are shaping the world of sewing, and you get a real sense of the passion and creativity that fuels the industry. If you love sewing or want to learn more, this podcast will keep you hooked with its engaging discussions and inspiring guests.

3. The Handmade Studio Podcast

Are you interested in handmade goods, DIY projects, and small businesses? The Handmade Studio Podcast is an excellent choice for anyone who enjoys crafting as both a hobby and a potential business. Hosted by Rachael, the podcast focuses on bringing together creators who have turned their craft into a thriving business.

The episodes are filled with tips for those looking to sell their handmade products, as well as discussions on the challenges and rewards of being a part of the handmade community. Whether you’re a hobbyist or an aspiring entrepreneur, The Handmade Studio Podcast will inspire you to take your craft to the next level.

4. The Yarn Thing Podcast

If you’re a fan of knitting or crocheting, The Yarn Thing Podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Marly Bird, this podcast has everything you need to fuel your passion for fiber arts. Marly interviews expert knitters, crocheters, and designers, offering listeners a wealth of knowledge on a variety of topics.

The podcast is especially great for those looking to improve their knitting and crochet skills, as it covers techniques, trends, and the latest in yarn and tools. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced crafter, The Yarn Thing Podcast is a fantastic resource for discovering new patterns, yarn brands, and techniques.

5. Crafty Planner Podcast

For those who are into paper crafts, scrapbooking, and journaling, The Crafty Planner Podcast is a delightful listen. Hosted by Jennifer, the podcast is all about the world of paper crafting, including card making, scrapbooking, and planners. Each episode features guest experts who share their knowledge and tips on various aspects of the craft.

One of the standout features of this podcast is its focus on mindfulness and organization through crafting. Whether you’re a planner addict or someone who loves to scrapbook, The Crafty Planner Podcast offers inspiration and practical advice to help you take your craft to new heights.

6. The Artful Crafter Podcast

If you’re looking for a podcast that covers a wide range of crafts, The Artful Crafter Podcast is a fantastic option. Hosted by Chris, this podcast is dedicated to inspiring crafters of all skill levels and interests. From jewelry making and painting to knitting and woodworking, The Artful Crafter offers something for everyone.

What makes this podcast particularly engaging is the way it encourages creativity across multiple mediums. The episodes feature interviews with crafters who explore different types of art, and Chris often provides detailed tutorials and tips to help listeners try new things. This podcast is perfect for those who love variety in their crafting adventures.

7. Make and Play Podcast

For those who enjoy combining creativity with playfulness, The Make and Play Podcast is an ideal choice. Hosted by a creative duo, this podcast is all about exploring craft projects that involve play and interaction. From DIY toys and games to fun activities for children, Make and Play encourages listeners to think outside the box and embrace their inner child.

The episodes are upbeat, fun, and full of great ideas for crafters who want to make something whimsical and imaginative. Whether you’re looking to create toys for your little ones or enjoy a craft project with your friends, this podcast will inspire you to bring some fun into your crafting routine.

8. Art for Your Ear Podcast

If you love mixing art with crafting, then Art for Your Ear is a podcast that should be on your radar. Hosted by artist and entrepreneur Danielle Krysa, this podcast features interviews with artists from a variety of fields, including painting, sculpture, and fiber arts. Danielle dives into the creative processes behind each artist’s work, offering insights into how they approach their craft and find inspiration.

The podcast is perfect for anyone looking to infuse their crafting with a bit of artistic flair. Whether you’re interested in mixed media art or want to explore new ways to express yourself through craft, Art for Your Ear will open your mind to new possibilities and approaches to creativity.

9. Crafty Business Podcast

For crafters looking to turn their passion into a business, The Crafty Business Podcast is a must. Hosted by a group of crafting entrepreneurs, this podcast provides practical advice on everything from pricing your products to marketing your handmade goods. Whether you’re just starting a craft business or are looking for ways to grow, the tips and strategies shared on this podcast are invaluable.

What’s great about Crafty Business is that it takes a no-nonsense approach to the realities of running a crafting business. The hosts share their own experiences, both the highs and the lows, and provide actionable advice to help listeners succeed in the competitive world of handmade goods.

10. Makers of the Makers Podcast

Makers are individuals who create with their hands and hearts, and the Makers of the Makers Podcast focuses on the journey of people who craft for both personal and professional fulfillment. This podcast is ideal for anyone interested in stories of passion, perseverance, and creativity.

Each episode features interviews with makers from all walks of life, from those who create art to those who build functional items. The podcast provides an in-depth look at how people use their craft to express themselves, solve problems, and make a living. Whether you’re interested in woodwork, ceramics, or digital arts, Makers of the Makers will inspire you to keep making.

11. The Well Crafted Podcast

The Well Crafted Podcast is perfect for those who love to create with intention. Hosted by a thoughtful and reflective crafter, the podcast explores the deeper connections between creativity, mindfulness, and well-being. Through interviews with makers and creators, the podcast delves into the importance of crafting as a form of self-expression and stress relief.

The episodes are calm, contemplative, and uplifting, offering listeners a way to connect with their creative side on a deeper level. If you’re seeking a podcast that highlights the personal and emotional aspects of crafting, The Well Crafted Podcast will resonate with you.

12. The Make: Podcast

For anyone who loves to tinker, experiment, and explore, The Make: Podcast is a great resource. This podcast is focused on the DIY, tech, and maker community, featuring discussions about various projects, gadgets, and innovations that are changing the way people craft and create. It covers everything from electronics and robotics to woodworking and 3D printing.

Whether you’re interested in the technical side of crafting or just want to learn more about how people are pushing the boundaries of creativity, The Make: Podcast will keep you on the edge of your seat. With interviews, tutorials, and fascinating project ideas, this podcast is a perfect listen for anyone who loves to make things.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking to expand your crafting skills, find inspiration for new projects, or connect with a community of passionate creators, these crafting podcasts offer something for everyone. From knitting and sewing to woodworking and paper crafts, each of these podcasts provides a wealth of knowledge, inspiration, and motivation to keep your creative juices flowing. So, grab your knitting needles, crochet hooks, or sewing machine, and tune in to these incredible podcasts to fuel your creativity!

10 Easy Instant Pot Recipes for Beginners


Instant Pot cooking has taken the world by storm, offering a convenient way to whip up delicious meals quickly and effortlessly. Whether you’re a busy parent looking for a quick dinner solution or you simply enjoy trying new dishes, you’ll love these 10 easy Instant Pot recipes that are perfect for beginners.

  1. Instant Pot Chicken Breast

Start your journey into the world of Instant Pot cooking with this simple yet mouth-watering recipe. Season your chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and any herbs of your choice, and let the Instant Pot do the rest. In just 25 minutes, you’ll have tender and juicy chicken that’s perfect for a quick and easy meal.

  1. Instant Pot Rice

Rice is a versatile side dish that pairs well with a variety of main courses. With the Instant Pot, you can easily make flavorful rice that’s fluffy and perfectly cooked. Combine your favorite rice variety with water and a dash of salt, and let the Instant Pot do the rest.

  1. Instant Pot Vegetables

Looking for a tasty and healthy side dish? Try steaming your favorite vegetables in the Instant Pot. Broccoli, carrots, and cauliflower all cook quickly and maintain their nutritional value in the Instant Pot. Add a touch of flavor with garlic and herbs, and you have a delicious and nutritious meal.

  1. Instant Pot Lentil Soup

Lentils are a protein-packed option that’s perfect for vegetarian and vegan diets. This Instant Pot recipe combines lentils with carrots, celery, and onion to create a hearty and satisfying soup. The Instant Pot does the work for you, leaving you with a hot and ready meal in no time.

  1. Instant Pot Spaghetti

Who says you can’t have restaurant-quality pasta at home? This Instant Pot spaghetti recipe is perfect for busy weeknights. Simply combine spaghetti noodles, a savory tomato sauce, and your choice of proteins in the Instant Pot, and let it do the work for you.

  1. Instant Pot Hard Boiled Eggs

No more waiting for hours for hard-boiled eggs to cook! With the Instant Pot, you can have perfectly cooked eggs in just 5 minutes. Simply place your eggs in a trivet inside the Instant Pot, add water, and let the pressure do the rest.

  1. Instant Pot Yogurt

Indulge in creamy, homemade yogurt without the constant stirring required by traditional methods. With the Instant Pot, you can create a perfect and dairy-rich yogurt in no time. Simply combine your favorite milk and yogurt starter culture, let the Instant Pot do its magic, and enjoy a tasty and nutritious breakfast option.

  1. Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

Who doesn’t love a classic comfort food like mac and cheese? With the Instant Pot, you can create a creamy, delicious version in a fraction of the time. Combine your favorite pasta with a cheese sauce and a touch of garlic for a meal that will delight your taste buds.

  1. Instant Pot Potato Soup

Nothing warms you up quite like a hearty potato soup. This Instant Pot recipe combines potatoes, garlic, celery, and onion with a creamy broth for a delicious soup option. Serve with crackers or crusty bread for a complete meal.

  1. Instant Pot Chili

Lastly, indulge in a flavorful chili that’s perfect for cooler nights. Combine ground beef or turkey with beans, tomatoes, and your favorite chili seasonings in the Instant Pot, and let the pressure create a comforting and delicious meal.

In conclusion, Instant Pot cooking is revolutionizing the way we prepare meals at home. With these easy and delicious recipes, even beginners can experience the joy of creating flavorful and quick dishes with this versatile kitchen appliance.

Tips for Beginners

  1. Read the Instruction Manual: Before you begin, make sure to read the instruction manual thoroughly. This will help you understand the different settings and functions of your Instant Pot, as well as safety precautions.

  2. Practice Patience: Instant Pot cooking requires some time to master. It’s important to be patient and learn the correct pressure and cooking times for each recipe. With practice, you’ll become an expert in no time!

  3. Experiment with Seasonings: Don’t be afraid to experiment with seasonings and herbs in your Instant Pot recipes. Adding unique flavors will help you create delicious and personalized dishes that your family and friends will love.

  4. Keep It Simple: As a beginner, it’s important to start with simple recipes. Once you’re comfortable with the Instant Pot, you can try more complex dishes and experiments.

Benefits of Instant Pot Cooking

  1. Saves Time: The Instant Pot significantly cuts down cooking time, allowing you to prepare delicious meals quickly and efficiently.

  2. Improves Nutrient Retention: The high-pressure cooking method used by the Instant Pot helps to retain nutrients in your food, making each dish more nutritious and flavorful.

  3. Versatile Cooking Options: With the Instant Pot, you can prepare a variety of meals, including soups, stews, rice, beans, and even desserts.

  4. Easy Cleanup: The Instant Pot is easy to clean and maintain, making it a perfect addition to any busy kitchen.

In summary, the Instant Pot has transformed the way we cook at home, offering a convenient and time-saving solution. These 10 easy recipes are perfect for beginners, helping you create delicious and satisfying meals with ease.

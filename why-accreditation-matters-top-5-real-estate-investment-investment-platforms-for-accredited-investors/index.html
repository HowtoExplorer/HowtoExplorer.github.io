Why Accreditation Matters: Top 5 Real Estate Investment Investment Platforms for Accredited Investors


Accreditation holds significant importance when it comes to making real estate investments. It not only grants entry into exclusive investment opportunities but also ensures that serious investors are making well-informed decisions. Real estate investment platforms cater to accredited investors who typically possess a net worth of at least $1 million or earned an annual income of $200,000 in the last two years. Given this eligibility criterion, it opens the door to a unique selection of top platforms that might not be accessible to the general public.

In this blog post, we will delve into five of the top real estate investment platforms exclusively for accredited investors. These platforms focus on global income portfolios, development projects, private placements, and more. Let us take a closer look:

  1. Fundrise

Fundrise is known for revolutionizing the traditional real estate investment scenario by providing easy access to a diversified income portfolio to accredited investors. This platform allows you to invest in eREITs (electronic Real Estate Investment Trusts) that focus on various residential properties and provide a steady income stream. The user-friendly platform emerges as an ideal choice for accredited investors who wish to monitor their investment progress through easy-to-understand reports and statements.

  1. RealtyMogul

RealtyMogul hosts a diverse range of commercial real estate (CRE) offerings ranging from office spaces to industrial buildings and multifamily residential properties. Accredited investors have access to investment opportunities through private placements and REITs. While investing on RealtyMogul, you can enjoy keen insights into each investment’s performance and the ability to make informed decisions better. Furthermore, the platform provides investors with professionals to manage property obligations, and boasts an impressive historical track record with minimal reports of default or foreclosures.

  1. CrowdStreet

CrowdStreet stands out as a dedicated real estate investment platform for accredited investors providing access to gap projects in the public market. The platform boasts a robust portal that facilitates communication between developers and investors, fostering active involvement. With CrowdStreet, accredited investors can engage in crowdfunding opportunities to invest in new development projects or rehabilitating existing properties. The platform’s performance has been solid, boasting an average return of over 20% for its investors.

  1. WealthForge

WealthForge is an alternative investment platform catering mainly to accredited investors seeking access to private placements, investment funds, and top-tier commercial properties. Accredited investors can dive into a diverse range of investment options, including equity REITs, debt funds, real estate operating companies, and more. The platform boasts a tight-knit community of expert real estate consultants, strategically structured to assist investors in making well-informed decisions and ensuring seamless transactions.

  1. RealtyShares

RealtyShares blends massive real estate opportunities with modern crowdfunding technology to offer accredited investors an innovative platform for investing. This platform invites investors to participate in various real estate projects, including commercial spaces, residential properties, and land use projects. With RealtyShares, investors can zero in on specific investment criteria, such as risk level, property type, and location, and enjoy an intuitive dashboard to monitor their investment activity.

It is vital to understand that investing in real estate comes with its risks: market fluctuations, property management issues, and more. However, accredited investors have historically found unique opportunities through platforms tailored for their needs. As you explore these investment options, bear in mind the complex nature of real estate investments and always conduct thorough research before committing your funds. Stay informed, stay vigilant, and let accreditation grant you the endless possibilities in the world of real estate investment.

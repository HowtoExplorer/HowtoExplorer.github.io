Top 10 Must-Do Activities in Dubai


Dubai, a city in the United Arab Emirates, is more than just a hub for business and luxury shopping. This desert oasis in the Middle East has transformed itself into a vibrant and must-visit destination for tourists seeking thrilling experiences. From hearing famous calls of prayer echoing through the mosques to marveling at majestic architectural wonders, Dubai is a feast for the senses. Here are the top 10 must-do activities to experience the essence of this extraordinary city.

Take a moment to enjoy a serene skydiving experience over the serene landscapes, and witness unparalleled tranquility before opening your parachute.
  2. Desert Safari The UAE is home to some of the most arid yet fascinating landscapes on the planet. One cannot miss a desert safari while in Dubai, surrounded by breathtaking dunes that shimmer under the scorching desert sun during the day and glisten with starlight at night.

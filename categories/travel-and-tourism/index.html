When it comes to exploring the world of craft beer, one of the best ways to experience the local flavor is to try the beers that are brewed and consumed in the area. In this article, we will take you on a tour of some of the best local brews from around the world, highlighting the nuanced flavors and brewing techniques that make each region's beer stand out. Asian Breweries to Watch Japan: Kirin's Ichiban Beer Located in the heart of Tokyo, Kirin Brewery is one of Japan's largest and most respected breweries. Their flagship beer, Ichiban Beer, is a crisp, refreshing lager that is perfect for hot summer days in the city. With a rich, malty flavor and a hint of sweetness, Ichiban Beer is a masterclass in Japanese brewing techniques. South Korea: Cass Original Beer South Korea's largest brewery, Cass, has been brewing beer since 1962. Their flagship beer, Cass Original Beer, is a malty, slightly sweet lager that is popular throughout the country. With a low ABV and a crisp finish, Cass Original Beer is the perfect accompaniment to spicy Korean cuisine. Taiwan: Strong Buck Lager Located in Taipei, Taiwan, Zhuang Song's Strong Buck Lager is a brewery that is making waves with its modern take on traditional brewing techniques. With a bold, hoppy flavor and a hint of spice, Strong Buck Lager is a beer that is sure to please even the most discerning palate. European Breweries to Watch Belgium: Delirium Tremens Located in the heart of Brussels, Brouwerij Huyghe's Delirium Tremens is one of the world's most famous beers. With a golden, slightly sweet flavor and a hint of spice, Delirium Tremens is a beer that is unlike anything else. This Strong Ale is brewed with a blend of traditional ingredients and a hint of sublimation, resulting in a truly transcendent brewing experience. Germany: Weihenstephaner Original Located just outside of Munich, Weihenstephaner Brewery is one of Germany's oldest and most respected breweries. Their original beer, brewed in the traditional German style, is a malty, slightly sweet lager that is perfect for warm summer days by the lake. With a rich, malty flavor and a crisp finish, Weihenstephaner Original is a beer that is sure to please even the most discerning palate. England: Adnams Ghost Ship Located on the coast of Suffolk, England, Adnams Brewery is one of the UK's most respected craft breweries.

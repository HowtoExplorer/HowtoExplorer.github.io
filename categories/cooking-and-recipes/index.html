Classic Mac and Cheese Let’s start with the timeless classic - a simple yet indulgent mac and cheese recipe that never goes out of style. To make this creamy delight, you’ll need elbow macaroni, butter, all-purpose flour, milk, cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions, and while it’s cooking, prepare the cheese sauce. Melt butter in a saucepan, whisk in the flour to make a roux, and then gradually add the milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Add the shredded cheddar cheese, season with salt and pepper, and mix until the cheese melts into a smooth, velvety sauce. Finally, combine the cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce, and bake it until the top is golden and bubbly. The result? A rich, gooey mac and cheese that’s sure to satisfy your cravings. ...

Classic Fresh Egg Pasta Ingredients: 2 cups all-purpose flour 2 large eggs 1/2 teaspoon salt Instructions: On a clean surface, pour the flour and make a well in the center. Crack the eggs into the well and add the salt. Using a fork, gently beat the eggs and gradually incorporate the flour until a dough forms. Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until smooth, then wrap it in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.

Double Up Your Lip Products Use your lipstick as a cream blush by dabbing a small amount on your cheeks and blending it out for a cohesive makeup look. Experimenting with shades can help you discover unique lip looks that complement your skin tone and outfit. 7. Use Scotch Tape for Perfect Winged Liner Place a small piece of scotch tape at the outer corner of your eye towards your temple before applying eyeliner. This hack creates a sharp, clean line for a flawless winged eyeliner look. 8. Make Your Eyeshadows Pop with White Base Apply a white eyeliner or eyeshadow base before applying colorful eyeshadows to make the colors appear more vibrant and true to their shade. This hack is perfect for creating bold eye looks. 9. Refresh Your Face with Facial Mist Keep a facial mist handy to refresh your makeup throughout the day. A quick spritz of facial mist can revive your makeup, hydrate your skin, and give you a radiant glow. 10. Clean Your Makeup Brushes Regularly Regularly cleaning your makeup brushes prevents bacteria buildup and ensures smooth and flawless makeup application. Use a gentle brush cleanser or baby shampoo to keep your brushes clean and your skin healthy. Incorporating these makeup hacks into your beauty routine can elevate your makeup skills and make the application process more fun and creative.

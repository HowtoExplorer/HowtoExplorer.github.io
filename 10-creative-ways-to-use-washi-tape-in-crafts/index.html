10 Creative Ways to Use Washi Tape in Crafts


Washi tape, a versatile and adhesive tape made from rice paper, has become a staple in the world of crafting. Its popularity can be attributed to its ease of use, wide range of colors and designs, and the countless creative ways it can be applied. From adding a touch of elegance to a handmade card to creating intricate designs on a canvas, washi tape is a must-have tool for any crafter. In this post, we will explore 10 creative ways to use washi tape in crafts.

1. Add a Pop of Color to a Handmade Card

Washi tape is a great way to add a pop of color to a handmade card. Simply cut out small strips of washi tape and apply them to the card in a random pattern. You can also use washi tape to create borders, frames, or even a background design. The possibilities are endless!

2. Create a Unique Book Cover

Washi tape can be used to create a unique and eye-catching book cover. Cut out a piece of washi tape that is slightly larger than the book cover, and apply it to the cover. You can also add other embellishments, such as stickers or stamps, to create a more intricate design.

3. Add a Touch of Elegance to a Journal

Washi tape is a great way to add a touch of elegance to a journal. Simply cut out small strips of washi tape and apply them to the journal pages in a random pattern. You can also use washi tape to create a bookmark or a page marker.

4. Create a Custom Phone Case

Washi tape can be used to create a custom phone case. Cut out a piece of washi tape that is slightly larger than the phone case, and apply it to the case. You can also add other embellishments, such as stickers or stamps, to create a more intricate design.

5. Make a DIY Photo Frame

Washi tape is a great way to make a DIY photo frame. Cut out a piece of washi tape that is slightly larger than the frame, and apply it to the frame. You can also add other embellishments, such as stickers or stamps, to create a more intricate design.

6. Create a Custom Laptop Skin

Washi tape can be used to create a custom laptop skin. Cut out a piece of washi tape that is slightly larger than the laptop skin, and apply it to the skin. You can also add other embellishments, such as stickers or stamps, to create a more intricate design.

7. Add a Touch of Whimsy to a Planter

Washi tape is a great way to add a touch of whimsy to a planter. Cut out small strips of washi tape and apply them to the planter in a random pattern. You can also use washi tape to create a design or a pattern on the planter.

8. Create a Custom Eraser

Washi tape can be used to create a custom eraser. Cut out a piece of washi tape that is slightly larger than the eraser, and apply it to the eraser. You can also add other embellishments, such as stickers or stamps, to create a more intricate design.

9. Make a DIY Zipper Pouch

Washi tape is a great way to make a DIY zipper pouch. Cut out a piece of washi tape that is slightly larger than the pouch, and apply it to the pouch. You can also add other embellishments, such as stickers or stamps, to create a more intricate design.

10. Create a Custom Keychain

Washi tape can be used to create a custom keychain. Cut out a piece of washi tape that is slightly larger than the keychain, and apply it to the keychain. You can also add other embellishments, such as stickers or stamps, to create a more intricate design.

In conclusion, washi tape is a versatile and creative tool that can be used in a variety of ways to add a touch of elegance, whimsy, or personality to any craft project. Whether you are a seasoned crafter or just starting out, washi tape is a must-have tool that is sure to inspire your creativity and add a unique touch to any project.

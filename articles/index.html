What is Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder? OCD is a chronic disorder that involves two main components: obsessions and compulsions. Obsessions are unwanted, disturbing thoughts, urges, or images that repeatedly invade the mind. These thoughts often create significant distress or anxiety in the individual. Compulsions are repetitive behaviors or mental acts that a person feels compelled to perform in response to an obsession or according to rigid rules.

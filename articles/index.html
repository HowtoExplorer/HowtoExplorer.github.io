Avocado: Avocado is rich in antioxidants and healthy fats, which nourish and hydrate the skin, leaving it glowing and radiant. Lemon: Lemon juice is a natural astringent, helping to tighten pores and brighten the skin. Instructions: Start by gathering your ingredients and a small mixing bowl. Make sure your face is clean and free of any makeup or dirt. In the mixing bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of honey, 1 tablespoon of yogurt, 1 tablespoon of oatmeal, ¼ of a ripe avocado (mashed), and 1 teaspoon of fresh lemon juice. Mix well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined. Once your mask is ready, apply it to your face, avoiding the eye and mouth areas. Use your fingertips to gently massage the mask into your skin in circular motions. Leave the mask on for 15-20 minutes to allow the ingredients to work their magic. Take this time to relax and unwind. After the recommended time, rinse off the mask with lukewarm water. Pat your face dry with a clean towel.

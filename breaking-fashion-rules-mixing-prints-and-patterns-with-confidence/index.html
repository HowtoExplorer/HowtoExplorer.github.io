Breaking Fashion Rules: Mixing Prints and Patterns with Confidence


You know those fashion rules that dictate what you should and shouldn’t wear? Well, it’s time to throw them out the window, especially when it comes to mixing prints and patterns. Gone are the days when you had to stick to one print or pattern per outfit. Today, fashion is all about embracing your creativity and expressing your unique style. So, let’s dive into the world of mixing prints and patterns with confidence!

Embrace Contrast

One of the keys to successfully mixing prints and patterns is to embrace contrast. When you pair contrasting prints together, it adds visual interest and creates a bold statement. Think stripes with florals, polka dots with animal prints, or plaids with geometric patterns. The key is to make sure that there is a clear distinction between the prints, so they don’t blend together and create a chaotic look.

Start Small

If you’re new to mixing prints and patterns, it’s best to start small. Begin by incorporating a printed accessory into your outfit, such as a scarf or a handbag. This allows you to experiment with different combinations without overwhelming your entire look. Once you gain confidence, you can gradually incorporate more prints and patterns into your ensemble.

Stick to a Color Palette

To maintain a cohesive and polished look, it’s important to stick to a color palette when mixing prints and patterns. Choose one or two dominant colors and use them as a base for your outfit. This will help tie everything together and create a harmonious look. For example, if you’re wearing a floral top with pink and green accents, opt for a striped skirt that features the same colors.

Mix Scale and Proportion

Playing with scale and proportion is another way to elevate your print mixing game. Pairing different-sized prints adds depth and dimension to your outfit. For instance, pair a large-scale floral print with a smaller polka dot pattern. This combination creates a visually appealing contrast and adds visual interest to your overall look.

Accessorize Wisely

When it comes to accessorizing a mixed print outfit, less is more. Since your outfit already makes a statement, opt for minimal accessories to avoid overwhelming your look. A simple pair of earrings, a dainty necklace, or a sleek belt can add a touch of elegance without distracting from the prints and patterns.

Trust Your Instincts

At the end of the day, fashion is all about self-expression, so trust your instincts when it comes to mixing prints and patterns. Don’t be afraid to experiment and step out of your comfort zone. After all, fashion is about having fun and expressing your unique personality.

So, the next time you’re feeling adventurous with your wardrobe, remember to break the fashion rules and mix prints and patterns with confidence. Embrace contrast, start small, stick to a color palette, play with scale and proportion, accessorize wisely, and most importantly, trust your instincts. With a little bit of creativity and confidence, you’ll be turning heads and setting trends in no time!

Now, go ahead and raid your closet. It’s time to unleash your inner fashionista and rock those mixed prints and patterns with confidence!

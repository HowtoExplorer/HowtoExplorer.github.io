The Best Fashion Podcasts to Tune Into for Style Tips


In today’s fast-paced world, staying up to date with the latest fashion trends and style tips can be challenging. With the constant influx of new collections, emerging designers, and shifting trends, it’s easy to feel overwhelmed by the ever-evolving landscape of the fashion industry. Thankfully, podcasts have become an essential resource for fashion lovers, offering a wealth of knowledge, inspiration, and advice from industry experts, stylists, and influencers. If you’re looking to refine your personal style or stay informed about the latest happenings in the fashion world, tuning into a fashion podcast can be your go-to solution.

In this blog post, we’ve curated a list of the best fashion podcasts to help you gain valuable insights, fresh style inspiration, and practical fashion tips. Whether you’re a seasoned fashionista or just starting your journey into the world of fashion, these podcasts offer something for everyone.

1. The Business of Fashion Podcast

For anyone interested in the behind-the-scenes workings of the fashion industry, The Business of Fashion Podcast is an essential listen. Hosted by Imran Amed, founder of The Business of Fashion, this podcast provides listeners with in-depth interviews with fashion industry insiders, including designers, brand founders, stylists, and business experts.

The podcast dives deep into the business side of fashion, discussing topics such as sustainability, supply chains, brand development, and the impact of social media on fashion. If you’re looking to learn about the intersection of creativity and commerce, this podcast is a must-listen. The Business of Fashion Podcast offers valuable insights into the challenges and opportunities within the fashion industry, providing listeners with a unique perspective on the business side of style.

2. Dressed: The History of Fashion

Fashion isn’t just about trends—it’s about history, culture, and storytelling. Dressed: The History of Fashion, hosted by fashion historians Cassidy Zachary and April Calahan, takes listeners on a journey through the fascinating history of fashion. This podcast is perfect for anyone curious about how fashion has evolved over the centuries and how historical events have influenced modern style.

Each episode explores iconic fashion moments, designers, trends, and cultural shifts, offering fascinating insights into how fashion has shaped society and vice versa. Whether you’re passionate about vintage fashion, haute couture, or the evolution of street style, Dressed will deepen your appreciation for the art of dressing and the rich history that shapes today’s fashion.

3. The Fashion Hacks Podcast

If you’re looking for practical style tips and life hacks to help you look your best without breaking the bank, The Fashion Hacks Podcast is a great choice. Hosted by stylist and personal shopper Laura McHugh, this podcast is packed with actionable fashion advice for individuals who want to elevate their wardrobe with minimal effort.

From learning how to build a versatile capsule wardrobe to tips on shopping smartly and finding the perfect fit, The Fashion Hacks Podcast offers expert advice on everything from accessorizing to mastering the art of layering. Whether you’re a fashion beginner or a seasoned pro, you’ll find helpful tips and tricks that will improve your personal style and boost your confidence.

4. The Cut on Tuesdays

The Cut on Tuesdays is an offshoot of The Cut, a well-known fashion and lifestyle publication. Hosted by journalist and editor, Haley Mlotek, the podcast offers a unique blend of style advice, interviews with fashion icons, and thought-provoking discussions on cultural and societal issues in the fashion world.

This podcast delves into topics such as gender and identity in fashion, body positivity, and the influence of social media on style. The Cut on Tuesdays is known for its insightful commentary and intelligent approach to fashion, making it an ideal listen for anyone interested in the intersection of fashion, culture, and social issues.

5. Fashion No Filter

For those who want to stay updated on the latest fashion news and trends, Fashion No Filter is the perfect podcast. Hosted by fashion journalists and influencers, Jessica and Kati, this podcast offers a no-nonsense take on the world of fashion. Each episode covers a variety of topics, from the latest runway shows to celebrity fashion and influencer culture.

What sets Fashion No Filter apart is its fun and approachable tone. The hosts offer candid and honest opinions on everything from fashion fails to the latest must-have items. If you’re looking for a podcast that’s both informative and entertaining, Fashion No Filter will keep you hooked with its witty banter and fresh takes on the ever-changing world of fashion.

6. Wardrobe Crisis

Sustainability has become a significant topic in the fashion industry, and Wardrobe Crisis tackles this issue head-on. Hosted by Clare Press, Vogue’s Sustainability Editor-at-Large, this podcast is dedicated to discussing the future of fashion in a world that is increasingly focused on environmental impact and ethical production.

Each episode explores the challenges and opportunities associated with creating a more sustainable fashion industry. Clare interviews thought leaders, designers, and innovators who are working to make fashion more responsible and conscious. If you’re passionate about sustainable fashion and want to learn more about how to make more eco-friendly choices in your wardrobe, Wardrobe Crisis is the podcast for you.

7. The Fashionista Podcast

The Fashionista Podcast is a must-listen for anyone interested in the business and culture of fashion. Hosted by editors and writers from Fashionista, one of the most reputable fashion publications, this podcast covers a wide range of topics related to the fashion industry, including fashion news, trend reports, and career advice.

The podcast features interviews with industry insiders, including designers, fashion journalists, and influencers, offering listeners an inside look into the fashion world. Whether you’re interested in learning about fashion journalism or exploring the latest trends, The Fashionista Podcast provides valuable insights for anyone passionate about style and the business of fashion.

8. Love and Design Podcast

For anyone interested in how design and creativity shape fashion, the Love and Design Podcast offers a unique perspective. Hosted by interior designer and fashion lover Leah, this podcast dives into the creative process behind fashion design, as well as the connections between fashion and interior design.

Leah speaks with a variety of guests, including fashion designers, creatives, and stylists, to explore how design influences not only clothing but also the way we live, think, and express ourselves. If you’re someone who loves to explore the artistic side of fashion, Love and Design will provide you with a fresh and inspiring take on the design world.

9. Fashion Revolution Podcast

The Fashion Revolution Podcast focuses on the social and environmental aspects of fashion, making it an ideal choice for listeners who want to understand the deeper issues surrounding the fashion industry. Hosted by fashion activists and industry experts, this podcast brings attention to important topics such as ethical fashion, workers’ rights, and sustainable design practices.

Through conversations with leading figures in the fashion revolution, the podcast discusses how we can all contribute to making the fashion industry more sustainable and just. Whether you’re interested in sustainable fashion practices or the ethical implications of the clothing you buy, this podcast offers valuable insights into how fashion can become a force for good.

10. Pop Fashion

If you’re a fan of celebrity style and pop culture, Pop Fashion is the podcast for you. Hosted by two fashion enthusiasts, Lisa and Karyn, this podcast combines style commentary with pop culture analysis. They break down celebrity looks, discuss fashion trends, and offer insights into how pop culture influences the fashion industry.

The hosts share their thoughts on everything from the latest red carpet looks to viral fashion moments and fashion week highlights. Pop Fashion is an entertaining and informative podcast that keeps you up-to-date with what’s happening in both the fashion world and the wider world of pop culture.

11. How I Built This - Fashion Edition

While How I Built This is not exclusively a fashion podcast, its fashion edition episodes provide inspiring stories of entrepreneurs who have built successful fashion brands. Hosted by Guy Raz, this podcast takes listeners on a journey through the origins of various brands, offering insights into how fashion entrepreneurs turned their ideas into reality.

From well-known brands like Rent the Runway to smaller, boutique labels, the How I Built This - Fashion Edition provides an inspiring look at the hard work, creativity, and dedication that goes into building a fashion business. If you’re looking for inspiration for your own fashion venture or simply enjoy hearing success stories, this podcast will give you the motivation and knowledge you need.

12. The Style Files

The Style Files is a podcast hosted by fashion stylist and personal shopper, Tori, that focuses on helping individuals build a wardrobe that works for their lifestyle and personal style. This podcast offers a range of practical tips for creating a wardrobe that reflects your personality, fits your body type, and suits your budget.

From seasonal wardrobe updates to advice on how to dress for specific occasions, The Style Files is a great resource for anyone looking to make better, more informed fashion choices. Whether you’re interested in learning how to mix and match pieces, accessorize, or simply create a wardrobe that makes you feel confident, this podcast has something to offer.

Conclusion

Fashion podcasts have become an invaluable resource for those looking to stay updated on trends, gain expert advice, and learn from the best in the industry. Whether you’re looking to refine your personal style, dive deep into the history of fashion, or explore the business side of the industry, there’s a podcast that caters to every fashion interest.

From in-depth interviews with designers to discussions about sustainability and ethical fashion, these podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge and inspiration that can help you elevate your style and stay ahead of the curve. So, grab your headphones, press play, and let these amazing fashion podcasts guide you through the world of fashion.

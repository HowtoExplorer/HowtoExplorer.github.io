The Pursuit of Aliveness: Solving Problems with Passion


In our lives, there’s one constant and ever-evolving factor - change. It not only brings challenges and obstacles but also incredible opportunities for growth and self-actualization. Embracing this change and finding the aliveness within it is what truly makes life worth living. The adventure of solving problems, when approached with a passionate spirit, can be wildly rewarding and lead to immense satisfaction and personal growth. Let’s dive into how we can cultivate this passion and willingly dive headfirst into the problem-solving game.

The Aliveness Within Challenges

The term “aliveness” may sound a bit odd and abstract, but let’s try to comprehend it. Being alive isn’t confined to simply existing or breathing; it’s about experiencing life with zest - feeling its essence and being utterly present in every moment. This aliveness extends to our interactions with problems - when we confront challenges head-on with boundless enthusiasm, we initiate a journey toward self-discovery, resilience, and ingenuity.

Why Passion?

When we talk about problem-solving with passion, we’re not merely discussing productivity or efficiency. Rather, we’re diving deep into a space that connects the spine-tingling thrill of overcoming obstacles with a profound sense of purpose. While the destination of solving a problem is enjoyable, the journey itself is the heart and soul of the passion we’re discussing. It’s a journey filled with learning, self-realization, and connecting the dots of one’s abilities.

  1. The Passionary Lens

To view the world through a passionate lens is to place yourself in a state of constant wonder and curiosity. It’s about embracing the unknown and willingly stepping into the uncharted territories of challenges, emerging with newfound insights, skills, and self-awareness. As you begin your journey with this lens, you’ll marvel at the intricacies of a situation that once seemed so daunting.

  1. The Fuel of Curiosity

Ah, the fuel that keeps the flame of passion burning bright. Curiosity is that little spark in our hearts that propels us to ask the ‘why’s and how’s of any challenge. It paves the way for innovation and helps us break the conventional norms that mold our thought processes. The moment you embrace curiosity, the fear of trying and failing loses its power. Instead, you exchange it with the exhilarating experience of exploring new horizons and confronting challenges knowing that you’ll probable emerge more enlightened.

  1. The Problem-Solving PLAY

Marshall Rosenberg, the founder of Nonviolent Communication, said, “The problem-solving power in each of us suppresses our playfulness.” Let’s redefine our approach to problem-solving by incorporating play. Look at challenges as invigorating puzzles waiting to be solved. Treat problems as if they were board games with unique rules and twists that make every attempt at solving them a thrilling adventure.

  1. The Power of Community

Rarely do we realize the power of community when faced with problems. The collective wisdom and knowledge can turn seemingly unbreakable barriers into steps to progress. By connecting with peers, mentors, and people with diverse backgrounds, we internalize different perspectives and strategies for tackling problems. This diversity not only enhances the problem-solving experience but also builds an enriching support network that nurtures growth and personal development.

  1. The Love for the Journey

This point brings together all the previous ones - this isn’t about rushing to the solution and engaging in a means-to-an-end mentality. Rather, it’s about savoring every moment you spend chasing that solution, making peace with it all. There is beauty in the struggle and growth in the loops of trial and error. The genuine love for the journey creates a space that allows passion to bloom and aliveness to take root.

As you venture on this stimulating and wildly rewarding journey of problem-solving with passion, remember that this pursuit of aliveness is an ongoing experiment in life. Embrace every challenge, every “failure,” and every “success” as necessary steps on this inexorable road toward self-actualization and a deeply fulfilling life. The more we relish the journey, the more alive we become, setting ourselves on a path to truly live, love, and leave an indelible mark on the world.

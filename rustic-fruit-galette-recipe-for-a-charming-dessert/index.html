Rustic Fruit Galette Recipe for a Charming Dessert


Are you looking for a delicious dessert that will impress your guests with its rustic charm? Look no further than this delightful Rustic Fruit Galette. With its golden, flaky crust and juicy, sweet filling, this galette is the perfect dessert for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a homemade treat, this recipe is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup ice water
  • 4 cups mixed fresh fruits (such as berries, peaches, or apples)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon coarse sugar (for sprinkling)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingertips to cut the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  2. Gradually add the ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix. Form the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  3. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  4. In another bowl, gently toss the mixed fresh fruits with the granulated sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice until well coated. Set aside.

  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle, about 1/8 inch thick. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto the prepared baking sheet.

  6. Arrange the fruit mixture in the center of the dough, leaving a 2-inch border around the edges. Fold the edges of the dough over the fruit, pleating as necessary to create a rustic look.

  7. Brush the edges of the dough with the beaten egg wash and sprinkle with coarse sugar for a beautiful golden crust.

  8. Bake the galette in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the fruit is bubbling.

  9. Remove from the oven and let the galette cool for a few minutes before serving. This dessert is delicious on its own, but you can also serve it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Tips and Variations

  • Experiment with different combinations of fruits to create your own unique galette. Try using peaches and blueberries, apples and cranberries, or strawberries and rhubarb.
  • For an extra touch of flavor, sprinkle some cinnamon or nutmeg over the fruit before folding the dough.
  • If you don’t have fresh fruits on hand, you can also use frozen fruits. Just make sure to thaw and drain them before using.
  • Feel free to adjust the amount of sugar in the filling according to your taste preference. If the fruits are particularly sweet, you can reduce the amount of sugar.

Enjoy the simple elegance of this Rustic Fruit Galette as a charming dessert for any occasion. Its rustic beauty and delicious flavor will surely make it a crowd-pleaser. So go ahead, dust off your apron, and give this recipe a try. Your taste buds will thank you!

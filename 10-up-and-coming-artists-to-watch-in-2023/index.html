10 Up-and-Coming Artists to Watch in 2023


As the music industry continues to evolve, new talents emerge, bringing fresh sounds and innovative styles that captivate audiences worldwide. In 2023, a diverse array of artists is making waves across various genres, each with a unique story and sound. Here’s a look at ten up-and-coming artists who are poised to take the music scene by storm this year.

1. Olivia Dean

Olivia Dean is a British singer-songwriter whose soulful voice and poignant lyrics have garnered attention from music lovers and critics alike. With her debut EP, “Growth,” she explores themes of love, self-discovery, and resilience. Dean’s ability to blend pop and R&B elements creates a sound that is both contemporary and timeless. Her live performances are known for their emotional depth, making her an artist to watch as she continues to rise in the industry.

2. PinkPantheress

PinkPantheress is a genre-defying artist who has quickly gained a following for her unique blend of pop, electronic, and garage sounds. Her viral hits on platforms like TikTok have introduced her to a global audience, and her debut mixtape, “To Hell With It,” showcases her innovative approach to music. With catchy hooks and relatable lyrics, PinkPantheress is redefining what it means to be a modern pop artist, and her influence is only expected to grow in 2023.

3. Fousheé

Fousheé is an artist whose music transcends traditional genre boundaries. With a sound that incorporates elements of R&B, rock, and alternative, she has carved out a niche for herself in the music landscape. Her breakout single, “Deep End,” became a viral sensation, and her debut album promises to showcase her versatility and artistic vision. Fousheé’s raw and authentic approach to songwriting resonates with listeners, making her a compelling artist to keep an eye on this year.

4. Tate McRae

Tate McRae is a Canadian singer-songwriter and dancer who has been making waves since her early teens. With her relatable lyrics and catchy melodies, she has quickly become a favorite among Gen Z listeners. Her debut album, “I’m Okay,” features a mix of pop and R&B influences, showcasing her growth as an artist. McRae’s ability to connect with her audience through her music and social media presence positions her as a significant force in the industry in 2023.

5. Sabrina Carpenter

Sabrina Carpenter is not just a talented actress; she is also an accomplished singer-songwriter. With her latest album, “Emails I Can’t Send,” Carpenter explores themes of love, heartbreak, and personal growth. Her pop-infused sound, combined with her powerful vocals, has earned her a dedicated fanbase. As she continues to evolve as an artist, Carpenter’s music is expected to resonate with listeners on a deeper level, making her one of the artists to watch this year.

6. Rina Sawayama

Rina Sawayama is a British-Japanese artist known for her eclectic sound and bold aesthetic. Her debut album, “SAWAYAMA,” received critical acclaim for its genre-blending tracks that incorporate pop, R&B, and rock elements. Sawayama’s music often addresses themes of identity, culture, and societal issues, making her a voice for a new generation. With her innovative approach to music and performance, she is set to make a significant impact in 2023.

7. GAYLE

GAYLE burst onto the scene with her hit single “abcdefu,” which quickly became a viral sensation. Her unapologetic lyrics and catchy melodies resonate with listeners, particularly younger audiences. GAYLE’s music combines elements of pop and rock, creating a sound that is both fresh and relatable. As she continues to release new music and connect with her fans, GAYLE is undoubtedly an artist to watch in the coming year.

8. Benee

Benee, a New Zealand singer-songwriter, has gained international recognition for her infectious pop sound and quirky style. Her breakout single, “Supalonely,” became a global hit, showcasing her ability to blend catchy hooks with introspective lyrics. Benee’s music often explores themes of youth, love, and self-acceptance, making her relatable to a wide audience. With her unique sound and vibrant personality, she is poised to continue her ascent in 2023.

9. Yaeji

Yaeji is a Korean-American artist known for her innovative approach to electronic music. Her unique blend of house, pop, and hip-hop elements creates a sound that is both experimental and accessible. Yaeji’s music often features bilingual lyrics, reflecting her cultural background and personal experiences. As she continues to push the boundaries of genre and sound, Yaeji is an artist to keep an eye on in 2023.

10 Expert-Approved Resume Templates to Give Your Job Application an Edge


Have you ever been in the unpleasant situation of receiving no response to your job application? Or perhaps you were invited for an interview, only to bomb it and leave wondering what went wrong? The problem may very well lie in your resume. With thousands of applicants competing for each job opening, your resume needs to stand out, differentiate you from others, and present your qualifications in the best possible light. This is where expert-approved resume templates come into play.

Without further ado, here are the top 10 resume templates, unanimously chosen by the best career experts and industry professionals:

  1. Modern Minimalist

Minimalism has been supremely popular for a reason – it’s extremely effective. A modern minimalist resume template keeps all the necessary information, but presents it with simplicity and elegance. This type of template helps your skills and work experience breathe, making them more digestible to recruiters.

  1. Chronological

A chronological resume template is a classic choice. Best for those with a solid work history, this layout emphasizes your work timeline, making it easy for recruiters to follow your career progression. Since most recruiters look for a steady growth in position and responsibilities, you can’t go wrong with this template.

  1. Functional

When experience is the problem, you want to spotlight your skills. A functional resume template emphasizes your capabilities, helping you bypass your brief work history. By doing so, this layout can be your ticket to securing interviews, even with limited experience.

  1. Combination

A hybrid of the chronological and functional layouts, a combination resume template is perfect for experienced professionals who potentially lack in relevant skills. This template highlights both your work experience and qualifications, providing a balanced view of your strengths.

  1. Infographic

Visuals are more appealing than plain text, and recruiters are humans too. An infographic resume template adds an engaging dimension to your CV, making your skills and experience pop with bold graphics and pleasing colors. Chosen by several tech startups and design firms, you increase your chances of impressing unique creatives recruiters.

  1. Two Columns

A two-column resume template offers a neat and clean look, making it easy to digest and scan in seconds. By dividing relevant sections like work experience and skills in separate columns, this format ensures crucial details do not get lost among heavy text blocks.

  1. Vertical

A vertical resume template, resembling a tall, linear layout from top to bottom, is a cool option for those with diverse interests and multiple skills. Perfect for switched career paths or professional changelings, this layout showcases your wide skill set and various experiences in a single view.

  1. Reverse-Chronological

Curating your expertise is not easy when just starting out. For recent grads or entry-level candidates, a reverse-chronological resume template can be beneficial. By presenting your education first, followed by your experience, this format lays a strong foundation for recruiters to engage your qualifications.

  1. Skills-based

Hide the coffee cup photo and keep the boring grids, for a skills-based resume template is all about showcasing your unique superpowers. Best for niche industries or unusual job roles where exact skills are non-negotiable, this layout doesn’t leave any doubts about your eligibility.

  1. Digital

Our world is digital – your resume should be too. A digital resume template reflects your adaptability and fluidity in a digital landscape, and can easily be shared via email or submitted using online application platforms. Tailored for technological firms and sales roles, a digital resume template is trendy and modern.

Let’s take a moment to recap. These 10 resume templates are chosen by industry experts to give your job application the edge over the competition. Now, it’s your turn to use them wisely. So polish your resume, pick the perfect template, and never doubt the impact of a visually appealing layout. Trust us; the outcome will amaze you. Happy job hunting!

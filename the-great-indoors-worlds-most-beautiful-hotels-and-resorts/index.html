The Great Indoors: World's Most Beautiful Hotels and Resorts


When it comes to vacation destinations, people often dream of exotic islands, stunning beaches, and breathtaking mountain views. However, the world’s most luxurious hotels and resorts have proven that you don’t need to venture far from the beaten path to find unparalleled beauty and indulgence. From lavish spas to impeccably designed interiors, these five hotels and resorts are the epitome of opulence and elegance, offering an equal, if not greater, dose of escapism - The Great Indoors!

Amanpuri, Phuket, Thailand

Perched on a serene island in the turquoise waters of the Andaman Sea lies Amanpuri, a premium resort in Phuket that seamlessly blends traditional Thai aesthetic with the tranquility of natural surroundings. Encapsulating the essence of barefoot luxury, the resort consists of spacious, private villas, nestled on a lush hillside. Each villa offers spectacular views of the sea, along with an infinity pool that seems to stretch endlessly towards the horizon. Among the highlights at Amanpuri is the chance to visit a private beach, unwind at the Prana Wellness Spa, and take part in personalized experiences such as yoga, scuba diving, and custom-designed journeys.

Aria Resort & Casino, Las Vegas, Nevada

Heading to the desert oasis of Las Vegas, ARIA Resort & Casino encapsulates the beauty of contemporary elegance with its sleek skyline and impressive architecture. This 5-star hotel boasts countless facilities and luxurious amenities that cater to your every whim. With its Michael Jackson ONE show, the resort brings the Jackson legacy to life in a groundbreaking show concept, and the ARIA’s Spa and Salon offers rejuvenating treatments with an emphasis on holistic health and wellness. Don’t miss out on the unique sky portfolios in the resort, encompassing unmatched views of the glittering Las Vegas Strip, and unparalleled culinary experiences at one of the many unique restaurants, featuring master chefs.

Six Senses Fiji, Malolo Island, Fiji

Head halfway across the world to this peaceful archipelago of Fiji, where Six Senses Fiji enchants with its picturesque setting alongside the turquoise waters of Malolo Island. The resort spans across a protected marine sanctuary, meaning a wealth of diving, snorkeling experiences, and water activities are just a stone’s throw away. The low-lying architecture and materials at the Six Senses Fiji perfectly complement the island’s natural landscape, with each room offering panoramic views of ocean and sky. Guests at the resort can indulge in customized wellness experiences, savor local and international cuisines, or escape to the serene Spa, which boasts a range of transformative treatments.

Hotel Hermitage, Montecarlo, Monaco

Retreat to the sophistication of the French Riviera as you set foot in Hotel Hermitage Montecarlo, a gem nestled in the heart of Monaco. Decorated with elegant and luxurious French decor, this belle epoque comes complete with antique furniture, fine artwork, and breathtaking panoramas of the Mediterranean Sea. The property is home to none other than the well-known Le Vistamar restaurant, which boasts a menu of delicious Mediterranean cuisine and excellent wine pairings. After indulging in the culinary experience, enjoy a relaxing spa escape and relax in one of the secluded outdoor pools, which offer unparalleled views of Monaco’s glittering skyline.

Baniyass Hotel, Cappadocia, Turkey

Venture to the enchanting landscape of Cappadocia, Turkey, where the Baniyass Hotel awaits with dreamy hill top accommodations that blend modern design with local history and culture. Designed by acclaimed architectural studio Autoban, this unique resort reflects the region’s rich heritage of warm hospitality and fairy chimneys through its clean lines and glamorous interiors. With private caves available for guests and spectacular hot air balloon rides that showcase the dramatic landscape, the Baniyass Hotel offers a truly romantic and unforgettable getaway. In addition, guests can sample delicious Anatolian cuisine at the in-house restaurant or unwind at the hotel’s spa, which caters to an all-encompassing sense of rejuvenation.

As we conclude our expedition of The Great Indoors, it is evident that the world’s most mesmerizing hotels and resorts offer experiences and designs that cater to every traveler’s taste. These luxurious retreats emphasize the balance of natural beauty and indulgent comfort, providing unforgettable journeys that transcend geographical and cultural boundaries. Let these dreamy escapes inspire your next adventure, and allow the wonders of the world’s most beautiful hotels and resorts to sweep you off your feet.

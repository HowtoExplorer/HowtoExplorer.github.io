10 Must-Try Craft Fairs and Shows in 2024


Craft fairs and shows are not just events; they are vibrant celebrations of creativity, community, and craftsmanship. As we step into 2024, a plethora of exciting craft fairs and shows are set to take place across the country, showcasing the talents of artisans, crafters, and makers from various backgrounds. Whether you’re an avid collector, a DIY enthusiast, or simply looking for unique gifts, these events offer a treasure trove of handmade goods, workshops, and experiences. Here are ten must-try craft fairs and shows you should consider attending in 2024.

1. The Renegade Craft Fair – Various Locations

The Renegade Craft Fair is a staple in the world of indie craft. In 2024, this beloved fair will pop up in cities like Chicago, Brooklyn, and San Francisco. Known for its eclectic mix of vendors, the fair features handmade goods ranging from jewelry and textiles to home decor and artisanal food. Attendees can engage with the crafters, participate in workshops, and enjoy live music, making it a perfect weekend outing for families and friends.

Highlights:

  • Over 200 local and national vendors
  • Hands-on workshops for all ages
  • Food trucks featuring local cuisine

2. The American Craft Council Show – Baltimore, MD

Scheduled for February 23-25, 2024, the American Craft Council Show in Baltimore is one of the largest and most prestigious craft shows in the country. This event will feature around 650 artists showcasing contemporary craft, including ceramics, glass, jewelry, and textiles. With its focus on high-quality craftsmanship and innovation, this show attracts both serious collectors and casual shoppers.

Highlights:

  • Meet the artists behind the work
  • Daily demonstrations and lectures
  • Special exhibitions featuring emerging artists

3. Unique LA – Los Angeles, CA

Unique LA is not just a craft fair; it’s a celebration of the local creative economy. Taking place on May 4-5, 2024, this event will feature over 300 designers and makers showcasing their unique products. From clothing and accessories to home goods and art, Unique LA is a fantastic opportunity to support local artisans while enjoying live music, food, and drinks.

Highlights:

  • Free DIY craft stations for attendees
  • A curated selection of local food vendors
  • Live music and entertainment throughout the weekend

4. The Country Living Fair – Nashville, TN

From June 14-16, 2024, the Country Living Fair will transform Nashville into a haven for craft lovers. This fair combines shopping with learning, offering a variety of workshops on topics like gardening, crafting, and home improvement. With a focus on vintage and handmade goods, visitors can browse through antiques, handmade crafts, and artisanal foods.

Highlights:

  • Workshops hosted by renowned experts
  • Unique vintage finds and handmade creations
  • Live demonstrations and book signings

5. CraftBoston Spring – Boston, MA

CraftBoston Spring, taking place from April 5-7, 2024, showcases the best in contemporary craft from across the country. This juried show features over 100 artists specializing in various mediums, including ceramics, textiles, and jewelry. Attendees can expect high-quality craftsmanship and the opportunity to purchase unique pieces directly from the artists.

Highlights:

  • Juried selection of top artists
  • Networking opportunities for aspiring crafters
  • Interactive sessions with master artisans

6. The Handmade Market – Raleigh, NC

On March 9, 2024, Raleigh will host The Handmade Market, a curated craft fair featuring local artisans. This event is perfect for those seeking unique, handmade gifts and home goods. With a focus on sustainability and local craftsmanship, visitors can shop for everything from handmade soaps to handcrafted furniture.

Highlights:

  • Emphasis on sustainable and eco-friendly products
  • Local food vendors offering tasty treats
  • Engaging activities for children

7. The Great American Craft Fair – San Diego, CA

Taking place on July 20-21, 2024, the Great American Craft Fair is a celebration of American craftsmanship. This event will feature hundreds of artisans from across the nation, showcasing their handmade goods. Visitors can enjoy live music, food trucks, and interactive craft stations, making it a fun day out for the whole family.

Highlights:

  • A diverse range of crafts and goods
  • Family-friendly activities and entertainment
  • Opportunities to meet and interact with artisans

8. The National Arts and Crafts Conference – Asheville, NC

Scheduled for February 15-17, 2024, the National Arts and Crafts Conference in Asheville is a must-visit for anyone interested in the Arts and Crafts movement. This event features a vintage craft fair, as well as workshops and lectures by experts in the field. It’s a great opportunity to learn about the history of craftsmanship while shopping for authentic Arts and Crafts pieces.

Highlights:

  • Workshops on traditional crafting techniques
  • Lectures from renowned historians and artisans
  • A focus on authentic vintage items

9. The Indie Craft Parade – Greenville, SC

The Indie Craft Parade, happening on September 20-22, 2024, is a premier event for handmade goods in the Southeast. This juried craft show features over 100 artists and makers, along with food vendors and live music. The festival atmosphere makes it a perfect outing for families and friends looking to explore the best of local craftsmanship.

Highlights:

  • A vibrant festival atmosphere with live entertainment
  • A diverse range of handmade goods
  • Opportunities to participate in hands-on workshops

10. The Philadelphia Museum of Art Craft Show – Philadelphia, PA

Taking place from November 8-10, 2024, the Philadelphia Museum of Art Craft Show is a prestigious event that features over 100 of the nation’s best contemporary craft artists. This juried show provides a unique shopping experience, with a wide array of handcrafted items, including jewelry, textiles, and pottery. Proceeds from the fair support the museum, making it a meaningful way to enjoy crafts while contributing to the arts.

Highlights:

  • A juried selection of top contemporary artists
  • Unique and high-quality handmade items
  • Special exhibitions and programs throughout the weekend

Conclusion

Craft fairs and shows offer an unparalleled opportunity to connect with artisans, explore unique handmade goods, and immerse yourself in the local creative community. As you plan your calendar for 2024, make sure to include these ten must-try events. Each one promises not only a chance to shop but also a chance to learn, create, and celebrate craftsmanship in all its forms. So gather your friends and family, and experience the joy of handmade at these incredible craft fairs and shows!

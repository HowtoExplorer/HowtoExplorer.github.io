The Best Robotics Podcasts to Follow in 2024


The field of robotics has seen leaps and bounds in innovation over the last decade, and 2024 is shaping up to be another groundbreaking year. As robotics continues to drive advancements in industries ranging from healthcare and manufacturing to space exploration and artificial intelligence, staying informed about the latest developments has never been more important. Podcasts have emerged as one of the most accessible and engaging ways to learn about the world of robotics. Whether you’re a passionate enthusiast, a professional in the field, or simply curious, there’s a podcast out there tailored to your interests.

In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best robotics podcasts to follow in 2024. From in-depth discussions with experts to thought-provoking debates about the ethical implications of robotics, these podcasts offer a wealth of insights and ideas.

1. Robotics Tomorrow: Industry Insights

As a mainstay for those actively working in the robotics sector, Robotics Tomorrow: Industry Insights is an essential listen. Hosted by a panel of robotics engineers and technologists, the podcast delves into emerging trends, technological breakthroughs, and challenges facing the robotics industry.

The episodes often venture into highly technical territory, making it a fantastic resource for professionals and students who want to deepen their understanding of topics like autonomous systems, robotic process automation (RPA), and machine learning integration in robotics. The show also features prominent guests—from researchers in academia to entrepreneurs launching disruptive robotics startups.

Why it’s worth listening:

  • Detailed coverage of robotics innovations shaping industries like logistics, agriculture, and defense.
  • Expert interviews that provide insider perspectives.
  • Actionable advice for those pursuing careers in robotics or engineering sectors.

2. AI and Beyond: The Robotics Edition

Known for its engaging exploration of high-level topics, AI and Beyond: The Robotics Edition sits at the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics. AI is increasingly intertwined with robotics, and this podcast discusses how that synergy is leading to smarter machines capable of complex decision-making.

Topics range from deep dives into reinforcement learning and neural networks to more accessible discussions about the future of human-robot collaboration in workplaces. The host’s conversational tone makes even dense subjects easy to follow.

What makes it stand out:

  • Broad accessibility, from technical explorations to episodes designed for beginners.
  • Unique focus on robotics’ integration with AI technologies.
  • Exploration of ethical and philosophical implications of autonomous robots.

3. Robohub Podcast

For a balanced mix of cutting-edge research and stories about practical real-world applications, Robohub Podcast is a must-follow. Now in its 10th year, this podcast has built a reputation for connecting listeners with insights from top innovators in robotics. The structure of each episode varies: some focus on interviews with industry leaders, while others highlight lesser-known applications of robots in areas like art, education, and disaster response.

The podcast’s strength lies in its ability to make the field of robotics relatable to a wide audience, including students, hobbyists, and industry veterans.

Key highlights:

  • A comprehensive range of robotics topics, from bipedal robots to drones.
  • Interviews with global thought leaders and startup founders.
  • Practical stories about how robots are impacting the world in unexpected ways.

4. Talking Robotics

Talking Robotics has steadily gained prominence as an in-depth, long-form podcast diving into the nuances of robotics development. Each episode features an insightful one-on-one conversation with a guest who has made notable contributions to the field, such as roboticists, policymakers, or venture capitalists specializing in tech innovation.

One of the unique aspects of this podcast is its focus on the challenges behind creating robotics startups, from securing funding to navigating ethical dilemmas. It’s particularly informative for anyone interested in entrepreneurship within the robotics space.

Reasons to tune in:

  • Long-form interviews provide nuanced insights into emerging robotics challenges.
  • Practical advice for robotics startups and small businesses.
  • Deep discussions about societal implications and future policy-making.

5. The Autonomous Future

This podcast is dedicated to exploring the role automation plays in shaping the future of work, travel, and everyday life. Hosted by a seasoned journalist with years of experience covering robotics and AI, The Autonomous Future delves into how robots and automated systems are changing the societal landscape.

Episodes often feature lively debates on topics such as the ethics of autonomous systems, the potential job displacement caused by automation, and the possibilities of a fully autonomous transportation system.

Why it’s essential listening:

  • Thought-provoking discussions about the societal ramifications of robotics.
  • Exploration of ways robotics impacts future workforce trends.
  • Balanced perspectives from multiple industries, including economics, tech, and public policy.

6. Robot Talk

Robot Talk is a playful and approachable podcast that makes robotics accessible to everyone. Unlike some more technical shows, this one is tailored to listeners who might be new to the field but have a sense of curiosity about where robotics is headed. Episodes often focus on quirky robotic applications, like robots that can paint, create music, or help clean up the environment.

With its broad audience appeal, Robot Talk manages to educate while keeping its tone lighthearted and enjoyable. This podcast is perfect for casual listeners, families, or educators looking for content to share with students.

Reasons to listen:

  • Fun and creative storytelling around robotics.
  • Less technical, while still informative and engaging.
  • Emphasis on robotics applications that benefit society.

7. RoboEthics Today

The intersection of ethics and robotics has become one of the most pressing debates of our time. RoboEthics Today tackles difficult questions such as: Should robots have rights? How do we ensure AI-driven robots are unbiased? What safeguards are needed to prevent misuse of autonomous weapons?

Each episode explores a critical question related to the ethical challenges of designing and deploying robots. Listeners will hear from ethicists, legal experts, and roboticists—making this podcast an ideal choice for those interested in the philosophical side of robotics and AI.

Why you’ll love it:

  • A focus on the ethical consequences of robotics and automation.
  • Expert insight into the future of robotics legislation and governance.
  • Encourages meaningful reflection on humanity’s relationship with technology.

8. Robotics Careers Spotlight

Geared toward listeners aspiring to join the robotics workforce, Robotics Careers Spotlight offers invaluable career advice for students, early career professionals, and even seasoned engineers who want to pivot into robotics. Each episode highlights different career paths within robotics, providing actionable advice on how to break into this high-demand industry.

The podcast also features interviews with professionals working in robotics, allowing listeners to gain first-hand insight into what a day in the life of a roboticist is like.

Why follow this podcast:

  • Tailored guidance on pursuing robotics-related careers.
  • Industry insider tips for landing internships, jobs, and promotions.
  • Exploration of diverse roles, from programming and hardware design to marketing in robotics businesses.

9. Space Robotics Frontiers

Robots play an increasingly critical role in space exploration, and Space Robotics Frontiers is the go-to podcast for understanding how these machines are expanding humanity’s reach into the cosmos. From Mars rovers to robotic lunar landers, the episodes cover how robotics is enabling groundbreaking discoveries in astronomy and beyond.

This podcast caters to space enthusiasts as well as robotics professionals who are curious about applying their expertise to extraterrestrial ventures.

Why space fans love it:

  • A deep dive into the cutting-edge field of space robotics.
  • Insights into NASA, SpaceX, and other players pushing boundaries.
  • Exciting discussions about the uncharted terrain robots will explore in the future.

Conclusion: Plug into the World of Robots

Robotics represents one of the most dynamic and future-facing technological fields, and there’s no better way to stay informed than through podcasts. The list above represents a diverse mix of shows catering to various interests in the robotics world, from technical deep dives to philosophical explorations and career-building advice. Whether you’re a seasoned professional or just getting started in your robotics journey, these podcasts are sure to enrich your understanding of the field and inspire new ideas.

Prepare to immerse yourself in fascinating discussions, bold predictions, and insightful conversations about the ever-evolving landscape of robotics. 2024 offers a wealth of knowledge just waiting to be explored—so plug in, press play, and enjoy the journey!

Family-Friendly Meals That Even Picky Eaters Will Love


Introducing new and healthy meals into your household, especially when dealing with picky eaters, can be a challenging task. If you’re in need of some inspiration, here are 10 tasty and nutritious family-friendly recipes that’ll have everyone looking forward to dinner time.

  1. Chicken and Cheese Quesadillas

One of the best aspects of quesadillas is their versatility – they can be customized with any vegetables and cheeses you like! Start by cooking chopped onions, bell peppers, and chicken in olive oil. Once the chicken is cooked through, add in your choice of cheese – cheddar, mozzarella, or even vegan cheese – then fold the tortillas over the filling and press them down lightly. Delicious!

  1. Vegetarian Chili

This hearty chili is not only a perfect meat-free meal but also a great way to include a variety of vegetables into your diet. Make your own vegetable stock and simmer with kidney beans, onions, chili powder, and fresh tomatoes. Top with avocado, cilantro, and a dash of lime.

  1. Whole Wheat Pasta with Tomato-Vegetable Sauce

This classic pasta dish gets a healthy upgrade by using whole wheat pasta and adding in a colorful array of vegetables. Cherry tomatoes, kale, and spinach make for a bright, flavorful sauce. Serve with garlic bread for an easy and family-friendly meal.

  1. Breakfast-for-Dinner Sandwiches

A fun spin on the classic breakfast sandwich, this lunchtime staple is perfect for serving at any time of day. Start with scrambled eggs in a buttery skillet, then place a slice of crunchy toast on top. Add bacon, tomato slices, and cheese for a morning-to-night creation.

  1. Grilled Cheese with Tomato Soup

Who can resist a childhood favorite? For this healthy rendition of comfort food, make your own tomato soup from scratch or invest in a quality jar – the immersion blender smooths it out beautifully. Serve alongside a grilled cheese sandwich made with your choice of bread and a variety of cheeses.

  1. Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Keep it simple with this flavorful, family-friendly chicken dish that is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. Whip up a simple marinade out of lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and any herbs you have on hand – and in no time, you’ll have a nutritious meal that your family will be asking for seconds on.

  1. Broiled Salmon with Ginger Soy Glaze

A quick trip to the market for fresh salmon yields this delicious, healthy dish. Drizzle the salmon with a ginger soy glaze made from fresh ginger, low-sodium soy sauce, and honey before broiling. The pleasing flavor of ginger lends a right balance to the savory taste of salmon – it’s a dinner win!

  1. Baked Sweet Potato Fries

To satisfy a French fry craving without the guilt, reach for sweet potatoes instead. Cut into wedges and bake in the oven with olive oil, garlic powder, and paprika seasoning. Your picky eater will be pleasantly surprised by the taste and goodness of this healthier fry option.

  1. Slow-Cooked Carnitas

Slow-cooked Carnitas are the ultimate crowd-pleaser. Shred a few pounds of pork and cook it low and slow with orange juice, garlic, and onion, and then bake until crispy. Serve with a side salad or roasted vegetables for a wholesome, satisfying dinner everyone will enjoy.

  1. Chicken_tacquitos

For a fun, easy meal, try chicken_tacquitos using leftover grilled chicken. Mix cream cheese, shredded chicken, and a dash of jalapeno peppers. Wrap in your favorite size tortillas and bake in the oven with your favorite sauce for dipping.

Delight your family with these savory recipes that’ll have even the pickiest eaters licking their plates clean. Implementing simple strategies like ingredient swaps and using a slow-cooker or oven can create dishes without sacrificing taste or time spent with your loved ones. Bon appétit!

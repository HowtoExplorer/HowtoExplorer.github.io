Top 5 Real Estate Investment Podcasts You Should Listen To


Are you looking to delve into the world of real estate investing? Whether you’re a seasoned investor or just getting started, podcasts can be an invaluable resource for gaining insights, learning strategies, and staying updated with the latest trends in the real estate market. With a myriad of options available, it can be overwhelming to find the best real estate investment podcasts. To help you navigate through the sea of podcasts, we’ve curated a list of the top 5 real estate investment podcasts that are worth tuning in to.

1. The Real Estate Guys Radio Show

Hosted by Robert Helms and Russell Gray, The Real Estate Guys Radio Show is a long-standing podcast that covers a wide range of real estate investing topics. From market analysis to property management and financing, this podcast offers in-depth discussions and interviews with industry experts. Whether you’re interested in residential, commercial, or international real estate, The Real Estate Guys Radio Show provides valuable insights and actionable tips for investors at every level.

2. BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcast

BiggerPockets has established itself as a go-to platform for real estate investors, and their podcast is no exception. Hosted by Brandon Turner and David Greene, the BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcast features interviews with successful investors, authors, and entrepreneurs, offering practical advice and real-life experiences. The podcast covers a wide array of real estate investment strategies, from flipping houses to passive income through rental properties, making it a valuable resource for investors looking to expand their knowledge.

3. The Real Wealth Show

Kathy Fettke, the host of The Real Wealth Show, brings a wealth of knowledge to the world of real estate investing. This podcast focuses on the intersection of real estate and wealth building, covering topics such as market trends, tax strategies, and investment opportunities. With a strong emphasis on creating passive income and building long-term wealth through real estate, The Real Wealth Show provides actionable insights that align with the financial goals of many investors.

4. Invest Like a Boss

While not exclusively focused on real estate, Invest Like a Boss, hosted by Sam Marks and Johnny FD, offers a broader perspective on investment strategies, including real estate. The podcast features interviews with successful investors and entrepreneurs from various industries, providing diverse insights into wealth creation and portfolio diversification. For real estate investors looking to gain a holistic view of investment opportunities, Invest Like a Boss offers valuable takeaways that can be applied to the real estate market.

5. Rental Income Podcast

The Rental Income Podcast, hosted by Dan Lane, is tailored for investors seeking to generate passive income through rental properties. Each episode features interviews with real estate investors who share their experiences, challenges, and success stories in building a rental property portfolio. From acquiring the first rental property to scaling up and maximizing rental income, this podcast offers practical guidance and real-world examples that resonate with both novice and experienced investors.

In conclusion, real estate investment podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge and inspiration for investors seeking to thrive in the dynamic world of real estate. Whether you’re interested in residential properties, commercial real estate, or wealth-building through passive income, the aforementioned podcasts provide valuable insights, strategies, and expert advice to help you navigate the complexities of real estate investing. So, tune in, absorb the wisdom, and take your real estate investment journey to new heights!

The Best Self-Care Podcasts to Inspire You


In our fast-paced world, taking care of our mental, emotional, and physical health is more important than ever. Whether you’re dealing with the demands of work, family, or simply trying to find balance in a busy lifestyle, self-care is crucial to maintaining your overall well-being. One of the best ways to prioritize self-care is by immersing yourself in inspiring content that helps you stay grounded, motivated, and informed.

Podcasts have become an incredibly popular medium for self-care enthusiasts, offering a convenient way to absorb insightful discussions, expert advice, and inspiring stories. Whether you’re commuting, doing household chores, or relaxing at home, podcasts provide a flexible way to engage in personal growth. Here’s a curated list of some of the best self-care podcasts that will inspire and motivate you to prioritize your health and wellness.

1. The Self-Care Podcast

Hosted by Tracy Duhs, a wellness expert, The Self-Care Podcast provides practical tips and expert advice on how to create space for self-care in your daily life. Each episode explores different aspects of self-care, from mindfulness practices to stress management, sleep optimization, and building healthy habits. Tracy invites a range of wellness experts to share their insights, making this podcast a fantastic resource for anyone looking to improve their self-care routine.

With a focus on actionable steps and a holistic approach to health, The Self-Care Podcast encourages listeners to prioritize their needs and take care of their mental, emotional, and physical well-being. Whether you’re new to self-care or looking to deepen your practice, Tracy’s thoughtful interviews and discussions provide inspiration and guidance.

Why Listen:

  • Expert advice on a range of self-care topics.
  • Actionable steps to implement into your daily life.
  • Focus on holistic well-being.

2. The Happiness Lab with Dr. Laurie Santos

Dr. Laurie Santos, a professor of psychology at Yale University, is the host of The Happiness Lab podcast. Drawing on scientific research and real-life applications, this podcast delves into the science of happiness and well-being. Dr. Santos explores evidence-based strategies to enhance mental health, increase happiness, and develop resilience. She interviews leading experts and shares research-backed insights that can help you lead a more fulfilling life.

This podcast is a treasure trove of scientific knowledge and practical strategies that will motivate you to cultivate positivity, mindfulness, and emotional intelligence. If you’re looking for a well-rounded approach to boosting your mood and improving your outlook on life, The Happiness Lab is the perfect listen.

Why Listen:

  • Evidence-based approach to happiness and well-being.
  • Practical strategies to enhance mental health and happiness.
  • Insights from leading experts in the field of psychology.

3. Therapy Chat

If you’re interested in exploring the therapeutic side of self-care, Laura Reagan, the host of Therapy Chat, offers an insightful and calming approach to mental health and wellness. This podcast is designed to help you learn about trauma recovery, mindfulness, and psychotherapy, providing valuable information for those on a healing journey.

Laura’s discussions often focus on trauma-informed practices, offering listeners resources and tools to support emotional well-being and self-compassion. She interviews trauma experts and therapists, covering topics such as attachment theory, polyvagal theory, and self-regulation techniques. Therapy Chat is an excellent resource for those looking to better understand their emotions and mental health through a compassionate lens.

Why Listen:

  • Deep dives into trauma recovery and therapy practices.
  • Compassionate, nurturing approach to mental health.
  • Expert interviews offering healing insights.

4. UnF*ck Your Brain

Hosted by Caroline Dooner, this podcast brings a unique and refreshing perspective to the self-care and wellness space. Caroline’s witty and candid approach encourages listeners to ditch societal norms and expectations in favor of living authentically. UnF*ck Your Brain tackles issues like body image, diet culture, and mental health with a dose of humor, making it easy to engage with challenging topics in a lighthearted yet impactful way.

Caroline’s mission is to empower women to take control of their health and well-being without the pressure of conforming to societal ideals. By shifting the conversation away from perfectionism and focusing on authenticity, this podcast fosters a sense of liberation and self-love.

Why Listen:

  • Straightforward, unapologetic approach to self-care.
  • Empowering listeners to break free from societal pressures.
  • Combines humor and empowerment to tackle tough topics.

5. The Mindful Kind

Hosted by Rachael Kable, The Mindful Kind podcast is a great resource for anyone interested in cultivating mindfulness in their daily life. Mindfulness is an essential component of self-care, and this podcast offers simple, practical tips on how to incorporate mindfulness practices into your routine. Whether you’re dealing with stress, anxiety, or just want to feel more present, Rachael’s episodes provide effective strategies that can be easily implemented.

The podcast covers various mindfulness techniques, including breathing exercises, meditation, and body awareness. Rachael also delves into topics like emotional intelligence, self-compassion, and the power of being present in the moment, helping listeners develop a deeper connection with themselves and their surroundings.

Why Listen:

  • Practical tips for incorporating mindfulness into daily life.
  • Focus on emotional intelligence and self-compassion.
  • Accessible advice for managing stress and anxiety.

6. The Life Coach School Podcast

If you’re seeking guidance on personal development and mindset, Brooke Castillo’s The Life Coach School Podcast is an excellent choice. Brooke is a renowned life coach who teaches listeners how to use their thoughts and emotions to create the life they desire. Her podcast covers a wide range of self-care topics, including mental clarity, confidence, productivity, and goal-setting.

Brooke’s powerful coaching techniques and straightforward advice empower listeners to take control of their thoughts, break through limiting beliefs, and create lasting change. Whether you’re looking to improve your career, relationships, or overall mindset, this podcast offers invaluable tools for achieving personal growth and success.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on mindset and personal development.
  • Practical coaching techniques to overcome limiting beliefs.
  • Tools for building confidence and achieving goals.

7. The Balanced Life

Robin Long, a Pilates instructor and wellness expert, hosts The Balanced Life podcast, which offers a holistic approach to health and well-being. Robin’s message is all about finding balance in life, especially when it comes to fitness, nutrition, and self-care. Each episode is filled with actionable advice that encourages listeners to take a gentle, sustainable approach to health.

In addition to discussing fitness routines and nutrition tips, Robin also dives into topics like body image, self-compassion, and mental wellness. She offers a compassionate approach to wellness that doesn’t focus on perfection but on creating healthy habits that align with your personal values and goals.

Why Listen:

  • Balanced approach to fitness, nutrition, and self-care.
  • Focus on sustainability and self-compassion.
  • Encouragement to create health routines that fit your lifestyle.

8. The Calm Collective

Cassandra Eldridge, the host of The Calm Collective, provides a soothing, peaceful space for listeners to explore the deeper aspects of self-care. This podcast covers a range of topics, from mindfulness and meditation to self-reflection and spirituality. Cassandra’s calming voice and introspective discussions help listeners slow down, tune in to their inner selves, and cultivate a sense of calm amidst the chaos of daily life.

The Calm Collective offers inspiration for those looking to connect with their intuition, practice mindfulness, and foster a deep sense of inner peace. This podcast is ideal for those who want to explore the spiritual side of self-care and create a more centered, grounded lifestyle.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on spirituality and inner peace.
  • Calming, introspective approach to self-care.
  • Helps listeners create a deeper connection with themselves.

9. The Mental Health Foundation Podcast

Hosted by the Mental Health Foundation, this podcast provides a wealth of resources on mental health, self-care, and wellness. With a focus on tackling mental health stigma and promoting well-being, each episode features interviews with experts, mental health advocates, and individuals who share their personal stories of resilience and recovery.

The Mental Health Foundation Podcast covers a wide array of topics, from managing stress and anxiety to building resilience and promoting positive mental health in communities. This podcast is a valuable resource for anyone looking to learn more about mental health care and self-care practices that can support emotional well-being.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on mental health awareness and self-care.
  • Interviews with experts and mental health advocates.
  • Provides actionable tips for managing stress and anxiety.

10. The Art of Charm

While The Art of Charm isn’t solely focused on self-care, it offers a wealth of information on building confidence, improving relationships, and creating a positive mindset. Hosted by Jordan Harbinger, this podcast features interviews with experts in various fields, from psychology and communication to business and personal development.

By focusing on personal growth, social dynamics, and emotional intelligence, The Art of Charm helps listeners build the skills they need to lead fulfilling, confident, and authentic lives. If you’re looking for inspiration to improve your relationships and mindset, this podcast is a great addition to your self-care routine.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on building confidence, emotional intelligence, and relationships.
  • Expert interviews offering insights into personal development.
  • Actionable tips for improving communication and social skills.

Conclusion

Taking care of your mind, body, and spirit is essential for leading a balanced and fulfilling life. Self-care isn’t just about relaxation; it’s about cultivating habits and mindsets that support your overall well-being. The podcasts mentioned above are excellent resources for learning more about how to nurture yourself, improve your mental health, and create a life of happiness and fulfillment.

Whether you’re looking to learn more about mindfulness, enhance your happiness, explore trauma recovery, or simply find inspiration for everyday self-care, these podcasts offer valuable insights and actionable advice. So grab your headphones and start tuning in to these incredible podcasts – your journey to self-care and personal growth is just a click away!

The Best Fitness Podcasts to Keep You Inspired


In the fast-paced world we live in, finding motivation to stay fit and healthy can sometimes feel like an uphill battle. Whether you’re a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, podcasts can be a fantastic source of inspiration, education, and entertainment. They allow you to absorb valuable information while multitasking—be it during your workout, commute, or even while cooking. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best fitness podcasts that can help you stay motivated and informed on your health and wellness journey.

1. The Rich Roll Podcast

Rich Roll, a former ultra-endurance athlete turned wellness advocate, hosts this enlightening podcast. With a focus on plant-based nutrition, mindfulness, and personal growth, Rich interviews a diverse range of guests, including athletes, authors, and wellness experts. Each episode dives deep into the guests’ journeys, offering listeners insights into overcoming obstacles and achieving their goals. The Rich Roll Podcast is perfect for those looking to enhance their physical and mental well-being.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Diverse Topics: From nutrition to mental health, the podcast covers a wide array of subjects.
  • Inspiring Guests: Rich’s guests often share their personal stories of transformation, providing motivation and practical advice.
  • Mindfulness Focus: The emphasis on mindfulness and self-awareness can help listeners cultivate a more balanced approach to fitness.

2. The Model Health Show

Hosted by Shawn Stevenson, The Model Health Show is a treasure trove of information on health, fitness, and personal development. Shawn combines expert interviews with actionable tips, making complex health topics accessible to everyone. The podcast covers everything from nutrition and exercise to sleep and stress management, ensuring that listeners have a well-rounded understanding of what it takes to live a healthy life.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Expert Insights: Shawn interviews leading experts in various fields, providing listeners with cutting-edge information.
  • Actionable Tips: Each episode is packed with practical advice that you can implement immediately.
  • Holistic Approach: The podcast emphasizes the interconnectedness of physical and mental health.

3. FoundMyFitness

Dr. Rhonda Patrick, a renowned biomedical science expert, hosts FoundMyFitness, where she delves into the science of health and fitness. The podcast focuses on topics such as nutrition, exercise, and aging, often featuring interviews with leading researchers and health professionals. Dr. Patrick’s ability to break down complex scientific concepts into digestible information makes this podcast a must-listen for anyone interested in the science behind fitness.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Scientific Approach: Gain insights based on the latest research in health and fitness.
  • Expert Interviews: Dr. Patrick’s guests are often top researchers in their fields, providing valuable knowledge.
  • Focus on Longevity: The podcast emphasizes strategies for healthy aging and longevity.

4. The Dumbbells

If you’re looking for a light-hearted yet informative take on fitness, The Dumbbells is the podcast for you. Hosted by comedians Eugene Cordero and Ryan Stanger, this show combines humor with fitness advice. Each episode features a guest who shares their fitness journey, and the hosts answer listener questions about health and exercise. The Dumbbells is perfect for those who want to learn while having a good laugh.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Humorous Approach: The comedic element makes fitness discussions enjoyable and relatable.
  • Real Stories: Guests share their personal experiences, making the content relatable and inspiring.
  • Listener Engagement: The hosts answer questions from listeners, creating a community feel.

5. The Mind Pump Podcast

Mind Pump is a fitness podcast that aims to debunk common fitness myths and provide listeners with evidence-based information. Hosted by a group of fitness professionals, the podcast covers a wide range of topics, including workout techniques, nutrition, and mental health. The hosts’ candid discussions and expert insights make this podcast a valuable resource for anyone looking to improve their fitness knowledge.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Myth-Busting: The hosts tackle common misconceptions in the fitness industry, helping listeners make informed decisions.
  • Expert Hosts: With backgrounds in personal training and nutrition, the hosts provide credible information.
  • Diverse Topics: The podcast covers everything from workout tips to mental health strategies.

6. The Strength Running Podcast

For those who are passionate about running, The Strength Running Podcast is a fantastic resource. Hosted by Jason Fitzgerald, a running coach and author, this podcast focuses on all aspects of running, from training techniques to injury prevention. Jason interviews elite runners, coaches, and experts, providing listeners with valuable insights to enhance their running performance.

Why You Should Listen:

  • Running-Specific Content: The podcast is tailored for runners, offering specialized advice and tips.
  • Expert Interviews: Gain insights from elite athletes and coaches who share their experiences and knowledge.
  • Injury Prevention

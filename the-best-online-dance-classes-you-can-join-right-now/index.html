The Best Online Dance Classes You Can Join Right Now


Dance is an incredible way to express yourself, relieve stress, and have fun. However, finding the right class that suits your style and schedule can be a challenge. Fortunately, with the rise of online dance classes, you can now learn and master various dance styles from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll highlight some of the best online dance classes you can join right now.

  1. MasterClass: Learn from the World's Best Dancers MasterClass is an online learning platform that offers classes in a wide range of dance styles, including ballet, jazz, contemporary, and hip-hop. With MasterClass, you can learn from some of the world's most renowned dancers, including Misty Copeland, Charles "Lil' Buck" Riley, and Amy Purdy. Each class is led by a different instructor, and you'll get to experience their unique teaching style and techniques. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced dancer, you'll find classes tailored to your level. MasterClass offers a free trial, and then it's $180 per year for unlimited access to their comprehensive library of dance classes.
  2. DanceWithMe: A Fun and Social Dancing Experience DanceWithMe is an online dance studio that offers classes in various dance styles, including salsa, swing, ballroom, and Latin. Their classes are designed to be fun and social, perfect for those who want to dance with a partner or join a dance community. You can take their classes at your own pace, and with their real-time chat feature, you can interact with your instructor and other dancers. DanceWithMe offers a free trial, and their prices are competitive, starting at $14.99 per month for their basic plan.
  3. FitnessBliss: Low-Impact Dance Classes for All Levels FitnessBliss is an online fitness studio that offers low-impact dance classes designed for all levels. Their classes focus on cardiovascular exercise, flexibility, and strength training, making them perfect for those who want to improve their overall fitness while dancing. With over 100 classes to choose from, you can select the ones that suit your schedule and fitness goals. FitnessBliss offers a free trial, and then it's $10.95 per month for their basic plan.
  4. Codarts: Online Dance Classes with a Dutch Twist Codarts is a renowned performing arts school based in Rotterdam, Netherlands. Their online dance classes offer a unique perspective on various dance styles, including ballet, contemporary, hip-hop, and jazz. With Codarts, you'll learn from experienced instructors who will guide you through a series of lessons, exercises, and choreographies. Codarts offers a free trial, and their basic plan starts at $15.95 per month.
  5. Ginga House: Learn Capoeira Online Ginga House is a US-based capoeira school that offers online classes for all levels. Capoeira is a unique dance-martial art that blends African, Portuguese, and Brazilian influences. With Ginga House, you'll learn about the history, culture, and techniques of capoeira. Their classes are designed to be fun and engaging, perfect for those who want to challenge themselves physically and mentally. Ginga House offers a free trial, and their basic plan starts at $20 per month.
  6. Online Dance Academy: Expert Instruction at Your Fingertips The Online Dance Academy is a comprehensive online dance studio that offers classes in various dance styles, including ballet, jazz, hip-hop, and contemporary. Their classes are taught by expert instructors who have extensive experience in teaching dance. You'll have access to over 500 videos, offering a comprehensive learning experience. The Online Dance Academy offers a free trial, and their basic plan starts at $19.95 per month.
  7. Udemy: A Wide Range of Dance Classes at an Affordable Price Udemy is an online learning platform that offers a vast range of courses on dance, including ballet, jazz, contemporary, hip-hop, and ballroom. With Udemy, you can choose from over 100 dance courses, each with its own price and duration. Most classes are affordably priced, with some starting as low as $2.99. You'll have access to course materials, instructor feedback, and a comprehensive learning experience.
  8. Dance House Academy: Expert Instruction in a Variety of Dance Styles Dance House Academy is an online dance studio that offers classes in various dance styles, including ballet, jazz, contemporary, hip-hop, and ballroom. Their classes are taught by expert instructors who have performed with top dance companies and choreographed for TV shows and films. You'll have access to a comprehensive library of classes, each with its own learning objectives and requirements. Dance House Academy offers a free trial, and their basic plan starts at $29.95 per month. With these online dance classes, you can learn from expert instructors, challenge yourself physically and mentally, and join a dance community that shares your passion for dance. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced dancer, you'll find classes tailored to your level and schedule. So why not join an online dance class today and unleash your rhythm? Conclusion Dance is an art form that brings people together. With online dance classes, you can now learn from the world's best dancers and instructors, challenge yourself physically and mentally, and join a dance community that shares your passion for dance. Whether you're looking to improve your dance skills, relieve stress, or have fun, online dance classes offer a flexible and affordable solution. So why not join an online dance class today and unleash your rhythm? Bonus Tip Before you join an online dance class, consider the following:

Look for classes that cater to your level and dance style. Check the instructor's qualifications and experience. Read reviews and testimonials from other students to get a sense of the class quality. Practice regularly to see improvement in your dance skills. Join online dance communities and forums to connect with other dancers and stay updated on new classes and developments.

By following these tips, you'll be well on your way to unleashing your rhythm and enjoying the many benefits of online dance classes.

DIY Macrame Plant Hangers: Adding Greenery to Your Space

4 min · 756 words

Are you looking to add a touch of nature to your living space? Look no further than DIY macrame plant hangers! These beautiful and functional creations are not only a great way to display your favorite plants, but they also bring a unique bohemian vibe to any room. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of creating your own macrame plant hangers, step by step.

Materials You Will Need

Before we dive into the instructions, let’s gather the materials you’ll need for this DIY project:

  • Macrame cord: Choose a sturdy and durable cord that can hold the weight of your plants. Natural fibers like cotton or jute work well for this purpose.

  • Scissors: A sharp pair of scissors will come in handy when cutting the cord to the desired length.

  • Metal rings or hoops: These will serve as the foundation for your macrame plant hangers. You can find them at your local craft store or repurpose old metal rings.

  • Potted plants: Select your favorite plants that will thrive in the environment where you plan to hang your macrame hangers. Consider factors like sunlight and humidity requirements.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you have all the necessary materials, let’s get started on creating your macrame plant hangers:

  1. Measure and cut your macrame cord: Decide how long you want your plant hangers to be and cut the cord accordingly. Remember to leave extra length for knots and hanging purposes.

  2. Attach the cord to the metal ring: Fold the cord in half and loop it through the metal ring. Pull the loose ends of the cord through the loop and tighten to secure it in place.

  3. Begin the macrame knots: Divide the cord into four equal sections. Take the outer left cord and cross it over the two middle cords, forming a loop. Pass the outer right cord under the loop and through the opening created by the left cord. Pull both ends to tighten the knot.

  4. Continue the macrame knots: Repeat step 3 until you’ve reached the desired length for your plant hanger. You can experiment with different knot patterns like square knots or spiral knots to add variety.

  5. Create a hanging loop: Once you’ve reached the desired length, gather all the cords together and create a loop by tying them in a knot. This loop will serve as the hanging mechanism for your macrame plant hanger.

  6. Attach your potted plant: Place your potted plant inside a decorative pot or container, and then hang it from the loop you created in the previous step. Make sure the plant is secure and balanced before letting go.

  7. Repeat the process: If you want to create multiple macrame plant hangers, simply repeat the steps above with different cord lengths and knot patterns.

Benefits of Macrame Plant Hangers

Now that you’ve learned how to make your own macrame plant hangers, let’s explore some of the benefits they bring to your space:

  1. Space-saving: Macrame plant hangers allow you to hang your plants from the ceiling or walls, freeing up valuable floor space.

  2. Natural air purifiers: Plants have the ability to improve air quality by filtering out toxins and releasing oxygen. By hanging your plants at eye level, you can maximize their air-purifying benefits.

  3. Aesthetic appeal: Macrame plant hangers add a touch of elegance and bohemian charm to any room. They create visual interest and make a unique statement piece.

  4. Stress relief: Studies have shown that being around plants can reduce stress levels and promote a sense of calm. By incorporating macrame plant hangers into your space, you create a soothing environment that promotes relaxation.

Conclusion

DIY macrame plant hangers are a fantastic way to bring nature indoors while adding a touch of creativity to your space. With just a few materials and some patience, you can create beautiful hangers that showcase your favorite plants. So why wait? Start crafting your own macrame plant hangers today and enjoy the benefits of greenery in your home or office. Happy crafting!

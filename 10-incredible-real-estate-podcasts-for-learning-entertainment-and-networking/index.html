10 Incredible Real Estate Podcasts for Learning, Entertainment, and Networking


In the ever-evolving world of real estate, it’s essential to stay up-to-date with the latest trends, strategies, and insights from industry experts. With countless podcasts available, selecting the best ones to suit your needs can be a daunting task. No worries, here we have compiled a list of 10 incredible real estate podcasts perfect for learning, entertainment, and networking.

  1. The BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcast

Hosted by David Greene and Travis Wright, The BiggerPockets Real Estate Podcast offers engaging and diverse discussions on various real estate topics. This podcast covers everything from buying and selling properties, investment strategies, and financing options to successful personal stories of real estate investors.

  1. The Real Estate Rookie Podcast

Perfect for those brand new to the world of real estate investing, The Real Estate Rookie Podcast features novice hosts Trent and Eric – who themselves began their investment journey just a few years ago. Their relatable experiences and helpful insights make this an ideal podcast for beginner real estate investors.

  1. Real Estate Investing with niyitiri

For real estate investors looking to dive deep into the nitty-gritty of investment strategies and success stories, the Real Estate Investing with niyitiri podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Niyyir, a real estate investor, and entrepreneur, the podcast provides valuable advice on finding great deals, analyzing properties, and implementing effective growth plans.

  1. The Investor’s Podcast Network

An exceptional platform for real estate investors looking to expand their knowledge and stay ahead of the competition, The Investor’s Podcast Network features a variety of podcasts addressing various investment topics. Guest experts from different industries share their success stories and knowledge, providing essential insights into navigating the complex world of real estate.

  1. The Witness Podcast - Real Estate Storytelling

Delve into the narratives behind extraordinary real estate deals and the minds behind them by subscribing to The Witness Podcast. In each episode, co-hosts Isaiahjad and Deuter interviews successful real estate investors, agents, and entrepreneurs, explaining their inspiring stories and discussing the lessons they learned throughout the process.

  1. The Property Profits Podcast

Hosted by real estate expert and media personality Hayden Ashley and successful real estate investor Al Hariri, the Property Profits Podcast showcases in-depth discussions on investment strategies, tax strategies, and marketing techniques to maximize property profits. This is an excellent podcast for investors seeking expert guidance in all aspects of real estate investing.

  1. Real Estate Rookie - Stories of First Time House Flippers

Dive into the highs and lows of first-time house flipping experiences with the Real Estate Rookie podcast. Hosted by Manny and married duo BiggerPockets, this podcast features the incredible stories of first-time house flippers, offering vital advice and encouragement for aspiring real estate investors.

  1. The Real Estate Financial Model Podcast

Designed for investors eager to hone their quantitative skills, The Real Estate Financial Model podcast focuses on the mathematical aspects of real estate investing. Host Justin Tuia, a certified financial planner, shares fundamental models and lessons to help listeners sharpen their analytical prowess and make data-driven investment decisions.

  1. Real Estate Investing Incubator Podcast

The Real Estate Investing Incubator Podcast is an insightful platform for ambitious real estate investors seeking guidance on business and investing strategies, negotiation tactics, marketing secrets, and profitability hacks. This podcast meticulously covers the entire investing journey, offering a wealth of actionable advice for success.

  1. Real Estate Coffee.Club

A podcast with a casual and engaging format, Real Estate Coffee.Club brings together agents, brokers, financiers, and investors to share their thoughts, experiences, and opinions over a virtual cup of coffee. The informal discussions offer valuable insights on industry trends, hot markets, and investment opportunities to stay informed and up-to-date.

Conclusion

Whether you’re a seasoned real estate investor seeking fresh perspectives or a beginner looking to start your journey, the podcasts listed above have you covered. Subscribe to any or all of these incredible real estate podcasts to stay updated with the latest trends, meet like-minded individuals, and enhance your education in real estate investing. Happy listening!

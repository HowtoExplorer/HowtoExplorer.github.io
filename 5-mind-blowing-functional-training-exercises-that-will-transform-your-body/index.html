5 Mind-Blowing Functional Training Exercises That Will Transform Your Body


Functional training exercises are designed to increase strength, stability, and overall body coordination. Instead of focusing solely on isolated muscle groups, functional training exercises aim to improve everyday movements, incorporating multiple muscle groups and improving real-life coordination. If you’re looking to transform your body and gain real-world benefits, these five mind-blowing exercises will have you well on your way.

1. Turkish Get-Up

Challenge yourself with the Turkish Get-Up, a full-body exercise that pushes your balance, core strength, and coordination. Consisting of seven steps, the Turkish Get-Up starts with lying flat on your back, then carefully transitioning through various positions until you’re standing upright.

A. Begin by lying on your back, with your legs straight and relaxed. B. Lift your legs and shift your hips to form a straight line from your shoulders to your ankles. Keep your eyes in line with your arms as you raise your body upwards. C. Extend your arms towards your toes and lean slightly forwards to create a ‘T’ position with your body. D. Drive through your heels, straightening your legs as you push up using your arms. Stand upright, fully extending your arms above your head. E. Reverse the process back down to the starting position, ensuring you maintain proper control and stability throughout.

2. Kettlebell Swings

Test your strength and stamina with the Kettlebell Swing, a tried-and-true exercise that works multiple muscles simultaneously. By mastering this exercise, you’ll not only increase your power but also improve your grip, core, and lower-back strength.

A. Hold a kettlebell with both hands, standing with your feet no wider than hip-width apart. B. Keep your back straight and hinge at your hips to lower your body and kettlebell, allowing the kettlebell to float in front of you. C. Swing the kettlebell up to chest level by extending your hips and standing upright. D. Let the kettlebell fall and repeat the process. Keep your breathing steady and your movements fluid.

3. Tricep Dip

Struggling with tricep strength? It’s time to level up with the Tricep Dip, a functional exercise that strengthens your triceps, shoulders and stabilizes your core. This compound movement can be performed anywhere – at home, at the gym or outdoors.

A. Find a sturdy surface or parallel bars and position your hands a shoulder-width apart. B. Extend your legs, straightening your arms and supporting your body weight using your triceps. C. Bend your elbows to lower your body towards the ground, then drive upwards and extend your arms to return to the starting position.

4. Battle Rope Waves

Improve your cardiovascular health and unleash your inner warrior by mastering the Battle Rope Waves. Incorporate undulated movements, creating a wave-like pattern, to work various muscle groups in your arms, shoulders, and core.

A. Stand in a slight squat position, holding battle ropes twisted in your hands. B. Move side-to-side, creating undulated waves in the ropes by alternating between pulling and pushing actions. Maintain your feet shoulder-width apart and your core tight for stability. C. Vary the speed or height for a more challenging workout, ensuring you maintain proper form and control.

5. Pistols

Feel the burn with the Pistol Squat, a single-leg exercise that builds lower-body strength and mobility. The mind-muscle connection required for this exercise will transfer to other daily activities, making this one exercise not to be missed in your functional training regimen.

A. Start standing with your feet hip-width apart. B. Step back with one foot and balance on your forward leg, holding it in a bent position as you straighten your back leg behind you. C. Lower your hips until your front thigh is parallel with the ground, keeping your back straight and core tight. D. Drive through your front heel to return to the starting position. Repeat on the other side.

Through consistent practice and persistence, you’ll start to notice improvements not only in these specific exercises but in your everyday life. By integrating these mind-blowing functional training exercises into your routine, you’ll be amazed by the transformations that your body will undergo.
